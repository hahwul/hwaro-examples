# THE SIMPLICITY OF THE UTILITY. THE DIGNITY OF THE ESSENTIAL.

Welcome to **Hornbook**, a traditional elementary theme designed for lessons that value both simplicity and clarity. Inspired by the utility of the historical hornbook, this is a space for essential knowledge, clear instruction, and the enduring beauty of the scholarly word.

## TRADITIONAL UTILITY

We believe that the best lessons deserve a stage as focused as their contents.

- **NO EMOJIS.**
- **NO GRADIENTS.**
- **ONLY THE CLARITY OF THE HORNBOOK.**
