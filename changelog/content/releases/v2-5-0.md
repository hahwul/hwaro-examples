## Added

- Full-text search with Fuse.js integration and `search.json` generation
- Custom taxonomy support beyond tags and categories
- `series` feature for grouping posts into ordered sequences
- Related posts recommendation engine based on shared taxonomy terms

## Changed

- Search index now includes content summaries for better result previews
- Taxonomy pages support pagination with configurable page sizes
- Improved feed generation with proper `<content:encoded>` support

## Fixed

- Taxonomy term pages returning 404 when term contained special characters
- Feed generation failing when posts had no date field
- Search index including draft posts in production builds
