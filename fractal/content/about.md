Fractal is a generative art gallery theme built around the mathematics of self-similarity. Recursive patterns, mathematical formulas as decoration, and layouts inspired by the Mandelbrot and Julia sets create an environment where computation becomes beauty.

Every piece in this gallery emerged from equations -- simple rules iterated until complexity appeared.

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).
