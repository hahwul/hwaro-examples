Welcome to The Apothecary, a journal dedicated to the quiet craft of herbal wellness. Here you will find remedies passed down through generations of herbalists, botanical profiles of medicinal plants, and practical wisdom for living well with nature's help.

## How This Journal Is Organized

The journal is divided into two main sections:

- **Journal** -- essays on herbalism, seasonal wellness practices, and botanical knowledge
- **Remedies** -- structured recipe cards with ingredients, benefits, and preparation methods

Each remedy card follows the format of a traditional apothecary label: the name of the preparation, its key ingredients, the conditions it addresses, and clear instructions for use.

## A Note on Practice

The preparations described here are rooted in traditional herbalism and are offered for educational purposes. They are not medical advice. Always consult a qualified practitioner before beginning any herbal regimen, especially if you are taking medications or managing a health condition.

## Getting Started

Browse the [Remedies](/remedies/) section for specific preparations, or read through the [Journal](/posts/) for broader discussions on herbs and wellness. The search function will help you find entries by plant name, condition, or preparation method.
