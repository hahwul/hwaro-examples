Lumière is an exploration into the elegance of minimal aesthetics. It prioritizes subtlety, emphasizing the gentle glow of properly spaced elements and the strength of contrasting tones.

> "True elegance is not about being noticed, it's about being remembered."

### A Focus on Light

The design draws inspiration from quiet corners lit by ambient light, creating depth through delicate shadows and carefully balanced luminance without resorting to excessive gradients.

### Features

- **Refined Typography**: Every letter spaced to breathe.
- **Ambient Glow**: Soft, luminescent `box-shadow` effects that guide the eye naturally.
- **Strictly Elegant**: Unnecessary adornments are stripped away.

We invite you to explore this refined space, crafted meticulously with Hwaro.
