Igloo is a reflection of the Nordic lifestyle. In a world full of noise, we seek silence. In a world full of clutter, we seek space.

### Design Principles

1. **Simplicity**: Every element must serve a purpose.
2. **Clarity**: Typography should be legible and breathing.
3. **Purity**: A cold palette of white and slate to reflect the northern landscape.

This theme was built as an example for the Hwaro static site generator.
