## About Sakura Storm

Welcome to Sakura Storm — a theme born from explosive cherry blossom winds and dramatic floral energy. This site captures the raw beauty of a sakura tempest in full bloom.

## Design Philosophy

Sakura Storm is inspired by the fleeting, breathtaking spectacle of cherry blossoms caught in fierce spring winds. Petals tear free in swirling storms of pink, creating a dramatic dance between delicacy and power.

### Key Design Elements

- **Cherry blossom particle effects** — animated petals that drift and swirl across the page
- **Bold pink-to-magenta gradients** that shift like storm-lit skies
- **Dark twilight backgrounds** that make sakura colors explode with intensity
- **Dramatic typography** with glowing bloom effects
- **Rich layered depth** with translucent cards and glowing accents
- **Dynamic motion** that brings the storm to life

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and lightweight static site generator written in Crystal. All visual effects are achieved with pure CSS animations — no JavaScript frameworks required.
