Dark themes are the perfect canvas for dramatic typography. When done right, text doesn't just sit on the page — it radiates.

## The Dark Canvas Advantage

Against a dark background, light-colored text naturally draws the eye. But going beyond simple white-on-dark opens up a world of possibilities:

1. **Colored headings** — Use your accent palette for maximum impact
2. **Glow effects** — `text-shadow` creates an ethereal luminance
3. **Weight contrast** — Ultra-bold headings against thin body text
4. **Gradient text** — `background-clip: text` for dynamic color fills

## Font Pairing Strategy

Sakura Storm pairs two complementary typefaces:

- **Cormorant Garamond** for headings — elegant, high-contrast serifs with dramatic thick-thin strokes
- **Nunito Sans** for body text — clean, rounded sans-serif for excellent readability

This pairing creates a tension between formal elegance and modern approachability that mirrors the sakura aesthetic itself.

## Glow Hierarchy

Not every element should glow. Reserve the strongest effects for:

- Primary headings (h1)
- Navigation links on hover
- Call-to-action elements

Secondary elements use softer, subtler glows to maintain visual hierarchy without overwhelming the reader.

## Readability First

Even in the most glamorous theme, readability is non-negotiable. Key rules:

- Minimum `1rem` body font size
- Line height of `1.7` or greater for body text
- Sufficient color contrast (at least 4.5:1 ratio)
- Generous paragraph spacing
- Limited line width (max `70ch`)

The goal is a theme that looks stunning at first glance and remains comfortable on the hundredth scroll.
