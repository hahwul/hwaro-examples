    <h2 style="margin-top:0">From the Dressing Room</h2>
    <p>The greenroom is the quietest corner of any venue. Costumes on rails, half-filled mugs, a whiteboard with a handwritten set list. Actors stretch. Musicians tune. Someone runs a monologue under their breath. The room holds a particular kind of focus &mdash; the last pocket of calm before the house opens.</p>
    <p>This site is a record of those rooms. We gather notes, photographs, and small interviews from the invited artists who play our seasonal residencies. Each entry is written in the hour after curtain, while the memory is still warm.</p>
  </div>
