## Ashbound Syndicate Claims the Throne

Ashbound Syndicate wins their second consecutive Clan Wars championship. Their strategic rotation through all three titles — maintaining top-three placement in each — proved decisive. Their leader, known as **Pyre_Actual**, issued a brief statement to the community: "We don't just play. We burn."
