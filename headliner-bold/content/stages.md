## Stage etiquette

Main stage and North Tent have separate wristband pits for accessible viewing; staff are stationed at the front-of-house rail for all three days. Sunset stage is open-standing on grass with no photographer pit. Late Night Pavilion is strictly 23:00&ndash;04:00 and operates independently of the main festival &mdash; wristband re-scan required at the door.
