**Welcome to the Mainframe.**

This is the central hub for the Y2K cyber/metallic aesthetic test. This layout utilizes pure CSS features—solid background colors, complex borders, and layered box-shadows—to emulate the chunky, metallic, and highly technical design language of early 2000s web interfaces.

> *Notice: No gradients or emojis were used in the construction of this module.*

Navigation relies on solid state transitions and high contrast elements. System functionality is nominal.
