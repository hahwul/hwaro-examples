## ACT V, Scene i

### Opening Reflection

*The house lights dim. A single spotlight illuminates centre stage. The audience settles into expectant silence.*

Before we begin our farewell, we pause. This moment of stillness is not emptiness -- it is the space between what was and what will be remembered.

### The Silence Before

Every closing ceremony begins with a breath held. The performers gather in the wings, the audience leans forward, and for one brief instant the entire theatre exists in perfect suspension.

This is that moment.

### What We Carry Forward

The opening reflection is not merely prologue. It is the acknowledgment that every ending contains within it the seeds of memory. What we witnessed here -- the performances, the collaborations, the unexpected moments of brilliance -- these do not vanish when the curtain falls.

They transform.

### Setting the Stage

The stage has been dressed for the final act. The lighting rig holds its patterns one last time. The orchestra tunes to a pitch that carries both celebration and farewell.

We begin where all great finales begin: in gratitude for the journey that brought us here.

---

*Scene i concludes. The spotlight widens.*
