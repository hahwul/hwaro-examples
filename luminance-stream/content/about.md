# About

Luminance Stream is designed for those who appreciate the subtle nuances of dark mode interfaces.

It is built to be fast, responsive, and visually quiet, allowing words to take the forefront. The styling utilizes a careful selection of grayscale tones to create depth without relying on harsh borders or gradients.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."
