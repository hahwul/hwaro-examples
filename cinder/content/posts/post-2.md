I turned off all notifications for a week. Not selectively -- all of them. The phone became a tool again instead of a slot machine.

The first two days were uncomfortable. By the third day I had read an entire book. By the fifth I had stopped reaching for my pocket every few minutes.

Silence is not the absence of something. It is the presence of attention.
