title = "The weight of less"
date = "2026-03-15"
tags = ["minimalism", "focus"]
+++

There is a particular kind of freedom that comes from owning fewer things. Not the dramatic purge that fills a dumpster in an afternoon, but the slow, deliberate process of noticing what you actually use.

A single notebook. One pen that writes well. A desk with nothing on it but the work in front of you.

The space that remains is not empty. It is available. And that distinction changes everything about how you think.
