# The Workshop

Welcome to Sawmill, a journal dedicated to the art and craft of woodworking. Here you will find detailed project walkthroughs, from selecting the right lumber to applying the final coat of finish.

Every project begins with a piece of raw wood and a plan. Through careful measurement, patient cutting, and steady hands, that raw material becomes something both useful and beautiful.

## What You Will Find Here

**Step-by-step project guides** -- Each project is broken down into clear stages, from material selection through final finishing. Whether you are building furniture or carving small goods, the process is documented in full.

**Tool and material notes** -- Every project lists the wood species, tools, and supplies used, so you can plan ahead and gather what you need before the first cut.

**Honest difficulty ratings** -- Projects are rated by difficulty so you can find work that matches your current skill level and challenges you to grow.
