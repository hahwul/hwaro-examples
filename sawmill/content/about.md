# About Sawmill

Sawmill is a woodworking project journal. It exists to document the process of building things from wood -- from rough lumber to finished piece.

## The Philosophy

Good woodworking is equal parts patience and precision. Every joint, every surface, every edge tells the story of the care put into it. This journal aims to capture that process honestly, including the mistakes and the lessons learned along the way.

## What Makes a Good Project

A good woodworking project is one that:

- Starts with quality materials suited to the task
- Uses the right tools for each operation
- Follows a clear plan while remaining adaptable
- Results in something both functional and well-crafted
- Teaches the maker something new

## The Workshop

The projects documented here are built in a modest home workshop equipped with a mix of hand tools and power tools. Nothing here requires an industrial setup. The goal is always to show what is achievable with reasonable equipment and careful technique.
