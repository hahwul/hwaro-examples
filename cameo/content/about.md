Cameo is a statement.

It was designed as an example for the Hwaro static site generator to demonstrate how a brutalist-inspired, minimal theme can still be extremely elegant when executed with strict constraints.

There are no subtle fades here. No playful icons. Just content, presented with absolute clarity and unapologetic boldness.
