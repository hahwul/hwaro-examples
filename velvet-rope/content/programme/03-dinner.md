## Six Courses

The gala dinner is served in the Grand Salon. Courses are served at the table; the kitchen runs two simultaneous service lines from the west and east pantries.

### Courses

1. Smoked trout, buttermilk, shaved shallot
2. Ember-roasted beetroot, aged sheep's cheese, walnut oil
3. Chestnut consommé, quail confit
4. Brown butter poached turbot, sea vegetables
5. Roast lamb saddle, savoy, mustard
6. Pear, chocolate, almond, fennel

### Dietary

Alternative menus for vegetarian, pescatarian, gluten-free, and nut-free diets are confirmed with your RSVP. Please do not rely on on-the-night changes; the kitchen requires the final list by the Tuesday before.
