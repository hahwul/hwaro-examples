## Remarks

The honorary chair, Pilar Ventura, offers a short address — traditionally fifteen minutes, never longer than twenty.

### Grant Recipients

The three season grant recipients are announced after the address. Each grant is 25,000 EUR and funds one season's work for an early-career artist nominated by the board.

### Tradition

Guests applaud at the end of each announcement. A short pause between the third and the closing remarks is customary; a toast is raised to the retiring chair emeritus.
