## Champagne &amp; Small Plates

The evening opens in the foyer with chilled champagne and a rotating small-plate service from the Mercer House kitchen. A short string duo performs under the staircase; they are not ticketed, they are simply the evening's welcome.

### Attire

Black tie from arrival. Outerwear is checked at the cloakroom; a cloakroom attendant will bring your pin once the programme begins.

### Timing

Reception closes at 20:00 sharp. Guests are seated in the Grand Salon between 19:55 and 20:00 by the ushers.
