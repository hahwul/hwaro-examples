## A New Commission

A forty-minute chamber work commissioned for the evening. Three movements, three instruments, one continuous arc.

### The Piece

- I. *Marble Hall* — 11 minutes
- II. *Interlude Beneath* — 4 minutes
- III. *Late Return* — 24 minutes

The trio performs without a break between the second and third movements.

### Personnel

Kira Saint-Clair (composition, violin), Wren Asante (piano), Olena Ivanko (viola).

> *Late Return* is a long slow exhalation — the piece you hear when everyone has eaten, and the table has settled, and no one is in a hurry to speak.
