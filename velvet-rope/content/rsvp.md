## Reply Card

Your invitation carries a single reply card. Please complete and return by first post no later than October 5. The address is printed on the reverse of the card.

If you prefer to reply electronically, the secretary's office accepts replies at **gala@velvetrope.example**. Please quote the invitation number printed on the lower right of the card.

## Dietary Notes

Please note any dietary requirements on the reply card. The kitchen plans alternatives for:

- Vegetarian
- Pescatarian
- Gluten-free
- Nut-free
- Specific allergies on request

## Guests

Each invitation carries one named guest. A second guest may be added at the patron tier; please contact the secretary's office in advance.

## Regrets

We hope you will be able to join us. If you cannot, a short note with the reply card is welcome and is always read carefully.
