## The Terrace Room

The two terrace-adjacent tables (IX and X) seat twenty guests who have requested the terrace room at RSVP. The recitals are broadcast into the room via the house loudspeakers and remain fully audible.

### Smokers' Service

A dedicated smokers' service is open from 23:30 on the terrace. Ashtrays are returned to the butler's pantry.

### Quiet Room

Off the terrace, a quiet room with two armchairs and one small table is available without booking — one guest at a time, door closed.
