The RSVP deadline is Sunday, October 5. Invitations carry a single reply card; kindly return by first post, or through the secretary's electronic address if you prefer.

The seating secretary confirms table placements the Thursday before the gala. Dietary requirements must be noted on the reply card; late notification cannot always be accommodated.
