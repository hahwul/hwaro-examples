# About Kaleidoscope

Kaleidoscope is a three-day festival celebrating the intersection of music, visual art, and community. Founded in 2022, the event draws inspiration from the psychedelic poster art of the 1960s and 70s, translating that era's bold colors and boundary-pushing creativity into a modern festival experience.

## Our Vision

We believe that art is not something to observe from a distance. Kaleidoscope is built around participation -- every attendee is invited to create, collaborate, and contribute. From hands-on workshops to open-stage sessions, the festival is designed to blur the line between audience and artist.

## What Makes Us Different

- **Multi-disciplinary programming** spanning live music, installation art, film, printmaking, and sound design
- **Workshop-first approach** with over 30 sessions across the three days, led by working artists and educators
- **Community partnerships** with local arts organizations, ensuring the festival strengthens the creative ecosystem year-round
- **Accessible pricing** with day passes, group rates, and a volunteer program that offers free entry in exchange for participation

## The Venue

The festival takes place at Meridian Park, a 40-acre riverside site with mature tree canopy, open meadows, and existing pavilion structures. The grounds are divided into six distinct zones, each with its own visual identity and programming focus.

## History

What began as a single-day block party in 2022 has grown into one of the region's most anticipated cultural events. Past editions have featured over 80 performing acts, 200 visual artists, and attendance of 12,000 across the weekend.

## Sustainability

Kaleidoscope operates on a zero-single-use-plastic policy. All food vendors use compostable serviceware, and the festival partners with regional recycling cooperatives for post-event waste processing. We offset travel emissions through verified reforestation programs.

## Get Involved

Whether you want to perform, exhibit, volunteer, or sponsor, we welcome collaboration. Visit the contact page or reach out through our social channels to start a conversation.
