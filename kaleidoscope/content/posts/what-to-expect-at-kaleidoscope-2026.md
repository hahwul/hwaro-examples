# What to Expect at Kaleidoscope 2026

Whether this is your first festival or your fifth, here is what you need to know about Kaleidoscope 2026.

## The Basics

Kaleidoscope runs October 17 through 19 at Meridian Park. Gates open at 14:00 on Day 1 and 10:00 on Days 2 and 3. Programming runs until approximately 23:00 each evening.

## Six Festival Zones

The grounds are divided into six distinct areas:

- **Music Stage** -- Two stages running simultaneously with acts ranging from solo performers to full ensembles
- **Art Installation** -- Twelve large-scale works spread across the riverside meadow, several of which are interactive
- **Workshop Zone** -- A covered pavilion hosting sessions on screen printing, sound design, color theory, mixed media, and more
- **Food Court** -- Twenty curated vendors serving fusion cuisine, craft beverages, and artisan coffee
- **Night Show** -- After dark, the eastern ridge transforms with projection mapping and light installations
- **VIP Lounge** -- An exclusive area for VIP pass holders with premium sightlines and artist access

## What to Bring

- Comfortable shoes suitable for grass and gravel paths
- Layers for evening temperatures, which can drop to 12 degrees Celsius
- A refillable water bottle (filling stations are located throughout the grounds)
- Sun protection for daytime hours
- A small bag or backpack (large bags are subject to search at entry)

## What Not to Bring

Outside food and beverages, glass containers, drones, professional camera equipment (without press credentials), and pets (service animals excepted) are not permitted.

## Getting There

Meridian Park is accessible by public transit (Route 14 stops at the main gate) and by car (parking lots open two hours before gates). Bicycle parking is available at the south entrance.

## Accessibility

All main pathways are paved or use compacted gravel suitable for wheelchairs and mobility devices. An accessibility tent near the main entrance provides assisted listening devices, large-print schedules, and a quiet rest area. Contact the accessibility team in advance if you have specific requirements.

## Tickets

Passes are available in three tiers: Day Pass ($45), Full Festival ($110), and VIP Experience ($250). Group rates are available for parties of eight or more. A limited number of volunteer positions offer free entry in exchange for a six-hour shift across the weekend.
