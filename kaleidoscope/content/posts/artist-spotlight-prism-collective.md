# Artist Spotlight: Prism Collective

We are thrilled to announce that Prism Collective will headline the main stage on opening night of Kaleidoscope 2026.

## Who They Are

Prism Collective is a six-piece ensemble blending electronic production with live instrumentation. Formed in 2019 in Portland, the group draws from krautrock, ambient, and West African polyrhythm traditions to create expansive, layered performances that regularly stretch past the two-hour mark.

## What to Expect

Their sets are known for evolving in real time. Rather than playing a fixed setlist, the group works from a set of melodic and rhythmic frameworks that they develop live on stage. Each performance is unique, shaped by the venue, the crowd, and the moment.

For Kaleidoscope, the collective is preparing a special extended set designed for the outdoor setting. Expect deep bass frequencies, interlocking percussion, and passages of quiet tension that build into full-spectrum releases.

## Discography Highlights

- **Refraction** (2021) -- Their debut LP, praised for its patient build and rich texture
- **Parallax** (2023) -- A double album recorded across four cities during a single tour
- **Diffuse** (2025) -- The most recent release, featuring collaborations with field recording artists from three continents

## Catch Them Live

Prism Collective takes the main stage on October 17 at 19:00. Arrive early -- their performances draw large crowds and the viewing area fills quickly.
