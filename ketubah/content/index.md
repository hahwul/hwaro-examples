<p class="hero-inscription">Herein you shall find a portfolio of contracts drawn by our studio for the solemnisation of unions great and small, arranged after their ornamental tradition: the Jerusalem Classic, the Florentine Baroque, the Modern Minimal, and the Sephardic Andalusi. Each is lettered by hand, each is unique to its couple.</p>

<div class="two-col drop">

Our atelier has served couples of every persuasion since the opening of our doors in the spring of the year two thousand and four. Each contract we draw is a unique object, lettered by the hand of the studio calligrapher upon paper of our own preparation, with pigments ground from earths and plants according to the old recipes. The ornamental frame is the particular signature of our house, and may be chosen from the portfolio displayed herein, or drawn afresh upon consultation for those who desire a wholly new device.

No contract leaves the studio before it has been read aloud in full by the calligrapher and the studio master, compared word for word against the approved text, and signed in the presence of two witnesses of the studio staff. It is then sealed beneath a transparent leaf of glassine and conveyed to the couple in a hand-sewn portfolio of cloth over board.

Consultations are taken in person upon the first and third Thursdays of the month, and by correspondence at all other times. The studio advises that commissions be placed not less than three months before the intended date of ceremony, that there may be time for the couple to approve successive drafts and for the calligrapher to proceed at the careful pace the work requires.

The pages that follow display four principal styles of our work. The reader is invited to browse the [portfolio](/gallery/) at length, to read our [note upon the craft](/craft/) and our [account of the tradition](/history/), and when ready to begin, to turn to the [terms of commission](/commission/).
