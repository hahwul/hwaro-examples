### Stage Summary

The cast piece comes to the anvil. Here it is struck, shaped, quenched in cold water, then struck again. Repeated cycles of stress and relief build internal strength. This final stage is about refinement through controlled impact. What emerges has been through the full process -- charged, melted, poured, and forged. It will not break easily.
