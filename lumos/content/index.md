Welcome to Lumos. A beautifully simple, elegant theme designed for reading.

Lumos strips away the unnecessary, leaving only the content in its purest form.

## Readability First

We believe that typography and white space are the key ingredients to a pleasant reading experience. By combining a classic serif body text with clean sans-serif headings, Lumos offers a timeless design suitable for essays, journals, and personal blogs.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — Leonardo da Vinci

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Scales beautifully on all devices.
- **Minimalist Aesthetics**: No excessive gradients or emojis.
- **Focus on Content**: Subtle color palette designed to reduce eye strain.
