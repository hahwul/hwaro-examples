Welcome to the **Topographic Gradient** theme for Hwaro. This design translates the organic flow of elevation maps into a vibrant, moving digital experience.

## The Concept

We took inspiration from classical cartography—specifically, the contour lines used to denote elevation changes—and merged it with modern web design trends. 

By utilizing dynamic CSS gradients that animate smoothly beneath a translucent topographic overlay, the interface feels alive and fluid. The glassmorphism elements allow the complex background to shine through while maintaining perfect legibility.

### Key Features

*   **Fluid Backgrounds:** CSS-animated gradients provide a continuous, calming shift of vibrant hues.
*   **Organic Overlay:** A custom-generated SVG topographic map sits just above the gradient, creating depth and texture.
*   **Glassmorphism UI:** Content areas utilize `backdrop-filter: blur()` to create frosted glass effects, giving the design a bold, creative, and elegant feel.
