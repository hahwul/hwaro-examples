## About Rosemary

Rosemary is a cooking journal dedicated to the art of cooking with fresh herbs. Every recipe on this site is built around the idea that herbs are not garnishes or afterthoughts -- they are the heart of the dish.

## Our Philosophy

We keep things simple. Good ingredients, treated well, speak for themselves. A perfectly roasted chicken needs little more than salt, olive oil, and a generous amount of rosemary. A summer salad becomes extraordinary with the right basil. A humble shortbread cookie becomes elegant with a touch of lavender.

## The Garden

Behind every recipe is a garden. We grow our own herbs whenever possible: rosemary and thyme in terracotta pots by the kitchen door, basil in raised beds that catch the morning sun, and lavender along the garden path where it fills the air on warm afternoons.

## In the Kitchen

Our recipes are written for home cooks who appreciate good food without unnecessary complexity. You will find clear instructions, honest ingredient lists, and the kind of practical advice that comes from actually cooking these dishes, not just photographing them.

We hope these recipes find a place in your kitchen.
