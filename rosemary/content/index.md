## From the Garden to the Table

Welcome to Rosemary, where every recipe begins with a walk through the herb garden. We believe that the simplest ingredients, handled with care and respect, create the most memorable meals.

Our kitchen is guided by the seasons and by the herbs that thrive in them. Whether it is a sprig of rosemary tucked beneath a roasting chicken, a handful of basil folded into fresh pesto, or a whisper of lavender in a butter shortbread, herbs transform the ordinary into something worth savoring.

- **Herb-Forward Recipes** -- Dishes built around the flavors of fresh herbs
- **Growing Notes** -- Tips for cultivating your own kitchen garden
- **Seasonal Pairings** -- Which herbs shine in each season and how to use them
