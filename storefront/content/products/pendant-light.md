<h2>Details</h2>
<p>Each Copper Pendant Light is hand-spun from a single sheet of copper and left to develop its natural patina over time. The warm, rosy tone of the copper casts a soft downward light that makes it perfect for task lighting or creating an intimate atmosphere.</p>
<h2>Patina</h2>
<p>Copper naturally oxidizes over time, deepening in color from bright rose to a warm brown. This is an intentional part of the design. To slow the patina process, apply a thin coat of copper wax. To accelerate it, simply leave the fixture untreated.</p>
