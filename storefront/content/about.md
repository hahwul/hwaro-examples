## Our Story

Storefront was founded with a simple belief: everyday objects should be both beautiful and functional. We partner with independent designers and makers who share our commitment to quality craftsmanship and sustainable materials.

## What We Do

We curate a collection of home goods, furniture, and accessories that balance form and function. Every product in our catalog has been selected for its design integrity, material quality, and lasting value.

## Our Values

- **Quality over quantity** -- We choose fewer, better things.
- **Honest materials** -- Solid wood, natural stone, quality metals. No shortcuts.
- **Timeless design** -- Clean lines and considered proportions that age well.
- **Transparent pricing** -- Fair prices that reflect real costs, not inflated markups.

## Shipping & Returns

We offer free shipping on orders over $150. Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. For furniture items, please contact us to arrange a return pickup.
