## About Diamond Facet

Diamond Facet is a glamorous, eye-catching blog theme inspired by the way light refracts through a diamond's facets — splitting white light into a dazzling spectrum of color.

Every element of this theme draws from the geometry and optics of precious stones: angular layouts, prismatic color gradients, and crystalline transparency effects.

### Design Philosophy

- **Bold & Vibrant** — Embrace rich color palettes and dynamic gradients
- **Faceted Geometry** — Angular shapes and crystalline patterns throughout
- **Light & Refraction** — Rainbow dispersions and spectral highlights
- **Glamorous Polish** — Every detail shines with care and intention
