# The Treasury

Gold gleams in the torchlight. The chamber is smaller than the others, but every surface has been crafted with care. This is where the most valued work is kept.

## The Project

A complete brand identity system for an independent coffee roaster. The project encompassed everything from logo design to packaging, signage, and digital presence.

## The Brief

The client wanted a brand that communicated craftsmanship without pretension. The target audience was coffee enthusiasts who care about origin and process but not about exclusivity.

## What Was Delivered

- **Logo system** -- A primary mark with horizontal, vertical, and icon-only variants
- **Color palette** -- Earth tones derived from the actual colors of coffee at different roast levels
- **Typography** -- A pairing of a geometric sans-serif for headings and a humanist serif for body text
- **Packaging** -- Labels for 12 single-origin coffees, with a consistent system that allows new origins to be added without design work
- **Signage** -- Interior and exterior signs for two retail locations
- **Website** -- A simple storefront with origin stories and ordering capabilities

## Design Process

The identity was developed through iterative refinement. Over 60 logo concepts were explored before arriving at the final mark. The color palette was calibrated using actual coffee samples photographed under controlled lighting.

## Impact

Post-rebrand, the roaster reported a 35% increase in retail sales and a measurable improvement in brand recognition surveys across their market area.
