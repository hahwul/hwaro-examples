# About the Labyrinth

The Labyrinth is a portfolio disguised as a dungeon. Navigate the corridors, enter the chambers, and discover the work within.

## Concept

Traditional portfolios present work in a linear list. The Labyrinth invites exploration. Each project is a room with its own atmosphere, connected to others by corridors that encourage discovery.

## Design Philosophy

The aesthetic draws from the ancient archetype of the labyrinth:

- **Stone walls** -- Thick borders define the space, creating a sense of enclosure
- **Torch light** -- Amber glows illuminate the darkness, guiding the way forward
- **Maze navigation** -- Directional choices replace conventional menus
- **Minimap** -- A birds-eye view keeps you oriented in the larger structure

## The Builder

This labyrinth was constructed by someone who believes that how work is presented shapes how it is understood. The medium is part of the message.
