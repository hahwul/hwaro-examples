# TECH RIDER

House-provided inventory for Stage A. Full CAD and lighting plots are attached to the advance packet. If a piece of your touring world doubles house equipment, tell the production manager on advance — we do not unrig house gear without a written request.

## sound &middot; front of house

- **Console**: Digico SD12 / 96 channels / 72 output busses
- **Main PA**: L-Acoustics K2 &middot; six per side, flown
- **Subs**: KS28 &middot; twelve, arc-delay ground-stacked
- **Side-fills**: Kara II &middot; two per side
- **Front-fills**: Arcs focus &middot; four across lip

## sound &middot; monitor world

- **Console**: Digico SD11 / 48-ch / 24-out
- **Wedges**: Radius 2 x 10-in &middot; twelve available
- **IEMs**: Shure PSM1000 &middot; six transmitters, ten receivers
- **Drum sub**: double-18 &middot; one, under riser

## lighting

- **Console**: GrandMA3 full size
- **Fixtures**:
  - 24x Robe Spiider (ovrhd truss)
  - 18x Martin MAC Aura XB (upstage tower)
  - 12x Clay Paky Mythos 2 (FOH balcony)
  - 48x Source Four LED Series 3 (cyc + front wash)
- **Haze**: MDG ATMe &middot; two units
- **Followspot**: Robert Juliat Lancelot &middot; two

## power

- Stage-right: 400A three-phase, camlock tails
- Stage-left: 200A three-phase, camlock tails
- Balcony FOH: 100A tri-phase
- Isolated ground run dedicated for audio

## stage

- **Width**: 42 ft apron
- **Depth**: 28 ft (32 with upstage thrust)
- **Height**: 4 ft from pit floor
- **Load rating**: 150 psf upstage, 250 psf downstage
- **Rigging**: 42 linesets &middot; 1500 lbs each &middot; motorized

## crew call

Four-person local crew on every date. Two upstage, two downstage. A runner is available from 14:00 to 01:00. Catering is served in the green room at 17:00 &mdash; if the advance does not include dietary needs in writing, it is a green-room buffet.

## what you bring

- Backline &mdash; we do not provide it
- Touring personnel credentials &mdash; laminates issued on arrival
- Microphones specific to your instrument world (we stock a standard drum-and-vocal package)
