## the set

Ninety minutes, no encore folded into the count — the encore window opens at 22:30 and the house timer runs three songs or ten minutes, whichever expires first. The band walks on in the dark to a single downstage special, LX cue A-GO, and the room holds its breath for the first four bars.

### production

The rig is built around 140 paper lanterns flown from the upstage truss on individual pickups. They drop to playing height on LX-03-B and retract on LX-03-K. The string section sits stage-right on a 12-inch riser with its own monitor world. No click tracks. The drummer counts the band in on a tambourine.
