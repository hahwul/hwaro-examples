# HOUSE OPEN

Doors go at 19:00. The opener walks on at 19:15. If you walk up to the box office at 19:14, you are going to miss the first song and that is the rule. Reserve a seat in advance if you want a balcony spot — balcony seating is fixed at 640 seats across three tiers, and balconies sell out before the pit.

## the math

- **Capacity**: 1,200 (pit 560, balcony 640)
- **Box office opens**: 18:30
- **Doors**: 19:00
- **Curtain**: 19:15 (opener)
- **Walk-ups**: first-come, first-served in pit &mdash; balconies require reservation

## day-of walk-ups

Box office opens at 18:30. Cash and card both. There is a strict no-refund policy after 19:00 &mdash; if you are stuck in traffic, call the box office line printed on your confirmation and we will hold your spot until the opener walks off.
