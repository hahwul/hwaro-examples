  <h2>The Architecture</h2>
  <p>Prism Nexus is an experimental digital space that combines the raw, glowing energy of cyberpunk with the clean, translucent elegance of glassmorphism.</p>

  <h3>Design Philosophy</h3>
  <p>We believe the future of interfaces lies in depth and light. By utilizing CSS `backdrop-filter: blur()` and animated fluid gradients, we create an environment that feels alive.</p>

  <ul>
    <li><strong>Colors:</strong> Deep space backgrounds (#030508) accented by neon cyan (#00f0ff) and magenta (#ff0055).</li>
    <li><strong>Typography:</strong> Space Grotesk for headings to provide a futuristic edge, combined with Inter for highly readable body text.</li>
    <li><strong>Motion:</strong> Subtle floating animations and glowing pulses simulate a living, breathing system.</li>
  </ul>
