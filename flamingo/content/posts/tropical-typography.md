Typography sets the mood before a single word is read. The right font can transport visitors to a sun-soaked poolside lounge or a chic rooftop bar.

## Modern Display Fonts

Geometric sans-serif fonts like Poppins bring a clean, contemporary feel with just enough personality to stand out. Their rounded letterforms feel friendly and approachable — perfect for a tropical aesthetic.

## Pairing Strategies

A successful type pairing balances contrast with cohesion:

- **Display + Body**: Use a bold display font for headings and a clean sans-serif for body text
- **Weight contrast**: Heavy headings against light body text create visual hierarchy
- **Size jumps**: Don't be afraid of large headlines — they make a statement

## The Resort Effect

Resort branding consistently uses typography to evoke luxury and relaxation. Wide letter-spacing, generous line heights, and bold weight choices all contribute to that effortless elegance.

When applied to web design, these same principles create a reading experience that feels curated and intentional rather than default.
