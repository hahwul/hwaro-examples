## From the Journal

Step inside and browse our latest reflections on the vintage life. From spinning rare vinyl finds to developing rolls of film in the darkroom, every entry is a love letter to a simpler, more beautiful era.

> "The past is never dead. It is not even past." -- William Faulkner

## What You Will Find Here

**Record Collecting** -- Deep dives into rare pressings, hi-fi setups, and the irreplaceable ritual of dropping the needle on wax.

**Film Photography** -- Embracing the grain, the light leaks, and the patience that comes with shooting on celluloid.

**Thrift Store Finds** -- The hunt for one-of-a-kind pieces hiding among the forgotten and overlooked.

**Retro Recipes** -- Kitchen classics from the decades that gave us fondue, Jell-O molds, and the perfect cocktail hour.
