## Our Story

Vintage was born from a shared obsession with the textures, sounds, and flavors of decades past. We believe that the best things in life were often made before we were born -- and that rediscovering them is one of life's greatest pleasures.

What started as weekend trips to flea markets and record fairs has grown into a full-fledged way of life. We shoot on film cameras from the 1970s, cook from handwritten recipe cards found in estate sales, and believe that a well-worn leather jacket tells a better story than anything fresh off the rack.

## Our Philosophy

We are not interested in nostalgia for its own sake. We seek quality, craftsmanship, and the kind of intentional design that stands the test of time. A Braun radio from 1965 is not just an antique -- it is a masterclass in form meeting function.

Every piece we collect, every recipe we revive, and every photograph we develop is an act of preservation. We are keeping alive the skills, aesthetics, and rituals that deserve to endure.

## What We Cover

- **Vinyl and Hi-Fi** -- From rare Northern Soul 45s to restoring vintage turntables
- **Analog Photography** -- Medium format, 35mm, instant film, and darkroom technique
- **Mid-Century Design** -- Furniture, typography, architecture, and everyday objects
- **Vintage Fashion** -- Workwear, Americana, and timeless wardrobe staples
- **Heritage Recipes** -- Dishes and drinks from the golden age of home entertaining
