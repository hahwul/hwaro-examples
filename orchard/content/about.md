## About Orchard

Orchard is a food and gardening journal devoted to the connection between what we grow and what we eat. Every recipe begins in the soil, and every garden plan is shaped by the meals we look forward to sharing.

### What You Will Find Here

- **Seasonal Recipes** -- Dishes built around what is ripe and ready, from slow-roasted apple butter in autumn to fresh herb salads in spring.
- **Growing Guides** -- Practical advice for the home gardener, whether you are managing a backyard plot or a few containers on a balcony.
- **Preservation & Pantry** -- Techniques for canning, fermenting, drying, and storing the harvest so nothing goes to waste.
- **Baking** -- Bread, pastry, and the patient craft of working with flour and time.

### Our Approach

We believe in simplicity. The best food comes from good ingredients treated with care, not from complicated techniques or exotic shopping lists. A ripe tomato with salt and olive oil. A loaf of bread pulled from the oven at the right moment. An apple picked at peak sweetness.

We also believe in patience. Gardens do not hurry, and neither should the cook. The rhythm of the seasons sets the pace, and we follow it willingly.

Orchard is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast static site generator. The design draws from the warmth of old seed catalogs and farmhouse kitchens -- clean, readable, and unhurried.
