Welcome to **Codex Illuminatus**, a medieval manuscript-inspired blog theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). Drawn from the tradition of illuminated manuscripts, this theme brings the elegance of the scriptorium to the modern web — ornamental initials, parchment textures, and blackletter headings assembled in a two-column codex layout.

Browse the [chapters](/chapters/) to read our collected writings, or visit the [colophon](/about/) to learn more about the making of this manuscript.
