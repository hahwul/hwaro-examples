Woven Tapestry was created to provide a warm, tactile digital experience. By leveraging modern CSS and SVG techniques, we can bring the comforting aesthetics of traditional crafts into the digital realm.

### Design Philosophy

The core idea was to avoid modern digital tropes like smooth gradients and sterile geometric shapes. Instead, we embrace:

1.  **Texture over smoothness**: Using SVG filters to add subtle grain and irregularity.
2.  **Physicality**: Giving elements physical characteristics like dashed 'stitching' and layered shadows.
3.  **Warmth**: Selecting a color palette that feels natural and grounding.

The result is a theme that invites the reader to linger and explore, much like a beautiful piece of fabric.
