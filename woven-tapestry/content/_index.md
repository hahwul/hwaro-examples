Welcome to the **Woven Tapestry** theme. This theme brings a warm, tactile aesthetic to your site through intricate fabric patterns, simulated cut edges, and thread-like textures. It avoids harsh gradients and instead focuses on earthy, organic colors and subtle SVG filter textures that mimic real weaving.

## Features of the Tapestry

The design utilizes several key elements to achieve its look:

*   **Tactile Backgrounds**: A repeating SVG background simulates a base woven texture, overlaid with subtle SVG noise filters for an organic feel.
*   **Stitched Details**: Dashed borders and carefully crafted drop-shadows create the illusion of stitched-on patches and thick yarn details.
*   **Earthy Palette**: A warm color scheme consisting of undyed wool white, terracotta red, mustard yellow, and forest green.
*   **Textured Typography**: Classic serif fonts (`Cormorant Garamond` and `Lora`) bring elegance, while custom text-shadows give headings a 'raised thread' appearance.
