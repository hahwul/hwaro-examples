Welcome to a design forged in fire and wax. Encaustic painting is one of the oldest known art forms, dating back to Greek shipbuilders who sealed their vessels with pigmented beeswax. The technique demands heat, precision, and irreversible commitment.

This site embodies that spirit: bold layered surfaces, warm luminous tones, and an unapologetic clarity that only comes from working with molten material.
