## About Dusk

Dusk is a blog theme built for the quiet hours -- when the sky deepens and the world grows still. It draws its palette from the last light of day: warm amber, muted coral, and the deep indigo of approaching night.

## Design Philosophy

- **Restraint over spectacle.** Every element serves a purpose. There is no decoration for its own sake.
- **Typography first.** Cormorant Garamond for headings brings warmth and elegance. Inter for body text ensures readability.
- **Dark canvas.** A deep background lets the warm accent colors breathe, creating natural contrast without strain.
- **Space as structure.** Generous whitespace guides the eye and gives content room to land.
