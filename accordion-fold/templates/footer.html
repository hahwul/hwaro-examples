Publication

Accordion Fold is a continuous-panel concertina issued four times yearly. Each number unfolds to a single strip, three metres in length, printed on long-grain Kozo paper.

Method

  • Mountain fold: peak
  • Valley fold: trough
  • Alternation: unbroken
  • Binding: spine-free
