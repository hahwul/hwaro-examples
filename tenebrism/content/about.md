Tenebrism is a style of painting characterized by deep shadows and a distinct contrast between light and dark areas. In web design, we apply this concept to create an immersive, highly focused user experience.

## The Principles

1.  **Eliminate the Superfluous:** Gradients, soft shadows, and emojis distract from the core message. We remove them entirely.
2.  **Embrace the Void:** Dark backgrounds are not empty; they provide the canvas against which the content shines.
3.  **High Contrast:** Text must be stark and legible. The interplay of white and near-black is central to this aesthetic.

This example site demonstrates how an elegant, bold look can be achieved with minimalistic markup and focused CSS.
