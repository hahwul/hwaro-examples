Traditional East Asian patterns are more than just decoration; they carry symbolism and meaning. From the geometric lattice of a window screen (Chang-sal) to the graceful curves of roof tiles (Giwa), these elements reflect a deep connection with nature and harmony.

In this theme, we attempt to incorporate these geometric patterns into the digital landscape. The borders, the dividers, and the layout all pay homage to the structure of a traditional residence.

The absence of modern gradients and flashy emojis is intentional, allowing the simplicity and the texture of the "paper" to speak for itself.
