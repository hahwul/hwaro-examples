+++
title = "The Lake at Dusk"
date = "2025-01-10"
description = "A meditation on water, light, and the hour between day and night."
tags = ["poetry", "nature"]
categories = ["verse"]
+++

The water holds the last light
like a word on the tip of the tongue,
not yet spoken, not yet lost.

Reeds bend without reason.
A bird crosses the surface
and leaves no trace.

This is the hour
when the world forgets its name,
when even stones
seem willing to dissolve.

---

I come here because stillness
is not the absence of motion
but the presence of attention.

The lake does not try to reflect the sky.
It simply does.
