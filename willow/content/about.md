## About Willow

Willow is a literary blog that takes its name from the tree that bends but does not break. Here, writing is given room to breathe. The focus is on poetry and prose that rewards slow, careful reading.

This site favors restraint over spectacle, the well-chosen word over the flood of content. Every piece published here has been selected for clarity of thought and beauty of expression.

## Philosophy

Good writing needs space. A poem crammed between advertisements loses its power. A short story interrupted by notifications cannot hold its reader. Willow exists as an antidote to the noise: a clean, quiet page where language can do its work.

## The Name

The willow tree grows near water. Its branches droop toward the surface but never quite touch it. There is something in that gesture, reaching without grasping, that mirrors the best writing. It points toward meaning without pinning it down.
