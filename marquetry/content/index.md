Welcome to Marquetry. This theme is designed for those who appreciate bold, geometric, and clean aesthetics.

## Design Philosophy

Marquetry relies on strong contrasts, solid colors, and sharp lines. It intentionally avoids gradients and complex textures, finding beauty in structural simplicity and geometric balance.

<div class="geo-block"></div>

### Features

*   **Bold Typography**: Strong headings that command attention.
*   **Solid Colors**: High-contrast, unambiguous color palette.
*   **Geometric Accents**: Simple shapes and borders that define space.
*   **Clean Layout**: Uncluttered presentation of your content.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." - Leonardo da Vinci
