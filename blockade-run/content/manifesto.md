Every wall was built by someone. Every wall can be taken down.

### We believe:

- **Fear is a signal, not a stop sign.** It tells you something matters. Run toward it.
- **Doubt is a crack, not a collapse.** The structure is already weakening. Keep pushing.
- **Limits are stories.** They were written by someone who stopped too soon. Write a new ending.

### The three stages are not metaphors.

They are the literal sequence every breakthrough follows:

1. You see the barrier (**INTACT**)
2. You hit it and it starts to give (**CRACKED**)
3. You push through and it falls apart (**SHATTERED**)

There is no stage four. On the other side, there is only open ground.
