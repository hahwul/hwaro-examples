<div class="faq-list">
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">How did the queue work?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">Queue positions were randomised two minutes before on-sale. Every buyer entered the queue at the same instant and was assigned a waiting-room slot by a server-side shuffle. No amount of early refreshing could improve your position.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">Can I still buy a ticket?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">Not from us directly. The only official route is the verified resale waitlist, which activates when returned tickets are released back into circulation. Third-party resellers advertising these nights are not authorised.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">What ID is required at entry?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">A government-issued photo ID matching the lead booker on the order. For VIP and front-floor tickets we scan ID and ticket together &mdash; names must match exactly. Minor exceptions for confirmed family transfers can be requested up to 48 hours before the show.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">Are refunds available?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">No. Every transaction is final as of the sell-out timestamp. Tickets returned by the original buyer are redistributed through the verified resale waitlist at face value plus processing fee; the original buyer is credited against their next purchase.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">When do doors open?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">VIP entry is 17:30, all other tiers at 18:30. Show starts at 20:00 on each of the three nights with no support act. The venue advises arriving at least 45 minutes before your entry slot to clear security and coat check.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">Can I bring a camera?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">Phones yes, interchangeable-lens cameras no. Recording or streaming the performance will result in removal without refund. Press passes are issued separately and require prior accreditation.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h2 class="faq-q">What happens to returned tickets?</h2>
    <p class="faq-a">They re-enter the official resale queue at face value. The platform sells them to the next verified fan on the waitlist in chronological order of sign-up. Typical release windows run between 48 hours and 12 hours before each show.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Still have questions?

Email boxoffice@meridiantheatre.example or use the contact form on the venue website. Expect a response within one business day. Phone lines are open 10:00&ndash;18:00 local time, Monday through Friday.
