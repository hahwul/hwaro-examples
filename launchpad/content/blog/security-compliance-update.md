We are proud to announce that Launchpad has achieved SOC 2 Type II certification. This milestone reflects our deep commitment to protecting your data and maintaining the highest standards of operational security.

## What is SOC 2?

SOC 2 is an auditing standard developed by the American Institute of CPAs. It evaluates an organization's controls related to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Type II means we were audited over a sustained period, not just at a single point in time.

## What This Means for You

If your organization requires vendor compliance certifications, you can now request our SOC 2 report directly from your account settings. This simplifies your procurement and audit processes significantly.

## Security Features Included

Every Launchpad plan includes:

- **Encryption at rest and in transit** using AES-256 and TLS 1.3
- **Role-based access control** with granular permissions
- **Two-factor authentication** for all accounts
- **Audit logs** for every action taken in your workspace

Enterprise plans additionally include SSO via SAML 2.0, custom data retention policies, and dedicated infrastructure options.

## What Comes Next

Our security roadmap for 2026 includes HIPAA compliance for healthcare teams, ISO 27001 certification, and advanced threat detection. We will share more details as each milestone approaches.
