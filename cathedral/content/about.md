## The Practice

Cathedral is an architecture portfolio dedicated to documenting buildings that shape the way we inhabit space. Each project is presented through careful curation of imagery, technical metadata, and narrative context.

## Editorial Approach

Our documentation follows a structured methodology. Every project entry includes comprehensive metadata --- architect, completion year, typology, structural style, and primary materials --- alongside long-form descriptions that explore the design intent and spatial experience.

Photography is central to our presentation. We pair exterior and interior views, cross-referencing them with contextual notes on materiality, structural logic, and site response.

## Submissions

We welcome project submissions from architects and photographers. Work should demonstrate a clear design thesis and be documented with high-quality imagery. Please include full project metadata and a brief written description.

## Contact

For inquiries regarding featured projects or submissions, reach out through our editorial channels.
