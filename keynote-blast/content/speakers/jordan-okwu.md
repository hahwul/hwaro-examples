## the talk

**"After the Fire."** Jordan is not an engineer. That is the point. Signal Press embeds a writer in residence at every keynote event and Jordan reads fifteen minutes of original prose written during the three previous talks. It is the shortest slot and the only one with no slides.

### why he is in slot 04

Slot 04 is the wind-down. The energy needs to come off one notch and the audience needs a moment to integrate what they just heard. Jordan does that without making it feel like a closing ceremony.

### what to expect

- Prose, not slides
- A piece written during the three earlier keynotes
- Quotes from all three of the night's other speakers, stitched in live
- A hard cutoff at T+02:00 &mdash; the mic goes dead on the clock
