## the talk

**"The Quiet Part of Shipping."** Ada walks on at T+00:05 and spends twenty-five minutes on one product that worked and seven that did not. No demo. No slides after the third one. Just a story with receipts.

### why she is opening

The opener sets the energy in the room. Ada does this for a living. She has opened five shows this year and she knows how to talk into a cold audience. If the first two minutes do not hit, the rest of the night is uphill.

### watch for

- The slide with the org chart at year three versus year five
- The part where she admits she nearly shut the company down in month fourteen
- The single sentence about what she would not build again
- The pause. It is not a technical problem. It is deliberate.
