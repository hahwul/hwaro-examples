In an age of digital minimalism and sans-serif ubiquity, the serif font stands as a bastion of tradition and readability.

Serifs—those small strokes at the end of character lines—are not merely decorative. They guide the eye, providing a horizontal flow that makes long-form reading a pleasure rather than a chore.

At Rosewood, we embrace the **EB Garamond** and **Playfair Display** families for their elegant proportions and historical weight. They remind us that while technology changes, the human eye still craves the familiar rhythm of the printed word.

### Why Serifs Matter

1.  **Readability**: Serifs help distinguish individual letters.
2.  **Authority**: They carry a sense of history and trust.
3.  **Elegance**: The varied stroke widths add a calligraphic beauty to the screen.

When we choose a typeface, we are choosing the voice of our content. Rosewood speaks in a whisper, not a shout.
