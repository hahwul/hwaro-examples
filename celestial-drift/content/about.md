# The Void and the Light

Celestial Drift is an experimental static site generator theme created to push the boundaries of what is possible with minimal, elegant code. Inspired by the natural phenomena of auroras and the vastness of space, this theme is designed for portfolios, deep-dive technical blogs, and personal logs.

Our mission is to help you present your thoughts in a way that feels boundless. Enjoy the drift.
