Color is the most immediate tool for communicating luxury. Before a viewer reads a single word, color sets the emotional tone of an experience.

## The Psychology of Luxury Colors

Certain hues have been associated with wealth and prestige across cultures:

- **Gold** — Universal symbol of value and achievement
- **Deep red and burgundy** — Power, passion, and richness
- **Royal purple** — Historically the most expensive dye, reserved for emperors
- **Black and charcoal** — Sophistication and exclusivity

## Applying Rich Palettes

When designing with luxury in mind, restraint is key. A rich palette doesn't mean using every opulent color at once. Instead, choose a dominant tone, a metallic accent, and neutral companions.

### Example Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Deep Burgundy | `#1A0A10` |
| Primary | Antique Gold | `#C9A44A` |
| Text | Warm Ivory | `#F5F0E8` |
| Accent | Rose Gold | `#D4A07A` |

## Digital Luxury

In web design, luxury is conveyed through generous whitespace, refined typography, and subtle animations. The goal is to make every element feel intentional and considered — like a hand-woven brocade where every thread has its place.
