## Doors

Gates open at 14:00 both days. Front barrier queuing from 13:30 with wristband check.

## Access

Anvil Fields is step-free to the main floor. The viewing deck has eight wheelchair-accessible positions for Patron tier. Contact the box office in advance for placement.

## Refund Policy

Tickets are non-refundable except in the event of festival cancellation. In the event of a weather postponement, tickets carry to the new date.
