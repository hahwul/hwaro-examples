Load-in at Anvil Fields begins 08:00 Monday. Stage rehearsal runs through Thursday night; the Iron Mouth double-kit reinforced riser is in from Tuesday.

Bands are on site from Wednesday for soundchecks. The Kreuzwerk anvil-percussion rig is the largest single stage piece on the tour this year and ships Tuesday night.
