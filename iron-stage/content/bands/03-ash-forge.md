## Ash & Forge

Sheffield five-piece. Sludge with occasional stoner detours. The 2024 record *Hollow Rivets* is still on rotation; the new EP *Lathe* is out the Monday after the festival.

### EP Premiere

Two tracks from *Lathe* — *Spur* and *Chuck* — are premiered during the festival set. The rest of the set is drawn from *Hollow Rivets*.

### Live Personnel

Emma Dunmore — vocals, guitar. Saul Patkin — guitar. Rohan Obi — bass. Toby Sandforth — drums. Touring keyboardist Nana Khoury joins for the two EP tracks only.
