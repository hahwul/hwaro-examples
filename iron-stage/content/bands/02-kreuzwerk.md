## Kreuzwerk

Three-piece from Berlin. Low, slow, mechanical. The *Bannwerk* record has been on the festival circuit since April.

### Stage Set

Two wardrobe-sized speaker stacks, one custom anvil-mounted percussion rig, and a small modular bank for the noise textures between songs. The stack is routed through the main PA; the anvil is mic'd separately.

### Record

*Bannwerk* — second LP, six tracks, average length 9:40. The title track is the set's centrepiece at 16:20.
