## Nordlicht

Two-piece. Guitar + drums + pedals. The *Nine Lamps* record won last year's festival best-record poll and the touring cycle is still running.

### Set

Seventy-five minutes. The opening twenty minutes are single-track with no gap between songs — the band's signature opening. The full *Nine Lamps* record will play in order after the opener.
