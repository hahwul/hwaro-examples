## Smelter

Six-piece. Industrial thrash, heavy on the two-drummer configuration. The festival's most-rehearsed first-time band this year — four days of stage rehearsal after load-in.

### Record

Third LP *Slag Hymnal* was released on Friday of festival week. The set is the record, front to back, plus two older songs to close.

### Notes

The two-drum setup requires reinforced risers on the main stage; the riser extensions are in place from Friday morning.
