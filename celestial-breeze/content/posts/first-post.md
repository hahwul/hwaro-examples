## The Silence Above

There is something inherently calming about stepping outside on a clear night. The air is crisp, carrying with it a gentle, celestial breeze that seems to whisper secrets of the cosmos.

As I look up, the stars aren't just points of light; they are ancient stories written across the vast canvas of the universe. In a world that is always rushing, the night sky asks nothing of us but to simply be.

> "To look at the stars is to step outside of time."

The light reaching my eyes has traveled for years, perhaps millennia. It's a humbling reminder of our place in the grand scheme of things. We are small, yes, but we are also connected to this infinite expanse.
