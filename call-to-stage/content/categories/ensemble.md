## Brief

Two to six performers sharing a single audition slot. Any style, any combination of the other categories. The panel judges the ensemble as a single act, not the sum of its members.

## Stage Setup

- Up to four mics on round-base stands.
- Two house DI boxes.
- One shared click or track from the submitted audio.
- Wings kept clear stage left and right.

## Registration

All ensemble members register through a single form. Name one person as the point of contact; that is the person who receives the call-back. We will not cc additional members to avoid reply-all storms.

## Prize Split

The ensemble prize (first place: 2,500 USD) is paid in a single cheque to the ensemble's point of contact. We do not split by member; that is your band's choice, not ours.

## What the Panel Rewards

- Tight transitions between members.
- Dramatic use of stillness; the whole ensemble does not need to be moving.
- A single, clear finishing pose or musical cadence.

## What the Panel Cuts

- Individual solos that do not serve the ensemble.
- Mic handovers that bleed more than 4 seconds.
- Any member speaking to the panel between the entrance and the end cue.
