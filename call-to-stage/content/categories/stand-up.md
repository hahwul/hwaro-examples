## Brief

Stand-up comedy. Submit a five-minute unedited video at registration. The panel will watch the full five minutes and invite you to an audition slot where you have ninety seconds to land your best material on a cold room.

## Stage Setup

- Stand mic, round base. You can take the mic off the stand.
- House lights at 30%. Front row is the panel.
- No audience members recruited from outside the panel.

## Content Policy

- No material targeting protected classes as the punchline.
- No use of stage names that impersonate real public figures in a way likely to deceive.
- Strong language is fine. Sexual content is fine. Cruelty is not.

## If the Panel Cuts You

A panel member raising their pen cuts your time. It happens when material crosses the content policy. We will tell you why in the call-back email. You do not lose your submission slot for future years.

## What to Bring

- A water bottle.
- A second, safer opener you have memorised.
- A reason you're here, in case the panel asks.
