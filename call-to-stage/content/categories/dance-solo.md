## Brief

A single dancer, any style: contemporary, street, classical, fusion, folkloric. The panel scores on technique, musicality, spatial use, and the clarity of the choreographic intent.

## Stage Setup

- Marley floor, taped at corners for grip reference.
- House PA fed from your submitted audio.
- No aerial apparatus, no moving props heavier than 2 kg.
- Wings clear on both sides; you are welcome to enter from either.

## What to Wear

- Soft-soled shoes or bare feet.
- No tap shoes - the marley floor will not hold.
- Costume pieces are fine but must be removable without pause if they catch on something.

## Floor Etiquette

- Do not mark on the marley during warm-up; use the corridor.
- Rosin is allowed in the designated tray at stage left.
- Water bottles stay in the wing, not on stage.

| Dimension | Weight |
