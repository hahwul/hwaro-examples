## Brief

Acoustic or electric, plucked, bowed, struck, or blown. The panel judges musicality, technique, and stage presence; the difficulty of the piece is noted but is not the whole scoring.

## Stage Setup

- House DI for electric instruments; bring your own cable.
- Overhead condenser for acoustic; the chair is stationary.
- Wedge monitor down-front.
- No full drum kit - a snare, a floor tom, and a single cymbal stand only.

## What to Bring

- Your instrument, tuned and in a case.
- Cables, stomp-box power, and any pedal chain.
- A backup plan if an amp hums; we have one spare combo.

## Scoring Rubric

| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Technique | 30% |
| Tone / Phrasing | 30% |
| Programming / Choice | 20% |
| Stage Presence | 20% |

## Notes for Duets

Duets belong in Category 08 Ensemble. A duet entering as "Instrumental" will be asked to move to Ensemble; you will not lose your slot.
