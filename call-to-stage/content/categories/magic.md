## Brief

A single effect, performed with one setup. The panel scores on clarity of effect, patter, misdirection, and whether the reveal lands without a second take.

## Stage Setup

- A half-skirted 90 cm table, centre-stage.
- Audience panel is seated 3 m back in bright house light.
- No assistants; duos and ensembles belong in 07 or 08.

## What You Can Use

- Flash paper, with the panel's approval at check-in.
- Small fire gags, handled and extinguished by you, pre-approved.
- Card sleight, coin work, rope, silk, linking rings, sponge balls.

## What You Cannot Use

- Live pyrotechnics of any kind, including fire poi.
- Live animals.
- Any effect requiring an audience volunteer.

## Panel Asks

- Perform at your normal pace. Do not apologise for the camera.
- If the effect fails, keep going. The panel is scoring the recovery, too.
- Leave the stage as you found it; we have fifteen minutes to reset.
