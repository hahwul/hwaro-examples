## Brief

Anything that doesn't fit in categories 01-06 and isn't an ensemble act. Tell us what you need when you register so we can plan the floor, the fly lines, and the clearance.

## Stage Setup (By Request)

- Solo unicycle: floor cleared to 6 m diameter.
- Juggling: we will raise the fly bar to 4 m; no clubs over 60 cm.
- Puppetry: side table provided; bring your own backing cloth.
- Ventriloquism: stool or chair; mic positioned below the sightline.
- Low-air acrobatics: mat provided; no apparatus suspended from the grid.

## Paperwork

Specialty acts must supply a one-page risk note at registration. We'll return it countersigned. Our house insurance covers the stage, not the act; bring your own if you're concerned.

## What the Panel Rewards

- Acts that work with the room, not against it.
- Reset under 60 seconds so the next act isn't penalised.
- A clear ending - the panel has to know when to clap.
