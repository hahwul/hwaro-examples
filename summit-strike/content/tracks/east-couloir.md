## the route

East Couloir is the steepest, most technical route. It is capped at 30 participants per session. Every session has mandatory pre-reading, delivered 14 days before the event. Show up unprepared and you will be asked to switch tracks.

### what to expect

- 3-hour workshops with live coding and group debugging
- No projectors &mdash; whiteboards only
- Pre-reading packets arrive two weeks before the event
- Small cohorts (8 &ndash; 12 people) with a dedicated facilitator

### the labs on this route

- **Lab 01: distributed consensus with bare tools** &mdash; Day 02 / 09:00
- **Lab 02: writing a scheduler from scratch** &mdash; Day 02 / 10:45
- **Lab 03: build a real-time pipeline, start to finish** &mdash; Day 02 / 14:00
- **Lab 04: debugging production under snow** &mdash; Day 03 / 10:00

### who this is for

You are a specialist looking for peers. You are a systems engineer, a language author, a distributed-systems grinder, or a researcher moving into practice. If you can bring a laptop and read a paper, you will be comfortable on this route. If you cannot, pick North Ridge.

### capacity

East Couloir is capped. Register early. Waitlist opens two weeks before the event.
