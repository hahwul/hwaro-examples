## what registration includes

- All three days of sessions
- Breakfast, lunch, and the Peak Pavilion dinner
- The pre-reading packet (if you select East Couloir)
- A conference jacket &mdash; sized at check-in, not before
- Shuttle service between the valley lot and Ridge Hall
- Admission to the summit pour on day three

## refunds

Full refund up to 30 days out. 50 pct refund from 29 to 14 days. After 14 days, transfer only &mdash; we can reassign your seat but not refund it.
