Supernova is a conference and event landing page theme inspired by the explosive energy of a dying star. The design captures that singular moment of cosmic release -- typography that scatters from center, radial layouts that expand outward, and a dark void backdrop that makes every element burn brighter.

Designed for events that refuse to be forgettable.

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).
