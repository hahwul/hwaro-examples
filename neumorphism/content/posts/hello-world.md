Neumorphism, or soft UI, is a design style that uses highlights and shadows to create elements that look like they are extruded from or pressed into the background.

It creates a realistic, tactile feel without relying on harsh lines. This theme uses these principles throughout its design, keeping the overall aesthetic very clean and minimalistic.
