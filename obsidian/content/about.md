## About This Wiki

Obsidian is a personal knowledge base focused on volcanic geology and the remarkable materials produced by Earth's most powerful forces.

The name references obsidian itself -- a naturally occurring volcanic glass formed when felsic lava cools rapidly with minimal crystal growth. Like its namesake, this wiki aims to be sharp, clear, and reflective.

## Structure

Notes are organized by topic and tagged for cross-referencing. Each entry aims to be concise and self-contained while linking to related concepts where relevant.

## Purpose

This wiki serves as a reference for anyone interested in volcanology, petrology, and the geological processes that produce some of nature's most striking materials. All content is written for clarity and accessibility.
