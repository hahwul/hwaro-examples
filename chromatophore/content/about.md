## The Concept

The Chromatophore theme is inspired by the pigment-containing and light-reflecting cells found in a wide range of animals. In biology, they create striking displays of color without the need for complex internal gradients.

In this design, we mimic that purity.

### Core Principles

1. **Absolute Solids.** We use stark, contrasting colors to delineate sections.
2. **Typographic Hierarchy.** Scale and weight, specifically using fonts like Impact or Helvetica Neue, communicate importance.
3. **Absence of Gradients.** Gradients soften the design. We want it raw and structural.
4. **No Emojis.** The text must stand on its own without illustrative crutches.
