## About MOSAIC

MOSAIC is a magazine-style publication that brings together voices from the worlds of design, technology, culture, and contemporary thought. Each issue is a carefully assembled collection of perspectives — a mosaic of ideas that, together, reveal a larger picture.

### Our Editorial Philosophy

We believe in the power of long-form journalism, sharp criticism, and visual storytelling. Every article is selected not for its ability to generate clicks, but for its capacity to shift how you see the world.

### The Name

A mosaic is composed of many small, distinct pieces. Individually, each piece is simple. Together, they form something far more complex and beautiful than any single fragment could suggest. That is the ambition of this publication: to assemble diverse perspectives into a coherent, compelling whole.

### Contact

For submissions and editorial inquiries, reach us through our contributor guidelines. We welcome pitches on design, technology, architecture, visual culture, and the intersections between them.
