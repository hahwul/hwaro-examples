---
title = "Spatial Typography"
date = "2023-10-26"
---

Typography is traditionally static, bound to a grid, and fixed in space. By releasing typography from its rigid constraints and allowing it to swing freely, we introduce a new dimension of expression.

The words are no longer just symbols; they become physical objects with mass and momentum. Reading becomes a dynamic interaction where the text responds to the reader's presence.

This approach challenges the standard paradigms of web design. It forces the user to engage with the interface as a spatial construct rather than a flat document. The resulting experience is playful, unexpected, and memorable.
