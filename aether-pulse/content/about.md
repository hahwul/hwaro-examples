## Design Philosophy

The Aether Pulse theme is inspired by deep space and digital minimalism. It combines high-contrast neon accents with soft, ambient lighting to create a sense of depth and modernity.

### Typography
We use **Space Grotesk** for headings to give a futuristic and geometric feel, while **Inter** is used for body text to ensure maximum readability and a clean aesthetic.

### Layout
The layout leverages *glassmorphism*, utilizing `backdrop-filter: blur()` to create translucent panels that let the ambient background pulse through, creating a dynamic and immersive user experience.
