## About Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum (국화, *Gukhwa*) is a theme inspired by the imperial flower of East Asia — symbol of longevity, nobility, and the beauty of autumn. Just as the chrysanthemum blooms when all other flowers fade, this design embraces bold richness when others retreat to minimalism.

## Design Philosophy

**화려함 (Hwalryeoham)** — the Korean concept of glamorous splendor. This theme channels that energy through:

- **Layered Petals** — Overlapping visual elements that create depth and dimension
- **Autumnal Warmth** — Deep crimsons, burnt golds, and rich ambers drawn from nature
- **Dynamic Movement** — Subtle animations that echo petals unfurling in the wind
- **Eastern Elegance** — Refined aesthetics rooted in centuries of floral artistry

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast static site generator written in Crystal. The theme uses pure CSS for all visual effects — no JavaScript frameworks required.
