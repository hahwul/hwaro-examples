Great typography has rhythm — just like great music. In designing the Fiesta theme, we paired two fonts that dance together beautifully.

## The Display: Abril Fatface

Abril Fatface is a high-contrast display typeface that commands the room. Its thick strokes and elegant curves evoke vintage poster art — the kind you'd see promoting a carnival or a concert in a sun-drenched plaza.

## The Body: Nunito

Nunito is a friendly, rounded sans-serif that balances Abril's drama with clarity and warmth. It reads well at small sizes and keeps long passages comfortable.

## Rhythm in Layout

Typography isn't just about fonts — it's about spacing, hierarchy, and flow:

1. **Headlines grab attention** with scale and weight
2. **Subheadings guide** the reader through sections
3. **Body text stays comfortable** with generous line-height
4. **Accents and tags** use weight and color for emphasis

The result is a reading experience that feels energetic yet approachable — like a fiesta that welcomes everyone.
