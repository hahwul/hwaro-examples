Welcome to Tale! This is your first post. Edit or delete it, then start blogging.

Tale is a classic blog theme built for Hwaro, inspired by the original [Tale Jekyll theme](https://github.com/chesterhow/tale) by Chester How. It focuses on clean typography and a timeless design that never goes out of style.

## Why Tale?

- **Readability first** — Serif body text makes long-form content a pleasure to read
- **No distractions** — A minimal layout that lets your words speak for themselves
- **Zero JavaScript** — Fast, accessible, and works everywhere

Get started by editing the files in the `content/posts/` directory.
