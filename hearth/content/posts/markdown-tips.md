The first signs of spring are appearing, and it's time to prepare our community garden.

A few simple steps can make a big difference for the coming season:
- **Clean the beds**: Clear away any debris from winter.
- **Check the soil**: Ensure it's ready for planting.
- **Plan the layout**: Think about what we want to grow together this year.

Let's make this our most beautiful year yet.
