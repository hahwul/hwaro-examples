# About Weathervane

Weathervane is a daily farm journal dedicated to recording weather patterns, seasonal shifts, and the quiet rhythms of agricultural life. Each entry captures observations from the field -- the direction of the wind, the color of the sky at dawn, the condition of the soil after rain.

This journal serves as both a personal record and a practical resource, documenting the relationship between weather and the land over the course of the farming year.
