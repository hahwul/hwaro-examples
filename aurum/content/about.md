# About This Theme

**Aurum** is a premium, dark-themed scaffold for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), designed with elegance and simplicity in mind.

It features:
- A minimalist, distraction-free layout.
- Classic typography combining serifs for headings and modern sans-serifs for body text.
- Golden accents that provide a touch of luxury without being overwhelming.

This theme is perfect for personal journals, essays, and portfolios.
