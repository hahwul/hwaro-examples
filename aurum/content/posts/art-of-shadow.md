Design is not just about the elements you place on a canvas; it's equally about the spaces you leave empty. In Aurum, we explore the interplay between deep shadows and precise highlights.

## Finding Balance

When working with a dark theme, contrast becomes your primary tool for establishing hierarchy. The golden accents in Aurum are not meant to overwhelm, but rather to guide the eye.

> "Shadows are as important as light. They give depth and context."

### A Subtle Approach

Consider how a simple blockquote or a highlighted link can transform a paragraph. By using `#D4AF37` sparingly against `#121212`, we create a focal point without causing visual fatigue.

We believe that less is often more. The beauty of minimalism lies in its restraint.
