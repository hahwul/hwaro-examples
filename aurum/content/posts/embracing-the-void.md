Dark mode has evolved from a developer-centric feature to a mainstream expectation. But its appeal goes beyond simple aesthetics.

## Cognitive Load

Reading bright text on a dark background can reduce eye strain, especially in low-light environments. However, pure black (`#000000`) and pure white (`#FFFFFF`) create a jarring contrast.

Instead, Aurum uses a softer palette:
- Background: `#121212`
- Surface: `#1e1e1e`
- Text: `#e0e0e0`

This subtle softening of the extremes makes long-form reading much more comfortable.

### The Focus

When the background recedes into darkness, the content naturally steps forward. It's a stage where your words are the sole performers.
