In design, what is removed is often more important than what is added.

This piece explores the concept of carving away the unnecessary to reveal the essential core of the message. The stark contrast between the deep ink and the pale canvas creates a bold visual anchor.

By embracing limitations, we find new avenues for expression. No gradients, no soft shadows, only absolute edges.
