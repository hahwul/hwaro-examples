Slow performance can have several causes:

- **Internet connection** -- Test your speed at a speed testing service. We recommend at least 5 Mbps.
- **Browser tabs** -- Having many tabs open can consume memory. Try closing unused tabs.
- **Browser cache** -- Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page.
- **Large projects** -- Projects with over 10,000 tasks may benefit from using filters to limit the visible items.

Check our [status page](https://status.example.com) to see if there are any ongoing incidents. If the issue persists, reach out to our support team.
