If notifications aren't working as expected:

1. **Check notification settings** -- Go to **Settings** then **Notifications** and verify your preferences
2. **Check email filters** -- Notification emails may be going to spam
3. **Browser permissions** -- Ensure your browser allows notifications from our domain
4. **Muted channels** -- You may have muted the project or channel. Look for the mute icon next to its name.
5. **Do Not Disturb** -- Check if DND mode is enabled in your profile settings

For in-app notifications, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
