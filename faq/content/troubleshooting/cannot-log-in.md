If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps:

1. **Check your email address** -- Make sure you're using the same email you registered with
2. **Reset your password** -- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
3. **Clear browser cache** -- Old session data can sometimes cause login issues
4. **Disable browser extensions** -- Ad blockers or privacy extensions may interfere
5. **Try a different browser** -- This helps isolate browser-specific issues

If none of these work, contact support@example.com with your account email and a description of the error you see.
