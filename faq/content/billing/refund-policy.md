We offer a full refund within the first 30 days of any paid subscription. After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on remaining time in the billing period.

To request a refund:

1. Contact our support team at support@example.com
2. Include your account email and the reason for your request
3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Annual subscriptions are eligible for a prorated refund at any time during the subscription period.
