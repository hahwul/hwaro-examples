+++

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account:

1. Navigate to **Settings** then **Security**
2. Under **Two-Factor Authentication**, click **Enable**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit verification code to confirm setup
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

Once enabled, you'll need both your password and a verification code to sign in.
