Creating an account takes just a few steps:

1. Visit the sign-up page and enter your email address
2. Choose a strong password (at least 12 characters)
3. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you
4. Complete your profile with your name and organization

You can also sign up using your existing Google or GitHub account for a faster onboarding experience.
