After signing in, follow these steps to create your first project:

1. Click the **New Project** button in the top navigation bar
2. Enter a project name and optional description
3. Choose a template or start from scratch
4. Invite team members by entering their email addresses
5. Click **Create** to finalize

Your project dashboard will open immediately, where you can start creating tasks and organizing your workflow.
