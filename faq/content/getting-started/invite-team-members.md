You can invite team members from your project settings or directly from the workspace:

1. Navigate to **Settings** then **Members**
2. Click **Invite Members**
3. Enter one or more email addresses separated by commas
4. Select a role: Viewer, Editor, or Admin
5. Click **Send Invitations**

Invited members will receive an email with a link to join your workspace. Pending invitations can be managed and revoked from the same settings page.
