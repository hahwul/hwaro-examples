Our platform runs entirely in the browser, so there's no software to install. We support:

- **Chrome** 90 or later
- **Firefox** 88 or later
- **Safari** 15 or later
- **Edge** 90 or later

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser and a stable internet connection with at least 5 Mbps download speed.
