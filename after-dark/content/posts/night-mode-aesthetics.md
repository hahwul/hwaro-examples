As the sun sets, our eyes crave a different kind of digital interface. Night mode aesthetics have moved from a developer's preference to a mainstream design requirement.

<!-- more -->

## Visual Comfort

A well-implemented dark mode reduces eye strain and helps readers focus on the content without the harsh glare of a white background. "After Dark" takes this further by using softened blacks and warm accents.

### Creating Atmosphere

The atmosphere of a blog can be transformed by subtle shadows and low-contrast borders. It's about creating a "mood" that matches the reader's environment.

1.  **Reduce Contrast:** Avoid pure white text on pure black.
2.  **Warm Accents:** Use golds or ambers to guide the eye.
3.  **Generous Spacing:** Allow the content to breathe.

By focusing on these elements, we can create a reading experience that is both trendy and timeless.
