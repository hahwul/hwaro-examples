Spring in the city is not a single moment -- it is a slow accumulation of sounds. The drip of meltwater. A window opening for the first time in months. Birds returning to ledges they abandoned in November.

## Recording Setup

For this episode, Sora spent two weeks capturing audio across the city using a portable stereo recorder. No post-processing, no equalization. What you hear is what the microphone heard.

## Featured Locations

- **Riverside Park, 6:14 AM** -- Morning joggers, geese, and the distant hum of the bridge
- **Central Market, 11:30 AM** -- Vendors calling, crates stacking, a busker playing accordion
- **Rooftop on 5th Avenue, 7:45 PM** -- Wind, distant traffic, and the first evening crickets

## Listener Submissions

We received over forty field recordings from listeners this month. Three of them made it into the final mix. If you want to contribute, record a two-minute clip of your neighborhood and send it to sounds@frequency987.fm.

The city has a soundtrack. We just have to stop and listen.
