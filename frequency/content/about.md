## The Station

Frequency FM 98.7 has been broadcasting since 2019, delivering curated music, interviews, and cultural commentary to listeners across the city. What started as a small community radio project has grown into a beloved independent station with a dedicated audience.

## Our Mission

We believe radio is more than background noise. It is a living medium that connects communities, surfaces new voices, and creates shared experiences in real time. Every program on Frequency is crafted with intention -- from the morning wake-up to the late-night wind-down.

## The Studio

Our broadcast studio sits on the fourth floor of the old Waverly Building downtown. The space was renovated in 2021, blending vintage broadcast equipment with modern digital tools. Visitors often remark on the warmth of the room -- a combination of wood paneling, soft amber lighting, and decades of sonic history embedded in the walls.

## Listen

Tune in at **FM 98.7 MHz** or stream live through our website. We broadcast 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
