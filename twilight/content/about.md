## About Twilight

Twilight is a photo essay journal dedicated to the transient beauty of dusk, golden hour, and the liminal space between day and night. Each essay pairs large-format photography with contemplative prose, exploring how the changing light transforms ordinary landscapes into something quietly extraordinary.

There is a particular stillness that settles over the world during the twilight hours. Shadows lengthen. Colors deepen. The sky becomes a canvas that exists for only a few fleeting minutes before darkness arrives. These essays attempt to hold those minutes in place, if only through words and images.

## The Categories

Essays are organized by the time of day they inhabit:

**Dawn** -- The first uncertain light before sunrise, when the world is still deciding whether to wake.

**Day** -- Bright hours and harsh clarity, the sun at its peak, shadows at their shortest.

**Dusk** -- The golden hour and its aftermath, when everything glows warm before cooling into night.

**Night** -- Full darkness, city lights, the world seen through a different lens entirely.

## Colophon

Twilight is built with Hwaro, a lightweight static site generator. The typefaces are DM Serif Display for headings and Karla for body text. The color palette is drawn from the sky itself: deep blue for the late evening, muted purple for the gathering dusk, and peach for the last warmth of the setting sun.
