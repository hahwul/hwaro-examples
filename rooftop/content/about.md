## About Rooftop

Rooftop is an urban lifestyle journal dedicated to the culture, aesthetics, and experiences found above the city streets. From the quiet solitude of a midnight skyline to the vibrant energy of a rooftop gathering, we explore every dimension of life at elevation.

This space was created for those who find inspiration in city lights, who seek out the highest vantage points, and who understand that the best perspectives come from looking down at the world below. Whether it is a hidden garden perched above the noise or a late-night jazz session under open skies, Rooftop captures the moments that make urban living extraordinary.

## What We Cover

We write about rooftop gardens and urban greenery, nighttime photography from elevated perspectives, the evolving culture of rooftop dining and social spaces, architectural appreciation, and the simple art of watching a city come alive after dark. Every piece is crafted to reflect the refined, contemplative side of urban life.

## The Philosophy

Cities are best understood from above. At street level, everything is movement and noise. But climb a few stories, step out onto a rooftop terrace, and the city transforms into something entirely different. Patterns emerge. The rhythm of traffic becomes a pulse. Buildings become silhouettes against the sky. Rooftop exists to celebrate that shift in perspective.
