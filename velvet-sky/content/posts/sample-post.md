There is something inherently tranquil about writing code late at night. The rest of the world has fallen quiet, leaving only the soft glow of the monitor and the gentle rhythm of the keyboard.

## The Canvas of the Night

Just as a painter starts with a blank canvas, a developer begins with an empty file. In the stillness of the night, thoughts flow more clearly.

> "The night is the hardest time to be alive and 4am knows all my secrets."
> — Poppy Z. Brite

### A Snippet of Serenity

```crystal
def greet_the_night(name : String)
  puts "Good evening, #{name}. The stars are bright tonight."
end

greet_the_night("Traveler")
```

May your code compile on the first try and your mind find rest in the elegance of logic.
