Rust, or iron oxide, is often seen as a sign of decay. However, the rich textures and intense orange and brown hues offer a striking palette for creative design. The natural degradation process produces intricate, chaotic patterns that stand in stark contrast to the clean, polished look of modern materials.

In architecture, Corten steel intentionally embraces this process. Its surface oxidizes to form a protective layer, rendering each structure unique as it weathers the elements over time.
