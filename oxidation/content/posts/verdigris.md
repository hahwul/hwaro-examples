When exposed to the elements, copper and its alloys undergo a transformation. The result is verdigris, a stunning blue-green patina that has captivated artists and architects for centuries. This process is not a flaw, but a natural evolution of the material.

The oxidation of copper creates a layer that actually protects the metal underneath. It is a perfect example of nature creating beauty through what seems like destruction.
