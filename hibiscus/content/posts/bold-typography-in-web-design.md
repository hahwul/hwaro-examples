Typography sets the tone before a single word is read. In glamorous web design, the typeface itself becomes a visual element — commanding attention and establishing authority.

## The Power of Serif

Serif typefaces carry centuries of typographic tradition. In a digital context, bold serifs like Playfair Display create an instant sense of sophistication and editorial quality.

### Why Serifs Work for Bold Themes

- **Visual Weight** — Thick strokes and ornamental details fill space with confidence
- **Contrast** — Pairing bold serifs with light sans-serif body text creates dynamic hierarchy
- **Personality** — Serifs evoke luxury, tradition, and editorial authority

## Hierarchy Through Scale

In bold design, size differences between heading levels should be dramatic:

- **H1**: Large and commanding, often with decorative effects
- **H2**: Still prominent, establishing section boundaries
- **Body**: Clean and comfortable, optimized for long-form reading

The contrast between heading and body creates rhythm — moments of visual intensity followed by calm reading flow.

## Color and Typography

Colored headings amplify the impact of bold typefaces. A gradient or vivid hue on an H1 creates an immediate focal point that draws the eye into the content below.

The best bold typography serves two masters: it dazzles on first impression and remains readable on sustained engagement.
