## About Nadir

Nadir is a writing experiment in extreme minimalism. The word itself means the lowest point -- the point on the celestial sphere directly below an observer. It is the opposite of the zenith.

This site embraces that downward gaze. Pure black backgrounds recall the OLED screens that first made true darkness possible on a display. Text appears as faint signals against the void, like distant starlight reaching across impossible distances.

### Philosophy

Less interface, more thought. Every element that does not serve the reading experience has been removed. What remains is ink on darkness -- the oldest form of communication, stripped to its essence.

### Colophon

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a static site generator. Set in system sans-serif at comfortable reading sizes. No tracking, no JavaScript frameworks, no unnecessary complexity.
