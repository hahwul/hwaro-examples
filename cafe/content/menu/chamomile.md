Our chamomile is sourced from the Nile Delta, where the dry climate produces flowers with an exceptionally high essential oil content. We use whole flower heads rather than dust or fannings, and steep them for five minutes in covered ceramic.

The cup is golden, aromatic, and naturally sweet with notes of apple and honey. Caffeine-free and perfect for winding down.

**Enhancement:** Add a spoonful of local wildflower honey for extra warmth.
