Our cappuccino is built on a double shot of espresso, topped with whole milk steamed to a silky microfoam. The ratio is traditional Italian: equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam.

We texture the milk between 55 and 65 degrees Celsius for the sweetest flavor and smoothest mouthfeel. The result is rich, creamy, and perfectly balanced.

**Milk alternatives:** Oat, almond, and soy available at no extra charge.
