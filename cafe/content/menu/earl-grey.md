Our Earl Grey is a full-leaf Ceylon blend infused with cold-pressed bergamot oil from Calabria. We steep it for four minutes in water just off the boil to extract the full depth of flavor without bitterness.

Served in a ceramic pot with enough for two generous cups. The aroma alone is worth the visit: floral, citrusy, and deeply comforting.

**Pairings:** Goes beautifully with our lemon tart or plain scone with clotted cream.
