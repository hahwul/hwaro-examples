Our croissants are laminated by hand using a traditional French method: 27 layers of organic flour dough and high-fat European butter, folded over three days. They are shaped each morning and baked until deeply golden and impossibly flaky.

The interior is soft, honeycomb-like, and slightly sweet. The crust shatters at the first bite. This is the pastry our regulars come back for day after day.

**Variations:** Plain, almond-filled, or chocolate available daily.
