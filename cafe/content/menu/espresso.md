Our signature espresso is a blend of sun-dried Brazilian Santos and washed Ethiopian Yirgacheffe. The result is a balanced shot with notes of dark chocolate, toasted almond, and a bright citrus finish.

We pull every shot on a La Marzocca Linea Mini at 9 bars of pressure, targeting a 25-second extraction for optimal sweetness and body.

**Serving options:** Single or double shot. Available as a ristretto (short pull) or lungo (long pull) on request.
