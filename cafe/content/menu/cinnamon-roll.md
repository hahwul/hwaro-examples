Our signature cinnamon roll starts with a rich brioche dough enriched with butter and egg yolks. The filling is a generous layer of Ceylon cinnamon, dark muscovado sugar, and a pinch of cardamom.

Each roll is baked until just set, then glazed with a tangy cream cheese icing while still warm. The result is soft, gooey, aromatic, and deeply satisfying.

**Best enjoyed:** Warm from the oven. Ask your barista when the next batch is due.
