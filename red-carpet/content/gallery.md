## Credits

All photographs on this page are credited on publication. Usage is limited to editorial and promotional purposes for the Gilded Hour release campaign. Re-use requires written permission from the Palatine press office.

## Access requests

Editorial access to the full high-resolution gallery requires a press credential issued through the Palatine's online portal. Decisions are returned within twenty-four hours. A selection of seventy-two images will be available free of charge for non-commercial use through the festival archive for ninety days.
