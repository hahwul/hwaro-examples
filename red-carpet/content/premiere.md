## Principal cast

The Gilded Hour is an adaptation of the 1927 novella by Margaux Holland, here reset to the early 1970s and reframed through the perspective of its supporting characters. Odessa Lumi&egrave;re leads as the letter writer's daughter; Mar&iacute;a Villaespa plays the letter writer herself in a dual timeline. Nomvula Dlamini's screenplay keeps Holland's original epistolary structure in every act.

## Direction and craft

Jun-ho Park directs his fifth feature. Tobias Rhodes returns as director of photography, Alessandro Di Falco has written an original score performed by the Palatine Symphony in the recording sessions of February 2026. Ada Okonjo's costumes were built across nine months in three cities.

## After the screening

The after-party transfers guests from the Palatine auditorium to the hotel across the square at 22:30, where a dinner for 180 is served in the conservatory. Press access to the after-party is limited to four invited publications and one broadcast crew. The carpet itself is struck at 23:00 by the venue's own rigging team.
