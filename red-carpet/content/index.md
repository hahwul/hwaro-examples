## Press access

Interior wall press positions were allocated at 17:45 and closed at 18:20. Step-and-repeat positions for wire photographers are held to publication priority; print and online credentials run a separate rotation along the colonnade. Broadcast crews are stationed at the two gold-roped interview islands, one at either end of the carpet.
