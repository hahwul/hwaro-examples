# About Lunar Tide

**Lunar Tide** was created to provide a serene, contemplative reading experience.

Designed with minimalist typography—pairing the classic elegance of *Playfair Display* for headings with the readable warmth of *Lora* for body text—this theme invites the reader into a calm digital space.

## Features

- **Dark Mode by Default**: Deep, oceanic background colors reduce eye strain.
- **Glowing Accents**: Subtle radial gradients mimic the glow of moonlight.
- **Elegant Typography**: Serif fonts bring a touch of traditional editorial design to the modern web.

Find peace in the rhythm of your words.
