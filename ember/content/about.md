## About Ember

Ember is a personal blog for slow, deliberate writing. The essays here circle around craft, solitude, memory, and the small rituals that structure a life. Nothing here is urgent. That is the point.

The name comes from the last stage of a fire -- not the roaring blaze, but the low, persistent heat that remains after the flames have died down. Embers are patient. They hold warmth long after the spectacle has passed.

### What You Will Find Here

Essays on writing, reading, the texture of daily life, and the kind of thinking that requires quiet. The pace is slow by design. Each piece is meant to be read in one sitting, preferably with something warm to drink.

### Design Philosophy

Dark, warm, unhurried. The interface stays out of the way so the words can do their work. Amber tones and generous spacing create a reading environment that feels like firelight on paper.
