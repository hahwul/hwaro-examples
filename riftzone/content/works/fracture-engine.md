## Overview

Fracture Engine is a simulation platform that models how dimensional breaches form, propagate, and can be contained. Originally developed as a safety tool, it became the foundation for controlled rift generation used in subsequent projects.

## Technical Approach

The engine calculates stress tensors across dimensional membranes in real time, predicting fracture points before they manifest. A containment field generator uses inverse-pressure algorithms to stabilize breaches at desired apertures without allowing uncontrolled expansion.

## Outcome

Zero uncontrolled breach events since deployment. Engine accuracy in predicting fracture initiation points exceeds 99.6%. The containment protocols developed here are now standard across all rift-adjacent operations. Licensed to four dimensional transit authorities.
