# Results at a glance

The full results section is [Section 3 of the manuscript](/sections/3-results/). This page presents headline findings.

## Acceptability at standard thresholds

| Willingness-to-pay (&lambda;) | Probability cost-effective |
| --- | --- |
| $20,000 per QALY | 0.21 |
| $30,000 per QALY | 0.65 |
| $50,000 per QALY | **0.924** |
| $100,000 per QALY | 0.99 |
| $150,000 per QALY | 1.00 |

## Incremental outcomes

- **Costs:** +$4,310 per person (lifetime, discounted)
- **Effects:** +0.18 QALYs per person; +0.25 life-years
- **Public-health outcome:** 34.2 late-stage cases averted per 1,000 screened

## Subgroup headline

- Age 45 at first screen: ICER $23,944 per QALY
- Age 55 at first screen: ICER $35,286 per QALY
- Age 65 at first screen: ICER $60,429 per QALY (above &lambda; = $50,000)
