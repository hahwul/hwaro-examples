Minimalism is not about taking things away; it is about making the important things stand out.

## Form and Function

In web design, form must always follow function. A beautiful site that is hard to read is a failed site. By focusing on typography, we ensure the message is delivered with clarity and grace.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."

### Typography

We use carefully selected serif fonts for headings to bring a touch of classic elegance, while readable sans-serif fonts carry the body text.

- Clean lines
- Soft contrast
- Perfect rhythm
