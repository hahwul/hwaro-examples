# About Tangram

Tangram is a design portfolio built around the idea that every project is a puzzle piece -- distinct in shape, but part of a larger picture.

## Philosophy

The tangram is one of the oldest dissection puzzles in the world, originating in China during the Song Dynasty. Seven geometric pieces -- five triangles, one square, and one parallelogram -- can be arranged into an infinite variety of recognizable forms. The constraint is absolute, but the possibilities are boundless.

This portfolio applies the same principle to creative work. Every project has fixed parameters: budgets, timelines, client needs, technical requirements. The craft lies in arranging these constraints into something that works beautifully.

## What You Will Find Here

- **Case Studies** -- Detailed walkthroughs of selected projects, from initial brief through final delivery.
- **Design Explorations** -- Experiments with form, typography, color, and layout.
- **Process Notes** -- Reflections on methodology, tools, and the practice of design.

## Approach

The work in this portfolio favors clarity over complexity, structure over decoration, and purpose over trend. Every choice -- from typeface selection to whitespace allocation -- serves the overall composition.

Like the tangram, the goal is not to use as many pieces as possible, but to use exactly the right ones.
