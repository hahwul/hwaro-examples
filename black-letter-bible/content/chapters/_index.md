The chapters are arranged chronologically from the manuscript ancestry of the textura hand through the successive generations of printed editions into the late survival of the Gothic type in the eighteenth and nineteenth centuries. Each chapter gives a brief survey of its subject with attention to the type, the press, the format, and the ornamental programme.
