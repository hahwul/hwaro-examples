Welcome to **Stellar Echoes**. This template explores the vastness of space through deep gradients, glassmorphism UI elements, and subtle starfield animations.

## Explore the Cosmos

Journey through the interactive nebula. Watch the floating stars.

> "The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself." — Carl Sagan

### Features
* **Animated Background:** Multi-layered CSS starfield animation
* **Glassmorphism:** Frosted glass content containers with soft blurs
* **Glowing Typography:** Inter and Orbitron fonts with neon shadows
* **Responsive:** Designed to scale beautifully across all screen sizes
