## The Mission

The **Stellar Echoes** template was forged in the heat of a supernova to provide a gorgeous, dark-themed starting point for your next [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com/) project.

Whether you're building a developer portfolio, a sci-fi blog, or an immersive narrative experience, this template provides the atmospheric scaffolding.

### Technologies
- Hwaro Static Site Generator
- Pure CSS animations
- CSS Backdrop Filter

Enjoy your stay in the void.
