# Keystone ADR

Architecture Decision Records for the Keystone platform. This site captures the significant architectural decisions made during the design and evolution of our system, along with their context and consequences.

## Decision Log

- [Architecture Decisions](/docs/decisions/) -- Browse all recorded ADRs with status tracking
- [Guidelines](/docs/guidelines/) -- Learn how to write and review ADRs

## What is an ADR?

An Architecture Decision Record is a short document that captures an important architectural decision made along with its context and consequences. ADRs are numbered sequentially, immutable once approved, and serve as the institutional memory of our engineering organization.
