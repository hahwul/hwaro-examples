Early morning fog in the Muir Woods.

The silence here is heavy. The fog dampens the sound, making every snapped twig sound like a gunshot. The scale of these ancient trees makes human worries feel incredibly small.

Converting this to black and white was necessary to strip away the distraction of color. The image is about texture, shadow, and scale.
