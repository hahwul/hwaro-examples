A study in contrast and artificial light. Taken after midnight during a heavy downpour, the streets cleared out entirely, leaving only the neon signs bleeding colors into the puddles below. Shot on 35mm at f/1.4.

The reflections tell a better story than the reality above them. Notice how the sharp edges of the buildings soften when viewed in the standing water.
