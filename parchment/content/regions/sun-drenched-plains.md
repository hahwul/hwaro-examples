The **Sun-Drenched Plains** represent the heart of our world's agriculture and the cradle of several major civilizations.

## Geography

A land of rolling hills and endless fields, the plains are known for their golden hue, especially during the long summer months.

*   **The Amber Sea:** A name given to the central grasslands.
*   **The Crystal River:** The primary source of life for the many farming communities.
*   **The Gilded City:** The central trading hub where caravans from all over the world meet.

### Points of Interest

| Location | Significance |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Empire's Gate** | A massive stone archway built during the First Age. |
| **Whistling Hills** | Known for the musical sound the wind makes as it passes through the limestone caverns. |
| **The Great Granary** | A fortress-like structure where most of the world's grain is stored. |
