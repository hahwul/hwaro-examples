Welcome, traveler, to the **Ancient Chronicles**. Within these digital scrolls, you shall find the accumulated knowledge of ages past—the lineages that shape our world, the regions that define our borders, and the history that binds us all.

## The Three Pillars of Lore

Explore the depth of our world through these primary volumes:

1.  **Lineages (Races):** Discover the origins and cultures of the various peoples inhabiting our lands.
2.  **Atlas (Regions):** Journey through the diverse landscapes, from the frozen peaks to the sun-drenched plains.
3.  **History:** Delve into the pivotal events that have defined the current era.
