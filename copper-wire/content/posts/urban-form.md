Cities are the original complex systems. From the rigid grids of Manhattan to the organic sprawl of London, urban forms provide a rich vocabulary for digital designers.

In this entry, we examine how "exposed" infrastructure in cities—like the elevated trains of Chicago—creates a unique visual rhythm that can be translated into web layouts.
