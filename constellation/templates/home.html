Seasonal Night Sky Guide
Winter Orion dominates the southern sky. The Pleiades ride high. Sirius blazes near the horizon. Best season for bright stellar targets.
Spring Leo climbs to the meridian. The Virgo galaxy cluster opens for deep-sky observers. The Milky Way lies low.
Summer The Summer Triangle rises. Sagittarius and the galactic center transit at midnight. Rich Milky Way star fields overhead.
Autumn Andromeda galaxy reaches prime position. Pegasus spans the zenith. Double Cluster in Perseus is a binocular showpiece.
