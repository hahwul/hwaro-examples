Welcome to the **Polaris Observatory**. This site is a tribute to the celestial navigator, the North Star, and the constellations that revolve around it.

## The North Star: Polaris

Polaris, also known as the North Star, is the brightest star in the constellation **Ursa Minor**. It is very close to the north celestial pole, making it the current northern pole star.

### Constellation Navigation

Using the stars to find your way is an ancient art. By identifying the Big Dipper and following the "pointer stars," you can locate Polaris and determine which way is North.
