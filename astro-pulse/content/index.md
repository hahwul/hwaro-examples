# Welcome to Astro Pulse

Experience the ethereal glow of the cosmos. This theme combines deep dark backgrounds (`#080812`) with fluid, glowing neon accents (`#00f0ff` & `#ff0055`) and sleek glassmorphism panels.

## The Aesthetic

- **Deep Space Void:** Immersive dark background that sets the tone.
- **Neon Luminance:** Striking cyan and magenta fluid animations.
- **Frosted Glass:** Translucent UI elements with `backdrop-filter: blur(15px)`.
- **Modern Typography:** Featuring *Space Grotesk* for headings and *Inter* for body text.

Explore the depths of this design and see how light interacts with glass.
