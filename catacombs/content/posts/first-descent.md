The entrance stood before us, a narrow archway carved into weathered stone. Beyond it, only darkness and the faint smell of earth and age.

We lit our torches and began the descent.

<!-- more -->

## The First Passage

The tunnel sloped downward, its walls rough-hewn and ancient. Moisture glistened on the stone, and our footsteps echoed in the silence. The air grew colder with each step.

After what felt like an eternity of walking, we reached the first chamber.

## The Chamber of Bones

Skulls lined the walls in neat rows, hundreds of them, watching us with empty eyes. This was no mere tunnel—these were the catacombs proper, a vast underground necropolis.

The torch flames danced, casting shifting shadows across the ancient remains. We stood in silence, humbled by the weight of history surrounding us.

### What We Found

* Ancient burial chambers carved deep into bedrock
* Passages extending in multiple directions
* Markings on the walls, symbols we could not decipher
* Evidence of previous explorers who had come and gone

## Moving Forward

This was only the beginning. The passages branch in countless directions, each one promising new discoveries. We marked our path carefully—getting lost down here could mean never finding the way back.

The journey continues tomorrow. The depths call to us.
