Welcome to Pointillism Art. This theme constructs images, backgrounds, and UI borders entirely out of tiny, vibrant dots to create a stunning optical effect.

<!-- more -->

### The Concept

Pointillism is a technique of painting in which small, distinct dots of color are applied in patterns to form an image. This theme applies the same concept to modern web design.

![Test Image](/test.jpg)

### Features

- **Dot Borders:** Borders are not solid lines, but rather clusters of vibrant dots.
- **Image Filters:** Images are processed with SVG filters to give them a pointillistic look.
- **Vibrant Colors:** Primary and secondary colors mix visually, just like classical pointillist paintings.
