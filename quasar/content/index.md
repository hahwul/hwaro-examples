# SYSTEM ONLINE

Quasar is a dark, high-contrast, geometric framework designed for ultimate clarity and impact. It rejects unnecessary embellishments, gradients, and trivialities in favor of pure structure and stark typography.

## Core Directives

1.  **Structure Over Style:** Form follows function.
2.  **Absolute Contrast:** Black and white. No gray areas.
3.  **Strict Geometry:** Clean lines, sharp corners.
4.  **Zero Distractions:** Content is the only focus.
