## About Ramble

Ramble is a walking diary for those who find peace on the trail. Whether it is a short morning stroll through the neighborhood park or a full-day ridge hike in the mountains, every walk deserves to be remembered.

This blog documents walks with honest detail: the distance covered, the time spent, the elevation gained, and most importantly, the experience itself. The sights, the sounds, the weather, and the thoughts that only come when you are moving at the speed of footsteps.

### What You Will Find Here

- **Walk logs** with distance, time, and elevation data
- **Trail observations** on terrain, flora, and seasonal changes
- **Route notes** for anyone who wants to follow the same path

### About the Format

Each walk entry includes key metadata at a glance: how far, how long, and how high. The rest is written in the spirit of a field journal, capturing the texture of each walk without embellishment.

Walking is simple. So is this site.
