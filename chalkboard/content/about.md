Welcome to the classroom. This is where ideas are sketched out, erased, rewritten, and refined until they stick.

Think of this space as a blackboard in the back of a lecture hall -- covered in half-finished proofs, reading lists, and the kind of notes that only make sense if you were there when they were written.

## The Philosophy

Every great idea starts as a rough sketch. The chalkboard is not a place for polished presentations. It is a place for working things out in real time, making mistakes, and learning from them.

## What You Will Find

- **Lecture notes** on topics ranging from mathematics to philosophy
- **Problem sets** that walk through solutions step by step
- **Reading lists** and annotated bibliographies
- **Research memos** documenting ongoing investigations

The dust never fully settles here. Pull up a chair and follow along.
