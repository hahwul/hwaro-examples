## Release Calendar

The tickets are released in three windows to make sure people who wanted a seat but did not wake up at 9am can still have one.

| Window | Opens | Closes | Allocated |
|---|---|---|---|
| First | Mon 12 Feb 2026, 9:00am | Mon 12 Feb, 6:00pm | 400 |
| Second | Sat 1 Mar 2026, 10:00am | Sat 1 Mar, 6:00pm | 240 |
| Final | Sat 3 Apr 2026, 10:00am | Sold out | 160 |

We have had more demand than seats in every Old Hall show since 2012. If you do not get a ticket in the first window, do not panic; more come in the second and third. If you still do not get one, the Radio 6 broadcast on Thursday 12 May is free.

## How To Buy

The box office is at The Old Hall, 27 Dockside Lane, Manchester M3. The box office is open Tuesday to Saturday, 10am to 6pm, and from 6pm on show nights.

You may buy in person, by telephone (0161 477 9102), or by post (cheque payable to The Old Hall Ltd.). We do not maintain an online purchase. We have thought about it, and we have decided against it.

## Returns &amp; Resales

Any ticket you cannot use must be returned to the box office, where it will be refunded at face value and resold to the waiting list. Do not resell. There is no secondary market, and we will refuse entry to tickets we can confirm were resold.

The waiting list opens two weeks before the show. If you add yourself to the list and a ticket becomes available, the box office will call you. If the box office cannot reach you in one working day, the ticket passes to the next name on the list.

## What You Get

Every ticket, of any tier, includes a printed setlist, hand-numbered, from an edition of 800. The setlist is printed the week of the show on a small letterpress in Salford and is numbered in the box office by hand on the afternoon of the show.

Balcony and Residency Circle tickets also include a printed program with liner notes, photographs, and a short essay by Maeve. The program is available to Floor Standing ticket holders at cost (four pounds) from the merch table.

## A Final Word

We have priced the show as low as we can, given the cost of the pressing and the printing, and we have held the prices flat since 2022 despite everything. If you cannot afford a ticket, write to us, care of the venue, and we will hold a seat for you. We have done this every year and we are doing it again tonight.

> A full room is better than a sold-out room.
>
> -- Maeve, in an interview, 2008.
