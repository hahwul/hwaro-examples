# About Coral

Coral is an ecological documentary project dedicated to chronicling the world's coral reef ecosystems. Through long-form writing, scientific research, and field observations, we aim to bring the hidden world beneath the waves into clearer focus.

## Our Mission

We believe that understanding precedes conservation. By documenting the extraordinary biodiversity of coral reefs, the threats they face, and the communities that depend on them, we hope to foster a deeper appreciation for these irreplaceable ecosystems.

## What We Cover

- **Species Profiles** -- In-depth examinations of individual coral species, their biology, and their ecological roles.
- **Conservation Stories** -- Reports from the front lines of reef restoration and marine protection efforts.
- **Ecosystem Science** -- Explorations of the complex relationships that sustain reef communities.
- **Field Dispatches** -- First-hand accounts from marine biologists, divers, and conservationists working in reef environments around the world.

## The Team

This project is maintained by a small group of marine science enthusiasts, writers, and photographers who share a deep fascination with the ocean's coral architecture. We collaborate with researchers and conservation organizations to ensure our reporting is accurate and meaningful.
