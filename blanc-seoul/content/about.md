## Blanc Seoul

Blanc Seoul is a light blog theme that draws inspiration from the refined elegance of Seoul, Korea. Where neon-drenched cyberpunk themes embrace the darkness, Blanc Seoul celebrates the beauty of light, space, and restraint.

### Design Philosophy

The theme embodies the Korean concept of **yeobaek** (the beauty of empty space). Every element is placed with intention, and the white space between them is just as important as the content itself.

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Warm White | #FEFDFB | Primary background |
| Parchment | #F8F6F1 | Alternate surface |
| Ink | #171412 | Primary text |
| Vermillion | #D64933 | Accent (Korean red) |
| Gold | #B69D6E | Decorative accent |
| Stone | #7A736B | Muted text |

### Typography

- **Cormorant Garamond** for elegant serif headings
- **Inter** for clean, modern body text
- **Noto Serif KR** for Korean typographic accents

### Features

- Light, airy design with generous white space
- Sticky header with backdrop blur
- Elegant post cards with subtle hover effects
- Responsive typography scaled for readability
- Korean-inspired decorative details
- Clean code block styling
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Dark accent colors for strong hierarchy
