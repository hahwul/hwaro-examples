## Blanc Seoul

Blanc Seoul is a light blog theme that draws inspiration from the serene beauty of snow-covered Seoul and the vibrant dancheong patterns of traditional Korean architecture. Where neon-drenched cyberpunk themes embrace the darkness, Blanc Seoul celebrates the quiet elegance of winter hanok rooftops and the rich colors painted on their wooden beams.

### Design Philosophy

The theme embodies two contrasts: the stillness of fresh snow and the vividness of dancheong. Cool, frosty backgrounds create a calm canvas, while the traditional five cardinal colors (red, green, blue, yellow, white) appear as deliberate accents throughout the design.

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Snow | #F5F6FA | Primary background |
| Frost | #ECEEF5 | Alternate surface |
| Ink | #1A1D28 | Primary text |
| Dancheong Red | #BE3A34 | Primary accent |
| Dancheong Green | #2D7A5B | Blockquote accent |
| Dancheong Blue | #2B5797 | Code accent |
| Dancheong Yellow | #D4A843 | Tag accent |

### Typography

- **Cormorant Garamond** for elegant serif headings
- **Inter** for clean, modern body text
- **Noto Serif KR** for Korean typographic accents

### Dancheong Details

The four-color dancheong strip appears at key structural moments — the header crown, the hero ornament, section dividers, table headers, article navigation borders, and the footer threshold — echoing how traditional dancheong painting adorns the structural beams and eaves of hanok architecture.

### Features

- Snow-inspired cool palette with dancheong color system
- Dancheong strip ornaments on header, footer, and cards
- Tags cycle through four dancheong colors
- Sticky header with backdrop blur
- Elegant post cards with hover reveal
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Print-friendly styles
