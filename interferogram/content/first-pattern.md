Interference is a phenomenon in which two waves superpose to form a resultant wave of greater, lower, or the same amplitude.

This theme is inspired by the bold, visual aesthetics of constructive and destructive interference, utilizing striking colors and sharp layouts to manifest these physical phenomena digitally.

### Constructive Interference

When peaks align, the signal is amplified. In design, we see this when typography and layout support the core message without distraction.

### Destructive Interference

When a peak meets a trough, they cancel out. The absence of gradients and emojis in this theme represents a deliberate cancellation of visual noise, allowing the raw, elegant structure to remain.
