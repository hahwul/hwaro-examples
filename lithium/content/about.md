# About Lithium

Lithium Energy Technologies is building the next generation of battery systems for a world transitioning to clean energy. Founded in 2023, we are a team of materials scientists, electrochemists, and engineers dedicated to solving the hardest problems in energy storage.

## Our Mission

The global energy transition demands storage solutions that are safer, longer-lasting, and faster-charging than anything available today. Current lithium-ion technology has reached its practical limits. We are developing solid-state battery architectures that push beyond those limits.

Our goal: make clean energy available on demand, everywhere.

## The Technology

Our core innovation is a proprietary solid-state electrolyte that eliminates the flammable liquid components found in conventional batteries. This enables:

- **Higher energy density** -- more power in a smaller footprint
- **Faster charging** -- 0 to 80% in under 15 minutes
- **Longer lifespan** -- over 10,000 cycles with minimal degradation
- **Intrinsic safety** -- non-flammable materials by design

We have validated these results across lab-scale, module-level, and pilot production environments.

## The Team

Our team brings experience from leading institutions and companies in the energy sector. With backgrounds spanning Stanford, MIT, CATL, Tesla, and the US Department of Energy, we combine deep scientific expertise with practical engineering know-how.

## Backed by Leading Investors

We have raised $85M in Series B funding led by Breakthrough Energy Ventures, with participation from Lowercarbon Capital and Khosla Ventures. Our investors share our conviction that advanced battery technology is the linchpin of the clean energy transition.
