The Observatory is a dedicated station for recording and documenting deep space celestial observations. Our primary mission is to catalog galaxies, nebulae, and clusters with precision and scientific rigor.

## Facilities

- **Primary Instrument:** 12-inch Schmidt-Cassegrain Telescope
- **Wide-field Instrument:** 80mm Apochromatic Refractor
- **Location:** Northern Hemisphere, Remote Site (Bortle 2)

## Research Goals

1.  Continuous monitoring of star-forming regions.
2.  High-resolution imaging of local group galaxies.
3.  Spectroscopic analysis of variable star systems.

Observations are recorded in UTC and cataloged for long-term study.
