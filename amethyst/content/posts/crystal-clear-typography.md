Typography in a visually bold theme is a balancing act. You want headings that dazzle, but body text that remains effortlessly readable even during long reading sessions.

## The Dual-Font Strategy

This theme employs two typefaces that complement each other:

- **Playfair Display** for headings — a high-contrast serif with dramatic thick-thin strokes that evoke luxury and elegance
- **Inter** for body text — a clean, highly legible sans-serif optimized for screen reading

The contrast between ornate headings and clean body text creates visual hierarchy while maintaining comfort.

## Glow with Purpose

Not every heading needs to glow. We reserve the strongest glow effects for:

1. **Page titles** — the first thing you see
2. **Home page branding** — setting the mood
3. **404 pages** — turning errors into experiences

Secondary headings use subtler effects — a faint violet text-shadow that adds depth without competing for attention.

## Size and Spacing

Generous line-height (`1.75`) and careful letter-spacing give the text room to breathe. On dark backgrounds, this extra whitespace is essential — text that's too tightly packed becomes harder to read against deep colors.

The result: text that feels as polished as the gemstone that inspired it.
