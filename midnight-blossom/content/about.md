# About

**Midnight Blossom** is a concept inspired by the contrast between organic life and digital technology. It visualizes a future where nature has adapted to thrive in a neon-lit, cyberpunk world.

### The Aesthetic

The color palette features a deep void-like dark background (`#08040a`) punctuated by vibrant accents:

- **Neon Pink**: Represents blooming flowers in the dark.
- **Electric Purple**: Represents the twilight sky and digital energy.
- **Cyan**: Represents the flowing data streams.

The interface elements use a frosted glass effect (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`) that mimics looking through a misty, rain-streaked window in a futuristic metropolis.
