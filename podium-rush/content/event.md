## Venue

The Stade Olympique de la Victoire is a 82,000-seat purpose-built athletics stadium with a nine-lane Mondo track and permanent photo-finish camera array at the finish line. All eight sprint lanes are calibrated to 0.001 metre tolerance; false-start reaction times are measured via pressure sensors beneath each starting block.

## Getting there

Public transport is the recommended option: Metro Line 7 stops at Stade Olympique, 200 metres from Gate A. Shuttle services from the city centre run every ten minutes from 15:30. Paid parking is limited and sells out by 17:00.

## Spectator notes

Cameras without interchangeable lenses are permitted in all seating areas. Professional rigs require a press pass. Drinks stands are cashless. Hearing-assistance loops are active in sections 104, 212, and 318.
