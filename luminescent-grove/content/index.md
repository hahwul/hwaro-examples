Welcome to the **Luminescent Grove**, an enchanted space where the neon flora thrives in the deep dark. This template showcases the power of Hwaro combined with modern CSS techniques like glassmorphism and subtle glowing animations.

Wander through the glowing pathways, discover hidden bioluminescent flora, and build your digital garden in the dark.

## Features

- **Dark Mode by Default**: Deep slate and obsidian backgrounds provide the perfect canvas.
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted, translucent panels (`backdrop-filter: blur()`) blend with the background seamlessly.
- **Glowing Accents**: Cybernetic cyan and neon green typography to illuminate the path.
- **Ambient Animations**: Subtle pulsating glows mimic a breathing, living ecosystem.
