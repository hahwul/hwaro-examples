Welcome to Velvet Nebula. This theme captures the majestic essence of deep space phenomena, wrapping your content in glowing atmospheric gradients and frosted glass elements.

### The Aesthetic
Velvet Nebula revolves around deep purples (`#080315`), fuchsia, and blue animated glows that gently float behind translucent UI elements.

- **Frosted Glass Panels:** Soft, blurred backgrounds to give depth.
- **Dynamic Glows:** Animated CSS radial gradients simulate distant nebulae.
- **Refined Typography:** High contrast, crisp sans-serif fonts (`Outfit` and `Inter`).

> "In the vastness of the digital void, content should shine like stars against the dark."

### Features Included
* Fully responsive layout.
* Custom shortcodes for enhanced content.
* Semantic HTML5 markup.

Feel free to explore the code, tweak the colors in `:root`, and make it your own!
