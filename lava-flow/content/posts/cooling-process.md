Eventually, everything cools. The bright yellows fade to deep oranges, then to dull reds, and finally to impenetrable black rock.

But beneath the surface, the heat remains.

1. **Eruption:** Violent, bright, fast.
2. **Flow:** Steady, glowing, destructive.
3. **Cooling:** Slow, dark, silent.
