## Ink Seoul

Ink Seoul is a monochrome blog theme that strips design down to its most essential elements: black ink on white paper. Inspired by the editorial culture of Seoul and the tradition of Korean brush calligraphy, this theme proves that color is optional when typography and space are handled with care.

### Design Philosophy

Every element exists in black, white, or the grays between. This constraint is not a limitation — it is a discipline. Without color to create hierarchy, the design relies entirely on weight, size, spacing, and contrast. The result is a reading experience that feels focused and unhurried.

### The Palette

| Tone | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| White | #FFFFFF | Primary background |
| Paper | #F7F7F7 | Alternate surface |
| Mist | #F0F0F0 | Code background |
| Silver | #E0E0E0 | Borders |
| Gray | #777777 | Muted text |
| Charcoal | #333333 | Secondary text |
| Ink | #111111 | Primary text |
| Black | #000000 | Accents and emphasis |

### Typography

- **Cormorant Garamond** for expressive serif headings
- **Inter** for clean, modern body text
- **Noto Serif KR** for Korean typographic accents

### Features

- Pure black and white design — no color
- Strong typographic hierarchy
- Post cards with monochrome hover effects
- Generous white space throughout
- Responsive from mobile to desktop
- Print-friendly by nature
