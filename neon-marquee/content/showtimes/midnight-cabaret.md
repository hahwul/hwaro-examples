## Program Card

A rotating Saturday late-night program: torch singer (Gladys Hyle), card magician (Teo Patrone), and ventriloquist (Marguerite Sands), separated by reel changes. Houselights at half for the live acts, full dark for the film.

| Day | Late Show |

## Ticket Note

Midnight Cabaret tickets are sold only at the box office window and only after 10 PM on Saturday evenings. Cash only after 10 PM. Two drinks per patron.

## House Notes

- Flash photography is not permitted during the live acts.
- Late seating after the first short is not available; please arrive by 11:40.
- Patrons under 18 are not admitted to this program.
