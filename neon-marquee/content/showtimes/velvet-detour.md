## Program Card

The Orpheum is proud to present the second-run engagement of *The Velvet Detour*, director Marlene Kocsis's first full-length feature since her 1981 short *Rotary Station*. Shot in 35 mm Eastmancolor on location in the California delta and on a diner set built inside an aircraft hangar.
