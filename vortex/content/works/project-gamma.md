A physical installation consisting of twelve suspended rotating panels, each casting patterned light onto the surrounding walls. As the panels turn at varying speeds, the projected patterns create interference and overlap.

Visitors stand at the center of the vortex, surrounded by slowly shifting geometries of light and shadow.
