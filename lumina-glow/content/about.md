# The Philosophy

Lumina Glow was born out of a desire for balance. We wanted to create a space that felt both cutting-edge and serene. By combining the vastness of a dark theme with the focused energy of neon accents, we've crafted an environment that feels alive but not overwhelming.

### The Elements

* **The Void (`#0b0c10`):** Provides a canvas of infinite depth.
* **The Surface (`#1f2833`):** Defines structure and hierarchy through subtle elevation.
* **The Spark (`#66fcf1`):** Guides the eye and adds a touch of digital magic.
