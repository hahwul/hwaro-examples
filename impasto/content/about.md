## About This Site

Impasto is a blog theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) inspired by the impasto painting technique -- where thick layers of paint are applied boldly to the canvas, creating visible texture and dimension.

## Design Philosophy

Every element on this site carries visual weight. Borders are thick and deliberate. Shadows are deep and unapologetic. Typography is heavy and confident. The palette draws from raw pigments: cadmium, ochre, ivory, and charcoal.

This is not a theme that whispers. It asserts itself on the page, just as impasto brushstrokes stand proud on the canvas.

### Features

- Thick, textured borders that evoke layered paint
- Deep box shadows for a sense of relief and dimension
- Bold serif typography with strong visual hierarchy
- Warm palette inspired by oil pigments on canvas
- Clean layout with generous whitespace
- Responsive design across all devices
- Syntax highlighting with Monokai theme
