## About This Playlist

There is a specific quality to Sunday mornings that this playlist tries to capture. Not the productive kind where you are already planning the week -- the slow kind, where the coffee is still too hot to drink and the light is coming through the blinds at a low angle.

The selection leans heavily on Bon Iver because nobody does quiet devastation better. But the sequencing balances those heavier emotional moments with warmer, more hopeful tracks from Hozier and Vance Joy.

> The best acoustic music sounds like it was recorded in the next room.

## Listening Notes

- Iron & Wine's nine-minute closer is non-negotiable -- let it play all the way through
- The Bon Iver cluster in the middle is intentional; it creates a meditative pocket
- This playlist works equally well in autumn and early spring
- Keep the volume low; these songs breathe better when they are not competing with anything
