# About Obelisk

Obelisk is a vertical narrative experience. It is a single, continuous scroll from foundation to summit, designed to evoke the sensation of standing at the base of a monumental stone structure and slowly raising your eyes toward the sky.

## Design Philosophy

Monuments endure because they commit to a single gesture. The obelisk is tall and narrow. It does not sprawl. It does not compromise. It simply rises.

This site follows the same principle. One page. One direction. One story that builds from bedrock to capstone.

## The Aesthetic

The visual language draws from ancient Egyptian architecture and desert landscapes:

- **Stone textures** simulated through subtle shadows and warm earth tones
- **Hieroglyphic borders** as decorative elements between sections
- **Monumental typography** that commands the vertical space
- **Narrow layout** that reinforces the upward trajectory
