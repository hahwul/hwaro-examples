## Opening night

Chinese Theatre, Hollywood, Friday 18 April, 19:00. Carpet open 17:30. Black tie. Dir. Vikram Saldana in attendance with principal cast.

## Synopsis

On the last daylight of a dying star system, a xenoarchaeologist walks into the shadow of her own translated language. The Nord Pictures seven-picture deal closes here, with what is widely expected to be the studio&rsquo;s final entry in the *Eclipse* sequence that began with *First Dusk* in 2019.

## Release pattern

Wide release across North America on the opening Friday, with a seven-day staggered international roll-out beginning Monday. The picture does not have a festival premiere and was first shown to the trade at the April industry screenings.

## Running time

148 minutes, no intermission, single reel. The studio has asked projection houses not to pause between the third and fourth acts.
