# About the Forge

Molten is a digital forge where craftsmanship meets creativity. Here, we document the journey of transforming raw materials into finished works through heat, force, and artistry.

## Our Philosophy

Like molten metal flowing from the crucible, ideas here take fluid forms before solidifying into lasting creations. We believe in:

- **Heat** - The passion that drives creation
- **Force** - The discipline to shape raw ideas
- **Patience** - Allowing work to cool and settle
- **Craft** - Honoring traditional techniques while embracing innovation

## The Process

Every project starts with fire. Whether it's heating steel to forge a blade, melting bronze for casting, or tempering tools for durability—fire is the catalyst that makes transformation possible.

In this space, we share:

- Forging techniques and tutorials
- Project build logs from start to finish
- Tool reviews and workshop setup guides
- The philosophy of craft and making

## Join the Community

Follow along as we document builds, experiment with techniques, and explore the endless possibilities that emerge when metal meets flame.
