Welcome to Titanium.

This example showcases a bold, eye-catching design with a hi-tech metallic finish, brushed textures, and precision engineering. Designed to provide maximum visual impact without excessive use of gradients.

## Features

* Elegant color palette based on slate and titanium silver.
* Clean and highly readable typography using Inter and JetBrains Mono.
* Perfect for high-end portfolios, product showcases, and cutting-edge tech blogs.
