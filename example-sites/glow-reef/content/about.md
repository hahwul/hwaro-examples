The Glow Reef theme is designed for those who appreciate high contrast, modern typography, and a deep-sea aesthetic.

## Features

- High contrast text for maximum legibility in low-light environments.
- Elegant, solid neon accent colors (cyan and magenta).
- Strict adherence to minimal and clean design principles.
- Completely emoji-free content and interface.
