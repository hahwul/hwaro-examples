# About the Design

The **Glamorous Fireworks** theme is built on a foundation of elegance and visual spectacle.

## The Philosophy

We believe in maximum visual impact balanced with absolute usability. By stripping away heavy gradients and distracting elements, we focus entirely on the purity of light against dark. The soft glow of golden particles creates a sense of luxury and dynamism.

- Focus on deep contrasts.
- Embrace subtle, glowing animations.
- Maintain typographic clarity amidst visual splendor.

*Created for those who appreciate the finer details in the night.*
