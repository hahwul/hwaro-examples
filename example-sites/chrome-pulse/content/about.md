## The Vision

Chrome Pulse is a demonstration of elegant restraint. In a digital landscape often cluttered with noise, this design chooses to speak quietly but with absolute precision.

The color palette is strictly limited to grayscale tones, mimicking the properties of titanium, steel, and brushed aluminum. The rhythmic pulsing effect serves as a heartbeat, confirming the digital presence without demanding constant attention.
