Welcome to the **Photon** experiment. This site is a showcase of glamorous, trendy, yet fundamentally elegant design principles inspired by light particle scattering and quantum energy.

We focus on the interplay of deep shadows and precise, vibrant light accents.

* **Dynamic glow effects**
* **Animated particle systems**
* **Refined typography**

The design completely avoids traditional gradients and emojis to maintain a pristine, sophisticated aesthetic.
