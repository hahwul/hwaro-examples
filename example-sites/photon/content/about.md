## The Philosophy

The Photon aesthetic is born from the desire to create maximum visual impact through high contrast. It is not about overwhelming the user with noise, but rather guiding their attention with carefully placed light in an otherwise void-like environment.

We believe in:
1.  **Stark Contrast:** Utilizing deep blacks against vibrant, luminescent accents.
2.  **Subtle Motion:** Employing gentle particle animations that suggest life and energy without distraction.
3.  **Restraint:** Removing unnecessary elements, specifically flat gradients and emojis, to ensure the design remains timeless and elegant.
