In an era previously dominated by contouring and elaborate routines, the tide has definitively turned towards minimalism. The new beauty standard is not about masking, but about revealing.

The focus has shifted entirely to skincare. A luminous, healthy complexion is the ultimate canvas. Makeup is now used sparingly—a touch of sheer tint, a groomed brow, and perhaps a subtle balm. It's a look that whispers confidence.

This approach is not merely an aesthetic choice; it's a lifestyle shift towards authenticity and self-acceptance.
