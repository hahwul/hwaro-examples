# About Apolo

Apolo is a conceptual dark mode theme focusing on elegance, utilizing subtle luminescent effects.

It's designed as an example for the [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) static site generator.

> "Elegance is not about being noticed, it's about being remembered."
> — Giorgio Armani
