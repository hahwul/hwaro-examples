Welcome to Riverbank, a nature journal dedicated to recording the quiet life along the river. Here you will find observations of birds, seasonal water changes, and the flora and fauna that make the riverside a place of endless wonder.

Each entry is a dated field note -- a record of what was seen, heard, and felt at the water's edge.

## Recent Observations

Browse the [journal entries](/posts/) to read through observations spanning the seasons. From the first kingfisher sighting of spring to the quiet freeze of midwinter, these notes aim to capture the rhythms of life by the river.
