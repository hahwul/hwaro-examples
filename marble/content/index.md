# Welcome to Marble

A curated space for fine art, sculpture, and the discerning collector. We present exhibitions, artist profiles, and insights from the international art market.

---

## Current Features

Explore our latest [journal entries]({{ base_url }}/posts/) for exhibition reviews, sculptor profiles, and market analysis. Browse by [subject]({{ base_url }}/tags/) to find works that resonate with your interests.

## About the Gallery

Marble was established to bridge the gap between the art world's inner circles and those who wish to enter them. Every piece we feature, every artist we profile, and every event we cover reflects our commitment to excellence and authenticity in the visual arts.
