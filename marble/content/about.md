# About Marble

Marble is an independent art publication dedicated to the presentation and discussion of fine art, sculpture, and luxury collections. Our editorial perspective draws from decades of experience in gallery curation, art history, and the international auction market.

## Our Philosophy

We believe that great art transcends trends. Our coverage focuses on craftsmanship, material integrity, and the enduring dialogue between artist and medium. From Carrara marble sculptures to contemporary mixed-media installations, we seek work that rewards sustained attention.

## The Editorial Team

Our contributors include gallery directors, art historians, independent curators, and practicing artists. Each brings a distinct perspective, united by a shared commitment to thoughtful, informed writing about the visual arts.

## Contact

For exhibition inquiries, editorial submissions, or partnership discussions, please reach out through our standard channels. We welcome correspondence from collectors, artists, and institutions alike.
