# About Bureau

Bureau is the internal policy and governance documentation system maintained by the Office of Governance and Compliance (OGC). It was established in 2021 to consolidate all organizational policy documents into a single, version-controlled, searchable portal.

## Mission

To ensure that every member of the organization has timely access to accurate, up-to-date policy documentation, and that all governance processes are transparent and well-documented.

## Governing Authority

The Governance Committee, chaired by the Chief Compliance Officer, holds ultimate authority over all documents published on this portal. Amendments require a formal review and approval process as described in the [Review Process](../docs/governance/review-process/) documentation.

## Contact

For questions regarding specific policies, contact the designated Policy Owner listed on each document. For general inquiries about this portal, contact the OGC at governance@example.org.
