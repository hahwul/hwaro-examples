# Document Types and Classifications

**Document ID:** GS-002 | **Effective Date:** 2025-01-15 | **Owner:** Office of Governance and Compliance

---

## Article 1. Document Categories

### Section 1.1 Policies

High-level statements of organizational intent. Policies define what the organization requires or prohibits. Approved by the Governance Committee; binding on all personnel. Review cycle: Annual.

### Section 1.2 Standards

Minimum acceptable criteria for implementing a policy. Standards provide measurable benchmarks for compliance assessment. Approved by Policy Owner with Governance Committee endorsement. Review cycle: Annual.

### Section 1.3 Procedures

Step-by-step instructions for carrying out tasks in accordance with policies and standards. Approved by Department Head. Review cycle: Semi-annual.

### Section 1.4 Guidelines

Recommended approaches and best practices. Advisory only; no binding authority. Approved by Policy Owner. Review cycle: As needed.

## Article 2. Classification Levels

| Level | Label | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Public | May be shared externally without restriction |
| 2 | Internal | Restricted to employees and authorized contractors |
| 3 | Confidential | Limited to named individuals or specific roles |
| 4 | Restricted | Board-level or legal review only |

### Section 2.1 Handling Requirements

- **Public** -- No special handling required
- **Internal** -- Shall not be posted on public-facing systems
- **Confidential** -- Encrypted at rest and in transit; access logged
- **Restricted** -- Secure repositories; dual-authorization for access

## Article 3. Document Lifecycle

Documents progress through these states:

1. **Draft** -- Under development; not yet binding
2. **Under Review** -- Submitted for formal review and comment
3. **Approved** -- Ratified; binding as of effective date
4. **Superseded** -- Replaced by a newer version; retained for reference
5. **Retired** -- No longer applicable; archived
