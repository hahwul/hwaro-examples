# Review Process

**Document ID:** GOV-002 | **Effective Date:** 2025-06-15 | **Owner:** Chief Compliance Officer

**Classification:** Internal | **Review Cycle:** Annual | **Next Review:** 2026-06-15

---

## Article 1. Purpose

This document defines the formal process for managing the lifecycle of policy documents. All policies, standards, and procedures shall follow this process.

## Article 2. Document Creation

A new policy may be initiated by the Governance Committee, a Policy Owner, or a Department Head. The draft shall include:

1. Document ID assigned by the OGC
2. Complete article and section structure
3. RACI matrix for key activities
4. Stakeholder review period of no less than 15 business days

## Article 3. Review Workflow

```
  Draft -> OGC Format Review -> Stakeholder Comment (15 days)
       -> Policy Owner Resolves -> Governance Committee Vote
            |                          |
         Approved                   Rejected
         Publish & Notify           Return to Draft
```

The Governance Committee evaluates each document for strategic alignment, regulatory compliance, implementation feasibility, and consistency with existing policies.

## Article 4. Amendment Process

**Minor amendments** (typographical, formatting, clarifications) may be approved by the Policy Owner with CCO endorsement. **Major amendments** (new requirements, scope changes) shall follow the full review workflow.

## Article 5. Periodic Review Schedule

| Document Type | Review Cycle | Responsible Party |
|---|---|---|
| Policies | Annual | Policy Owner |
| Standards | Annual | Policy Owner |
| Procedures | Semi-annual | Department Head |
| Guidelines | As needed | Policy Owner |

Review outcomes: **Reaffirmed** (no changes), **Amended** (changes processed), or **Retired** (archived).

## Article 6. Document Retirement

1. Policy Owner submits retirement request with justification
2. OGC assesses impact and dependent documents
3. Governance Committee approves retirement
4. Document archived; all personnel notified
