The hexagon is nature's most efficient shape. It allows for the tightest packing of cells, minimizing wasted space while maximizing structural integrity.

In this layout, we aim to reflect that same efficiency. The grid systems align, the borders define clear boundaries, and the negative space acts as the mortar holding the components together.

> "The universe is built on a plan the profound symmetry of which is somehow present in the inner structure of our intellect."

### Observations
* Sharp corners define intent.
* High contrast ensures readability.
* Vector graphics provide infinite scalability.
