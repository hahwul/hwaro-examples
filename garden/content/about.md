## What is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is a different approach to publishing on the web. Rather than presenting polished, chronological articles, a garden is a collection of evolving ideas at various stages of development. Notes link to one another, forming a web of interconnected thoughts.

## Growth Stages

Each note is marked with a growth stage to set expectations:

- **Seedling** -- Early ideas, rough drafts, and initial explorations. These may be incomplete or change significantly.
- **Budding** -- Notes that have developed some structure and substance. The core idea is taking shape but may still evolve.
- **Evergreen** -- Well-developed, stable notes. These represent mature thinking and are regularly maintained.

## How to Navigate

There is no right way to explore a garden. You can:

- Start with [recently updated notes](/notes/) on the homepage
- Browse by [topic](/tags/) to find related ideas
- Follow the links between notes to discover connections

## About Hwaro

This garden is built with [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com), a fast static site generator written in Crystal. The garden theme is designed to support the Zettelkasten method of note-taking and knowledge management.
