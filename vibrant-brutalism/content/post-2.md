Modern web design has become sterile. Everything is rounded corners, soft shadows, and pastel gradients. Where did the *edge* go?

Vibrant Brutalism answers this question by turning the dial to 11.

### Structural Honesty

Like the Brutalist architecture movement of the 1950s, we believe in **structural honesty**. 

1. We use thick, solid borders.
2. We employ harsh drop shadows without blur.
3. We utilize colors that command attention, not colors that blend in.

We are redefining user experience by creating a *user encounter*.
