## After You RSVP

You will receive an email confirmation within twenty-four hours. The confirmation includes the precise door location, the arrival instructions, and a unique QR code which is checked against the list at the door.

If you do not receive the confirmation within twenty-four hours, check your spam folder before writing to us - the confirmations are sent from an unusual domain and are sometimes mistakenly filtered.

## Cancellation

If your plans change, please reply to the confirmation email to release your place. We do not over-list and every released spot becomes available to someone on the waiting list. It takes thirty seconds to release and is much appreciated.

## The Waiting List

Once the guest list is full, the RSVP form above will change to a waiting list registration. Waiting list members are notified in order as releases come in, up to 20:00 on the Saturday of the session.

## Door Admission (Not Guest List)

If you are not on the list and wish to come by on spec, arrive between 00:00 and 01:30. After 01:30 the door team focuses on readmissions of dancers returning from outside, and new admissions are paused. Door admission is ten pounds cash or card; guest list admission is free.
