## On the Close

The closing slot is the emotional counterweight to the headline. Oron takes the floor at 138 bpm from Dex and brings it, across the hour, down to something nearer 114 by the closing record. The brief is not to empty the floor but to prepare it for release; the last ten minutes are typically a single long Italo disco record or a cosmic instrumental, played uninterrupted to the final fade.

## A Short Biography

Oron Blake operates between Manchester and Athens, with residencies at both the Reserve and at the Romantso rooftop in Athens. His selections draw heavily upon the Italian 12-inch disco catalogues of 1979-1985, the Greek demo disco of the same period, and the cosmic instrumentals of the early 1980s (Fontana, Baldelli, Mozart).

The closing set has been Oron's residency at the Reserve since 2020, with only three sessions missed (all for the Romantso programme in Athens).

## A Note from the Resident

> "The last record of the session is not the loudest. It is the one everyone goes home humming. Choose it carefully. Play it unhurriedly. Let the room finish at its own tempo."

The session's traditional closing record is a different one for every session and is never announced in advance. Regulars have catalogued them and shared the list among themselves. The present session's close will be the one hundred and eighty-third in the series.
