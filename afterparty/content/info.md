## Where

The Reserve, Arch 42, beneath the old Bethnal Green viaduct. The door is on the right-hand side as you approach from the south; look for the small blue light above the gate and ignore the larger yellow light above the gate to the left, which is the delivery entrance for the adjacent bakery.

The nearest overground station is Cambridge Heath (three minutes on foot). Night buses 26, 55, and 242 pass the end of the street every fifteen minutes through the small hours. A small car park is available at the far end of the viaduct for those who must drive, though the Reserve strongly prefers that you do not.

## When

Doors at 00:00. Last admission 02:30. Last round 05:30. Lights up at 06:00. The programme is a single uninterrupted session from doors to lights - there are no further sets after 06:00 and no afterparty to the afterparty.

## Access

Step-free access throughout. The Ballroom and Annex have a single shallow step which is ramped by a temporary platform during the session. The Garden is entirely level. Accessible toilets are available in the entrance lobby and in the Garden. Please inform us at RSVP if you require any particular accommodations and we will ensure they are ready for you at the door.

The sound is loud. The principal rooms are calibrated to 100 dB average at the centre of the floor, with peaks around 108 dB. Foam earplugs are available at the door at no charge; musician-grade filtered plugs are available at the cloakroom for two pounds (refundable on return).

## Who

The session is 18+, proof of age required at the door. We check IDs randomly and often; if you look anywhere near the line, bring your passport or driving licence.

The door team operates a considered admission policy. Guest list takes priority; beyond guest list, the door team reserves the right to exercise judgement, which they will exercise fairly and which will not be explained to you at length if the judgement is against admission.

## Conduct

**No phones on the floor.** Cameras up in the corridors and in the Garden are fine; on any of the four dancefloors, please keep the phone in your pocket.

**Respect the staff.** Bar, door, cloakroom, and security are working a long shift. They are not obstacles to your night; they are the people who make your night possible.

**Look after each other.** If someone is in difficulty, flag the nearest member of staff. Every member of staff carries a torch and has been trained in basic first aid. The venue's welfare team is present from 01:00 to 06:00 and is identifiable by a pink armband.

**Drugs and the door policy.** We will not repeat the law at you. We will say that the door team will turn away anyone who arrives visibly impaired, and the welfare team will gently help anyone who becomes so during the session. If you are concerned about yourself or a friend, the welfare team's office is in the Garden; walking over is easier than you expect.

## Cloakroom

Coats, bags, and valuables at the cloakroom in the entrance lobby. Two pounds per item; receipts issued upon deposit and required upon collection. The cloakroom stays open until 06:15 to allow for unhurried departure.

## Weather

The Garden is covered but not heated beyond the portable heaters in the corner. If the forecast is below eight degrees, bring a jumper; if rain, the cover is adequate but not waterproof against sideways rain.
