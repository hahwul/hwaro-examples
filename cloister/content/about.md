Cloister is a contemplative space dedicated to the art of slow, reflective writing. Inspired by the monastic traditions of silence, discipline, and devotion, this site gathers meditations on the inner life and the architecture of stillness.

Here you will find essays on silence, ritual, walking meditation, and the quiet rhythms that sustain a life of attention. Each piece is written in the spirit of the cloister garden -- a place where the practical and the sacred meet.
