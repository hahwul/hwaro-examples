# Obsidian Statement Ring

Born from volcanic fire and shaped by master lapidaries, this statement ring features a cabochon-cut obsidian of extraordinary depth. Set in blackened platinum, it is a piece that commands attention without raising its voice.

## Specifications

- **Primary Stone:** Natural obsidian cabochon, 14mm x 10mm
- **Metal:** 950 platinum, blackened rhodium finish
- **Band Width:** 6mm, tapering to 4mm
- **Weight:** 18.2 grams
- **Setting:** Bezel-set with micro-pave black diamond accents

## The Stone

Obsidian is volcanic glass, formed when molten lava cools so rapidly that mineral crystals cannot form. The result is a stone of absolute darkness -- a void that absorbs light and returns only a polished, mirror-like surface. Our obsidian is sourced from a single deposit in the highlands of Mexico, where the volcanic conditions produce stones of unmatched purity.

## Design Philosophy

The ring's design is a meditation on contrast. The organic, flowing form of the obsidian cabochon sits within a precisely engineered platinum bezel, its curves meeting the angular facets of the band in a dialogue between nature and human ingenuity. Forty black diamonds, each hand-selected and set by a master jeweler, trace a subtle constellation along the shoulders of the band.

## The Finish

The blackened rhodium finish on the platinum creates an almost seamless transition between metal and stone, as if the obsidian were emerging from the ring itself. Over time, the high points of the band will develop a natural patina, revealing glimpses of the white platinum beneath -- a living finish that evolves with its wearer.
