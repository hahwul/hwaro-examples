# Rose Gold Pendant

Suspended from a delicate chain of 18-karat rose gold, this pendant captures the soft warmth of a winter sunrise. At its heart, a cushion-cut morganite radiates a blush pink light that complements the copper tones of the rose gold setting.

## Specifications

- **Center Stone:** Cushion-cut morganite, 3.8 carats, eye-clean
- **Metal:** 18-karat rose gold
- **Chain:** 18-inch cable chain, 1.2mm width
- **Pendant Dimensions:** 16mm x 12mm
- **Clasp:** Lobster claw with micro logo engraving

## The Morganite

Our morganite is sourced from the gem-bearing pegmatites of Minas Gerais, Brazil. This particular stone displays a warm peach-pink hue that shifts subtly in different lighting -- cooler and more lilac under daylight, warmer and more salmon by candlelight. It has been precision-cut with 57 facets to maximize its natural brilliance and fire.

## Setting Design

The pendant setting is a contemporary interpretation of the classic halo design. Rather than surrounding the center stone with a ring of smaller gems, we have created an open framework of rose gold that traces the outline of the morganite, allowing light to enter from all angles. The result is a stone that appears to float, held in place by nothing more than a whisper of gold.

## The Chain

The accompanying chain is not an afterthought. Each link is individually soldered and inspected, resulting in a chain that drapes with a liquid smoothness rarely found in production jewelry. The lobster clasp features a discreet engraving of our mark -- visible only to those who know to look for it.

## Rose Gold

Our rose gold alloy is formulated in-house, combining pure gold with a precise measure of copper and silver to achieve a warm, blush tone that avoids the overly red appearance of lower-karat rose golds. The result is a metal with the depth of gold and the warmth of a setting sun.
