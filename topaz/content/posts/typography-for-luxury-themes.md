Typography makes or breaks a luxury design. The right typeface pairing can elevate a simple layout into something truly special.

## Serif for Headings

Display serifs carry the weight and character that luxury themes demand. Look for typefaces with:

- High stroke contrast
- Elegant terminals and swashes
- Strong vertical stress

Fonts like Playfair Display, Cinzel, and DM Serif Display all work beautifully with golden color palettes.

## Clean Body Text

Pair ornate headings with readable body text. The contrast between decorative and functional creates visual hierarchy:

- **Lora** — Balanced serif with good screen readability
- **Source Serif Pro** — Designed specifically for digital reading
- **Inter** — A modern sans-serif alternative for longer passages

## Scale and Spacing

Luxury typography needs room to breathe:

- Use generous line heights (1.7–1.8 for body text)
- Increase letter spacing slightly on uppercase text
- Give headings ample margin above and below
- Let whitespace do the heavy lifting

The goal is to create a reading experience that feels unhurried and refined.
