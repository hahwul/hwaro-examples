On-call rotations are necessary but often poorly managed. Priya Sharma shares frameworks for building on-call practices that actually work without burning out your team.

## Topics Covered

- Defining clear escalation paths and runbooks
- Compensation models for on-call work
- Alert fatigue and how to fight it systematically
- Post-incident reviews that lead to real changes
- Balancing reliability goals with team well-being
