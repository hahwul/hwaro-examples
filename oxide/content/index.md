# Welcome to Oxide

An industrial photography gallery documenting the beauty of decay—rust textures, patina surfaces, and the slow transformation of metal through oxidation and time.

---

## Featured Works

Explore our [gallery]({{ base_url }}/posts/) showcasing abandoned factories, corroded machinery, and the rich textures of weathered industrial surfaces. Each photograph captures the aesthetic of material degradation and the passage of time.

## Philosophy

Time is the ultimate sculptor. In abandoned warehouses and forgotten industrial sites, we find a raw beauty that emerges as metals oxidize, surfaces corrode, and human-made structures slowly return to the earth. Oxide documents this process—celebrating the rust-orange of iron oxide, the blue-green of copper patina, and the rich browns of surface corrosion.

### What We Document

- **Rust Textures**: The orange and brown patterns of iron oxidation
- **Patina Surfaces**: Green and blue copper oxidation on aged metal
- **Industrial Decay**: Abandoned factories, machinery, and infrastructure
- **Urban Archaeology**: Finding beauty in forgotten industrial spaces
