## About Laser Show

Laser Show is a glamorous and trendy theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). It draws inspiration from concert laser displays — vivid beams cutting through fog, bold color palettes, and the electric energy of a live stage.

### Design Philosophy

- **Visual spectacle** — Animated laser beams, glowing typography, and gradient-rich surfaces
- **Dark foundation** — Deep purple-black backgrounds let the colors pop
- **Readability first** — Despite the flashy effects, content remains clear and accessible
- **Responsive** — Looks great from phone screens to ultra-wide monitors

### Built With

Pure HTML, CSS, and Jinja2 templates. No JavaScript frameworks required — all animations use CSS keyframes and transitions.
