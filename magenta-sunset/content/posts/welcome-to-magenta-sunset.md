Welcome to Magenta Sunset — a theme where the sky burns with impossible color and every pixel radiates warmth.

## The Vision

Magenta Sunset captures the most dramatic moment of twilight: that breathtaking instant when deep magenta bleeds into molten orange, and the horizon catches fire. This theme brings that spectacle to your screen.

Every heading glows with sunset warmth. Every element is layered with the rich depth of a twilight sky. The dark backdrop lets each fiery accent burn bright.

## Design Principles

1. **Maximum Visual Impact** — Bold gradients, rich warm colors, and dramatic contrasts
2. **Twilight Canvas** — Deep backgrounds that evoke the night sky moments after sunset
3. **Fiery Warmth** — Colors that radiate heat and energy from every element
4. **Glamorous Readability** — Spectacular visuals that never sacrifice content clarity

## What Powers the Glow

All visual effects are achieved through pure CSS:

- `text-shadow` creates the warm glow on headings
- `background: linear-gradient()` drives the horizon color transitions
- `box-shadow` adds depth and warmth to cards
- CSS custom properties make the sunset palette easy to customize
- `@keyframes` bring the sunset shimmer to life
