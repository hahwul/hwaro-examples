# About Obsidian Fractal

Obsidian Fractal was crafted for those who prefer their content framed in darkness, allowing the words and subtle design elements to glow. It is perfect for personal portfolios, tech blogs, and creative writing where atmosphere is paramount.

## Design Philosophy

The core philosophy is built around a few central ideas:
1.  **High Contrast**: Light text on dark backgrounds ensures readability.
2.  **Subtle Depth**: The use of radial gradients simulates a faint light source in the distance.
3.  **Neon Flourishes**: Interactive elements pop with bright colors.
