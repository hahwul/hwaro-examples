# Welcome to the Tessellation Gallery

This gallery explores the mesmerizing world of tessellations, inspired by the masterful works of M.C. Escher. Here you'll find seamless tiling patterns, impossible geometries, and interlocking forms that challenge perception.

## The Art of Tessellation

A tessellation is a repeating pattern of shapes that fit together perfectly without gaps or overlaps. M.C. Escher elevated this mathematical concept into high art, creating intricate designs where lizards, birds, and fish metamorphose into one another across infinite planes.

> "We adore chaos because we love to produce order." — M.C. Escher

## Featured Patterns

Browse our collection of tessellation artworks, each demonstrating unique geometric transformations and the interplay between positive and negative space. From simple hexagonal grids to complex metamorphic sequences, discover the infinite possibilities of pattern art.
