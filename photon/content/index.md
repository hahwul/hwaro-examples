## The Nature of Photons

In physics, a photon is a bundle of electromagnetic energy. It is the basic unit that makes up all light. By manipulating color, shadow, and space, this design attempts to capture the fleeting, glowing essence of these particles.

* **Speed:** Light travels at 299,792 kilometers per second in a vacuum.
* **Mass:** Photons have zero rest mass, allowing them to travel at the speed of light.
* **Wave-Particle Duality:** They exhibit properties of both waves and particles, a fundamental concept of quantum mechanics.

### Energy Dispersal

When light scatters, it illuminates the void. The subtle glows across this interface mimic the scattering of photons as they interact with matter, creating focal points of intense energy that gently fade into the surrounding darkness.

```python
def calculate_photon_energy(frequency):
    """
    Calculates the energy of a photon given its frequency.
    E = h * f
    """
    planck_constant = 6.62607015e-34 # J*Hz^-1
    energy = planck_constant * frequency
    return energy

# Energy of blue light (approx 6.66e14 Hz)
blue_energy = calculate_photon_energy(6.66e14)
print(f"Energy: {blue_energy} Joules")
```
