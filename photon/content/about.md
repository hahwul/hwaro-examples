## The Design Philosophy

The design of **Photon** is rooted in the concepts of deep space and luminescent phenomena. By embracing a dark canvas, we allow accents of cyan, magenta, and purple to represent points of high energy.

### Elegance Through Restraint

Rather than overwhelming the senses with continuous gradients, Photon utilizes targeted `box-shadow` effects and semi-transparent radial backgrounds to create localized glowing elements. This approach maintains a high level of legibility and elegance while still delivering a striking visual impact.

#### Key Principles

1.  **Dark Void as Canvas:** A profound dark background provides infinite depth.
2.  **Luminescent Accents:** High-contrast neon hues are applied sparingly to interactive elements and focal points.
3.  **Structural Clarity:** Typography remains sharp and structured, contrasting with the soft glows.
4.  **Absence of Clutter:** Emojis and excessive decorations are omitted to maintain an atmosphere of pure, abstract energy.
