## About This Site

This is a space for slow, deliberate writing. In a world of infinite scroll and fleeting attention, this site is built for readers who still believe in the weight of words pressed onto paper.

## The Typewriter Aesthetic

There is something honest about a typewriter. Every keystroke is permanent. Every word is considered. This site channels that spirit through its design:

- **Monospace typography** -- every character occupies the same space, just like a real typewriter
- **Sepia tones** -- the warm color of aged paper, ink, and memory
- **Minimal interface** -- nothing between you and the words
- **Deliberate pacing** -- whitespace invites you to slow down and read
