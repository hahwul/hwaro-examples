# Code of Conduct

Our Code of Conduct outlines the ethical standards and professional behaviors expected from all employees.

## Core Principles

1.  **Respect:** We treat everyone with dignity and respect.
2.  **Inclusivity:** We foster a diverse and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued.
3.  **Integrity:** We act honestly and ethically in all business dealings.

## Zero Tolerance Policy

We have a zero-tolerance policy against any form of:

*   Discrimination
*   Harassment
*   Bullying
*   Retaliation

> **Reporting Concerns:** If you witness or experience any behavior that violates this code, please report it immediately to HR or your manager. You can report anonymously if you prefer.

Your commitment to these principles ensures a positive work environment for everyone.
