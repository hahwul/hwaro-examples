# Remote Work

We offer flexible remote work options to support work-life balance and productivity.

## Eligibility

Most roles are eligible for remote work, but the frequency and specific arrangements depend on your job responsibilities and manager approval.

## Requirements

If you are working remotely, you must have:

*   A dedicated, quiet workspace.
*   A reliable, high-speed internet connection.
*   Availability during core business hours (typically 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM).

> **Communication is Key:** When working remotely, clear and frequent communication with your team is essential. Use our standard messaging and video conferencing tools to stay connected.

## Security

Please adhere to all IT security guidelines when working remotely, including using VPNs and secure networks.
