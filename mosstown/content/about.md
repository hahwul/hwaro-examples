## About Mosstown

Mosstown is a quiet corner where the pace of life slows to something gentler. This blog is a collection of thoughts about tending gardens, baking bread, pressing flowers, and finding beauty in the everyday.

## About This Site

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) using the **Mosstown** theme, a cottagecore-inspired design that feels like stepping into a sun-drenched kitchen in the countryside.

What makes Mosstown feel like home:

- **Handwritten headings** -- Caveat font brings a personal, letter-writing quality
- **Pastel palette** -- soft lavender, mint, peach, and cream tones
- **Card layout** -- rounded corners and gentle shadows, like pinned recipe cards
- **Floral textures** -- subtle patterned backgrounds that echo vintage wallpaper
- **Warm typography** -- Crimson Text for body, easy on the eyes during long reads
- **RSS feed** -- subscribe and read at your own pace
- **Search** -- find articles with client-side search
