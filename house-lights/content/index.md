## Safety notes

The truss is cleared for 480kg SWL when distributed evenly across both hang points. Any single fixture exceeding 12kg must be checked for a secondary safety bond. House electrician retains the master sign-off on the load schedule and will not authorise a cold hang test without a signed rigger's certificate on file.
