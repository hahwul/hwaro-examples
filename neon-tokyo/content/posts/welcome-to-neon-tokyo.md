Welcome to **Neon Tokyo** — where the glow of a thousand signs paints the night sky in electric hues of pink, cyan, and gold.

## 東京のネオンの世界

This theme draws inspiration from the iconic streets of Tokyo after dark. The narrow alleys of **Golden Gai**, the towering screens of **Shibuya Crossing**, and the electric chaos of **Kabukichō** — all distilled into a digital experience.

### What Makes It Glow

Every design choice serves the vision of a neon-drenched cityscape:

- **Deep midnight backgrounds** let the neon colors truly shine
- **Glowing text effects** mimic the warm buzz of gas-filled tubes
- **Gradient overlays** create depth and atmosphere
- **Japanese typography** grounds the design in its cultural inspiration

> 東京の夜は、ネオンの光で満たされている。
> *Tokyo's nights are filled with neon light.*

### The Color of Night

The palette is drawn from real Tokyo signage — the hot pink of entertainment district signs, the cool cyan of vending machines casting light into dark alleys, and the warm gold of traditional lanterns mixing with modern LEDs.
