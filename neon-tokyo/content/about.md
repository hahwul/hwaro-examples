## ネオン東京について

Neon Tokyo is a glamorous, eye-catching theme inspired by the vibrant neon-lit streets of Tokyo's nightlife districts. From the glowing signage of Shinjuku to the electric energy of Akihabara, this theme captures the essence of the cybercity nightscape.

### デザインコンセプト

The design embraces **華麗さ (glamour)** and **大胆さ (boldness)** — prioritizing visual spectacle while maintaining readability. Every element glows with purpose, from the animated neon signs to the gradient-lit cards.

### カラーパレット

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Midnight Black | `#0a0a12` | Background |
| Neon Pink | `#ff2d78` | Primary accent, headings |
| Electric Cyan | `#00f0ff` | Links, highlights |
| Hot Magenta | `#ff00aa` | Hover states, gradients |
| Neon Gold | `#ffdd00` | Special accents |
| Soft White | `#e8e6f0` | Body text |

### 特徴

- **Neon glow effects** — CSS text-shadow and box-shadow create authentic neon signage
- **Japanese typography** — Noto Sans JP mixed with Orbitron for a Tokyo street feel
- **Animated elements** — Flickering neon, pulsing glows, smooth transitions
- **Card-based layout** — Posts displayed as glowing cards with hover effects
- **Full-screen hero** — Dramatic landing with animated neon sign
- **Responsive design** — Beautiful from desktop monitors to mobile screens
- **Dark theme** — Deep midnight background that lets the neon colors pop
