Micro-terrazzo utilizes finely crushed aggregates to create a subtle, almost speckled texture. This contemporary approach is perfect for spaces requiring a refined, elegant aesthetic. From a distance, it reads as a solid color, but upon closer inspection, the intricate mix of tiny stones reveals itself, adding depth and character to the design.
