Moving away from traditional neutral tones, this high-contrast terrazzo explores the dramatic effect of embedding vibrant, deeply colored chips within a dark base matrix. This bold choice creates a striking visual impact, turning the floor or surface into a focal piece of art. It is a testament to the endless creative possibilities of the medium.
