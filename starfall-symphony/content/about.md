# The Conductors of the Cosmos

**Starfall Symphony** was founded in 2042 by a group of visionary astronomers and classical composers. Our mission is to bridge the gap between hard astrophysics and emotional resonance.

## Our Mission

By utilizing advanced radio-telescope data and converting electromagnetic frequencies into audible soundwaves, our composers arrange symphonies that literally capture the "music of the spheres."

> "When a star falls, it doesn't die silently. It sings a song that ripples across the void. We are just the listeners."
>
> — Dr. Aris Thorne, Lead Composer

We perform globally, bringing the grandeur of space down to Earth in an elegant, modern dark setting.
