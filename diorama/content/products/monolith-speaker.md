## Monolith Speaker

Carved from a single block of aerospace-grade aluminum, Monolith delivers 360-degree omnidirectional sound with room-filling clarity.

### Specifications

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Drivers | 4 full-range + 1 subwoofer |
| Power Output | 40W peak |
| Battery Life | 18 hours |
| Weight | 1.2 kg |
| Connectivity | Bluetooth 5.3, Wi-Fi 6, AirPlay 2 |
| Material | Aluminum unibody |

### What Sets It Apart

The custom DSP chip inside Monolith analyzes room acoustics in real time, adapting output for optimal staging regardless of placement. Seven-band parametric equalizer with intelligent presets learns your listening preferences over time.

Each unit takes over four hours to CNC-machine from a single aluminum billet, ensuring zero resonance and zero compromise.
