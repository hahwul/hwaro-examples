### User Experience

A well-crafted user experience considers every interaction point between a person and a product. From the moment of discovery through daily use, each touchpoint shapes perception and satisfaction.

The key principles that guide effective UX design:

1. **Clarity** - Remove ambiguity from every interaction
2. **Efficiency** - Minimize the steps to accomplish goals
3. **Consistency** - Establish patterns users can rely on
4. **Feedback** - Communicate the results of every action
