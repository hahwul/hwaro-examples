# About Canopy

Canopy is a trail journal for hikers, backpackers, and anyone who finds clarity on the trail. We document routes across mountain ranges, forest corridors, and alpine ridges with detailed logs covering distance, elevation, duration, and difficulty.

## What We Record

Each trail entry includes practical data for planning your own adventure:

- **Distance** -- Total round-trip or one-way mileage
- **Elevation Gain** -- Cumulative vertical ascent
- **Duration** -- Typical hiking time at moderate pace
- **Difficulty** -- Rated from Beginner to Expert based on terrain, exposure, and fitness demands

## Rating System

Our difficulty ratings follow established trail grading conventions:

- **Beginner** -- Well-maintained paths with minimal elevation change. Suitable for all fitness levels.
- **Intermediate** -- Moderate elevation gain with some uneven terrain. Basic hiking experience recommended.
- **Advanced** -- Sustained steep sections, route-finding required, and significant physical demands.
- **Expert** -- Technical scrambling, exposed ridgelines, or multi-day backcountry travel. Prior experience essential.

## Philosophy

The best trails teach patience. We believe in leaving no trace, staying on marked paths when available, and respecting the land that gives us these experiences. This journal is our way of sharing what we find out there.
