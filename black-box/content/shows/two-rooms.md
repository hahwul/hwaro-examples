## a constraint becomes the structure

The pool of light is six feet across. The piece has two characters. There is only one performer in the light at any given moment. The other is in the dark &mdash; present in the room, present in the voice, not visible.

### the rule

The rule is enforced by the lighting designer, not by the stage manager. The lighting designer is a character in the play. There is no attempt to hide this.

### what happens when they try to occupy the light together

They can't. That is the entire structure of the play.

### the reveal

In the final three minutes, something changes in the lighting design. The rule is broken. That is the piece. It is not a twist. It is the point the piece has been walking toward for 41 minutes.
