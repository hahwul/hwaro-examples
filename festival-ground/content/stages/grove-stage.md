## Footprint

A 36 ft canvas bell tent pitched in a cleared pocket of the birch grove. Sound reflection from the leaf canopy is excellent for acoustic sets. The stage is raised 18 in and skirted with local hemlock boards.

## Backline & Sound

- Meyer UPJ-1P mains with point-source fill
- SPL cap 92 dB(A) per county forestry easement
- Gentle stage lighting - no moving heads, no strobes
- Acoustic upright piano tuned daily at 11:00
