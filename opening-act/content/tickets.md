## Single Nights

Single-night tickets are sold from the Tuesday two weeks before each show. Opening night has its own window.

| Tier     | Price    | Notes                      |
|----------|----------|----------------------------|
| Standing | 24 EUR   | All ages, bar access       |
| Seated   | 38 EUR   | Reserved seat, early entry |

## Season Pass

Six nights, one price. Priority rebooking for any night you miss, and two passes to the public dress rehearsal.

- Pass — 185 EUR
- Pass (Patron tier) — 420 EUR

## Doors

Doors open one hour before curtain. Programme booklets are included with every ticket. Cloakroom is free for patron tier, 2 EUR otherwise.

## Access

Horizon Hall and Atrium Stage are step-free. Basement Black has partial access — contact the box office in advance for seating placement.
