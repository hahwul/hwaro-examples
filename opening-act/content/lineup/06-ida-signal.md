## First-Night Closer

By tradition the First Light series ends with a song. Ida Kjaer and her band close the opening night for the fourth consecutive season.

**Set**

A thirty-minute set drawn from the 2025 record *Morning Tide* and two unreleased songs written in the weeks before the premiere.

**Encore**

Tradition holds that the audience sing the final chorus of *Opening Song* together. Lyrics are printed on the back of the programme.
