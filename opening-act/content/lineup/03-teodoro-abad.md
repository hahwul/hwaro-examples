## World Premiere

Teodoro Abad closes the first half with a new solo piano work, *Threshold*, written for the opening of Season 07. The piece is structured as a single arc — no breaks — and uses only the lowest three octaves of the instrument.

**About the Work**

*Threshold* was drafted during a residency in Lisbon in early 2026 and completed in the Horizon Hall rehearsal rooms. The score asks the pianist to hold the first chord until the hall is completely still.

**Set Notes**

- One work, no interval
- Steinway D, tuned the morning of the performance
- Score will be published after the premiere run
