The pressure builds as we descend past 1,000 meters. Sunlight can no longer reach us here. The only light comes from flashes of bioluminescence outside the viewport.

We observed several instances of small creatures flashing to confuse predators or attract prey. The display of solid, unbroken light against absolute darkness is spectacular.
