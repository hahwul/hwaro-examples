This theme was created as an example for the Hwaro static site generator.

It features a dark, oceanic aesthetic with bright, glowing accents designed to mimic bioluminescent creatures.

### Design Principles

- **No Gradients**: All lighting effects are achieved using solid colors, box-shadows, and text-shadows.
- **Deep Contrast**: High contrast between the dark background and the glowing text ensures readability and visual impact.
- **Organic Movement**: Subtle animations bring the theme to life, simulating the slow drift of deep-sea particles.
