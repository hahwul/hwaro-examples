Welcome to the abyss. Here, light is created not by the sun, but by the creatures that inhabit the darkness.

This theme explores the concept of bioluminescence, using neon accents and deep, dark backgrounds to simulate the glowing life forms of the deep sea.

### Glowing Entities

The depths are not empty. They are filled with strange and beautiful life, illuminating the water with chemical reactions.

- **Anglerfish**: A predatory fish with a glowing lure.
- **Jellyfish**: Pulsating bodies of soft, ghostly light.
- **Plankton**: Microscopic organisms that light up the water when disturbed.
