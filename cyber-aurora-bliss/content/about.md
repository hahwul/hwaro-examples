Cyber Aurora Bliss is a unique theme crafted to showcase the capabilities of Hwaro for building statically generated websites.

By utilizing modern CSS features such as `backdrop-filter` and keyframe animations, the theme provides a visually engaging experience out of the box.

This page serves as a simple demonstration of how secondary content is styled and structured within the theme's glassmorphism container.
