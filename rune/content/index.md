Within these halls lie the gathered inscriptions of the North. Each mark upon stone carries the weight of ages -- the whispered knowledge of skalds, the navigational secrets of seafarers, and the cosmological understanding of those who walked beneath the great ash tree.

The runes are not mere letters. They are forces. Each carries meaning beyond its sound, a connection to the natural world and the unseen realms that surround it. To read them is to listen to voices that spoke a thousand years before our time.
