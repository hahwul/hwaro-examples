# About This Theme

Burnt Charcoal is a design exploration into dark, textured aesthetics. It focuses on raw, unpolished textures contrasted with sharp, bright accents that mimic the glowing of embers in a dying fire.

## Design Philosophy

- **Raw Textures**: The use of SVG noise filters applies a subtle, grainy texture across the site, simulating ash or charred wood.
- **Glowing Embers**: Bright oranges, reds, and yellows are used sparingly as accents, often appearing on hover or as structural highlights.
- **No Gradients**: True to its concept, the glowing effect is achieved strictly through solid colors and layered `box-shadow` properties, avoiding CSS gradients entirely.
- **No Emojis**: The theme maintains a serious, moody tone, strictly avoiding emojis in its design and presentation.

### Technical Details

The theme is built for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), utilizing its templating engine to apply a consistent, dark aesthetic across all pages.
