# About Zenith

Zenith is the point in the sky directly above an observer. It represents the absolute peak -- the highest altitude one can perceive from any given position on Earth.

This site embodies that concept through design. Each layer strips away complexity, rising through atmospheric strata until only essential form remains. White yields to blue, blue yields to darkness, and within that darkness, clarity.

The philosophy is simple: remove everything that does not serve the message. What remains is the zenith.
