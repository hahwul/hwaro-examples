## THE FAVORITE

Lane four goes to the fastest qualifier from the semifinal round. Tonight that is Rui Santos. He ran 9.81 in the semis without a trailing wind, without looking fully extended, without turning to the camera. He was conserving energy. He is saving something.

### The Start

Santos starts slow. Reaction time of 0.162 seconds &mdash; perfectly average. His strength is not the gun. His strength is the drive phase. The first forty meters where the body stays low, where the stride grows but stays piston-clean, where the ground keeps feeding itself into the push-off.

### The Drive

By 60 meters Santos is upright. By 70 he is in full extension. The question for tonight is not whether he reaches top speed &mdash; it is whether he reaches it two meters before anyone else.

### The Tape

He ran 9.76 at altitude two years ago. Tonight is sea level. The record might not fall. But the gold will.
