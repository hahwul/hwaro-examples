## Summary Across Studies

Of 24 included studies:

- **15 (63 pct)** rated as low overall risk of bias
- **5 (21 pct)** rated as some concerns (primarily in missing data or measurement domains)
- **4 (17 pct)** rated as high risk (all due to >20 pct attrition with differential dropout)

The most common source of concern was missing outcome data (Domain 3), particularly in waitlist-controlled trials where differential attrition was higher. Sensitivity analysis excluding high-risk studies (see Section 3) showed the pooled effect remained significant and of similar magnitude.
