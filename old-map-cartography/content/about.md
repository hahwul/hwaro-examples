# Legends & Bestiary

Cartographers throughout the ages have documented numerous beasts that threaten the intrepid explorer. Whether they are real or figments of a scurvy-riddled mind, it is best to be prepared.

## The Kraken

A monster of colossal size, capable of wrapping its tentacles around the largest galleons and dragging them down to the abyss. Commonly sighted off the frozen coasts of the north.

## Leviathan

The great serpent of the deep. Some say its breath causes tempests and its thrashing creates maelstroms. When it sleeps, it is often mistaken for an uncharted island.

> "I saw an island where no island should be. We approached to gather fresh water, but the island opened a golden eye and sank beneath the waves."
> — *Captain Elias Vance, logbook entry*

## Sirens

Half-bird, half-woman creatures whose songs entrance sailors, causing them to steer their vessels into treacherous rocks. Only the most disciplined crews can resist their call.
