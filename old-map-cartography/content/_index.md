# Terra Incognita

Welcome to the great chart of the known and unknown worlds. Here be the documented coasts, the treacherous deeps, and the creatures that dwell beyond the edges of human understanding. 

## The Cartographer's Duty

To map the world is to tame it on paper. The lines we draw separate civilization from wilderness, but they are only lines. Beyond the *Mare Tenebrosum* (the Sea of Darkness), mariners tell tales of serpents as long as galleons and islands that sink when ships approach. 

> "Do not sail past the setting sun, for there the sea falls into the abyss."
> — *An old sailor's warning, 1542*

## Legendary Locations

*   **The Isle of Sirens**: Avoid the rocky straits to the East.
*   **The Maelstrom of Charybdis**: A whirlpool that devours entire fleets.
*   **Ultima Thule**: The farthest northern land, frozen and desolate.
*   **The Drowning Spires**: Ruins of an ancient kingdom swallowed by the waves.

The compass rose points the way, but it is your courage that will chart the course. May the winds be favorable and the sea monsters sated.
