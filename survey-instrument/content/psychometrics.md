# Psychometrics at a glance

Full psychometric results are distributed across Sections 2, 3, and 4 of the manuscript. This page is a headline summary.

## Factor structure

- **Model:** Five-factor oblique solution
- **Variance explained (EFA):** 64.2 pct
- **CFI:** 0.947 &middot; **TLI:** 0.941 &middot; **RMSEA:** 0.048 &middot; **SRMR:** 0.041
- **Primary loadings range:** 0.58 &ndash; 0.84
- **Cross-loadings &gt; 0.30:** 1 (Q18, retained)

## Reliability

| Subscale | Items | &alpha; | &omega; | ICC (test-retest) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Access | 5 | 0.84 | 0.85 | 0.81 |
| Communication | 5 | **0.91** | 0.91 | 0.86 |
| Comprehension | 5 | 0.81 | 0.82 | 0.79 |
| Autonomy | 5 | 0.78 | 0.79 | 0.76 |
| Environment | 5 | 0.87 | 0.88 | 0.83 |
| **Composite** | 25 | **0.93** | 0.93 | **0.82** |

## Validity

- **Convergent:** All five hypothesized correlations with external scales &geq; 0.59 (<em>rho</em>), confirming strong overlap with established constructs.
- **Discriminant:** All HTMT ratios &lt; 0.85, supporting construct distinction.
- **Known-groups:** Mean differences of 0.4 &ndash; 0.8 SD units between a priori contrasting groups on every subscale.

## Sample

- **Field sample:** N = 1,412 adults, age 18 &ndash; 87
- **Calibration split:** n = 706 (EFA)
- **Validation split:** n = 706 (CFA)
- **Test-retest subsample:** n = 214, 14-day interval
- **Cognitive pretest:** n = 24 initial + n = 12 second round
