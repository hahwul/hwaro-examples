## 3.1 Internal consistency

## 3.3 Test-retest reliability

A subsample of 214 respondents completed the BASIS-25 a second time two weeks after the initial administration. Intraclass correlation coefficients (ICC 2,1) for subscale scores ranged from 0.76 to 0.86. The composite ICC was <strong>0.82</strong>.

## 3.4 Item-level statistics

Inter-item correlations within each subscale ranged from 0.28 to 0.71, with a mean of 0.46. All items had item-total correlations (corrected for overlap) greater than 0.40. No item, if removed, would have produced an increase in subscale <em>alpha</em> greater than 0.01.
