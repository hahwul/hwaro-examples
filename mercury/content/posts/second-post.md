Typography in the Mercury theme is designed to be as sharp and clear as a mirror's reflection. We utilize a clean sans-serif stack to provide a modern, unadorned reading experience.

### The Typographic Hierarchy

1.  **Display Headings:** Large, sweeping, and visually dominant, much like the surface of a still pool of mercury.
2.  **Section Titles:** Grounded and structured, providing clear boundaries within the content.
3.  **Body Copy:** Highly legible, ensuring that the visual flair of the site does not compromise its primary function: reading.

When combined with our carefully curated palette of silvers, greys, and muted accents, the text itself becomes a part of the metallic texture.
