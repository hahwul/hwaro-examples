## The Premise

This is a space for strong opinions, loosely held. Every piece here is written with conviction -- not because the author is always right, but because hedging every sentence is a disservice to the reader.

Technology moves fast. The discourse around it moves faster, and most of it says nothing. This site exists to cut through that noise.

## The Approach

**Say what you mean.** If a framework is broken, say it is broken. If a pattern works, defend it. Half-measures in writing produce half-measures in thinking.

**Long form only.** Ideas worth having are worth developing. No listicles, no hot takes dressed up as insight. Every post earns its length or it does not get published.

**Design is argument.** The typography, the whitespace, the contrast -- these are not decoration. They are emphasis. The way something looks shapes how it is read.

## The Stack

Built with [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com), a static site generator that stays out of the way. No JavaScript frameworks. No build pipelines that take longer than the writing. Just content, templates, and a fast compiler.
