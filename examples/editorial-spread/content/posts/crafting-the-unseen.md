True luxury is designed for the wearer, not the spectator. It is found in the soft, smooth embrace of hand-rolled silk habotai linings, the invisible support of horsehair canvas interlinings, and the precise weight of lead drapery weights hidden in hemlines.

### The Interior Architecture
An exquisite blazer retains its crisp drape over decades because of the complex, hand-stitched interior matrix. This unseen canvas molds over time to the exact posture and shape of the owner, transforming a standard garment into a custom-fitted second skin.
