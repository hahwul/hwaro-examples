Garment durability has become the ultimate fashion statement. As consumers move away from rapid fast-fashion cycles, they are embracing slow tailoring: local dye houses, bespoke tailoring studios, and traditional hand-mend crafts.

### Visible Mending as Ornament
A visible, hand-stitched Sashiko patch on a vintage denim jacket is no longer a sign of neglect; it is a proud medal of lifetime utility and circular respect, adding rich character and texture to standard industrial materials.
