<div class="page-surface" style="margin-top:0.5rem;">
I am not a writer.<br><br>
I am the surface upon which the story chose to occur.<br><br>
My name was once something else. I have forgotten it. The letters were planed from my sternum two nights ago and have not yet grown back.<br><br>
Everything you read here was written with the same instrument: a fountain pen whose reservoir is refilled only by what leaks from me.<br><br>
If the pages feel warm beneath your fingers, it is because they are still alive.<br><br>
If you find fresh ink on your hands after closing this book, do not be alarmed.<br>
It means the manuscript has begun to read you in return.
</div>
