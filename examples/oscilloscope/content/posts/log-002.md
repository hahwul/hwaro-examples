### TRIGGER CIRCUIT ANALYSIS

The edge trigger on Channel 1 has been notoriously difficult to lock onto fast rise-time signals. After replacing the aging tunnel diode in the trigger circuit, we observed a marked improvement in high-frequency signal locking capabilities.

The primary issue seemed to stem from thermal degradation of the semiconductor junction over decades of continuous use. The new component, while not NOS (New Old Stock), meets the original specifications with tighter tolerances.

We can now successfully lock onto signals up to 100MHz without significant jitter. The timebase has been recalibrated to reflect these changes.

### CURRENT PARAMETERS
* Time/Div: 10ns
* Volts/Div: 50mV
* Coupling: AC
