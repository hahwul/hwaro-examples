### WARM-UP PHASE INITIATED

The primary electron gun requires approximately 45 seconds to reach optimal operating temperature. During this phase, you may notice significant thermal drift on the X and Y axes. This is completely normal and expected behavior for a cold CRT tube.

We are currently tracking a phantom signal on channel 2. Initial analysis suggests it may be cross-talk from the power supply's switching regulator, operating at precisely 50kHz. We will need to implement additional shielding around the high-voltage flyback transformer to isolate this noise.

### CALIBRATION STATUS
* X-Axis: Uncalibrated (Drift > 5%)
* Y-Axis: Nominal
* Trigger: Unstable

Further adjustments will be logged as they are performed.
