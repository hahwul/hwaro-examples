# IT and Systems Setup

Our IT department ensures you have the necessary tools to perform your role effectively.

## Standard Equipment

As part of your onboarding, you will receive:

*   A company laptop (Windows or macOS based on your role requirement).
*   A monitor, keyboard, and mouse for your primary workspace.
*   Access to our internal network and cloud services.

## Software Access

You will be granted access to the following standard software:

1.  **Email and Calendar:** We use the corporate standard suite for all communication.
2.  **Messaging:** Our primary tool for instant communication.
3.  **File Sharing:** Secure cloud storage for document collaboration.

> **Security Reminder:** Please ensure you review our IT security policies before connecting any personal devices to the company network.

If you require any specific software for your role, please submit a request through the IT Helpdesk.
