# Your First Day

Your first day with us is focused on getting you acquainted with your team and the environment.

## Schedule

Here's a typical schedule for your first day:

*   **9:00 AM - 9:30 AM:** Welcome and orientation with Human Resources.
*   **9:30 AM - 10:30 AM:** IT setup and equipment handover.
*   **10:30 AM - 11:30 AM:** Meet and greet with your direct manager.
*   **11:30 AM - 12:30 PM:** Introduction to your team members.
*   **12:30 PM - 1:30 PM:** Welcome lunch with the team.
*   **1:30 PM - 5:00 PM:** Independent review of this handbook and initial project onboarding.

> **Important:** Please bring the necessary documentation for payroll setup, as requested in your welcome email.

## Dress Code

We maintain a smart-casual dress code. Dress comfortably, but professionally.
