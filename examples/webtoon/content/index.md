## Visual Flow

Webtoon is about the power of the vertical scroll. By breaking the narrative into a sequence of panels that reveal themselves as the user moves down the page, we create a sense of pace and immersion that traditional layouts cannot match. Our focus is on the transition between scenes and the overall flow of the story.

### Storytelling Principles

- **Immersion**: Pulling the user into the world through consistent visual language.
- **Transition**: The space between panels where the imagination fills the gaps.
- **Pace**: Controlling the speed at which the information is consumed.
