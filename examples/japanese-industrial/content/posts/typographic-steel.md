In industrial design, elements must support weight. Typography in our system is no different. We use bold, utilitarian sans-serif fonts to construct hierarchies that withstand heavy visual loads.

The Inter typeface provides this structural support. Its stark geometries and clear letterforms ensure that the message is delivered without ambiguity. It is the steel I-beam of our digital architecture.
