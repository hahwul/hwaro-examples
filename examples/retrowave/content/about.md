# About Retrowave

Retrowave is a digital broadcast from the neon-lit highways of 1985. This blog explores the sounds, aesthetics, and culture of synthwave -- the music genre that refuses to let the 80s die.

## The Mission

We document the artists, the albums, and the visual language of a retrofuture that never existed but feels more real than the present. From the pulsing basslines of darksynth to the shimmering arpeggios of dreamwave, every frequency gets its signal here.

## What We Cover

- **Synthwave music** -- artist profiles, album reviews, and track breakdowns
- **Retro aesthetics** -- the visual grammar of neon grids, chrome surfaces, and endless sunsets
- **Retrofuturism** -- the culture of imagining tomorrow through the lens of yesterday
- **The scene** -- events, communities, and the people keeping the signal alive

## The Name

A retrowave is a signal bouncing back from the future we were promised. It carries the warmth of analog synthesizers, the glow of CRT monitors, and the conviction that the horizon always holds something worth driving toward.
