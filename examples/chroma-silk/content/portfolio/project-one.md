An exploration of urban environments under the glow of neon lights. The project focused on capturing the contrast between harsh concrete structures and vibrant, ethereal light sources.

### Process

We spent three weeks scouting locations in major metropolitan areas, looking for the perfect interplay of light and shadow. The final series consists of 12 carefully selected images that tell a story of a city that never truly sleeps.
