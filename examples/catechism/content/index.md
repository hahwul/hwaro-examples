## The Essential Truths

Catechism is an exploration of the foundational principles that support every major work. We don't believe in fleeting trends; we believe in the truths that remain stable over time. By using a structured, Q&A format, we reveal the core logic of the project and the way it connects to the broader world.

<div class="qa-section">
    <div class="question">Question 01: What is the primary goal of innovation?</div>
    <div class="answer">The primary goal of innovation is the meaningful reorganization of the material to better serve the user and the world.</div>

    <div class="question">Question 02: Where does truth exist within the design?</div>
    <div class="answer">Truth exists in the integrity of the structure and the transparency of the intent. It is the absence of deception in the relationship between the work and the viewer.</div>

    <div class="question">Question 03: Why do we focus on the foundational?</div>
    <div class="answer">We focus on the foundational because it is the only part of the work that is built to endure. Everything else is secondary to the stability of the core.</div>
</div>
