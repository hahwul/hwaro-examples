Hologram is a product showcase theme inspired by holographic and iridescent materials. Cards shift color as you interact, glass-like surfaces catch light differently from every angle, and the interface feels alive with subtle prismatic effects.

Every product is presented as if suspended in a display case of light -- translucent, luminous, and impossible to look away from.

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).
