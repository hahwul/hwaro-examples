Serica is an exploration of minimalism in web design.

In a world filled with cluttered interfaces and aggressive notifications, Serica offers a moment of calm. It strips away the non-essential to focus on what truly matters: your words, your images, and your story.

## The Design Process

The color palette was chosen to evoke the natural, un-dyed color of raw silk—an off-white canvas (`#faf9f6`) that is easy on the eyes. The accent color is a muted gold (`#cba153`), providing a touch of warmth and luxury without being overpowering.

### Typography

Typography is the heart of Serica.

* **Cormorant Garamond** was selected for headings due to its classical, almost historical proportions. It lends authority and grace to the layout.
* **Inter** provides a modern, highly legible contrast for the body text, ensuring the site remains highly readable on screens of all sizes.

We hope you enjoy using Serica as much as we enjoyed creating it.
