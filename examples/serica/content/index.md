Welcome to **Serica**. Inspired by the delicate yet enduring qualities of silk, this theme is designed to provide an elegant foundation for writers, photographers, and creatives.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — Leonardo da Vinci

## Philosophy

Serica emphasizes typography, generous whitespace, and a subtle color palette. The goal is to let your content speak for itself, wrapped in a presentation that feels timeless and refined.

Here are the key aspects of Serica:
- **Elegant Typography**: Utilizing Cormorant Garamond for headings and Inter for body text.
- **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Off-white background, muted text, and soft gold accents.
- **Content-First**: Distraction-free reading experience.
