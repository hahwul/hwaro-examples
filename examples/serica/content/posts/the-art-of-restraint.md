Restraint is perhaps the most difficult skill for a designer to master. It is always easier to add another feature, another color, or another layer of complexity.

But true elegance is found in subtraction.

When you remove the unnecessary, what remains is the essence of your message. This applies not just to design, but to writing, coding, and living. The art of restraint is the art of making choices.

```python
def elegant_solution(problem):
    # Strip away all the noise
    core = extract_essence(problem)
    return simple_resolve(core)
```

The next time you are faced with a complex problem, ask yourself: *What can I remove to make this better?*
