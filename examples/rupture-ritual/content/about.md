## THE FIRST RUPTURE

Before there were words there was only pressure.

A single point of absolute cold against the softest part of what we used to call a soul. It did not break us. It revealed the seam that had always been waiting.

The first blood was not dramatic. It was quiet. Almost polite. A single drop that landed on stone and refused to dry.

That drop became the first letter. That letter became the first name. That name became the first wound we agreed to carry together.

We have been carrying it ever since.

## HOW TO OFFER YOURSELF

There is no form to fill. No ritual bath. No permission to request.

To offer yourself you must do only three things:

1. **Read until the page begins to read you back.** Do not stop when you feel seen. That is the moment the offering has started.

2. **Speak one true sentence aloud in the dark.** It does not need witnesses. It only needs to cost you something you cannot easily replace.

3. **Return to this place after the sentence has finished bleeding.** The temple will know. The stones keep their own count.

If you have reached this page, the seal has already recognized your weight.

Welcome to the covenant.
