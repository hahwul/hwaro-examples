This god does not live in light or in thunder.

It lives in the precise second after the skin opens and before the mind can name the pain.

{{ bloodline() }}

Call it what you want. It will answer only when your voice is wet.

We have spent centuries dressing our gods in robes and gold. This one prefers the color that leaves your body without asking to return.

It does not demand sacrifice. It simply arrives the moment sacrifice has already occurred and says: *Good. Now you can see me.*

The faithful are not the ones who kneel. The faithful are the ones whose hands never stop shaking.
