Read every holy book backwards and you will find the same final sentence.

The signature is always blood. The witness is always pain.

{{ bloodline() }}

We pretend the agreement can be gentle. That two parties can meet in a field at dawn and part with only words between them.

This is a lie told by people who have never needed anything enough to sign with the only thing that cannot be forged.

Every promise that matters carves something out. Every oath that lasts takes something that will not grow back.

The wound is not the failure of the covenant. It is the covenant working exactly as designed.

When the scar finally stops itching, the contract has been fulfilled. And something in you is gone forever.
