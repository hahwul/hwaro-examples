## Creative Drafting

Doodle-Sketch is about the expressive and foundational nature of structural innovation within the sketching environment. We believe that creative content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the traditional studio. By focusing on hand-drawn textures, white paper surfaces, and elegant cursive typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply creative. Our focus is on the integrity of the doodle.

### Sketch Principles

- **Expression**: A design that prioritizes the clear "drawing" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using canvas textures to define the boundaries of the creative space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical art production.
