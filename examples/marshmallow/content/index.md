## Airy Innovation

Marshmallow is about the lightest possible state of tactile innovation. We believe that design can be both substantial and as light as air. By focusing on very soft, high-depth shadows and a pale, whimsical palette, we create a design that feels both comforting and deeply playful. Our focus is on the effortless nature of the interface.

### Sweet Principles

- **Lightness**: A design that feels like it could float away.
- **Softness**: Using extreme rounding and airy shadows to reduce visual weight.
- **Whimsy**: A palette that encourages a sense of joy and discovery.
