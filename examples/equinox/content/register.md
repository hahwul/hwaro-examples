Equinox is deliberately small — the Havnehuset hall seats 220, and we hold it to
that so the room stays a room where people can actually talk. Registration is open
now and closes when the hall is full or on **1 September 2026**, whichever comes
first. In recent years, that has meant July.

## Rates

All rates cover both days, lunch and coffee, and the full program across every
track. There is no separate charge for workshops.

| Ticket | Rate (EUR) | For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Municipal / public sector | €180 | Council staff, planners, emergency management |
| Community & non-profit | €90 | Residents' groups, NGOs, volunteer stewards |
| Academic & student | €120 | Researchers and enrolled students |
| Supporter | €340 | Covers your seat plus one subsidised place |

Every **Supporter** ticket funds a fully subsidised seat for someone from a
frontline coastal community who could not otherwise attend. In 2025 that programme
brought fourteen residents and local organisers into the room, and their questions
were consistently the sharpest of the summit.

<div class="callout">
<strong>Subsidised places.</strong> If cost is the only thing standing between you
and the hall, write to the committee. We keep a block of free places aside for
residents of affected coastlines and never publish who used them.
</div>

## What your seat includes

- Both days of the full plenary program, all five tracks.
- Lunch and coffee on both days, served in the harbourside foyer.
- The printed program and the post-summit briefing, a short document that
  distils what was actually decided and argued into something you can hand to a
  council.
- Standing access to the recorded sessions, captioned, for a year afterward.

## How to register

Registration is handled by email while we keep the process human and the hall
small. Write to the organising committee with your name, your role, and the ticket
you would like, and we will send a confirmation and an invoice within two working
days. Group bookings from a single municipality are welcome — tell us how many
seats and we will sort the details.

We read every message. If you are unsure whether Equinox is for you, ask — the
honest answer is usually yes. First, skim the [full program](/program/) to see
what two days will cover, and read the [venue and travel notes](/venue/) so you
can plan the trip.
