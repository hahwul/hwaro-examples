The program is organised as five tracks, sequenced the way the days themselves
run: we begin with the water and the forecasts, move through the hard choices of
retreat and defence, and close on the plans, budgets, and communities that carry
the work forward. Every session is plenary or single-stream — there are no
clashes to agonise over, because the point of Equinox is that everyone leaves
having heard the same argument.

Use the finder below to jump straight to a session by topic — try a place, a
mechanism, or a name. When you have found your tracks, check the [venue and
travel notes](/venue/) and [reserve a seat](/register/) before the hall fills.
