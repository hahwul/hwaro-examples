Equinox takes place at the **Havnehuset Assembly Hall**, a converted grain
warehouse on the inner harbour of Aarhus — a fitting room for a conversation about
the waterline, since the building's own ground floor has flooded twice in the last
decade. Both days run in a single plenary hall, with the harbourside foyer open
for coffee, the poster wall, and the quieter conversations that are half the point
of coming.

## Address

Havnehuset Assembly Hall
Nordhavnsgade 4, 8000 Aarhus C, Denmark

The hall sits on the water's edge, a fifteen-minute walk from Aarhus Central
Station along the harbour promenade. Look for the row of five painted stripes on
the north façade — cool to warm, the same sequence that runs through this program.

## Getting here

- **By train.** Aarhus Central Station connects to Copenhagen in just over three
  hours and to Hamburg in about five. From the station, walk east toward the water
  or take the light rail one stop to Skolebakken.
- **By light rail (Letbane).** The Skolebakken stop is a five-minute walk from the
  hall. A single ticket covers the ride from the station.
- **By bike.** Aarhus is flat and cyclable; there is covered, monitored parking for
  roughly forty bicycles beside the main entrance.

We ask that you not drive. Harbour parking is scarce, and a summit about coastal
futures is not the place to arrive by car if you have any alternative.

## Accessibility

The hall is fully step-free, with a level entrance from the promenade and a lift to
the foyer. Every session is captioned live on screens beside the stage, and a
hearing loop covers the front two-thirds of the hall. A quiet room off the foyer is
open both days for anyone who needs a break from the crowd.

## Food and the long breaks

Lunch and coffee are included both days and served in the foyer — vegetarian by
default, with clearly labelled options. Breaks are deliberately generous: forty
minutes, not fifteen. The best ideas at Equinox tend to happen over the second cup
of coffee, so we have built the schedule to give them room.
