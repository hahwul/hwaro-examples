Orbit is a search engine with a rule built into its foundation: if a machine wrote it, crawled it, or summarized it, it does not belong in the index. Every one of the 8,328 pages listed here was found, opened, and read by a person on our index desk before it earned a place in a category.

We built Orbit because the rest of search stopped being about pages and started being about predicting what you might have wanted a page to say. We would rather show you fewer results and be certain a human made every one of them.
