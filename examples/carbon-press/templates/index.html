FORM 24-B / SECTION IV / FILING WORKFLOW

How a matter moves through the desk.

  1. 01

    Intake

    Bring the paperwork, the disputed amount, the date the trouble started. The desk records the matter in the day-book, in pencil.

    →
  2. 02

    Drafting

    A scribe types the instrument on a Selectric. You read it aloud once. We correct it twice, in red pencil, on the white copy.

    →
  3. 03

    Witnessing

    Two co-op members sign as witnesses. The stamp goes on all three sheets — pink, yellow, white — through carbon impression.

    →
  4. 04

    Filing

    The white copy is filed with the court or registry. You keep the yellow. We keep the pink in the bottom drawer for seven years.
