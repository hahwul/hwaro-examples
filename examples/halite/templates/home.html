Why Halite

Five typed values

Every cell resolves to Number, Text, Bool, Date, or Error. Formulas are type-checked when they are written, not when they happen to run.

Dependency-graph recalculation

Changing a cell recomputes only the formulas that actually read it. A thousand-cell workbook and a five-cell one recalculate in the same, small amount of work.

One embeddable core

A single library with a stable C ABI and no runtime dependencies beyond libc. Bring your own grid, report, or headless batch job — Halite has no UI of its own.
