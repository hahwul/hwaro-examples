## Wolfshead Choir

Eight years, four records, first time at Iron Stage. Tromsø's most-discussed black metal export return with the new record *Hall of Silent Oaths* in tow.

### New Record

*Hall of Silent Oaths* — the third full-length — was released in February. Thirty-six minutes, six tracks, no ballads. Side B is recorded live in a single take, a first for the band.

### Set

Expected ninety minutes including the twelve-minute closer, *Bellow*. Rehearsal this week confirmed the full record will be played in order, front to back.

### Band

Hjalmar Steen — vocals, guitar. Reidun Alm — guitar. Kjell Norgaard — bass. Odd Berg — drums. Live members on synth and tympani on the touring cycle.
