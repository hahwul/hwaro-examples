## The Iron Mouth

Iron Stage's most-requested headliner for three consecutive years. First time on the bill.

### Set

One hundred and five minutes. Six songs from the 2023 record *Rotary*, five from the preceding records, and the traditional closing encore *The Iron Tongue Remains*. The closing encore's length varies year to year; the record stands at 22:08.

### Production

The drum riser is reinforced this year for the Iron Mouth's traditional double-kit setup. Both kits are mic'd individually and mixed as a single stereo pair for the PA.
