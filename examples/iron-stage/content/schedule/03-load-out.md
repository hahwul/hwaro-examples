## Sunday

The hangar closes to the public at 02:00 Sunday morning. Load-out begins at 06:00 with stage truss and lighting rigs, runs through the day, and ends with PA strike after dark.

### Crew Schedule

| Time  | Crew              | Task                  |
|-------|-------------------|-----------------------|
| 06:00 | Riggers           | Truss down · motors   |
| 08:00 | Lighting          | Fixtures · cabling    |
| 10:00 | Backline          | Drum risers · amps    |
| 12:00 | Stagehands        | Decking · skirts      |
| 14:00 | Audio             | Monitors · sub stack  |
| 17:00 | Audio             | Main hangs · FOH      |
| 20:00 | All hands         | Power · sweep · keys  |

### Pickup

Bands collect their backline from Bay 4 between 09:00 and 14:00. After 14:00, anything not signed for moves into bonded storage and incurs the standard recovery fee. The production office stays open until 18:00 for invoices, settlement, and lost-and-found. Final hangar walk-through with the venue is at 21:30.
