Every interaction leaves a trace. These traces form a digital shadow, a secondary entity that is more visible to the system than the physical self.

## Data Persistence

Unlike the physical shadow, the digital shadow does not vanish when the light is removed. It is stored, indexed, and analyzed.

- Persistent Storage
- Metadata Correlation
- Algorithmic Prediction

The audit of these shadows is the primary task of the modern observer.
