Total transparency does not lead to total understanding. Instead, it leads to a flood of data that can obscure the very truths it was meant to reveal.

## Signal vs Noise

In the Panopticon, the observer must distinguish between relevant behavior and background noise. As the volume of data increases, the risk of misinterpretation grows.

The Architecture of Visibility requires a corresponding Architecture of Analysis.
