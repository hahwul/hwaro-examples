The Panopticon is not merely a building, but a diagram of power. By placing the subject in a state of permanent visibility, the need for physical force is replaced by the psychological weight of potential observation.

## Structural Integrity

The circular design ensures that every cell is visible from the central watchtower, while the tower remains opaque to the subjects. This asymmetry is the core of the system.

<!-- more -->

In the digital age, this architecture has been abstracted into data streams and metadata. We are no longer limited by stone walls; the watchtower is now everywhere and nowhere.
