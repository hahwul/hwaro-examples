The Panopticon is an institutional framework designed to maximize visibility and optimize the efficiency of observation.

## Objectives

1. Maintain continuous data streams.
2. Ensure structural integrity of the watchtower.
3. Validate all incoming logs.

## Compliance

All entities within the perimeter must adhere to the established protocols. Failure to comply will result in immediate re-calibration of the observation parameters.
