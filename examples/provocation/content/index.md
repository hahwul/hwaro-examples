## Why Provoke?

Comfort is the enemy of innovation. Provocation is the intentional disruption of the status quo to force a new perspective. We don't just present information; we challenge the way you receive it.

<div class="challenge-box">
    <h3>THE UNCOMFORTABLE TRUTH</h3>
    <p>Most systems are built for stability at the cost of progress. We prefer the volatility of growth over the stagnation of safety.</p>

### Redefining the Expected

- **Friction**: The heat generated by conflicting ideas.
- **Dissonance**: The sound of a system being tested.
- **Clarity**: The result of surviving the challenge.
