## Projecting the Idea

Megaphone is about the intentional projection of a single, powerful message. In a landscape filled with whispers, we choose to speak with a volume that cannot be ignored. Our focus is on the reach and clarity of the broadcast.

### Amplification

- **Directivity**: Focusing the energy in a specific direction.
- **Gain**: Increasing the impact of the original message.
- **Coverage**: Ensuring the message reaches the intended audience.

### The Message

1. **Clarity**: Stripping away the static.
2. **Intent**: Knowing exactly what needs to be said.
3. **Resonance**: Ensuring the message sticks.
