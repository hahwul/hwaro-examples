High-contrast serif typefaces, like **Playfair Display**, bring a sense of heritage and sophistication to the digital screen. Inspired by the transition from the Enlightenment to the Industrial Age, these fonts excel in large display sizes where their delicate thins and robust thicks can truly shine.

<!-- more -->

When pairing a font as characterful as Playfair, simplicity is key. A clean, functional sans-serif like **Inter** provides the perfect counterpoint, allowing the headers to command attention without overwhelming the reader.

## Why it works

1. **Contrast:** The sharp difference between the serif's curves and the sans-serif's geometric lines creates visual interest.
2. **Hierarchy:** Playfair naturally draws the eye to titles, while Inter ensures the body copy remains highly legible.
3. **Mood:** This pairing strikes a balance between "classic" and "contemporary."

In this project, we utilize this combination to emphasize our focus on refined aesthetics.
