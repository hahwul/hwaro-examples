**Syne** is a typeface that refuses to blend into the background. Designed by Bonjour Monde, it was originally conceived for an art center, which explains its unconventional and artistic proportions.

<!-- more -->

In its "Extra Bold" weight, Syne becomes a statement piece. It is wide, almost aggressive, yet remarkably balanced. It represents a shift in web design away from the "safe" Swiss-style minimalism toward something more expressive and individualistic.

## Pairing with Lora

To ground the experimental nature of Syne, we pair it with **Lora** for body text. Lora is a contemporary serif with roots in calligraphy, offering a gentle, rhythmic reading experience that contrasts beautifully with Syne's industrial boldness.

> "Typography is the craft of endowing a human language with a durable visual form." — Robert Bringhurst

By mixing these two, we achieve a look that feels both "trendy" and "literary."
