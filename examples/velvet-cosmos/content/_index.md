Welcome to **Velvet Cosmos**, an exploration of dark aesthetics, deep purple voids, and glowing celestial bodies framed in frosted glass.

Here, we wander through the silence of the interstellar medium, drifting past neon cyan nebulae and vibrant magenta supernovas. This is a sanctuary for those who find peace in the dark and beauty in the glow.

### Featured Anomalies

- **The Magenta Pulsar**: A rapidly spinning neutron star that bathes its surrounding planetary system in a vivid pink light.
- **Cyan Nebula Clouds**: Vast expanses of interstellar dust that glow with a cold, electric blue energy.
- **Golden Asteroid Fields**: Ancient remnants of shattered worlds, catching the light of distant suns to gleam like scattered gold.
