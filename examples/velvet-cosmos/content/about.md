## Origins

The **Velvet Cosmos** was born from a desire to merge the vast, unknown depths of space with the sleek, modern elegance of glassmorphism. It is a visual representation of the intersection between raw cosmic power and refined digital design.

### Our Mission

Our mission is to chart the uncharted, to bring light to the deepest corners of the void, and to document the ethereal beauty of the universe. We believe that by understanding the cosmos, we better understand ourselves.

### The Technology

This transmission is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a high-performance static site generator that builds these digital realms with blazing speed. The aesthetics rely on modern CSS features, specifically `backdrop-filter`, to create the illusion of frosted glass floating in the void.

Join us on this journey. The cosmos is vast, and there is much to see.
