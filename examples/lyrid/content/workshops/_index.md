Every session at Lyrid is a bench, not a lecture. You leave with something physical — a cut punch, a proofed page, a font file with a working axis — and with the vocabulary to keep going. Sessions run in parallel across the two halls of De Zetterij, so read the [schedule](/schedule/) before you [register](/register/): most benches seat twelve and they fill in the order tickets clear.

Bring nothing but your hands. Steel, type, presses, laptops, and loupes are all provided. Photography is welcome; noise, dust, and ink are guaranteed.
