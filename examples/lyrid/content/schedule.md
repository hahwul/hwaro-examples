Two days, two halls, and the open press room running straight through. Benches run in parallel, so you cannot do everything — pick a lane and commit. Doors open thirty minutes before the first session each day, and the bar in the courtyard stays open until the presses are cleaned.

The opening lecture is free and unticketed; the courtyard holds two hundred and it is first come, first seated. Expect it to run long — punchcutters do not stop mid-sentence.

## The quiet hours

The open studio is busiest right after lunch. If you want a press to yourself, come at opening or after 15:00. The last forme locks at 15:45 so we have time to wash up before the closing pull.

Everything runs at **De Zetterij** — see the [venue](/venue/) page for the door, the trams, and the one staircase that matters. When you know your lane, [register](/register/); benches are capped and they clear in ticket order.
