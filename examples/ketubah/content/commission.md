## Timing

The studio requests at least twelve weeks between the placing of a commission and the intended date of the ceremony. Earlier commissions are welcome; rush work may be accepted upon application at a supplement of thirty per cent.

## Consultation

A first consultation of approximately one hour is offered without charge, either at the studio or by correspondence. This consultation establishes the broad outlines of the text, the ornamental direction, the dimensions, and the materials. Subsequent consultations are billed at the studio's hourly rate where they exceed three hours in total.

## Materials and Dimensions

The studio works principally upon Arches and Fabriano cotton rag in 300 and 640 gsm weights, and upon kid vellum prepared to specification by a Roman supplier. Dimensions are chosen by the couple from a standard set (16 by 20, 18 by 22, 20 by 26, 22 by 22 square) or specified freely. Larger documents, and those upon vellum, carry a corresponding supplement to the base fee.

## Fees

Fees are established after the first consultation and depend upon the complexity of the ornament, the length of the text, the dimensions, and the materials. The studio's present range is as follows:

### Contemporary Minimal

From USD 2,400 for a single ruled rectangle with plain lettering upon cotton rag. Additional scripts, multilingual texts, or mixed pigments upon a minimal frame carry supplements.

### Classical and Sephardic

From USD 4,800 for a fully ornamented contract in the Jerusalem Classic or Sephardic Andalusi traditions, with gilt leaf and ground pigments, upon cotton rag.

### Baroque, Persian, and Bespoke

From USD 7,200 for work in the Florentine Baroque, Persian, or entirely bespoke ornamental traditions, upon cotton rag; from USD 9,600 upon kid vellum.

## Schedule of Payment

One third is payable upon placement of the commission and fixes the date. One third is payable upon approval of the ornamental sketch. The balance is payable upon delivery of the completed document. The studio accepts bank transfer, cheque upon a recognised bank, and (at a small administrative supplement) letter of credit. Cash and digital currency are declined upon principle.

## Shipping and Delivery

The completed document is delivered in a hand-sewn cloth-over-board portfolio of our own manufacture. Shipping is by registered courier with full insurance; international shipping is available to all jurisdictions at cost. Personal delivery within the studio's region is offered without charge.

## Conservation

The studio maintains a perpetual record of each contract it has produced, and offers a conservation and repair service to its own work for as long as the studio exists. Clients are encouraged to return their documents to the studio every decade for inspection and, where appropriate, minor remedial work.
