# Glossary of Terms

**Document ID:** GS-003 | **Effective Date:** 2025-03-01 | **Owner:** Office of Governance and Compliance

---

## Article 1. Purpose

This glossary defines terms that carry a specific meaning when capitalized in Bureau policy documents. Terms not listed here shall be interpreted using their ordinary English meaning.

## Article 2. Defined Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Approver | The named role authorized to ratify a document for publication. |
| Authority | The legal or organizational basis upon which a policy may be enforced. |
| Binding | A document or clause whose requirements personnel are obligated to follow. |
| Effective Date | The date on which a document becomes binding on personnel. |
| Material Change | A modification that alters the scope, requirements, or applicability of a document. |
| Owner | The role accountable for the contents and currency of a document. |
| Personnel | Employees, contractors, and third parties acting on behalf of the organization. |
| Policy | A high-level statement of organizational intent. See document types. |
| Procedure | A step-by-step instruction for executing a task in compliance with policy. |
| Reaffirmed | A document reviewed and confirmed without textual change. |
| Retired | A document withdrawn from binding force; retained only for reference. |
| Review Cycle | The maximum interval between formal reviews of a document. |
| Standard | A measurable requirement that implements one or more policies. |
| Stakeholder | A person or group with a defined interest in the outcome of a document. |
| Superseded | A document replaced by a newer version of the same document. |

## Article 3. Interpretation

In case of ambiguity between this glossary and an individual document, the document's local definitions shall govern only within that document. The Office of Governance and Compliance is the final arbiter of glossary interpretation.
