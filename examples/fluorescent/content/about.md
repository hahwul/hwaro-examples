## About Fluorescent

Welcome to a world of electric color. Fluorescent is a theme that celebrates the bold, the vivid, and the unapologetically bright. Inspired by neon pop art, day-glo signage, and the raw energy of fluorescent light, this site proves that maximum vibrancy and readability can coexist.

## About This Site

This is a demonstration of the **Fluorescent** theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) — a glamorous, trendy design with day-glo colors, high contrast, and electric vibrancy. Every element is designed to catch the eye while keeping content readable.

### Features

- Day-glo color palette with hot pink, electric lime, vivid orange, and cyan
- Gradient text effects and glowing borders
- Animated hover states with color-shifting transitions
- Pop art-inspired card layouts with bold outlines
- High contrast dark background for maximum color pop
- Responsive grid design for all devices
- Pure CSS animations — no JavaScript required for effects
