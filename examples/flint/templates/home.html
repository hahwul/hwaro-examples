[ why flint ]

Fixed timesteps

The world advances in whole ticks of a constant dt. No variable-step drift, no spiral of death: an accumulator drains time in fixed slices and the renderer interpolates the remainder.

Honest manifolds

Narrowphase returns a compact contact manifold — points, normal, depth — that the sequential-impulse solver warm-starts each frame. Stacks settle; jitter stays bounded.

Rollback-safe state

Integer-anchored math and a strict iteration order make every step reproducible. Snapshot the world, restore it, re-simulate — the bytes match, so lockstep netcode and replays just work.
