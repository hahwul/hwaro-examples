## Front-Page Impact

Cover-Story is about the bold and foundational nature of structural innovation within the publishing environment. We believe that high-impact content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic magazine cover. By focusing on white surfaces, massive display typography, and high-contrast borders, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the headline.

### Cover Principles

- **Impact**: A design that prioritizes the clear "headline" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and shadows to define the boundaries of the front-page space.
- **Authority**: A layout that reflects the high-value nature of the primary story.
