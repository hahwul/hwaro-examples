Welcome to the first issue of Letterbox. This theme is designed for writers who love the clean, focused format of email newsletters but want the permanence and flexibility of a static site.

## Why a Newsletter Theme?

Email newsletters have experienced a renaissance. Readers appreciate their directness -- no algorithmic feeds, no infinite scrolling, just one person's thoughts delivered in a clean format. Letterbox brings that same aesthetic to the open web.

## What Makes Letterbox Different

Unlike traditional blog themes, Letterbox is built around the **single-column, centered layout** that newsletters are known for:

- A **660px max-width** container that feels like an email
- **Issue numbers** to give your posts a sense of continuity
- A **subscribe call-to-action** built into every page
- **Archive-style listings** that show your full history at a glance

## Getting Started

Every post in Letterbox is an "issue." Add an issue number to your front matter to track them:

```toml
+++
title = "Your Post Title"
date = "2026-03-20"

[extra]
issue = 1
+++
```

The issue number appears at the top of each post and in the archive listing, giving readers a clear sense of your publishing history.
