## Calling channels

Headset radio uses Clear-Com MS-702 with four channels: channel 1 for stage manager cues, channel 2 for follow-spot callers, channel 3 for FOH/booth cross-talk, and channel 4 as a dedicated production manager line. Test all headsets at load-in &mdash; we stock replacement cable ends in the dimmer room.
