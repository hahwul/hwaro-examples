We explore the boundaries of primary form.

This site is an active artistic exploration of the **Bauhaus design revival**. By stripping away the visual clutter of the modern web—rejecting complex color gradients, transient emojis, and heavy client-side scripts—we return to the purity of primary shapes, bold solid lines, and expressive, structural typography.

---

## our design manifesto

1. **primary solid accents**: We use raw, flat pigments: Bauhaus Red, Bauhaus Blue, and Bauhaus Ochre. No color gradients are permitted.
2. **geometric shapes**: Layouts are anchored by clean physical circles, rectangles, and triangles built directly into the CSS structure.
3. **expressive typography**: We use Syne for geometric title power, contrasted with Outfit for highly readable sans-serif reading grids.
4. **structural immutability**: Pages are generated as optimized, static file systems, compiling in single-digit milliseconds.
