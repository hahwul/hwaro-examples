Prices are in pounds, before VAT, and assume your artwork arrives print-ready.
They are honest averages, not a quote engine. Odd sizes and stubborn ideas get a
real number by email.

## Risograph, A4, per run

| Sheets | 1 colour | 2 colour | 3 colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| 50 | 72 | 108 | 148 |
| 100 | 96 | 150 | 205 |
| 250 | 170 | 268 | 360 |
| 500 | 280 | 440 | 590 |

Halve the sheet to A5 and the per-run price drops by roughly a third. Double it
to A3 and add about half again. Double-sided adds one pass per side.

## What you are paying for

- **Setup, per colour: 18.** Covers making the master, mounting the drum, and
  registering the pass. This is why one long run is cheaper per sheet than three
  short ones.
- **The second colour is the expensive one.** It doubles the passes and the
  registration work. If a job can carry on one ink plus the paper colour, we will
  steer you there.
- **Paper** is billed at cost from the [Papers](/papers/) shelf, or free if you
  bring your own that feeds cleanly.

## Letterpress

Letterpress is priced per project because impression, ink mixing, and hand
registration do not scale like riso. As a rough anchor: **100 cotton-rag cards,
one colour, from 180**. Foil adds a pass and a plate.

Deposit is 50% to book a [press slot](/visit/); the balance is due when you
collect. We hold artwork files for twelve months so reruns skip the setup fee.
