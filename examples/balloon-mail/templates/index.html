ASCEND OVER THE VALLEY at first light.

The Aerostat Club flies dawn ascents above the wine valley by hot-air balloon, and carries a single postcard per flight — sealed, numbered, and delivered to the landing field below.

§ II

Sky-Mail

One postcard, one balloon, one landing field. The amateur postal service for those who write at dawn.
No. 01

WRITE

A single postcard, by hand — cream stock, no envelope. Sealed at the pilot's table.

No. 02

CARRY

Tied to the basket by twine. Lofted with the dawn flight, witnessed by the pilot.

No. 03

DELIVER

Stamped at the field, dated by the postmaster, mailed by hand to the addressee.

Seat, per ascent $ 240

Safety brief. Boarding at 05:30 sharp. Closed shoes, no scent. Flights require clear ceiling above 3,000 ft and surface wind below 8 knots. Cancellations are refunded; postponements are flown next dawn.

To book: write to the Postmaster — post@aerostat.club. Confirmed by return post.
