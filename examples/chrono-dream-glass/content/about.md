## The Genesis of Chrono Dream Glass

We are the architects of time, constructing a medium where the past and the future coexist as parallel reflections. This project was developed as a testament to the speed and power of the **Hwaro** engine.

### Why Glassmorphism?

In the vast emptiness of the void, solid structures block out the ambient glow of creation. By employing frosted glass (`backdrop-filter`), we allow the light of passing temporal orbs to permeate our interfaces, keeping the void connected.

### Our Directive

- **Performance**: Maintain temporal integrity by ensuring blazingly fast load times.
- **Aesthetics**: Blend the elegance of space and time into the user interface.
- **Simplicity**: No clunky frameworks; pure HTML, CSS, and Hwaro templates.
