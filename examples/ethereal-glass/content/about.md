# Through the Looking Glass

This interface is an exploration of depth without weight. By layering translucent surfaces over vibrant, shifting backgrounds, we create a sense of hierarchy that feels organic rather than rigid.

The glassmorphism trend leans heavily on:
1. **Background Blur**: Creating the frosted glass effect.
2. **Semi-transparent Borders**: Defining edges that catch the "light".
3. **Vivid Backdrops**: Giving the glass something interesting to blur.

We hope you enjoy the view.
