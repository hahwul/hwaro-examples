Welcome to Crystalline Aura. This is a unique and elegant design study showcasing solid colors and strong structural elements. No gradients or emojis are used.

## Principles

- **Solid Surfaces**: Color is applied as flat fields. Edges remain crisp; transitions happen through layout, not blur.
- **Structural Clarity**: Every panel and divider participates in the visual rhythm. Spacing is treated as a first-class element.
- **Restrained Palette**: A small set of carefully chosen tones carries the entire interface, keeping focus on content rather than chrome.

## Studies

The library currently includes three short explorations, each examining a single facet of the aesthetic in depth.

- **Aura I — Field**: A reading study on flat backgrounds, undecorated headings, and the role of negative space in long-form text.
- **Aura II — Edge**: A series investigating how thin rules and high-contrast borders define hierarchy without ornament.
- **Aura III — Anchor**: Notes on aligning blocks to a stable baseline grid so structure becomes legible at a glance.
