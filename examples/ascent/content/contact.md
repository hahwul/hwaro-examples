## Studio visits

We keep a small working studio and are usually glad to show visitors the archive in person, by appointment. We're not set up for drop-ins &mdash; write ahead through the studio address above and we'll find a time.

## Response times

Licensing and general questions: within two business days. Commission briefs: within three business days, with a scope and timeline attached. Press requests during a launch week may take a little longer; we're a two-person design studio before we're anything else.
