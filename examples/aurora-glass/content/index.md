## The Beauty of Light

Aurora-Glass is about the refraction of innovation. We believe that ideas should be allowed to glow and interact with the light of the environment. By using dark, translucent elements and vibrant, glowing accents, we create a design that feels both futuristic and deeply atmospheric.

### Prism Innovation

- **Refraction**: The way the design bends and interacts with the background.
- **Glow**: Vibrant accents that draw the eye to the most important content.
- **Depth**: Using multiple layers of translucency to create a complex, immersive experience.
