# About Lunar Mirage

The Lunar Mirage is a concept born from the intersection of minimalist design and cosmic wonder.

It uses frosted glass effects (`backdrop-filter`) floating over deep space backgrounds to evoke a sense of weightlessness and tranquility.

> "In the vastness of space, the smallest light can be a mirage of hope."
