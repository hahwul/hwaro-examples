## OUR MISSION

Founded in 2018, **Strobe** was born from a desire to return to the raw, uncompromising roots of the electronic music scene.

### THE PHILOSOPHY

We don't do vip tables. We don't do commercial hits. We provide a space for artistic expression and communal liberation through repetitive, driving rhythms.

### SAFETY & ACCESSIBILITY

While our main floor utilizes high-intensity strobe lighting, we are committed to accessibility. Our website includes a **strobe toggle** to disable flashing animations, and our venue provides quiet zones and ear protection for all guests.

*Respect the space. Respect each other.*
