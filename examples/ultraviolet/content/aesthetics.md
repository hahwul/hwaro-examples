The concept behind the Ultraviolet theme is to create a striking contrast that demands attention, yet maintains an underlying elegance. By relying entirely on solid colors, sharp typography, and deliberate spacing, we avoid the softness of gradients.

## Core Principles

The design language is built upon a few core tenets:

1. **High Contrast:** Using deep, dark backgrounds (`#0b0512`) juxtaposed with vibrant neon accents (`#b026ff`).
2. **Structural Lines:** Utilizing thin borders to create a sense of order and structure, reminiscent of wireframes or blueprints, but executed with high-fidelity color.
3. **Typographic Hierarchy:** Mixing the classic elegance of 'Playfair Display' for headings with the stark, modern clarity of 'Inter' for body text.

> "True elegance is not about adding complexity, but about achieving maximum impact with minimal elements."

This approach forces us to be intentional with every line drawn and every color applied. There is no hiding behind blurry shadows or smooth transitions. Every element must justify its existence.
