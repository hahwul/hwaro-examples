## The Short Story

Novella is built for stories that are meant to be consumed in a single, focused sitting. We believe that innovation doesn't always require a massive tome; sometimes a concise, beautifully typeset narrative is more effective.

### Focus and Space

- **Space**: Generous line height and margins give the words room to breathe.
- **Typography**: A classic, readable serif that feels like printed text.
- **Pacing**: A design that encourages a slow, deliberate reading experience.

---

> "The novella finds its power not in length, but in its absolute focus on a single, compelling idea."
