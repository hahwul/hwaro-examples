The two most requested items in support tickets this quarter were the same
two items, over and over: let us get the raw numbers out, and stop deleting
last year's data before we can compare against it. 1.3 answers both.

<!-- more -->

## CSV export

Every dashboard view now has an export control that generates a CSV for
the selected date range and granularity — hourly, daily, or monthly — with
columns for path, referrer, visitors, and sessions. Exports are generated
on demand, not pre-computed, so there is no delay for common ranges and no
storage cost for ranges nobody asks for. Files are streamed directly to
your browser; we do not keep a copy after the request completes.

```bash
curl "https://api.ohm.eu/v1/export?domain=example.eu&from=2025-01-01&to=2025-12-31&granularity=daily" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $OHM_TOKEN" -o traffic-2025.csv
```

## Retention increase

Default retention on Growth moves from 12 to 14 months, and Scale moves
from 24 to 26 months, both at no price change. The extra two months exist
for one reason: so a January dashboard can always show the previous
January next to it, even for a customer who joined mid-year. Starter's
6-month window is unchanged, since it's designed for short-lived projects
rather than year-over-year comparison.

## Dashboard tile

Export lives behind a small icon button next to each stat tile rather than
as a separate page — pick a tile, pick a range, get a file. It does not
introduce a chart anywhere; the tile you exported from still shows the
same bare number it always did.
