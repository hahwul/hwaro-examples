Pricing scales with pageviews, not with how many people on your team look at
the dashboard. Every tier includes every feature — there is no "enterprise
only" version of privacy.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly traffic | Price | Retention |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | up to 10,000 pageviews | Free | 6 months |
| Growth | up to 250,000 pageviews | €29 / month | 14 months |
| Scale | up to 2,000,000 pageviews | €99 / month | 26 months |
| Custom | above 2,000,000 pageviews | Talk to us | Negotiable |

Overage on Starter and Growth is metered at €0.10 per 1,000 additional
pageviews and shows up as a single line on the following month's invoice —
we don't hard-cap your site mid-month. Scale customers agree a committed
rate up front instead.

## What's included at every tier

- Unlimited sites under one account
- The full event API for server-side and backend tracking
- CSV export of any date range, any granularity
- Region pinning to Frankfurt or Dublin
- A signed data processing agreement, issued automatically from account settings

## Billing

Invoices are issued by Ohm Analytics B.V., registered in Amsterdam, in
euros. We accept card and SEPA direct debit. There is no annual contract —
every plan is month-to-month, and downgrading takes effect at the start of
the next billing cycle with no penalty. If you cancel, we hold your
aggregated history for 30 days in case you come back, then delete it.

## Nonprofits and open source

Registered nonprofits and public open-source projects get Growth-tier
limits at no charge. Email billing with a link to your registration or
repository and we'll apply the discount within one business day.
