Vehicle integration operations are complete. All stages have been mated and electrical connections verified. The integrated vehicle is now in launch configuration on Pad 39A.

## Completed Milestones

- First stage erection and leveling
- Second stage mate and separation system verification
- Payload fairing encapsulation
- Umbilical connection and leak checks
- Integrated systems test (IST) passed

The vehicle now proceeds to the wet dress rehearsal phase, where propellant loading operations will be verified end-to-end without committing to ignition.
