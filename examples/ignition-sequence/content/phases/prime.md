## Overview

The PRIME phase initiates the launch sequence with comprehensive systems verification. Every subsystem must report NOMINAL before advancing to IGNITE.

## Pre-Flight Checklist

- Flight computer boot and self-test sequence
- Inertial measurement unit alignment and calibration
- Propellant tank pressurization to flight levels
- Ground support equipment disconnection verification
- Range safety transponder activation and verification
- Weather monitoring stations confirm GO criteria

## Systems Under Review

All nine primary subsystems undergo automated verification during PRIME:

1. **Propulsion** -- Main engine controller initialization
2. **Avionics** -- Flight computer redundancy check
3. **Power** -- Battery and generator status verification
4. **Thermal** -- Cryogenic system temperature monitoring
5. **Communications** -- Uplink and downlink signal confirmation
6. **Guidance** -- Navigation system alignment
7. **Structures** -- Hold-down clamp integrity check
8. **Ground Systems** -- Umbilical connection status
9. **Range Safety** -- Flight termination system arm

## Duration

T-120:00 to T-60:00 -- Approximately 60 minutes under nominal conditions.
