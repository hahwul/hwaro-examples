Four acts, four slots, one power strip. Every set at Penumbra is patched live in the room — nothing is pre-rendered, and the artists talk through what they are doing as they go. The programme runs from the most structured set of the night to the loosest, so the room can settle as the hours get later. Times below are set times, not door times.
