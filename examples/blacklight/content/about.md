## The Concept

The Blacklight aesthetic is inspired by ultraviolet reactivity. It transforms negative space into a canvas for brilliant, saturated hues.

### Design Principles

*   **Absolute Darkness**: Backgrounds are stripped to their minimal, darkest state to allow foreground elements to command attention.
*   **Vivid Illumination**: Accent colors such as cyan and magenta act as the light source, providing structure, hierarchy, and interaction cues.
*   **Refined Elegance**: The design avoids chaotic embellishments. There are no excessive gradients or emojis here—only sharp, deliberate visual impact.

This layout demonstrates how a static site can be both radically stylized and elegantly minimal.
