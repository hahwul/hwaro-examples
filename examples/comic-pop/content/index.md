## Graphic Tactility

Comic-Pop is about the vibrant and dynamic nature of innovation within the graphic environment. We believe that action-oriented content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a silver age comic book. By focusing on high-contrast black borders, solid shadows, and bold display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the panel.

### Pop Principles

- **Energy**: A design that prioritizes the clear "blast" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and flat colors to define the boundaries of the action space.
- **Vibrancy**: A layout that evokes the feeling of mid-20th-century pop culture.
