## The Beauty of the Draft

Manuscript is about the value of the unpolished idea. We believe that innovation is often found in the raw, energetic state of the first draft. By focusing on the handwritten aesthetic and the integrity of the creative process, we reveal the underlying logic of the work before it is obscured by the final polish.

### Raw Innovation

- **Process**: The visible path of the idea from inception to draft.
- **Potential**: The energy that exists in the unrealized state of the work.
- **Honesty**: A design that doesn't try to hide its origins.

---

> "The draft is the most honest state of any great work."
