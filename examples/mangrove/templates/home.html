Mangrove

A Coastal Navigation Handbook for Small-Craft Skippers

Scope and Datum

This handbook sets out pilotage — coastal navigation within sight of land — for craft under twelve metres working the coast between Sable Head and the Coot Skerries. Every method here can be worked with a hand-bearing compass, a soft pencil, and a watch; none of it depends on a screen staying lit.

Bearings are given true and referenced to WGS84 unless marked (M) for magnetic. Tidal heights are quoted above Chart Datum, not mean sea level — read the difference off the chart before trusting a sounding close to it.
