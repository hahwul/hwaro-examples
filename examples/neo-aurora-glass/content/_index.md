# Welcome to Neo Aurora Glass

Dive into a world where neon auroras meet sleek, frosted glass. This theme showcases the bleeding edge of modern web design, blending **deep space aesthetics** with **vibrant cyberpunk elements**.

## Features

- **Animated Backgrounds:** Immersive, slow-moving aurora gradients that breathe life into the void.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** Translucent, frosted panels that create depth and visual hierarchy.
- **Vibrant Neon Accents:** Cyan, magenta, and deep purple highlights that pop against the dark background.
- **Modern Typography:** Utilizing *Inter* for readability and *Space Grotesk* for striking display headers.
