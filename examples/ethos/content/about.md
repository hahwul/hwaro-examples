# About This Template

Engineering Ethos is a Hwaro example designed for teams who want to document their culture and principles in a clean, professional manner.

## Design Philosophy

This template follows a strict design philosophy:
- **No Gradients:** We use solid colors and sharp borders to create a stable, reliable feel.
- **No Emojis:** We use SVG icons and strong typography to maintain a professional tone.
- **Warm Palette:** The combination of warm gray and coral creates a welcoming yet serious environment.

## How to Use

1. **Clone this example.**
2. **Update the principles** in `content/index.md` to reflect your team's values.
3. **Customize the colors** in `templates/header.html` if needed (but keep the no-gradient rule!).
4. **Deploy with Hwaro.**

> "A team without principles is like a ship without a rudder."
