The back is the new front. We have grown tired of mystery.

For this look we made a single, perfect, uninterrupted cut from the base of the skull to the tailbone. The client was positioned in front of three-way mirrors so she could watch the transformation in real time. She said it felt like unzipping a very expensive dress that had been sewn onto her at birth.
