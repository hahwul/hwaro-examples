## More Is More

Maximalism rejects the notion that restraint equals sophistication. Instead, it celebrates abundance, contrast, and the sheer joy of visual overload. Every surface is an opportunity. Every color deserves a companion. Every pattern craves another pattern.

## The Philosophy

Where minimalism whispers, maximalism **screams**. It draws from Art Deco opulence, Memphis Group irreverence, Baroque grandeur, and contemporary streetwear culture. It says yes to clashing palettes, layered textures, bold typography, and unapologetic decoration.

## Why Maximalism?

- **Self-expression** — your space, your rules
- **Warmth** — rich layers create inviting, lived-in environments
- **Storytelling** — every element carries meaning and memory
- **Joy** — bold design sparks bold emotions
