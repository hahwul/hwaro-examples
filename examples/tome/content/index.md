## Structural Significance

Tome is about the weight of the permanent record. We believe that true innovation should be established with the same authority as a massive, bound volume. By focusing on the structural significance of the layout and the heavyweight nature of the typography, we create a design that feels both immovable and deeply meaningful.

### Heavyweight Innovation

- **Authority**: A design that establishes itself as the final word on the subject.
- **Permanence**: Built to endure through the removal of anything fleeting or unstable.
- **Depth**: The layers of meaning that can only be achieved through sustained, massive effort.
