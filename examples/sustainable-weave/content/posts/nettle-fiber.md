While industrial cotton requires massive volumes of water and synthetic pesticides, wild nettles grow abundantly in marginal soils with virtually zero artificial support. When harvested and retted, the inner bark yields a incredibly strong bast fiber with a hollow structure.

### Hollow Core Insulation
This hollow core delivers natural thermo-regulation: keeping the wearer exceptionally cool in summer by dispersing body heat, and providing dense air insulation pockets in winter.

> "A plant often treated as a weed is actually one of our strongest circular allies."
