Unlike synthetic coal-tar derived indigo, natural indigo extracted from the *Indigofera* plant is fully organic and carbon-neutral. The fermentation process relies on a living ecosystem of bacteria, sugar (often molasses or dried fruits), and wood ash lye to reduce the pigment into a soluble state.

### The Living Shade
Dipping fabric into a natural vat yields a living shade. When the fiber emerges, it is a bright lime green, transforming into a deep, celestial blue only as it drinks in oxygen from the open air. This physical alchemy results in blues that age beautifully, telling the tale of the garment's wear over years.
