## Closing Formation

Final roll call of the day. All units form at the Main Parade Ground.

### Closing Sequence

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 15:50 | Units move to parade ground |
| 16:00 | Final roll call -- full alphabetical muster |
| 16:20 | Attendance discrepancies reconciled |
| 16:30 | Day summary read by adjutant |
| 16:45 | Dismissal order issued |
| 17:00 | Retreat -- colors lowered |

### End-of-Day Attendance Summary

All unit leaders submit final attendance reports to HQ by **17:30**. Reports must include:

- Total present at morning formation
- Total present at midday formation
- Total present at closing formation
- Names and reasons for any absences
