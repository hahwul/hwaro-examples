## Morning Formation Protocol

All personnel report to designated assembly points by **05:50** for the **06:00** formation.

### Sequence of Events

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 05:50 | Unit leaders verify positions |
| 05:55 | Platoon sergeants begin headcount |
| 06:00 | Roll call commences -- alphabetical by surname |
| 06:15 | Absent personnel reported to HQ |
| 06:20 | Formation dismissed to inspection areas |

### Assembly Point Assignments

- **Point A (North Quad)** -- Alpha Company, 1st Battalion
- **Point B (East Wing)** -- Bravo Company, 1st Battalion
- **Point C (Main Hall)** -- Charlie Company, 2nd Battalion
- **Point D (South Field)** -- Headquarters & Support
