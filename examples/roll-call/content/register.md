All personnel must register with their unit commander prior to the assembly date. Registration closes **14 March 2026 at 23:59**.

### Registration Requirements

- Full name (surname, given names)
- Rank and unit designation
- Assembly point assignment
- Emergency contact information

### Important Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| 01 Mar 2026 | Registration opens |
| 14 Mar 2026 | Registration closes (23:59) |
| 15 Mar 2026 | Assembly day |

### Contact

For registration inquiries, contact the Assembly Coordination Office at **assembly@rollcall.mil** or extension **4200**.
