## Mission

The Archive exists to collect, catalog, and preserve documents of lasting value. In the tradition of great libraries, we believe that knowledge organized is knowledge made accessible.

## Cataloging Standards

Each document in our collection is assigned a classification number following the conventions of library science. Metadata fields include author, publication date, subject headings, and a brief abstract. This structured approach ensures that every item can be discovered through multiple pathways -- by subject, by date, by author, or through full-text search.

## Technology

This archive is generated by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and lightweight static site generator. The design draws inspiration from library card catalogs, archival finding aids, and the reading rooms of research institutions.
