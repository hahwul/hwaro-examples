Welcome to the first post in the **Zenyth** theme. This theme takes inspiration from cybernetic glow and dark space.

> "The true light shines brightest against the darkest canvas."

### Typography

Zenyth uses **Inter** for standard text and **JetBrains Mono** for code and meta-information. This provides a sleek, technical look while maintaining excellent readability.

Here is an example of code:

```rust
fn main() {
    println!("Welcome to Zenyth.");
}
```

And inline `code snippets` are styled beautifully.

### The aesthetic

The primary background color is `#0b0f19`, providing a very dark blue/black slate that minimizes eye strain. Accents are brought in with `#00f2fe`, giving a modern edge to the entire layout. Enjoy your time here!
