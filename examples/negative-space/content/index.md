## The Power of the Void

Negative-Space is about the structural importance of what is *not* there. We believe that innovation is often best revealed through the absence of traditional elements. By focusing on the relationship between presence and absence, we create a design that feels both bold and profoundly intentional.

<div class="negative-block">
    <h3>STRUCTURAL ABSENCE</h3>
    <p>We define the boundaries of the innovation through the void. The content exists not on the surface, but in the space created by the removal of the unnecessary.</p>
</div>

### Designing the Void

- **Contrast**: Using extreme opposites to define the shape of the work.
- **Absence**: The intentional removal of primary elements.
- **Tension**: The visual energy that exists between the present and the absent.
