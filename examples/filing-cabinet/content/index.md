## Systematic Tactility

Filing-Cabinet is about the organized and systematic nature of structural innovation. We believe that archival content deserves a presentation that is both utilitarian and deeply substantial. By focusing on industrial-inspired textures and shadows and a structured, grid-based layout, we create a design that feels both authoritative and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the system.

### Organizing Principles

- **Order**: A design that prioritizes the logical arrangement of information.
- **System**: Using industrial textures to define the boundaries of the archival space.
- **Permanence**: A layout that reflects the long-term value of the records.
