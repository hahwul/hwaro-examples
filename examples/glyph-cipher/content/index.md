## about this channel

glyph_cipher is a public research log for a small team of cryptographers, network operators, and software defenders. We post observations of cryptographic protocols, post-mortems of compromised systems we have analyzed, and occasional speculative notes on what the failures suggest about the practice of building secure software.

Every transmission is hashed and signed at publication. The transmission ID, the operator's handle, and the SHA-256 hash are recorded in our integrity log. If a transmission has been edited after publication, the integrity log will say so.

We post under handles. We do not run advertisements. We do not track readers. We do not respond to unsolicited requests for private analysis.
