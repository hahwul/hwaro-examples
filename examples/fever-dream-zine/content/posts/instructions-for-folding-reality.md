**WARNING:** Do not attempt while sober. Do not attempt while certain of anything. Side effects include duplicate selves, spontaneous poetry, and the sudden understanding that "up" was always a suggestion.

### STEP ONE: Locate the Crease

Look at any ordinary object. A coffee cup. Your own hand. The last text you sent that you immediately regretted.

There is a line where the thing stops being one thing and becomes another. It is usually invisible until you stop looking directly at it.

Run your finger along the edge of your vision. You'll feel it — a slight resistance, like silk catching on a splinter.

That's the crease.

### STEP TWO: Ignore the Screaming

The first time you fold, something will scream.

It might be the air. It might be your childhood. It might be the version of you that was supposed to become an accountant.

This is normal.

The screaming is just the sound of the universe noticing it's being edited. Wave politely. Keep folding.

### STEP THREE: Commit to the New Angle

Press firmly.

You will feel a pop behind your eyes like a soap bubble made of memory. This is the moment the new timeline branches.

In the new branch you are slightly to the left of yourself. Or you have always had an extra finger on your left hand. Or the sky is now a color that doesn't exist yet and everyone acts like it's always been that way.

Do not panic. Do not unfold. The fold is a commitment.

### STEP FOUR: Do Not Look Directly at the You You Left Behind

There will be another version of you standing exactly where you were a moment ago, looking confused and slightly transparent.

They are fine. Or they will be. Or they already were.

Wave. Or don't. Both choices create new branches.

### STEP FIVE: Live in the Crease

Most people spend their entire lives trying to unfold back to the "original" shape.

There isn't one.

The crease is the only honest place. Everything else is just different ways of lying to yourself about which version is real.

### EMERGENCY UNFOLDING PROCEDURE

If you create a version of yourself that wants to hurt people, stop.

If you create a version of yourself that is kinder than you have ever been capable of being, study them. Copy their homework.

If you accidentally fold yourself out of existence entirely, congratulations. You have achieved the highest possible score.

Now go be a different shape.

The universe is running out of interesting configurations.

It needs your help.
