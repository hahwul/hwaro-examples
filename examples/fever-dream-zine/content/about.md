## We Are All Just Very Convincing Hallucinations

This zine was born in a bathtub in 1969 during a thunderstorm that lasted six weeks. The editor (me, or possibly the version of me that only exists between 2:17 and 2:19 a.m.) woke up with ink on their hands and the phrase "ALL COLORS, NO APOLOGIES" tattooed backwards on their forehead in mirror writing.

We have been publishing irregularly ever since — sometimes weekly, sometimes once every seventeen years, sometimes before the reader was even born.

### Our Editorial Policy

- If it makes your third eye twitch, it goes in.
- If it makes perfect sense, burn it immediately.
- We accept submissions written on napkins, the back of gas station receipts, or directly into the fabric of spacetime via intense staring.
- No topic is too sacred. Especially the sacred ones.
