# About Playlist

Playlist is a curated space for music lovers. Each entry is a carefully assembled collection of tracks -- spanning genres, moods, and eras -- designed to accompany a moment, a feeling, or a state of mind.

Whether it is a late-night electronic mix, a Sunday morning acoustic set, or a deep dive into a specific artist's catalogue, every playlist here is built with intention.

## How It Works

Each playlist includes full track listings with artist credits, duration, and genre tags. Browse by mood, genre, or simply explore what is new.

## The Curator

This site is maintained by someone who believes the right sequence of songs can change the shape of a day. All playlists are original compilations -- no algorithms, no sponsors, just taste.
