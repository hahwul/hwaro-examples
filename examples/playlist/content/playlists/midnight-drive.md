## About This Playlist

Built for those stretches of highway where the city thins out and the streetlights start to space apart. The sequencing matters here -- it opens with Kavinsky's muscular synth lines, builds through the anthemic middle section, and winds down into the reflective closing tracks.

> The best driving music does not demand your attention. It shares the road with you.

The electronic selections lean toward the warmer end of the spectrum. No harsh drops, no abrupt tempo shifts. Every transition is deliberate, designed to keep the momentum without jarring you out of the zone.

## Listening Notes

- Best experienced with windows down and volume up
- Pairs well with highways after 11 PM
- The Chromatics track is the emotional center of the playlist
- HOME's "Resonance" provides the perfect cool-down before the closer
