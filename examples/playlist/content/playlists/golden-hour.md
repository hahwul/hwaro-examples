## About This Playlist

Golden hour lasts roughly forty minutes, and this playlist is calibrated to match. It opens as the light starts to turn warm and closes as the last color drains from the sky.

Beach House appears twice because their sound is practically synonymous with this time of day. "Myth" sets the tone with its shimmering guitars, and "Space Song" deepens it. Between them, Cigarettes After Sex and Neil Young provide contrasting textures that keep the palette from becoming too uniform.

## The Arc

The sequencing follows the light:

- **Tracks 1-3**: Full golden warmth, bright and open
- **Tracks 4-6**: The light begins to soften, more intimate
- **Tracks 7-8**: Approaching dusk, reflective
- **Tracks 9-10**: The last light fades, bittersweet and beautiful

> Every day ends differently, but the best ones end with the right music.

## Listening Notes

- Mazzy Star's "Fade Into You" is placed at track 9 for a reason -- it is the emotional climax
- M83's "Outro" is the only acceptable closing track; nothing follows that orchestral swell
- Best experienced outdoors or near a west-facing window
- This playlist does not repeat well -- it is designed as a single arc
