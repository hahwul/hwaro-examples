## About This Story

*The Last Cartographer* is a serialized fantasy novel exploring themes of discovery, the nature of maps and territories, and the boundaries between the known and unknown world.

New chapters are published weekly. The story is currently ongoing, with twelve parts planned in total.

## About the Theme

Quill is a Hwaro theme designed for creative writing and serialized fiction. It prioritizes the reading experience above all else, with careful typography, comfortable reading width, and navigation designed for chapter-by-chapter consumption.

### Features

- Reading-optimized layout with narrow body width and generous font size
- Chapter epigraphs and decorative first-letter styling
- Series navigation for moving between chapters
- Genre, word count, and serialization status metadata
- Light, paper-like color scheme for extended reading sessions
