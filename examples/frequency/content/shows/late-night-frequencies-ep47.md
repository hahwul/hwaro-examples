Tonight we tried something different. No track under eight minutes. Generous pauses between songs. No commentary during playback. Just sound, silence, and time.

## The Concept

Slow Radio is an idea borrowed from the Norwegian broadcasting tradition of "slow television" -- extended, unhurried coverage of mundane events. A train journey. A fireplace. The idea is that duration changes perception. A five-minute song is a song. A twelve-minute song is an experience.

## Listener Response

The phone lines were quiet -- which felt appropriate. But the messages that came in afterward were some of the most thoughtful we have received all year.

One listener wrote: "I fell asleep during the third track and woke up during the fifth. It felt like the show was designed for that."

It was.
