## Animated Depth

Cel-Shade is about the layered and frame-focused nature of innovation within the animation environment. We believe that visual content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the early digital animation era. By focusing on dark backgrounds, flat shadows, and bold display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the frame.

### Shade Principles

- **Layering**: A design that prioritizes the clear arrangement of visual depth.
- **Tactility**: Using flat shadows and lines to define the boundaries of the animated space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of late-20th-century digital ink and paint.
