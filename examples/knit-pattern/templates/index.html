{# ============== ERRATA ============== #}
IV.

Errata

Corrections to past issues, typed up by the desk. If you are knitting from an earlier print, please write these in by hand.

  • Issue 13 · p. 18

    Row 24 of the yoke chart should read “k2, yo, ssk, k2, k2tog, yo, k2” — not “k2, yo, k2tog, k2, ssk, yo, k2”. The chart on p. 19 is correct; the written line is the typo.

    Filed {{ current_year }}.06
  • Issue 12 · p. 7

    Finished bust for size L should be 108 cm, not 106 cm. The schematic was drawn to 108; the table on p. 7 was set from an earlier draft.

    Filed {{ current_year }}.03
  • Issue 11 · p. 24

    The thumb gusset on the right-hand mitten should mirror the left. The chart printed on p. 24 has the increases on the wrong side of the thumbgore. A corrected single-page insert is available on request.

    Filed 2024.11
