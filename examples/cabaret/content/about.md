# About Cabaret

Cabaret is a celebration of the theatrical, the glamorous, and the daringly bold. This theme draws inspiration from the golden age of cabaret — smoky Parisian stages, sequined curtains catching the spotlight, and the electric anticipation before the show begins.

## Our Philosophy

**Showtime Energy** — Every page should feel like the moment the curtain rises. Bold colors, dramatic contrasts, and an unmistakable sense of spectacle.

**Red Velvet & Gold** — The classic palette of the cabaret stage: deep crimson curtains framing golden spotlights, dark shadows hiding infinite possibility.

**Drama in Every Detail** — From sweeping gradients to theatrical typography, every element is designed to captivate and hold attention like a performer commands the stage.

## The Art of the Stage

The cabaret tradition dates back to 1880s Paris — intimate venues where artists, poets, and musicians gathered to perform. From the Moulin Rouge to the Kit Kat Klub, these spaces were defined by their atmospheric lighting, rich textures, and the intoxicating blend of art and entertainment.

In the digital realm, we capture that spirit through dramatic color contrasts, theatrical animations, and design that refuses to blend into the background.
