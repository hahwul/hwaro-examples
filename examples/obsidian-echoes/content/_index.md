Welcome to **Obsidian Echoes**.

This theme features a sophisticated dark aesthetic, deep space backgrounds, glowing neon obsidian and cyan accents, and refined glassmorphism UI elements. It is designed to showcase portfolios and premium content with an immersive experience.

### Key Features
- **Frosted Glass Panels:** Utilize `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)` for smooth translucent containers.
- **Glowing Elements:** Vibrant radial gradients that bring the dark canvas to life.
- **Premium Typography:** Integrates `Space Grotesk` and `Inter` for clear, futuristic text rendering.
