<section class="section">
  <p class="section-label">in the pool of light</p>
  <h2 class="section-title">what this room is for</h2>
  <p style="text-align:center;max-width:620px;margin:0 auto;color:var(--ink);line-height:2;">A black-box theater is not a smaller version of a proscenium. It is a different kind of room. No fly tower, no orchestra pit, no rake. The audience sits five feet from the actors. Every breath is heard. Every small gesture is a large one.</p>
</section>
