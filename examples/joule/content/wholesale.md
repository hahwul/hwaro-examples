We bake a limited number of wholesale loaves each morning alongside the
retail bake, from the same dough, on the same hearth. There is no separate
"wholesale recipe" and no shortcut version. What lands on your pass is the
country boule we would hand to anyone standing at our counter.

## Who we bake for

We work best with kitchens and shops within a short drive of Alderbrook,
where a loaf baked at 4 a.m. can be on your shelf by 7. Our current partners
are a handful of neighbourhood restaurants, two cafés, and a greengrocer who
sells our miche by the quarter. We keep the list small on purpose so we can
bake to actual demand rather than to a forecast.

## Standing orders

Wholesale is order-ahead only. Send us your standing weekly quantities and a
delivery or collection window and we lock it into the bake. Because we mix to
order, we ask for changes by 2 p.m. the day before — after the levain is
built, the day's volume is fixed.

## What we offer at trade

- **Country boule**, whole or par-scored for your own final bake.
- **Seeded rye miche**, whole wheels or halves.
- **Canelés** by the tray of twenty-four, baked to your service time.

Trade pricing depends on volume and standing frequency; it lands well below
counter price for a committed weekly order. Delivery is free above a modest
minimum within the Alderbrook ring, or collect from the back door before we
open to the public.

To open an account, write to trade@joulebakery.example with your kitchen,
your rough weekly numbers, and the days you would want bread. We will send
back a sample loaf and a price sheet, and bake you a trial week before either
of us commits to anything.
