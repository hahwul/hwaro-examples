The single most reliable way to eat Joule bread is to never leave it to
chance. The Bread Club is a standing weekly order: you tell us what you want
and when, we set it aside before the doors open, and it waits behind the
counter with your name on the bag while the walk-in queue works through what
is left.

## How it works

You choose a plan and a pickup day. On that morning your loaves are reserved
the moment they come off the hearth — before anything hits the front shelf.
Come in any time before we close and it is yours. Miss a week? Pause from
your account by 6 p.m. the night before and we simply will not bake it.

## The plans

- **The Loaf — 6.00 a week.** One country boule, every week. The classic.
- **The Table — 15.00 a week.** Two loaves of your choosing plus a bag of
  four canelés. Enough for a household that takes toast seriously.
- **The Standing Six — 16.00 a fortnight.** Six canelés, every other Friday,
  for the person who has quietly organised their life around them.

## The fine print

Payment is monthly, in advance. There is no minimum term and no fee to
leave; just tell us and we will close it out at the end of the month. Pickup
only — we hold reservations until close on your day, after which an unclaimed
order goes out onto the shelf so it finds a home the same morning it was
baked. Nothing good should go stale waiting.

To join, have a word with whoever is at the counter, or write to
hello@joulebakery.example with your plan and preferred day.
