We are a small shopfront on a quiet lane, five minutes on foot from the
Alderbrook market square. There is a red door, a chalkboard with the day's
bake, and usually a short, good-natured queue. No table service, no seating —
this is a bakery, not a café. Come, choose, and carry it home warm.

"Sold out" is a real time here, usually somewhere between eleven and noon on
a weekday. Canelés go earliest. If your heart is set on something specific,
come before nine or reserve it through the [Bread Club](@/subscriptions.md).

## Finding us

**Joule Bakehouse**
14 Ferment Lane, Alderbrook

We are the red door between the framer and the bicycle shop. The lane is
pedestrian-only, so park on Market Street and walk the last two minutes.
There is a cycle rack directly outside and step-free access at the door.
