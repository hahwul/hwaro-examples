## About Antimatter

Antimatter is an experimental design blog built on the principle that inversion reveals truth. What happens when you reverse every assumption about how a website should look? When dark becomes the canvas and light becomes the ink? When the negative space defines the positive?

## Design Philosophy

This theme treats color inversion as its core design language:

- **Inverted color scheme** -- the opposite of conventional dark and light modes
- **mix-blend-mode: difference** -- bold visual manipulation where elements interact with their backgrounds
- **Negative space as structure** -- absence defines presence
- **Collision energy borders** -- matter meets antimatter at element boundaries
- **Monospace accents** -- Space Mono for metadata, evoking terminal aesthetics
- **Sharp geometry** -- minimal border-radius, precise edges

## The Concept

In particle physics, every particle has a corresponding antiparticle. When they meet, they annihilate each other, releasing pure energy. This theme applies that concept to design: conventional expectations collide with their inversions, and the energy of that collision becomes the visual experience.
