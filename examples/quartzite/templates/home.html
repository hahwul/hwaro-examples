Three Guarantees

01

Idempotent by construction

Every write accepts an Idempotency-Key. Replays of a known key return the original result — never a second charge, never a duplicate invoice.

02

Versioned, never silent

Response shapes only change behind a dated version header. Nothing you integrate against today is rewritten under you tomorrow.

03

Signed, verifiable events

Webhook payloads carry an HMAC signature you verify before you trust them. No event reaches your handler unauthenticated.
