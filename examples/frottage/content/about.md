# About Frottage

The *Frottage* theme explores the intersection of brutalist architecture and printmaking techniques within a digital space.

By explicitly avoiding smooth gradients, shadows, and ubiquitous colorful icons, the design forces focus onto the actual content and the typographic grid. The subtle background texture mimics paper grain, an homage to the physical act of rubbing graphite on paper over a rough surface.

This theme is designed for art portfolios, architectural blogs, and critical essays.
