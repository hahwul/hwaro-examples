## Sonic Tactility

Vinyl-Record is about the physical and continuous nature of innovation within the music environment. We believe that high-fidelity content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the analog audio era. By focusing on dark, grooved surfaces, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the groove.

### Spin Principles

- **Continuity**: A design that prioritizes the clear "flow" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using grooved textures to define the boundaries of the musical space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century audio technology.
