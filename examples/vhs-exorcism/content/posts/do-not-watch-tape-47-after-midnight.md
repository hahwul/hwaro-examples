The label said exactly that in thick red letters.

I watched it anyway. I was 19. I was invincible.

At 12:01 the picture stabilized.

A living room. Christmas lights still up in January. A little girl on the floor playing with a doll that had no face.

At 12:03 she looks directly at the camera and says my name.

Not the name I use now. The name I had before I changed it in 1989.

At 12:07 she stands up, walks toward the lens until her face fills the frame, and whispers:

"You are already inside the tape. You just haven't reached the end yet."

The tape ends at 11:59.

It has always ended at 11:59.

I have watched it 41 times. Every time the runtime is exactly 11 minutes and 59 seconds.

The 42nd time I will not be the one holding the remote.
