We tested it for six nights.

We put a chair in front of the lens.  
We put the station manager.  
We put a test pattern card.  
We even put a live chicken (don't ask).

Every time we rolled tape, the only thing on the monitor was the same man.

He is always facing the camera. He never blinks. His mouth moves like he is speaking but the audio track is pure carrier tone at exactly 13 kHz.

When we played the tapes back in a different building the face was still there, but now he was smiling like he recognized the room.

We burned the tapes. The ash formed the shape of a face on the concrete floor of the parking lot.

The night guard still refuses to talk about what he saw when he tried to sweep it up.

There is only one face on Channel 13.  
It has always been yours.
