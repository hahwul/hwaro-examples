We were just kids flipping channels after the national anthem.

The screen went to snow. Then the bars. Then the girl.

She stood perfectly centered in the SMPTE pattern like she had always been there. When we waved — just once, laughing — she waved back.

Not a tape delay. Not a reflection.

Her hand moved the exact same moment ours did.

We turned the set off.

It turned itself back on at 3:33.
