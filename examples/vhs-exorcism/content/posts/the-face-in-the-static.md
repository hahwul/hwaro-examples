Subject 13 was shown 47 minutes of pure white noise.

When asked what they saw, they described a man standing just behind the snow "as if the snow was a curtain and he was waiting for the wind to move it."

We frame-averaged the recording.

The face appears in 412 of 400 frames.

It is the same face in every enhanced still. It is always looking slightly to the left of camera, as though it is watching someone just off-screen.

When we showed the enhanced stills to Subject 13 they began screaming and did not stop for 19 minutes.

The last thing they said before they were taken away was:

"He isn't in the static. The static is in him."
