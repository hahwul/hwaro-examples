A pristine, hand-knitted Shetland wool sweater sourced from the northern islands of Scotland. Featuring traditional multi-colored geometric bands dyed using regional plant extracts.

### Pattern & Knit Craft
The Fair Isle knitting technique involves complex floats of colored wool yarns to create high-integrity, dense fabrics. The organic earthy palette—combining ochre, sage, rust, and natural sand—renders it a highly sought-after collector garment.
