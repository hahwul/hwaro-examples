# Welcome to Cosmic Velvet Glass

A stunning ethereal experience combining deep cosmic aesthetics with velvet glass panels. The theme highlights neon glows and floating elements with subtle backdrop filters.

## Design Pillars

The theme is built around three ideas that have been refined across releases since late 2025.

### Depth Without Noise

Every panel uses a single layered backdrop filter rather than a stack of competing shadows. The result reads as depth on a high-density display while still printing cleanly on plain paper for archival exports.

### Restrained Color

The palette ships with five hues. Three are reserved for surfaces, two for accents. Adding more is possible through CSS variables, but the included presets are tuned to work together under both dark and dim ambient light.

### Predictable Motion

Floating orbs follow a slow, deterministic loop. Animations pause when the visitor sets their system preference to reduced motion. Nothing pulses on hover, nothing chases the cursor, and nothing surprises the reader during a long article.
