# On Hairlines

A one-pixel rule is a promise: here, the thought changed. A two-pixel rule is an argument. A four-pixel rule is a headline. Anything thicker is a bruise.

Pick one weight and stay with it. A page with three rule weights is a page with three competing authors. The reader will not pick a favorite. They will leave.
