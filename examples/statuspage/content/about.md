This status page provides real-time monitoring information for all of our services. We track uptime, performance, and incident history to keep you informed.

## How We Monitor

Our monitoring infrastructure checks each service endpoint every 30 seconds from multiple geographic regions. When a service degrades or goes down, our on-call team is immediately alerted.

## Status Definitions

- **Operational** -- The service is running normally with no issues detected.
- **Degraded** -- The service is experiencing slower response times or partial functionality loss.
- **Down** -- The service is currently unavailable.

## Incident Communication

When an incident occurs, we post updates on this page within 5 minutes of detection. We provide regular updates until the issue is fully resolved, and publish a post-mortem within 48 hours for any major incident.

## Contact

If you are experiencing issues not reflected on this page, please contact our support team at support@example.com.
