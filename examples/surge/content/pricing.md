One price, one bag size, and a cadence you set. Every plan includes the brewer
match, roast-to-rest timing, and grind set for your machine or whole bean &mdash;
your call on each order. All bags are 340 grams (12 oz) of coffee roasted within
18 hours of shipping.

## The plans

| Plan | Cadence | Per bag | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Counter** | 1 bag / 2 weeks | $17 | One brewer, a cup or two a day |
| **Daily** | 1 bag / week | $16 | A steady household habit |
| **Open Bar** | 2&ndash;3 bags / week | $15 | Multiple brewers or a full house |

Shipping is flat and included in the continental delivery zone. Your first bag
ships free on any plan.

## What every plan includes

- A roast matched to your registered brewer, re-tuned whenever you change gear.
- Grind dialed for your method, or whole bean if you own a grinder.
- Roast date and rest window printed on the bag &mdash; no mystery freshness.
- Skip, swap the roast, change cadence, or pause from the account page before
  the Thursday cutoff.

## No lock-in

There is no contract and no cancellation fee. Pause for a month while you
travel; the roster will still be here. If a bag ever arrives outside its rest
window or ground for the wrong machine, tell us and the next one is on the house.

Ready to set it up? [Register your brewer](/gear/) first so your very first bag
arrives matched, then pick a cadence at checkout.
