The counter test

We roast to your grinder, not the trend

Extraction is physics. A hand grinder with a wide burr set, a superautomatic that grinds fine and hot, a moka pot that runs the water past boiling — each one wants a different roast level and rest window. Register your gear once and every future bag arrives dialed for it.

Register your brewer

  • V60 light, coarse-medium, 12–16 day rest
  • AeroPress medium, medium-fine, 8–20 day rest
  • Moka pot dark, fine, 5–12 day rest
  • Espresso medium-dark, fine, 10–24 day rest
