I take over deployment pipelines after the one person who understood them has left. Usually there is a wiki page that lied about the process two reorganizations ago, a shell script named `deploy_FINAL_v3.sh`, and a release calendar built entirely around one engineer's vacation schedule.

My job is to make the pipeline boring again — reproducible, documented, and safe to run by whoever is on call, not just the person who wrote it.
