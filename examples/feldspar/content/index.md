Feldspar is a CSS utility compiler. You describe your design system as layered
tokens; it emits a lean utility stylesheet containing exactly the classes your
markup uses. This documentation is compiled by Feldspar too — every rounded
corner, swatch, and shadow you see is one of the utilities described inside.
