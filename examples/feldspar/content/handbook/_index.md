Six chapters, front to back. The early ones explain how Feldspar turns tokens
into utilities; the reference chapters document each utility family with a
specimen and a paired example; the last chapter composes them into the patterns
you actually ship. Read in order the first time — every chapter assumes the
tokens introduced before it.
