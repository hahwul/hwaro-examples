How it works

feldspar build

Only the classes your markup references are emitted. Rename a token, and every utility that derives from it recompiles in lockstep.

Why Feldspar

Layered by design

Base tokens feed semantic tokens feed component tokens. Override one layer and the ones above resolve against it — no find-and-replace across a stylesheet.

Nothing you don't use

The compiler scans your templates, keeps the utilities they reference, and drops the rest. A typical build ships a few kilobytes of CSS, gzipped.

Readable at 3am

Class names map to tokens, not magic numbers. .p-4 is one step of padding, not fifteen pixels you have to remember. The scale is the documentation.
