The quietness of the evening is often accompanied by an array of hidden wonders. As the stars emerge, so do the delicate blossoms that thrive under the lunar gaze.

These nocturnal beauties rely on different pollinators and have evolved to reflect the moonlight, giving them an ethereal glow.

## Characteristics of Night-Blooming Flora

- **Pale Colors**: Often white or pale yellow to reflect moonlight.
- **Strong Fragrance**: Scent is used to attract pollinators like moths and bats from afar.
- **Rapid Opening**: Many of these flowers can open entirely in just a few minutes once the sun sets.

> "The night is more alive and more richly colored than the day." — Vincent van Gogh

Embrace the stillness and find inspiration in the quiet blooming that happens when the world is asleep.
