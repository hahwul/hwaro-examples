The Obsidian Framework explores the use of pure, light-absorbing materials in modern structural design. By eliminating all reflective surfaces, the architecture defines itself entirely through silhouette and mass.

### Core Principles

1. **Absolute Density**: Materials must convey a sense of unbreakable mass.
2. **Void Interaction**: Empty spaces are treated as structural components.
3. **Absence of Ornamentation**: Any element that does not serve a structural or foundational purpose is removed.

The stark contrast between the monolith and the surrounding environment creates a profound sense of isolation and focus.
