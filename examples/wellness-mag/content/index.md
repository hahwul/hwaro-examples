## Aesthetic Restoration

Wellness-Mag is about the warm and foundational nature of structural innovation within the healthcare environment. We believe that vitality content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic wellness publication. By focusing on white surfaces, subtle shadows, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply welcoming. Our focus is on the integrity of the vitality process.

### Mag Principles

- **Vitality**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of health reviews.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the aesthetic space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical wellness publishing.
