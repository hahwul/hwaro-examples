## Animated Mystery

Cartoon-Noir is about the atmospheric and foundational nature of innovation within the cinematic environment. We believe that mystery-focused content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the early digital animation era. By focusing on dark backgrounds, flat shadows, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the shadow.

### Noir Principles

- **Atmosphere**: A design that prioritizes the clear "depth" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using flat shadows and lines to define the boundaries of the nocturnal space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of mid-20th-century digital ink and paint.
