The Premise

This is a space for strong opinions, loosely held. Every piece is written with conviction -- because hedging every sentence is a disservice to the reader.

Say what you mean. If a framework is broken, say it is broken. If a pattern works, defend it. Half-measures in writing produce half-measures in thinking.

Long form only. Ideas worth having are worth developing. No listicles, no hot takes dressed up as insight.
