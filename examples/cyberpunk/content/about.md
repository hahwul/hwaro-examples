# About Cyberpunk

Cyberpunk is an independent publication covering the intersection of technology, humanity, and power in the urban sprawl. Founded in 2074, we have operated continuously from a secure node somewhere in the lower districts.

## Our Mission

We believe in transparent reporting on the technologies that define modern existence. While megacorps control mainstream media channels, Cyberpunk remains committed to unfiltered truth -- whether covering black-market neural implants or exposing corporate surveillance programs.

## The Team

Our contributors are scattered across multiple city-states, connected through encrypted channels. Reporters, hackers, ex-corp engineers, and street-level observers all feed into our network.

## Contact

Secure transmissions only. Reach us through the usual encrypted channels.
