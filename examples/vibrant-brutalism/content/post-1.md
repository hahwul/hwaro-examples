In the realm of design, comfort is the enemy of innovation. When we pair neon cyan with a jarring magenta, we aren't creating a visual disaster—we are creating visual **tension**. This tension forces the viewer to pay attention.

> "Ugly is a form of design that requires courage."

### Raw and Unfiltered

Notice the thick borders and the unapologetic use of monospace fonts. We are not trying to be subtle. We are putting our structural elements on full display.

* Bold contrasts
* Visible frameworks
* Clashing colors

Here is some code that represents our structural integrity:

```css
.container {
  border: 5px solid #000;
  box-shadow: 10px 10px 0 #000;
}
```

Embrace the noise.
