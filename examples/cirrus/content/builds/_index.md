The bench log, newest first. Each entry is a whole kite: the sail plan I cut
from, the bridle I settled on after arguing with the wind, and a flight report
that does not pretend the first launch went well. Measurements are metric on
the ruler and imperial on the line spool, because that is how the drawer of
offcuts actually looks.
