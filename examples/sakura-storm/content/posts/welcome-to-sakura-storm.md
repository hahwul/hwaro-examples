Welcome to Sakura Storm — where cherry blossoms meet dramatic winds in a spectacle of color and motion.

## The Vision

Sakura Storm captures a singular moment: the instant a fierce spring wind tears through a grove of cherry trees, sending millions of petals spiraling into a breathtaking tempest. It's beauty amplified by chaos, delicacy powered by force.

This theme channels that energy into every pixel. Bold gradients surge from deep magenta to soft petal pink. Text glows with the warmth of twilight through blossom-filled air. Cards shimmer like petals caught in the light.

## Design Principles

1. **Maximum Visual Impact** — Explosive colors and dramatic gradients that demand attention
2. **Storm Energy** — Dynamic motion and swirling effects inspired by wind-scattered petals
3. **Twilight Atmosphere** — Deep dark backgrounds that make sakura colors burn with intensity
4. **Glamorous Readability** — Bold and beautiful, but never sacrificing content clarity

## The Palette

The colors are drawn from the most dramatic moments of cherry blossom season:

- Deep twilight purples for the background canvas
- Hot pinks and magentas for primary accents
- Soft petal pinks for subtle highlights
- Warm golds for hover effects and emphasis
- Cool violets for depth and contrast

Step into the storm. Let the petals guide you.
