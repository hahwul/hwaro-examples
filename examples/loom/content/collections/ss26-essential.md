## Concept

Essential is a return to fundamentals. Twelve pieces built from three fabrics -- washed linen, organic cotton jersey, and a silk-cotton blend -- in a palette of undyed ivory, stone, and charcoal. Every garment in this collection is designed to be worn year after year.

## Key Pieces

### Draped Linen Coat

An unstructured overcoat in heavyweight Belgian linen. The silhouette falls just below the knee with a single interior button closure. Worn open, it catches air and movement.

- Fabric: 100% Belgian linen, 280gsm
- Colors: Ivory, Stone
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Collarless Jersey Shirt

A clean, collarless shirt in brushed organic cotton jersey. The absence of a traditional collar creates a modern neckline that works equally well tucked or untucked.

- Fabric: 100% organic cotton jersey, 180gsm
- Colors: Ivory, Charcoal
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Wide-Leg Trousers

High-waisted trousers with a gentle drape. Double-pleated front, slash pockets, and a clean back. The wide leg tapers slightly at the ankle for structure.

- Fabric: Silk-cotton blend, 160gsm
- Colors: Stone, Charcoal
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Boxy Crop Tee

A boxy-fit tee that sits at the natural waist. Dropped shoulders and a slightly thicker neckband give it quiet presence without embellishment.

- Fabric: 100% organic cotton jersey, 200gsm
- Colors: Ivory, Stone, Charcoal
- Sizes: XS -- XL
