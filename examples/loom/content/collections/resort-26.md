## Concept

Interlude is a capsule for unhurried days. Eight pieces in sun-faded neutrals -- sand, ecru, washed blue -- made from fabrics that soften with every wear. Relaxed tailoring with just enough structure to carry from morning to evening.

## Key Pieces

### Camp Collar Shirt

A classic camp collar shirt in washed linen. The relaxed fit sits comfortably untucked. Mother-of-pearl buttons and a single chest pocket.

- Fabric: 100% French linen, 150gsm
- Colors: Sand, Washed Blue
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Drawstring Trousers

Easy-fitting trousers with a drawstring waist and tapered leg. Side seam pockets and a single back welt pocket. Designed for comfort without sacrificing shape.

- Fabric: Linen-silk blend, 140gsm
- Colors: Ecru, Sand
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Sleeveless Knit

A fine-gauge sleeveless knit in mercerized cotton. Deep armholes and a slightly cropped length make it an ideal layering piece over shirts or under jackets.

- Fabric: 100% mercerized cotton, 16-gauge
- Colors: Ecru, Sand, Washed Blue
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Unlined Blazer

A two-button blazer with no lining and minimal internal structure. Patch pockets, a soft roll lapel, and a slightly shortened length give it a relaxed Mediterranean sensibility.

- Fabric: Linen-cotton blend, 200gsm
- Colors: Sand, Ecru
- Sizes: XS -- XL
