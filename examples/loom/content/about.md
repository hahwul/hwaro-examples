## Philosophy

LOOM is a design house dedicated to the art of textile and garment construction. We believe that clothing is architecture for the body -- every seam, every fold, every drape tells a story of intention and craft.

## Materials First

We source fabrics from mills across Italy, Japan, and Portugal. Each textile is selected for its hand-feel, durability, and the way it responds to light and movement. Our palette is restrained by design: we let the material speak.

## Process

Every collection begins with fabric swatches pinned to the studio wall. We drape, cut, and construct by hand before any pattern is digitized. The result is garments that carry the memory of the maker's hand, even in production.

## Studio

Based in Seoul, our atelier occupies a converted textile warehouse in the Seongsu district. The space reflects our work: raw concrete, natural light, and long cutting tables where ideas become form.
