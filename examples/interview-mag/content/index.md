## Personal Excellence

Interview-Mag is about the focused and foundational nature of structural innovation within the biographical environment. We believe that personal stories deserve a presentation that captures the authority of a classic print interview. By focusing on white surfaces, clear hierarchies, and elegant sans-serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the profile.

### Interview Principles

- **Focus**: A design that prioritizes the clear "voice" of the subject.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the biographical space.
- **Narrative**: A layout that reflects the depth and complexity of a professional interview.
