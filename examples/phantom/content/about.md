## About Phantom

Phantom is a digital journal born from the silence between midnight and dawn. It exists in the liminal space where stories dissolve into mist and thoughts become translucent whispers.

This site was built as a study in absence and presence -- how elements can haunt a page without fully materializing, how text can speak in hushed tones through careful opacity, and how a reading experience can feel like walking through a fog-laden corridor of an old manor.

Every post here is a specter of thought. Some appear clearly when you draw near. Others fade the moment you look away.

### The Design Philosophy

The aesthetic draws from the atmosphere of abandoned places -- not ruined, but waiting. Each element on the page exists at partial opacity, as though the entire site is caught between two worlds. Hover over any element and watch it materialize, as if your attention alone gives it substance.

### Who Writes Here

The author prefers anonymity. Think of them as a voice echoing through hallways you cannot quite see the end of. The writing concerns itself with mystery, atmosphere, the uncanny, and the quiet dread that lives in peripheral vision.
