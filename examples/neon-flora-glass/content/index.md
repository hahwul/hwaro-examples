Welcome to **Neon Flora Glass**, where organic beauty meets the digital frontier.

> "In the glow of the synthetic canopy, true beauty remains organic at its core."
> — Cyber-botanist Dr. Aris

This theme showcases a dark aesthetic highlighted by glowing neon greens, vibrant pinks, and deep cyan ambient lights. It's built entirely on the powerful [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com) static site generator.

### Features

* **Glassmorphism:** Frosted translucent panels using `backdrop-filter`.
* **Animated Gradients:** Slow-moving background orbs that bring the page to life.
* **Modern Typography:** Utilizing *Space Grotesk* for headings and *Inter* for body text.
