# About the Aesthetic

**Neon Aurora Synth** merges two visually stunning themes:

1.  **Synthwave / Cyberpunk:** Bright neon colors, grid elements, and dark, high-contrast spaces.
2.  **Aurora Glassmorphism:** Soft, fluid gradients and blurred, translucent interfaces that give a sense of depth and lightness.

### Typography

The typography leverages **Space Grotesk** for headings, providing a sleek, futuristic geometric feel, and **Inter** for clean, readable body copy.

### The Background

The deep background contains shifting, organic blob shapes animated with CSS to simulate an aurora or a neon nebula, providing a mesmerizing base for the frosted glass containers layered above.
