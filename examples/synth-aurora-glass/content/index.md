# Welcome to Neon Aurora Synth

Experience the vibrant collision of retro-futuristic synthwave and ethereal aurora glassmorphism. This Hwaro example showcases a deep void background illuminated by fluid glowing animations and neon mesh gradients.

## Ethereal Glass

The layout relies on heavily frosted glass panels utilizing `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)` to create depth and dimension over the shifting background elements.

## Features

- **Dark Void Palette:** Deep `#030108` background for maximum contrast.
- **Neon Glow:** Accents of fuchsia, cyan, and deep purple.
- **Fluid Animation:** Ethereal aurora effects drift softly in the background.
