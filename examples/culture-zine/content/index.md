## Cultural Underground

Culture-Zine is about the independent and dynamic nature of structural innovation within the cultural environment. We believe that underground content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a classic subculture zine. By focusing on white surfaces, bold black borders, and impactful display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the issue.

### Zine Principles

- **Independence**: A design that prioritizes the clear "voice" of the creator.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and flat colors to define the boundaries of the cultural space.
- **Vibrancy**: A layout that evokes the feeling of late-20th-century independent publishing.
