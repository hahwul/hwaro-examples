## Program Card

The Orpheum's Wurlitzer 260 has been in continuous service since 1931. Greta Halloran has played this house since 1968 and picks her setlist on the day, usually with one request accepted from the lobby.

## House Notes

- Seat by 6:45; the organ rises hydraulically and needs the front row clear.
- No flash photography during the overture; the bulbs on the console face up.
- Set list is posted in the lobby after each performance.
