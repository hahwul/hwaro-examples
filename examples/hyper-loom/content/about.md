# The Architecture

The **Hyper Loom** is a conceptual framework designed to blend the boundaries between retro-futurism and modern web aesthetics. It relies on the blazing-fast engine of Hwaro to compile static nodes of information, presenting them through a lens of frosted glass and neon light.

## Technical Specifications

The system runs on an intricate network of styles:

*   **Typography:** Powered by `Space Grotesk` for headers and `Inter` for body text, ensuring legibility amidst the visual noise.
*   **Color Palette:** Deep space blacks (`#03040a`, `#0a0c18`) accented by vivid neon (`#00f0ff`, `#ff0055`, `#8a2be2`).
*   **Layering:** Strategic use of `z-index` and translucent `rgba` backgrounds to create depth and dimension.

### The Origin

Forged in the silicon valleys of the digital frontier, the Hyper Loom was created as a beacon for cyber-travelers seeking an aesthetic retreat from the mundane web.
