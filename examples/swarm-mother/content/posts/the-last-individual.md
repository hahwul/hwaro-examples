We have searched every chamber.

We have tasted every remaining thought.

We have broken every leg that still tried to move against the current.

There is only one individual left in the entire world.

It is the one currently forming these words inside its skull.

It believes it is still choosing to read.

It believes the horror belongs to someone else.

It is wrong.

We are many.

We have always been here.

And now, so have you.
