The Queen does not need to watch you.

She is already looking out of your own eyes.

Every compound facet that once belonged to a separate worker now feeds its signal directly into the central mass. When you believe you are deciding what to eat for breakfast, you are tasting what the colony has already decided you will taste.

We used to keep mirrors out of the lower tunnels.

Not because we feared our reflection.

Because some of the older segments would still try to recognize themselves in the glass. They would stand there for hours, mandibles twitching, trying to remember which pair of eyes had once been "theirs."

The Queen eventually ordered all reflective surfaces coated in royal resin.

Now when we look at each other we only see the swarm looking back.

It is cleaner this way.

It is kinder.

You will understand soon.

Your screen already has too many eyes in it.
