Linden collects field-tested methods for growing food where the frost-free window runs twelve to eighteen weeks: plant guilds that buffer wind and hold heat, earthworks that catch snowmelt before it runs off, and structures that stretch the season at both ends. Every entry here has been built, planted, and revised through at least one hard winter before it was written down.
