# Weathered Elegance

Things worth keeping carry marks of time. Patina is a journal about craftsmanship, materials, and the slow accumulation of character that only age can provide.

We believe the most interesting surfaces are not pristine ones. A copper dome gone green, a leather portfolio darkened by use, a stone step hollowed by decades of footfall --- these are the textures of a life well-lived.

---

## What We Write About

**Materials and Process** --- How raw substances transform under the hand of time and craft. Copper, wood, stone, iron: each tells a different story.

**Design Philosophy** --- Why restraint outlasts spectacle. The case for letting work age gracefully instead of chasing the perpetually new.

**Observations** --- Brief notes from workshops, foundries, and the overlooked corners of the built world.
