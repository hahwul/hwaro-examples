## Material Prestige

Brass-Plate is about the prestigious and commemorative nature of structural innovation. We believe that historical content deserves a presentation that captures the warmth of the early institutional era. By focusing on brass-inspired textures, warm backgrounds, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the record.

### Plate Principles

- **Honor**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of commemorative content.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the prestigious space.
- **Warmth**: A layout that evokes the rich, organic nature of physical history.
