Propel charges by seat, not by inbox size or message volume — a triage-first product shouldn't punish you for having a full inbox. Every plan, including Solo, includes the same core triage engine: one-key archive, snooze, and send-later, plus the command palette that runs both search and actions.

The difference between tiers is who else is triaging with you. Solo is one person and one inbox. Crew adds shared split inboxes and delegated triage, so a support desk or small agency can route the same address to different people without anyone forwarding threads by hand. Company adds the provisioning and audit tooling that IT and security teams ask for once headcount crosses a few dozen seats.

Every plan starts with a fourteen-day trial at full feature depth — no card, no seat cap during the trial, and no automatic charge when it ends. If a shortcut isn't saving you time, we'd rather know before you've paid for it.
