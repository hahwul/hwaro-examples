## Soft Innovation

Pillow is about the physical comfort of the digital experience. We believe that design should feel as soft and supportive as a pillow. By focusing on high-depth, rounded neumorphic shadows and a warm, inviting palette, we create a design that feels both comforting and deeply sophisticated. Our focus is on the welcoming nature of the interface.

### Resting Principles

- **Support**: A design that provides a stable and comfortable base for the content.
- **Softness**: Using extreme rounding and soft shadows to reduce visual tension.
- **Welcoming**: A design that invites the user to stay and explore.
