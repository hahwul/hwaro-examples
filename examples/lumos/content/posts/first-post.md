Simplicity is not merely the absence of clutter, but the deliberate focus on what truly matters. In an age of constant notification and visual noise, a return to minimalism offers a breath of fresh air.

## The Essence of Minimal Design

When designing Lumos, the goal was to create an environment where the reader's attention is undividedly given to the words. This meant:

1. Choosing a legible serif font for long-form reading.
2. Employing generous margins to give the text room to breathe.
3. Using a restrained color palette that doesn't distract.

By embracing these principles, we can create spaces—both digital and physical—that foster deep thought and reflection.
