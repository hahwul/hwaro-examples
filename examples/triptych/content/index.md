## Narrative Unity

Triptych is an exploration of the relationship between three distinct but connected elements. We believe that a complete understanding of a project requires a beginning, a transformation, and a final state. By presenting these phases side-by-side, we reveal the thematic unity that binds them together.

### The Synthesis

1. **Foundation**: The initial conditions and historical context.
2. **Transformation**: The application of innovation and the resulting conflict.
3. **Resolution**: The emergence of a new, stable structure.

---

> "The whole is not just the sum of its parts, but the story that connects them."
