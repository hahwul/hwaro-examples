Our latest offshore wind project represents a significant leap forward in renewable energy infrastructure. Located 25 kilometers off the northern coast, this expansion will deploy 150 cutting-edge wind turbines across 85 square kilometers of ocean.

## Project Overview

- **Capacity**: 900 MW additional generation
- **Turbines**: 150 units, 6 MW each
- **Height**: 180 meters from sea level
- **Blade Diameter**: 164 meters
- **Expected Output**: 3.2 TWh annually

## Environmental Impact

This project will reduce carbon emissions by an estimated 2.1 million tons per year, equivalent to removing 450,000 cars from the road.

## Timeline

- Q2 2025: Foundation installation
- Q3 2025: Turbine assembly
- Q4 2025: Grid connection
- Q1 2026: Full commercial operation
