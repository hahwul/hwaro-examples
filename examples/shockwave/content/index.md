## Energy Dispersion

Shockwave is about the way an idea travels once it is released. It starts at a single point of origin and ripples outward, changing every medium it passes through.

### Propagation

- **Intensity**: The strength of the initial release.
- **Medium**: The environment through which the wave travels.
- **Reach**: The final extent of the dispersion.
