## Input Tactility

Typewriter-Key is about the precise and tactile nature of innovation through the lens of a mechanical key. We believe that input-focused content deserves a presentation that captures the substantiality of the physical press. By focusing on white surfaces, high-depth shadows, and bold monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply sophisticated. Our focus is on the integrity of the input.

### Press Principles

- **Precision**: A design that prioritizes the clear "click" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using deep shadows to define the boundaries of the key surface.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century mechanical typing.
