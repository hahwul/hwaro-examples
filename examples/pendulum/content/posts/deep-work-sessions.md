The key to meaningful output is not more hours, but better hours. Deep work sessions, properly structured, can triple your productive output in half the time.

<!-- more -->

## The 90-Minute Block

Research on ultradian rhythms suggests that the brain works best in 90-minute cycles. Structure your deep work around this natural pattern.

### Session Structure

1. **Minutes 0-5** — Set intention. Write down exactly what you will accomplish.
2. **Minutes 5-80** — Work without interruption. No email, no messages, no context switching.
3. **Minutes 80-90** — Review what you accomplished. Note where you left off.

### Environment Matters

- Close all unnecessary tabs and applications
- Put your phone in another room
- Use noise-canceling headphones or silence
- Keep water and coffee at your desk

## Recovery Between Sessions

Take a genuine 20-minute break. Walk. Stretch. Do not check social media — it fragments attention. The goal is to let your brain rest so the next session is equally productive.
