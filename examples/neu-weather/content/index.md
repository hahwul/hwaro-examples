## Meteorological Tactility

Neu-Weather is about the relationship between atmospheric data and the digital interface. We believe that weather information should be presented with a sense of clarity and substantiality. By focusing on neumorphic-inspired soft shadows and clean, blue-toned elements, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply accurate. Our focus is on the integrity of the data and the way it is visualized.

### Atmospheric Principles

- **Clarity**: A design that prioritizes readability and ease of access to information.
- **Accuracy**: Using high-contrast elements to define the metrics.
- **Expansion**: A layout that reflects the expansive nature of the atmosphere.
