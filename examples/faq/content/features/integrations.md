We integrate with a wide range of tools you already use:

- **Communication:** Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord
- **Development:** GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Jira
- **Storage:** Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive
- **Automation:** Zapier, Make (Integromat), webhooks

To connect an integration, go to **Settings** then **Integrations**, find the service you want to connect, and follow the authorization flow. Most integrations can be set up in under two minutes.
