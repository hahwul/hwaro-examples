The analytics dashboard provides insights into your team's performance:

- **Velocity chart** showing tasks completed per week
- **Burndown chart** tracking progress toward project milestones
- **Workload view** displaying task distribution across team members
- **Time tracking reports** summarizing hours logged by project or member

Reports can be exported as CSV or PDF. Pro and Enterprise plans include custom report builders where you can define your own metrics and filters.
