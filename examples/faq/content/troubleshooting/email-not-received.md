Verification emails typically arrive within a few minutes. If you haven't received one:

1. **Check your spam/junk folder** -- Email filters sometimes misclassify our messages
2. **Check the correct inbox** -- Verify you entered the right email address during signup
3. **Resend the email** -- Click the "Resend verification email" link on the login page
4. **Add to contacts** -- Add noreply@example.com to your contacts to prevent future filtering

If you still don't receive the email after 15 minutes, contact our support team for manual verification.
