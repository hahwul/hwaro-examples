date = "2026-03-03"

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

1. Go to **Settings** then **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select a reason for cancellation (optional)
4. Confirm by clicking **Cancel Plan**

Your access to paid features continues until the end of your current billing period. After that, your workspace will revert to the Free plan. All your data is preserved and you can resubscribe at any time.
