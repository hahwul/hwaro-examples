To update your payment method:

1. Go to **Settings** then **Billing**
2. Under **Payment Method**, click **Update**
3. Enter your new credit card or bank account details
4. Click **Save** to confirm

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. Your next invoice will automatically use the updated payment method.
