We offer three plans to fit your needs:

- **Free** -- Up to 3 projects, 5 members, and 1 GB storage. Ideal for personal use and small teams getting started.
- **Pro** ($12/member/month) -- Unlimited projects, priority support, 50 GB storage, and advanced analytics.
- **Enterprise** (custom pricing) -- Everything in Pro plus SSO, audit logs, dedicated support, and custom integrations.

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
