To change your password:

1. Go to **Settings** then **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password for verification
4. Enter your new password (minimum 12 characters)
5. Confirm the new password and click **Save**

If you've forgotten your current password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to reset it via email.
