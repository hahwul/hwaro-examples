Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Before proceeding:

- Export any data you want to keep from **Settings** then **Data Export**
- Transfer ownership of any projects you own to another member
- Cancel any active subscriptions

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings** then **Account**
2. Scroll to the bottom and click **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days
