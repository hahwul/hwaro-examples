Control which notifications you receive and how:

1. Go to **Settings** then **Notifications**
2. Configure your preferences for each notification type:
   - **Email notifications** for mentions, assignments, and updates
   - **In-app notifications** for real-time activity in your workspace
   - **Weekly digest** summarizing activity across your projects

You can also mute specific projects or channels by clicking the mute icon next to their name.
