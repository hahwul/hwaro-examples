Nestled behind private gates in the hills of Beverly Hills, Villa Aurora is a masterpiece of Mediterranean architecture. This sprawling estate offers a secluded oasis while remaining just minutes from world-class dining, shopping, and entertainment.

## A Grand Entrance

The approach to the villa winds through manicured gardens and olive groves, arriving at a grand motor court with an elegant fountain centerpiece. The entrance reveals soaring ceilings, limestone flooring, and an intricate wrought-iron staircase that sets the tone for the rest of the home.

The main level boasts a sequence of light-filled reception rooms, perfect for grand-scale entertaining or intimate family gatherings. The living room features a hand-carved stone fireplace, while the formal dining room accommodates a grand table and opens onto an expansive loggia for seamless indoor-outdoor living.

## The Grounds

The estate encompasses over an acre of perfectly landscaped grounds. The expansive rear terrace overlooks the infinity-edge pool and spa, surrounded by lush lawns and sweeping views of the canyon and city lights beyond. The property also features a fully-equipped outdoor kitchen, a tennis court, and a detached guest house.

Villa Aurora represents a rare opportunity to own a timeless estate in one of the world's most coveted locations.
