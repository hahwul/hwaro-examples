## The Art of Frost

Welcome to **Macro Snowflake**, an exploration of ice, geometry, and perfect symmetry. This digital exhibit focuses on the macro-level beauty of crystalline structures.

> "A snowflake is one of God's most fragile creations, but look what they can do when they stick together!"

### Design Philosophy

The aesthetics here are driven by:
1.  **Strict Symmetry**: Emulating the six-fold radial symmetry of ice crystals.
2.  **Frosted Glass**: Utilizing modern CSS `backdrop-filter` to create glassmorphic panels that mimic looking through an icy window.
3.  **Cold Palette**: Deep space blues, stark whites, and piercing cyans.

### The Code

```css
.glass-panel {
    background: rgba(10, 20, 35, 0.4);
    backdrop-filter: blur(12px);
    border: 1px solid rgba(150, 200, 255, 0.2);
    box-shadow: 0 8px 32px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3);
}
```
