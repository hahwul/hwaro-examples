## The Foundation of Ice

At the heart of every snowflake is a speck of dust, and around that dust, water molecules begin to crystallize. 

Due to the V-shape of water molecules (H2O), when they freeze and bond together, they naturally form a hexagonal lattice.

*   This is the reason for the six arms.
*   This is why we see 60-degree and 120-degree angles.

### Environmental Impact

The specific intricate pattern that grows upon this hexagonal base is entirely dependent on the micro-fluctuations in temperature and humidity as the snowflake falls through the atmosphere.
