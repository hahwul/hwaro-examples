# SubZero Cryogenic Research Laboratory

Welcome to SubZero, a cutting-edge cryogenic research facility dedicated to the study of matter at ultra-low temperatures. Our research spans from fundamental physics at near absolute zero to practical applications in cryopreservation and quantum computing.

## Research Areas

- **Liquid Nitrogen Studies** (-196°C)
- **Helium-4 Research** (-269°C)
- **Quantum Condensates** (nK regime)
- **Cryogenic Materials Science**
- **Biological Preservation Techniques**

## Current Projects

Our team is actively investigating phase transitions in exotic materials, superconductivity phenomena, and the behavior of biological systems at cryogenic temperatures. Join us as we push the boundaries of what's possible at the coldest temperatures achievable in the laboratory.
