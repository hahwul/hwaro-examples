The courtyard is the first room

  • Gyeolgu · the joint

    Beams lock by shape, not by fastener. Every member can be cut out, repaired, and slid back — a frame designed for its own second century.

  • Tongpung · the through-breeze

    Rooms face the madang so that opening two doors moves air across the whole plan. Summer cooling begins as geometry, not machinery.

  • Ondol · the held warmth

    The old floor stored a fire's heat for a day. Our slab and envelope store a winter afternoon's sun for three — the ondol logic, airtight.
