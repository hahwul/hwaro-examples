## The Architect

Riftzone is an experimental portfolio exploring the boundaries between digital dimensions. Each project exists as a tear in the fabric of conventional design -- a window into spaces where physics bends and light fractures.

## Philosophy

We do not design within constraints. We fracture them. Every pixel is a coordinate in a space that extends beyond the screen, every interaction a tremor that widens the rift between the known and the impossible.

## Method

Using spatial separation techniques, clip-path geometries, and dimensional layering, each piece is constructed as a portal. The cracks you see are not flaws. They are doorways.

## Contact

Transmissions accepted through the void. Coordinates available upon request.
