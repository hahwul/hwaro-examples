## Overview

Signal Tessera is a communication framework that encodes information into patterns capable of surviving transit through dimensional boundaries. Standard electromagnetic signals degrade when crossing between planes of reality -- Tessera fragments data into tessellated shards that reassemble on arrival.

## Technical Approach

Each message is decomposed into geometric primitives that map to stable configurations in the target dimension's physics. The protocol auto-adapts encoding based on measured membrane permeability at the transmission point.

## Outcome

First confirmed bidirectional data exchange between Plane-0 and Plane-7. Latency under 400ms for text payloads. Image reconstruction fidelity at 97.2% across compatible dimensional pairs.
