The **Silk Road** was not a single path, but a sprawling network of interconnected trade routes that linked the East and West. It served as a conduit not only for goods but for the exchange of ideas, art, and culture.

## A Legacy of Exchange

Originating during the Han Dynasty, these routes expanded across Central Asia, the Middle East, and eventually reached the Mediterranean. Merchants, scholars, and explorers traveled these paths, creating a profound fusion of diverse traditions.

We built this site to honor that legacy, embracing an aesthetic of deep, rich colors and glowing accents to represent the timeless allure of the treasures once carried across the desert sands.
