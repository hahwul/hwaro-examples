## The Opulence of the East

Our collections highlight the meticulous craftsmanship of ancient artisans. From the shimmering threads of raw silk to the intricate patterns woven into legendary tapestries, every artifact tells a story of enduring beauty and human ingenuity.

* **Spices & Aromatics:** Saffron, cinnamon, and frankincense.
* **Precious Stones:** Jade, lapis lazuli, and gold adornments.
