Rhyolite is a GPU-accelerated 2D vector graphics library with a retained scene graph. Describe your artwork as bezier paths and groups, let the tessellator turn fills and strokes into GPU-ready geometry once, and let the scene graph decide which of that geometry actually needs to be touched again this frame.
