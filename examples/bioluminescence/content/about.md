This theme was created as an example for the Hwaro static site generator.

It features a dark, oceanic aesthetic with bright, glowing accents designed to mimic bioluminescent creatures.

### Design Principles

- **No Gradients**: All lighting effects are achieved using solid, high-contrast flat colors and sharp, bold borders.
- **Deep Contrast**: Intense contrast between the deep dark background and the neon solid text ensures readability and visual impact.
- **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Zero use of emojis or fuzzy drop-shadows. The design relies on pure geometric and solid-color relationships.
- **Organic Movement**: Subtle animations bring the theme to life, simulating the slow drift of deep-sea entities.
