Three things a cold kitchen has to get right

Stove craft

Match the burner to the cold. White gas you can prime with mittens on beats a canister that sulks at altitude — but only if you know how to run it.

Ration math

A day is a spreadsheet you can eat. Count calories per gram, not per meal, and the pack gets lighter without anyone going hungry.

Altitude

Water boils cooler the higher you climb, so pasta turns to paste and bread refuses to rise. Every recipe here carries an altitude correction.
