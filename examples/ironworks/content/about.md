# About Ironworks

Ironworks is a historical blog dedicated to the Industrial Revolution and the age of steam-powered innovation. We chronicle the engineers, inventions, and transformations that reshaped civilization between the 18th and 19th centuries.

From the earliest Newcomen atmospheric engines to the great railway networks that connected nations, these pages document the mechanical ingenuity that forged the modern world.
