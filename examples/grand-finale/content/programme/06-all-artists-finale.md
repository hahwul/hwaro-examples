## The Finale

All twenty-two acts take the main stage together for a thirty-minute closing set. The programme is the festival's traditional closing round — a single piece developed during the week of rehearsals and performed only on the final night.

**The Confetti Drop**

Confetti drops from the main stage ceiling at 00:00 sharp. The rig is rigged, tested, and armed each morning during the festival. A technical hold at 23:58 confirms the crew is clear before the drop.

**Post-finale**

The stage stays lit for thirty minutes after. Audience members are welcome to remain on the main stage floor until 01:00.
