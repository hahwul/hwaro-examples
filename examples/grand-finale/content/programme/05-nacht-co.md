## Two Uninterrupted Hours

Nacht & Co. close Saturday's main stage with the longest set on the bill — two hours, no breaks, no interval. The ensemble's eleven members rotate in and out of the set at predetermined points, a staging choice that has become their signature.

**Structure**

- 23:00 — 23:42 · Opening movement, full ensemble
- 23:42 — 00:18 · Rotating solos, three lines remain
- 00:18 — 00:54 · Full re-entry, central motif returns
- 00:54 — 01:00 · Close
