## Opening Fanfare

Lantana Brass open night one with an eight-minute arrangement of the festival's opening fanfare, written in 2013 and performed every year since. Eight horn players, one trombone, one tuba, and a small string backing stand in for the usual recorded version.

**Set Notes**

- Opening fanfare · 8 min
- Original set · 22 min
- Closing round · 4 min

**Line**

Lantana Brass (leader), Rafael Okoye, Mina Shiroki, Nikos Evangelou, Maria Skov, Tommy Ferreira, Dara Mehta, Ismail Terzi plus a six-piece string backing.

> Twelve years since our first set here. We still play the same fanfare because you still sing along.
