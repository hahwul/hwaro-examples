## Sunday

The festival's last day. The outdoor stages are cleared by 15:00 and the bulk of the programme moves inside. Five final sets build to the all-artists finale and the confetti drop at midnight.

### Running Order

| Time     | Stage | Act                       |
|----------|-------|---------------------------|
| 15:00    | Hall  | Children of the Choir     |
| 16:30    | Hall  | Lúa Delacroix             |
| 18:00    | Hall  | The Wayward Thirteen      |
| 20:30    | Main  | Tomio & the Saints        |
| 23:30    | Main  | All Artists Finale        |
| 00:00    | Main  | Confetti drop             |

### Confetti Drop

The confetti drops at midnight from the main stage ceiling rig. Technical hold at 23:58; drop at 00:00 sharp. Audience are invited to stay on the floor for a further thirty minutes after the drop.
