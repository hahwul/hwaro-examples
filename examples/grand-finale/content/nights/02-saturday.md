## Saturday

The longest day. Ten acts, three stages, a brass procession moving the audience from the East stage to the Main at 21:00. This year's Saturday is the first ever to run across all three stages without a break.

### Running Order

| Time     | Stage      | Act                            |
|----------|------------|--------------------------------|
| 16:00    | Hall       | Korvus & Quill (circus)        |
| 17:30    | East       | Nour Hassani Trio              |
| 19:00    | Main       | Six-Hand Parade Orchestra      |
| 21:00    | Move       | Procession to Main             |
| 21:30    | Main       | Ivar Sund (headline)           |
| 23:00    | Main       | Nacht & Co. (2h set)           |

### Procession

The procession leaves the East stage at 21:00 and moves in a single line through the market path to the Main stage. Audience follow; the Parade Orchestra plays the whole way.
