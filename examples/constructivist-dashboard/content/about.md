The Production Plan is not decoration. It is a working instrument for the collective, built in the constructivist tradition of Rodchenko and El Lissitzky: art put to labor, form serving function, the page assembled like a machine.

We measure output, not ornament. Every band, bar, and numeral on this dashboard does work. Red carries the eye. Black holds the structure. Bone paper carries the weight.

## the construction principles

1. **Output above all.** Numbers are set large and condensed so the brigade reads them at a glance. The figure is the message.
2. **Diagonal energy.** Composition runs on the angle. Skewed red bands and oversized arrows drive the eye across the plan, never letting it rest.
3. **Geometry does the work.** Solid circles, bars, and cut-corner panels build the layout. No soft effects, no gradients, no idle decoration.

> The plan is the engine. The collective is the hand. Output is the proof.

## engine parameters

- **Generator**: Hwaro, statically compiled in Crystal.
- **Type**: Anton and Oswald for the slab caps, Archivo for the body.
- **Palette**: bone paper, constructivist red, and stark black &mdash; solid fills only.
- **Compile**: full plan rendered in single-digit milliseconds.
