ACID ZINE is made by three people in a back room, on a table held together by a single issue of a different zine. We take turns running the copier. We take turns buying the toner. We do not take turns writing the manifesto because the manifesto, as it turns out, writes itself after midnight.

## Who does what

- **Editorial** &mdash; the one with the minidisc.
- **Layout** &mdash; the one who still owns a ruler.
- **Circulation** &mdash; the one with the bicycle.

Nobody has a last name in this masthead. That is on purpose.

> The staff box is a short list because the zine is a short zine. The zine is a short zine because we only have so many evenings.

Send corrections. We print them, unedited, on the same page they were earned on.
