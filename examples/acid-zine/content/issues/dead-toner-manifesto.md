We kept printing after the toner went. Nobody agreed to it, which made it an editorial decision. Stripes showed up on page three and stayed through page six. We put a note in the margin explaining nothing. The note read: *the stripes are the issue*.

This is not a style. It is an argument.

## What we learned

- A missed deadline is louder than a striped page.
- A polished zine is a contradiction in terms.
- The copier is a collaborator. Treat it with suspicion.

We have two spare toner cartridges for issue eight. We may not use them.
