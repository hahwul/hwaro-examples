The back room was louder than usual. Somebody had brought a minidisc player. Somebody else had brought a pamphlet, which we read aloud until the joke turned on us. We wrote down three fragments and mislaid two of them on the walk home.

## What we kept

1. A sentence about regret that we will use in issue eight.
2. A sentence about ambition that we will not.
3. A sentence about the bicycle, which was already written on the bicycle.

> If you have a back room, use it. If you do not have a back room, find one, and leave the door unlocked.

Next issue: more of this. We are not subtle about our pattern.
