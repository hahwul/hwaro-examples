Page six is a liar. It has been a liar since issue two. The typewriter on page six was never actually for sale. The glove, for the record, was a mitten. The bicycle was a shopping cart with ambitions.

We keep printing it anyway.

## Why

Because page six is the only page that readers write in about. Because the classifieds, fictional or otherwise, are how readers know someone is reading. Because a zine without a classifieds page is a newsletter, and a newsletter is a different thing.

The notebook, in last issue, was real. We got it back. Thank you.
