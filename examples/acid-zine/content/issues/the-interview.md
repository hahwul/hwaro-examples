We took the late train on a Tuesday. She sat across the aisle. She had a clipboard, which, as a reader of zines, you will understand, is a kind of invitation.

> Q / What are you writing?
>
> A / I&rsquo;m writing a letter to a friend I haven&rsquo;t met.

We took notes for an hour. We did not ask her name. We printed the entire conversation unedited, including the bits where we were wrong.

That is the policy. We are not recovering from it.
