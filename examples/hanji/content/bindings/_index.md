A ledger of everything sewn at this bench since the bindery opened its books. Edition sizes are set in the margin, the way they are pencilled on the colophon page of each copy. Open any binding to see its sewing diagram drawn down the margin — the same drawing that sits taped to the bench while the edition is in progress.
