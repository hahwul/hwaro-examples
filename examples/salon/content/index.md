Salon reviews photobooks and exhibition prints in prose alone. We do not license, scan, or reproduce the work under discussion — not from squeamishness about copyright, but as an editorial position: a photograph described precisely is a different, and sometimes more honest, encounter than a photograph reduced to a JPEG beside a paragraph. Every essay here asks you to build the print in your own head from evidence, argument, and the critic's eye, then go look at the original somewhere that isn't a screen. Read more about the policy, and how to pitch, [on the about page](/about/).
