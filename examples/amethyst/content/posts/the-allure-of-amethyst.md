The amethyst has captivated humanity for millennia. From ancient Greek mythology — where it was believed to ward off intoxication — to modern crystal healing practices, this violet gemstone carries an aura of mysticism and refinement.

## Why Amethyst?

When designing a blog theme, color palette is everything. The amethyst color family offers something unique:

- **Deep violets** provide comfortable dark backgrounds for extended reading
- **Bright magentas** create striking accents without causing eye strain
- **Lavender tones** offer gentle highlights that feel luxurious

This theme uses these three layers to create depth, much like looking into an actual amethyst crystal where light refracts through multiple facets.

## The Crystalline Effect

Every visual element in this theme draws from the gem metaphor:

- Card borders that shimmer like faceted edges
- Gradient dividers that mimic light passing through crystal
- Glow effects that pulse like inner luminescence
- Background particles that drift like inclusions in stone

## Building with Hwaro

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a static site generator written in Crystal — fitting, given our gemstone theme. The entire visual experience is achieved with pure CSS animations and minimal JavaScript, ensuring fast load times and smooth performance.
