Every slab at Cement Type is rated before publication. The grade is not a quality score. It is an estimate of the structural load the slab is expected to carry in the archive.

## Grades

- **C30.** A short observation. Holds its own weight. Does not carry others.
- **C40.** A working argument. Can be cited by a C30.
- **C50.** A foundational piece. Can be cited by a C40 or a C30. Rarely poured.

We aim for one C50 per issue. More than that, and the issue is structurally top-heavy. Fewer than that, and the issue does not carry load.

> A good issue has one wall, two beams, and a pavement of small slabs. Publishing is engineering.

The grade, once assigned, is final. We do not regrade. We pour again if a slab needs to be heavier.
