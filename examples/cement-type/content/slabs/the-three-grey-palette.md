Three greys. That is the palette. One for the background, one for subtle contrast, one for line work. Black does the headings. Nothing else is invited to the site.

We have tried adding a fourth grey. It did not survive the first revision. We have tried adding an accent colour. It did not survive the second revision. We have tried removing a grey. The page lost a structural element. We keep three.

> A palette is a budget, not a wardrobe.

Three greys, one black. It has held up for four issues. It will hold up for four more.
