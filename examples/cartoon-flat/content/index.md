## Animated Simplicity

Cartoon-Flat is about the simple and foundational nature of structural innovation within the modern animated environment. We believe that energetic content deserves a presentation that captures the clarity of early 21st-century digital production. By focusing on flat surfaces, clean lines, and elegant sans-serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the process.

### Flat Principles

- **Simplicity**: A design that prioritizes the clear arrangement of visual units.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the modern space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical digital production.
