## Narrative Depth

Photo-Essay is about the continuous and narrative nature of structural innovation within the visual environment. We believe that visual stories deserve a presentation that captures the depth of a classic print essay. By focusing on dark backgrounds, high-contrast white borders, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the sequence.

### Essay Principles

- **Narrative**: A design that prioritizes the clear "flow" of scenes and information.
- **Tactility**: Using dark textures to define the boundaries of the story space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical documentary production.
