## Sequential Radiance

Carousel-Gallery is about the continuous and foundational nature of structural innovation within the immersive environment. We believe that high-impact visual stories deserve a presentation that captures the authority of a classic print carousel. By focusing on dark backgrounds, high-depth shadows, and bold monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the sequence.

### Gallery Principles

- **Dynamics**: A design that prioritizes the clear "cycle" of visual units.
- **Tactility**: Using dark textures to define the boundaries of the cinematic space.
- **Authority**: A layout that reflects the solid, uncompromised nature of the primary narrative.
