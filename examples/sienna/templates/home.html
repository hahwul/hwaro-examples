How the practice works

  1. 01

    Survey

    Before a design exists, we walk the site with a level and a spade. What's under the ballast, what the water table does after rain, what's already growing back on its own — the survey sets the limits everything else has to work inside.

  2. 02

    Regrade

    Cut and fill is kept on site wherever the contamination profile allows it. The old industrial geometry stays legible in the new grading — a berm following a rail cutting, a lawn stepping down quarry benches.

  3. 03

    Replant

    Planting follows soil, not fashion. Pioneer species that already tolerate the ground go in first; a slower, more diverse palette follows once the topsoil has had a season to settle and the drainage has proven itself.
