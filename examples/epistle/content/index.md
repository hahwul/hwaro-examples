Every month brings one letter from Josiah Teal, keeper of Verge Light, written to his sister on the mainland and kept, since, exactly as he sent it. The serial began the month he took up the post and has run without a missed installment since — through a wreck on the reef, a stranger who would not give his name, and a predecessor's logbook that should not have stayed hidden as long as it did.

Below is where the current letter opens. The full run, oldest to newest, waits in the archive, each installment carrying its number and the serial's running word count in the margin, the way Josiah numbered his own pages against the light's log.
