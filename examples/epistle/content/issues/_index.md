Josiah Teal keeps Verge Light on Solitary Rock, four hundred yards off Fenhollow Point, and writes to his sister Constance in Aldercombe once a month, whenever Ruskin's boat can carry a letter across the strait. What follows is the correspondence in full, from his first month at the lamp onward, unabridged and given in the order it was written rather than the order it was received — the two are not always the same, out here.
