The codex proper begins here. Each entry below was compiled from harbor logs, house archives, and the marginal corrections of at least two rival scholars, which is as close to consensus as Veyra ever produces. Entries are filed under two rubrics — **regions**, the isles and waters of the archipelago, and **houses**, the trading dynasties that claim them — and each carries a ledger infobox recording its ruler, banner, and founding year as best as these can be verified.

Where houses and regions dispute a fact, the compiler has noted both claims rather than adjudicate between them. Readers accustomed to tidier gazetteers should adjust their expectations accordingly.
