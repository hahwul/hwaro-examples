House Ilsara is the newest house to hold a permanent seat in the Assembly of Sails, founded in 67 YS by traders from the eastern isles who had, until then, sold their spice and dye harvests to Thornreach middlemen at margins the eastern growers eventually decided to stop tolerating. The house built its own fleet, cut out the middlemen, and within two decades was moving enough saffron, indigo, and silk-dye tonnage to demand a seat that older houses were reluctant to grant and unable to justify refusing.

<!-- more -->

## A seat still being paid for

Ilsara has chaired the Assembly of Sails twice since its founding, against eleven turns for [House Vance](/codex/house-vance/) and nine for [House Corvane](/codex/house-corvane/) — a gap the house attributes to the founding-eleven houses quietly protecting their rotation order, and the older houses attribute to Ilsara simply not having existed for the first sixty-seven years the rotation was running. Both explanations are true simultaneously, which is the usual condition of Assembly politics.

## Eastern routes, eastern risk

Ilsara's trade concentrates in the eastern isles, well outside the waters Corvane patrols as a matter of course, and the house maintains its own small escort fleet rather than pay for coverage it considers unreliably thin that far east. This independence is a point of pride domestically and a point of friction in Thornreach, where Ilsara ships are sometimes waved through customs with less scrutiny than their cargo value would normally attract — an oversight the house has never volunteered to correct.

## The Drowned Marches interest

In the past decade Ilsara has taken more salvage blocks in the [Drowned Marches](/codex/drowned-marches/) than any other house, chasing dye vats and cellared stock that the old grain coast's clay soil preserved better than anyone predicted. Whether this counts as shrewd diversification or simple opportunism depends on which house's chronicler is asked, a caveat that applies to a great deal of what follows this entry as well.
