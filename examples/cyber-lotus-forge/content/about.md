The Cyber Lotus Forge was constructed as an experiment in digital aesthetics.

By combining the cold precision of the void with the vibrant energy of neon blooms, we created a sanctuary for rogue processes and lost data fragments.

Here, everything is visible yet protected by layers of crystalline structure.
