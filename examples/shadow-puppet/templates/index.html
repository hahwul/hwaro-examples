Bab 3

Lentera Lab, the apprentice cuts.

The Lab is two tables and four lamps, in a back room behind the green-room. Six apprentices, one master cutter, all the patience the night demands. We accept new students each autumn, after the harvest, never by application alone.

An apprentice begins on rough paper, then waxed paper, then buffalo hide. A single hand — the small carved hand of Arjuna — takes three weeks to complete. The pattern below took two evenings. The master will tell you it took twenty-two years.

— Mas Ruslan, dalang & head cutter
