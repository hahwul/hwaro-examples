Everything that has gone into a jar and stayed there long enough to write about,
newest first. The number on each card is the batch's age in days, counted from
the morning it was packed. Some of these are finished and eaten; the long ones
are still going. Follow the [feed](/ferments/rss.xml) to catch new jars as they
land on the shelf.
