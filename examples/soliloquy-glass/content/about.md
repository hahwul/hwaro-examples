# About Soliloquy Glass

This site serves as a digital soliloquy—an intimate exploration of design, code, and aesthetics. The 'glass' motif represents transparency, depth, and the fluid boundary between the creator and the audience.

## The Aesthetic

The design language here relies heavily on **glassmorphism**:
- Deep, dynamic dark backgrounds that suggest infinite space.
- Translucent panels that blur the line between foreground and background.
- Crisp typography and subtle glowing accents that guide the eye without overwhelming it.

> "In the space between light and shadow, the most interesting stories are told."

We hope you enjoy exploring this space as much as we enjoyed creating it.
