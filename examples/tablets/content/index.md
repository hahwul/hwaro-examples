## The Weight of the Law

Tablets is about the permanence of foundational rules. We believe that true innovation requires a set of laws that remain unchangeable despite the shifting world around them. By focusing on the stone-carved aesthetic and the massive nature of the structure, we create a design that feels both authoritative and deeply immovable.

### Permanent Innovation

- **Authority**: A design that establishes the final word on the subject.
- **Endurance**: Built to survive for millennia through the strength of the material.
- **Immovability**: A structural commitment that cannot be shaken.
