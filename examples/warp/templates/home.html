Philosophy

Retro-cosmic, not retro-flavored

We don't skin a modern engine with a scanline shader and call it a look.

Every visual choice traces back to a hardware reason: Cathode, our own engine, actually redraws at a fixed rate a CRT gun would recognize, and the palette is narrow because the cabinet amplifier only has so much headroom. The cosmic setting isn't neon paint either — it comes from an honest fascination with orbital mechanics.
