## Boilerplate

Warp is a three-person arcade studio based in Providence, Rhode Island, founded in 2019 and operating out of Satellite Skate, a former roller rink. The studio builds physical arcade cabinets first and ports second, running on Cathode, an in-house engine tuned for CRT-accurate scanline timing. Warp has shipped three titles — ORBIT JAM (2020), PULSE RUNNER (2022, Providence Arcade Fest Best New Cabinet), and NOVA WAKE (2026) — and remains three people by design.

## Fact sheet

- Founded: 2019, Providence, Rhode Island
- Team: 3 — Mireille Sato (director/design), Devon Okafor (engineering), Priya Anand (art/music)
- Engine: Cathode, in-house, fixed-timestep, 240Hz scanline redraw
- Titles shipped: 3 (see the [games](/games/) page for the full roster)
- Awards: Providence Arcade Fest, Best New Cabinet, 2022 (PULSE RUNNER)

## What's in the kit

The full press kit — vector logotype in two colorways, three production screenshots, and a one-page cabinet spec sheet for NOVA WAKE — goes out by email on request; we don't host binary assets on this site. Write to press@warp.game with your outlet and deadline and we'll send it same day.
