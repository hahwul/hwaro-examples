Warp is three people and one cabinet workshop in the back corner of a shuttered roller rink, building arcade games about the parts of space that are actually frightening: debris fields, orbital decay, the silence between transmissions. We are not chasing an eight-bit filter. Every scanline our engine draws exists because the hardware redraws that way, not because a shader said so.

We ship physical cabinets before we ship ports. If you can't drop a quarter in it, we're not done.
