Three people means every inquiry gets read by someone who actually opens the cabinet's coin box for a living. It also means we're slower than a studio with a support inbox — give us a few days before you follow up.

## By email

- General and player questions: hello@warp.game
- Press and interview requests: press@warp.game
- Contract and playtest crew inquiries: careers@warp.game

## In person

Warp operates out of Satellite Skate, 118 Ledge Street, Providence, Rhode Island. We run open playtest hours Thursdays from 6 to 9pm, and a longer public session the first Saturday of every month — cabinets out, feedback sheets on the counter, first game free. No appointment needed for the Thursday sessions; the first Saturday tends to fill up, so a note to hello@warp.game ahead of time gets you a guaranteed slot.

## For everything else

If you're not sure which address fits, hello@warp.game reaches all three of us and we'll route it internally — which mostly means someone shouts across the workshop.
