## What this record is

Gil is the Korean word for road, and this is the record of one: the Hanmaru Ridge Trail, six hundred kilometres of granite crest running south to north between Dolbat Gate and Cheonmun Gate, the Sky Gate. I walked it in seven stages between April and June of 2025, coming down to resupply and let my feet recover between each one.

Every entry hangs off the same dotted line, the way the trail itself hangs off the ridge. The number beside each stage is the cumulative distance — how much of the road was behind me when I sat down to write. Start at [Stage One](stages/six-days-of-pine-stairs/) if you want the blisters in order, or read [about the trail](about/) first.
