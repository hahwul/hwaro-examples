Fire does not add anything. It takes away. What is left after burning is the part that could not be reduced further -- the calcium in bone, the iron in blood, the carbon in wood.

Good writing works the same way. You do not build a sentence. You reduce one until only the necessary words remain.

Start with too much. Remove until it holds. That is the method. That is enough.
