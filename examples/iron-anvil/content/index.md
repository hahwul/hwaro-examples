## Material Strength

Iron-Anvil is about the unshakeable and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that heavy content deserves a presentation that captures the permanence of the forged metal. By focusing on dark iron textures, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the forge.

### Anvil Principles

- **Permanence**: A design that prioritizes the long-term stability of the work.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the forged space.
- **Strength**: A layout that reflects the solid, uncompromised nature of core ideas.
