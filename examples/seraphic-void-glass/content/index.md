Welcome to **Seraphic Void Glass**, an aesthetic exploration where absolute darkness meets celestial illumination.

Here, soft amber and gold orbs drift in the void, casting gentle, ethereal glows across frosted glass panels. It's a design language built on the contrast between nothingness and divine warmth.

Feel free to wander the void. The glass will guide your way.
