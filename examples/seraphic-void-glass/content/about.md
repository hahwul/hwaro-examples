---
title: "About the Void"
description: "Understanding the Seraphic Glass design."
---

The **Seraphic Void Glass** aesthetic is a minimalist approach to glassmorphism, prioritizing deep shadows and singular, warm focal points of light.

Instead of overwhelming the user with colors, this design isolates golden hues against a pure void, drawing inspiration from celestial bodies, ancient artifacts, and the serene quiet of empty space.

### Typography
The interplay of **Space Grotesk** for structured headings and **Inter** for readable body text ensures the design remains modern while retaining its otherworldly charm.
