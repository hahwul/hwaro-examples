## Welcome, Credentialed

You are reading the crew portal for the <strong>Blackbird Festival</strong>, a three-day
music and performance event held across four linked venues. Your pass is your access
document &#8212; it gets you through the security perimeter, into your working zones,
and out again at call times. Lose it, and you will be politely returned to the general
admission queue.

The following pages are the front-of-house view of the event: the run of show, the zone
definitions, the crew call times, and the emergency notes. If you need anything that is
not here, reach the production office on channel 2 of the crew radio.

<h2>Critical Protocol</h2>

> If the pass is not on you, you are not on shift.

The lanyard must be worn outside the shirt, barcode visible, at all times while on the
site. Plainclothes security are empowered to ask for the pass at any point on the
perimeter; refuse, and you will be walked off the site and your pass voided for the
remainder of the run.

If you lose your pass, report to the production office immediately. A replacement can be
issued, but it takes time, and every minute without a visible pass is a minute you are not
working.
