## Call

The opening act walks on at 15:30. Stage manager calls ACT ONE STANDBY at 15:25 on
channel 2. Lighting and sound cues are called from the stage-left riser; spot ops take
cues from the follow-spot caller.

## Stage Plot

Band positions are marked on the stage deck in tape. Risers are rolled in at 15:15 and
locked by 15:20. No changes to the stage plot are permitted after 15:20 without stage
manager approval.

## Crowd Density

Pit density is monitored by the head of security; if the pit reaches 85% of rated
capacity, the pit gate closes and overflow is redirected to the general admission
sightlines on the west. The pit-closed call is radioed on channel 2 as PIT HOLD.

## Set Length

The opening act has been given a 35-minute set. Production radios a five-minute warning
at 16:00 and a hard-stop at 16:05. If the act runs over, the stage manager calls
THANK THE BAND and the follow-spot is dropped.
