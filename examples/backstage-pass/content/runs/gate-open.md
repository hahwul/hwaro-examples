## Call

Front-of-house gates unlock at 14:00 precisely. Crew at the gate must be in position, in
high-vis, with radios on channel 2, by 13:45. Latecomers to the gate will be reassigned.

## Positions

- **Gate 01 (north):** two crew, one check, one bag search.
- **Gate 02 (east):** two crew, one check, one bag search.
- **Overflow queue:** one crew member, walking the line.
- **Supervisor:** one crew member, stationary, with a spare radio and a lanyard replacement kit.

## Emergency Holds

If the emergency hold is called (&#8220;HOLD GATE&#8221; on channel 2), gates are closed
immediately and the crowd is asked to wait in place. Under no circumstances is the crowd
to be moved away from the gate without a second call from production.

## Cues

1. 13:45 &#8212; Crew positions checked by supervisor.
2. 13:55 &#8212; Radio check, all four gate crew + supervisor.
3. 14:00 &#8212; Gate open. First ticket scanned.
4. 14:30 &#8212; First volume check; supervisor reports queue length to production.
5. 15:00 &#8212; Second volume check; overflow queue crew redeployed if line is clear.
