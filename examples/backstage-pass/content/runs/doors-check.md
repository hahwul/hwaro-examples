## Call

Doors check takes place thirty minutes before the opening set. The stage manager and the
head of security walk the stage-left and stage-right passages, checking barricade
integrity, confirming that all stage-adjacent doors are locked and marked, and verifying
that the monitor engineer is in position and on comms.

## What the Check Covers

1. Barricade integrity &#8212; no gaps, no loose bolts, no exposed cabling.
2. Stage-left and stage-right doors &#8212; locked, tagged, fire-kit present.
3. Monitor engineer &#8212; in position, headset on, patch confirmed.
4. Front-of-house engineer &#8212; in position, faders down, patch confirmed.
5. Spot operators &#8212; in tower, radios on channel 4, cue sheets visible.

## What Triggers a Hold

If any of the above is not confirmed, the stage manager calls HOLD on channel 2, and the
opening set is delayed until the issue is resolved. Holds are reported to production in
writing on the hold log.
