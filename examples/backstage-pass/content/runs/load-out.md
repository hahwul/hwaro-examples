## Call

Load-out commences at 23:30, immediately after the headliner&#39;s last encore and the
stage cue to LIGHTS UP. Crew are pre-positioned in the east dock at 23:15; trucks are on
the east lane from 23:20.

## Sequence

1. Backline broken down first: drums, amps, keyboards.
2. Lighting rig dropped from 23:45.
3. Monitor desk struck, cased, loaded.
4. Front-of-house desk struck, cased, loaded.
5. Stage risers broken down last.

## Truck Assignments

- **Truck 1:** Backline.
- **Truck 2:** Lighting.
- **Truck 3:** Sound.
- **Truck 4:** Risers and stage.

## Crew Overtime

Any crew working past 00:30 is on double-time for the remaining hours. The production
coordinator tracks overtime on the master crew sheet. Overtime is not guaranteed; crew
will be released as their departments complete load-out.

## Zero Tolerance

&#8220;Zero tolerance&#8221; applies to load-out as to all other calls. Any crew member
observed leaving a truck unattended, or handling equipment without the appropriate PPE
(steel-toe boots, high-vis, gloves where applicable) will be removed from the crew list
for the remaining days.
