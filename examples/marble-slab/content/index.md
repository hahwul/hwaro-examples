## Material Excellence

Marble-Slab is about the sophisticated and permanent nature of structural innovation. We believe that high-value content deserves a presentation that captures the excellence of the natural stone. By focusing on white marble textures, subtle veining, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both luxurious and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the surface.

### Slab Principles

- **Excellence**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of premium content.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the luxurious space.
- **Permanence**: A layout that reflects the long-term value of the work.
