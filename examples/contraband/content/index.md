## Hidden Value

Contraband is an exploration of the ideas that exist on the periphery of the mainstream. We believe that the most valuable innovation is often found in the places where others are forbidden to look. Our focus is on the protection and dissemination of restricted content.

### The Manifest

- **Encrypted**: The initial state of all high-value ideas.
- **Distributed**: The method by which we ensure the survival of the work.
- **Value**: The inherent worth that exists despite the lack of official recognition.

<div class="stamped">CONFIDENTIAL</div>

### Security Protocols

1. **Obfuscation**: Hiding the true nature of the work behind multiple layers.
2. **Redaction**: Removing identifiable markers that could lead back to the source.
3. **Resilience**: A structure built to survive even the most aggressive scrutiny.
