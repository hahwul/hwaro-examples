## Commemorative Tactility

Photo-Album is about the personal and foundational nature of innovation within the commemorative environment. We believe that collections of memories deserve a presentation that captures the warmth of a physical album. By focusing on white paper surfaces, deep shadows, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply personal. Our focus is on the integrity of the collection.

### Album Principles

- **Memory**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of commemorative content.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the memory space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical photo collection.
