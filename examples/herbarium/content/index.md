## Preserving the Essence

Herbarium is an exploration of curation and preservation. We believe that ideas, like rare specimens, should be carefully gathered, pressed, and displayed with intent. By focusing on a light, botanical aesthetic and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both natural and deeply considered.

<div class="specimen-label">SPECIMEN_01: INNOVATION</div>
<p>The process of drying and pressing removes the moisture but preserves the structure. We apply this same philosophy to design, stripping away the fleeting to reveal the permanent.</p>

### Curated Growth

- **Selection**: Choosing only the ideas that have structural integrity.
- **Preservation**: Documenting the process so that it can be studied later.
- **Display**: Presenting the work in a way that honors its natural form.
