## Modular Clarity

Glass-Deck is about the organization of information into discrete, modular units. We believe that clarity is achieved when ideas are contained and presented as a unified stack. By focusing on the card-based glassmorphic aesthetic and the integrity of the grid, we create a design that feels both structured and profoundly transparent.

### Card Innovation

- **Modularity**: Ideas that can be rearranged and combined to fit the context.
- **Translucency**: Using light and glass-like elements to define the cards.
- **Hierarchy**: The clear arrangement of units within the deck.
