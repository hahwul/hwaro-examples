This year's Gala dinner has been curated by the celebrated Chef Laurent Dubois, holder of three Michelin stars, who has designed an extraordinary five-course menu exclusively for our evening.

<!-- more -->

## The Menu

**First Course** — Oyster tartare with champagne foam, caviar, and microgreens on a bed of crushed ice

**Second Course** — Truffle velouté with aged Parmesan crisp and black truffle shavings

**Third Course** — Pan-seared foie gras with fig compote, toasted brioche, and Sauternes reduction

**Main Course** — Choice of Wagyu beef tenderloin with Périgueux sauce or lobster thermidor with saffron risotto

**Dessert** — Gold leaf chocolate fondant with vanilla bean ice cream and raspberry coulis

## Wine Pairings

Each course will be accompanied by hand-selected wines from premier estates in Burgundy, Champagne, and Bordeaux. Our sommelier has spent months crafting the perfect pairings to complement Chef Dubois' creations.

Guests with dietary requirements are kindly asked to notify us when confirming attendance. Alternative menus are available for vegetarian, vegan, and allergen-free preferences.
