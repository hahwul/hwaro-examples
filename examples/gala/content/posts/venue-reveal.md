We are delighted to announce that this year's Grand Gala will be held at the breathtaking Crystal Ballroom in the Grand Metropolitan Hotel.

<!-- more -->

The Crystal Ballroom is renowned for its magnificent architecture, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows that offer panoramic views of the city skyline, hand-cut Bohemian crystal chandeliers dating back to the 1920s, and Italian marble floors that have hosted generations of unforgettable evenings.

## A Space Like No Other

The ballroom spans over 10,000 square feet of event space, with soaring 30-foot ceilings that create an atmosphere of grandeur and intimacy simultaneously. The newly restored gold leaf moldings and Art Deco details provide a timeless backdrop for our celebration.

## Getting There

The Grand Metropolitan Hotel is located in the heart of the city's cultural district, with complimentary valet parking and a dedicated drop-off zone for our guests.

We cannot wait to welcome you to this extraordinary space for what promises to be our most spectacular evening yet.
