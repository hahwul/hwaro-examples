# The Aesthetic

Velvet Abyss is a design system crafted to evoke the feeling of peering into an infinitely deep, luxurious void.

It heavily relies on the **Glassmorphism** trend, utilizing `backdrop-filter: blur()` properties to create frosted glass panels that sit atop floating, glowing orbs.

### Core Colors

- **Void Background**: `#05000a`
- **Velvet Orb**: `rgba(255, 0, 85, 0.4)`
- **Crimson Orb**: `rgba(138, 43, 226, 0.4)`
- **Glass Panel**: `rgba(20, 10, 30, 0.2)`

### Typography

We pair **Space Grotesk** for our headings to give a modern, slightly structural feel, while relying on **Inter** for our body text to ensure maximum readability and a clean aesthetic.
