Our affogato is the collision of two perfect things: a freshly pulled double espresso and a scoop of house-made gelato. The hot espresso melts into the frozen cream, creating a drink that is simultaneously hot and cold, bitter and sweet.

The gelato flavor changes with the seasons. Past rotations have included salted caramel, pistachio, dark chocolate, and roasted fig. Ask your barista for this month's selection.

**Serving note:** Best consumed immediately. Linger over the conversation, not the affogato.
