A classic French tarte au citron made with juice and zest from organic Meyer lemons. The curd is silky, intensely tangy, and perfectly balanced against the buttery shortcrust pastry.

Topped with a Swiss meringue that is torched to order, giving a caramelized, slightly smoky sweetness. Each tart is individual-sized and made fresh daily.

**Pairings:** Pairs wonderfully with an Earl Grey or a straight espresso.
