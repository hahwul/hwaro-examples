Our latte is a double shot of espresso blended with generously steamed whole milk and finished with a thin layer of microfoam. It is our most popular drink for good reason: approachable, warming, and endlessly versatile.

Every latte is poured with care. Our baristas train extensively in free-pour latte art, so expect a rosetta or tulip gracing the surface of your cup.

**Flavored options:** Vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, or seasonal syrups available.
