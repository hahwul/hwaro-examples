We use ceremonial-grade Uji matcha sourced from a family farm in Kyoto. The powder is sifted and whisked with a small amount of hot water to form a smooth paste before being combined with steamed milk.

The result is vibrant green, naturally sweet, and rich with umami. A grounding alternative to coffee that provides calm, sustained energy.

**Serving options:** Hot or iced. Oat milk recommended for the creamiest result.
