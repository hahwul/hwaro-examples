# Our Story

The Roastery was born from a simple belief: that a truly great cup of coffee can change the course of your day. Founded in 2019 by a pair of lifelong friends with a shared obsession for specialty coffee, we set out to create a space where quality and warmth come together.

## Our Approach

We work directly with smallholder farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Indonesia. Every lot we purchase is traceable to the farm level, and we pay well above commodity prices to support sustainable farming practices.

Our roasting philosophy is straightforward: let the bean speak. We roast in small batches on a vintage Probat drum roaster, developing each origin to highlight its natural sweetness, acidity, and complexity. No dark-roast cover-ups, no shortcuts.

## The Space

Walking into The Roastery, you will find exposed brick walls lined with shelves of ceramic mugs, the hum of the grinder, and the aroma of freshly pulled espresso. We designed the space to feel like an extension of your living room &mdash; somewhere you can settle in with a book, catch up with a friend, or simply watch the world go by.

## Our Pastries

Everything in our pastry case is baked in-house each morning. Our head baker trained in Paris and brings a meticulous French technique to every croissant, tart, and loaf. We source organic flour and butter, and adjust our offerings with the seasons.

## Community

We host monthly cupping sessions, latte art workshops, and a weekly open-mic night. The Roastery is more than a coffee shop &mdash; it is a gathering place for our neighborhood.

We look forward to welcoming you.
