Every nocturne here was exposed once, outdoors, under whatever moon happened to be up that night, and printed without correction. Nothing is composited and nothing is relit after the fact — if a plate looks thin, the night it was made was thin. The order below is the order the moon grew fuller across the making of the collection: crescent, waxing, waxing, waxing, nearly full.
