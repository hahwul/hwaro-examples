Be it known to all citizens of the realm, from the highest noble to the lowliest peasant, that the practice of alchemy must henceforth be subjected to royal scrutiny. The transmutation of lead into gold, while long considered a myth by some, has recently been proven possible by the master alchemists of the capital.

To prevent the collapse of our economy and the devaluation of the royal treasury, the following laws are enacted:

*   All alchemists must register with the Royal Guild.
*   Any transmutation of base metals into precious metals requires a writ from the King.
*   The creation of philosopher's stones is strictly forbidden under penalty of exile.

Let this decree be copied and distributed to all provinces, that none may claim ignorance of the law.
