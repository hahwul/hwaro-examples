## Melodic Tactility

Music-Box is about the mechanical and foundational nature of structural innovation within the sonic environment. We believe that melodic content deserves a presentation that captures the warmth of the early automatic music era. By focusing on dark wood textures, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply nostalgic. Our focus is on the integrity of the tune.

### Tune Principles

- **Melody**: A design that prioritizes the clear flow of musical units.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the mechanical space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical music box production.
