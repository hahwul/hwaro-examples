# Golden Hour Timepiece

A masterwork of horological artistry, the Golden Hour represents the pinnacle of watchmaking tradition. Crafted from 18-karat yellow gold, its case catches the ambient light with a warmth that recalls the last golden minutes before sunset.

## Specifications

- **Case Material:** 18-karat yellow gold, hand-polished to a mirror finish
- **Diameter:** 40mm
- **Movement:** Swiss automatic, 72-hour power reserve
- **Crystal:** Sapphire, anti-reflective coating on both sides
- **Water Resistance:** 50 meters
- **Dial:** Champagne sunburst with applied gold hour markers

## The Dial

The champagne sunburst dial is a study in restraint. Applied hour markers in polished gold catch the light at every angle, while the dauphine hands sweep across the surface with silent precision. A date aperture at the three o'clock position is framed by a polished gold surround.

## Craftsmanship

Each Golden Hour timepiece requires over 400 hours of skilled labor to complete. The case is machined from a single block of gold, then hand-finished with alternating polished and brushed surfaces that create a subtle interplay of light and shadow. The movement, visible through a sapphire caseback, features hand-beveled bridges and Geneva stripes.

## Heritage

The Golden Hour draws inspiration from the golden age of watchmaking, when timepieces were as much works of art as instruments of precision. It is a fitting tribute to the artisans who devoted their lives to the pursuit of mechanical perfection.
