## About Diorama

Diorama is a 3D product showcase theme for Hwaro. Inspired by Apple's product pages and premium diorama-style presentations, it places your product center stage with deep shadows, layered depth, and meticulous detail sections.

### Design Philosophy

Every element is crafted to draw focus toward the product. The dark canvas recedes, letting the showcase speak for itself. Typographic hierarchy guides the eye from headline to specification, while dimensional card layouts create a tactile sense of space.

### Built With

- **Hwaro** -- Fast static site generator written in Crystal
- **Pure CSS** -- No JavaScript frameworks, no dependencies
- **Dark Theme** -- Designed for premium product presentation
