# Quantum Mirage

Welcome to the **Quantum Mirage Glass** experience. This design explores the boundaries between the deep void and vibrant, ethereal energy.

## Ethereal Nodes

Our network is powered by quantum fluctuations that manifest as glowing orbs in the dark abyss:

- **Cyan Resonance**: The primary frequency of our data streams.
- **Neon Pink Pulses**: The heartbeat of the quantum core.
- **Deep Purple Voids**: The silent spaces between the nodes.

## Glassmorphism

The interface floats above the void, utilizing frosted glass to bend and diffuse the intense light of the quantum mirages below. It offers clarity while maintaining a connection to the chaotic energy beneath.
