Glassmorphism continues to be a captivating aesthetic in web design. By combining deep, near-black backgrounds with vibrant, animated glowing orbs, we can create a sense of depth and elegance.

The `backdrop-filter: blur()` CSS property is the star of the show here, allowing the colorful background to bleed through content containers softly, mimicking the look of frosted glass.

> "A design that feels alive, breathing through the subtle movements of light beneath the surface."

When paired with a clean geometric sans-serif font like Space Grotesk for headings and the soft readability of Outfit for the body, the overall experience is modern and refined.
