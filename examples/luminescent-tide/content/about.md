Luminescent Tide is an example theme designed for Hwaro. It aims to capture the essence of a deep-sea bioluminescence meets modern digital glassmorphism.

The background pulses softly with cyan and indigo gradients, while the content floats smoothly above in translucent containers that blur the vibrant colors underneath.

### Features
* Deep slate background (#030a16)
* Animated glowing radial gradients
* Frosted glass UI components
* Elegant typography with Space Grotesk and Outfit
