<div class="content-block">
  <h1>Form Follows Fragment</h1>
  <p>
    We believe that architecture should be as dynamic and complex as the life it contains. 
    Our approach to design is rooted in Deconstructivism—breaking away from the rigid 
    constraints of Euclidean geometry to create spaces that are fluid, unexpected, and 
    intellectually stimulating.
  </p>
  <p>
    By utilizing advanced computational design and a deep understanding of structural 
    possibilities, we bring the visionary concepts of architectural theory into the 
    built environment.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="overlapping-block">
  <h2>Our Philosophy</h2>
  <p>
    Deconstruction is not destruction. It is the critical analysis of the relationship 
    between form and meaning. We dissect traditional architectural tropes to reveal 
    new possibilities for human interaction.
  </p>
</div>
