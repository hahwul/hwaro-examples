Copper Wire is a design manifesto disguised as a blog. It explores the intersection of raw urban environments and sophisticated digital interfaces.

### Our Philosophy

We believe that the structures that support our digital lives should be as visible and celebrated as the girders of a skyscraper.

*   **Transparency:** No hidden logic.
*   **Materiality:** Colors that evoke metal, stone, and light.
*   **Precision:** Monospaced typography and grid-aligned layouts.

Join us as we document the evolution of the modern urban aesthetic.
