Industrial design is often characterized by its honesty. It doesn't hide the bolts, the beams, or the wires. In this digital interpretation, we embrace that "exposed-structure" philosophy.

> "Form follows function, but beauty resides in the details of the assembly."

### Materiality in Digital Space

By using a palette dominated by deep charcols and glowing copper, we create a sense of weight and luminescence. The background grid isn't just a guide; it's a part of the visual narrative.

```css
:root {
    --copper: #b87333;
    --copper-glow: #d99058;
}
```

This is the beginning of a new design journey.
