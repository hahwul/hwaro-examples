We quote turnkey prices in the currency of a finished, tested, delivered house —
not a bare shell that needs another contractor to become livable. The figures
below are 2026 starting prices for a standard specification. Site work, permits,
and utility hookups at your end are not included, because they vary too much to
guess honestly.

## What is included

- The full timber frame, envelope, and air-sealing package
- Triple-glazed, argon-filled windows and an insulated exterior door
- A cold-climate mini-split heat pump and a heat-recovery ventilator
- Kitchen, bathroom, and built-in storage in our standard finish
- Blower-door testing and a signed commissioning report
- Delivery and leveling within 500 km of the shop

## What is not included

Foundation piers or a pad, the trailer registration if you tow it yourself,
solar and battery systems, and any local permitting. We are glad to advise on
all of these and can recommend people we trust for the site work.

## Reserving a build

A build is reserved with a 15% deposit, which locks your place in the queue and
your price against material increases. We take a small number of builds each
season on purpose — it is the only way we can keep testing every house instead
of trusting the spec sheet. Start with the [FAQ](/faq/) or read about
[the people](/about/) who will actually build it.
