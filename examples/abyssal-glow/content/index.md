# Abyssal Glow

Welcome to the **Abyssal Glow** theme.
Dive into the deep ocean where no sunlight reaches, but life finds a way to shine.

This example features:
- Deep dark backgrounds (#02050D)
- Bioluminescent glowing accents (teal, neon green, soft blue)
- Frosted glass containers (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`)
- Smooth, fluid floating animations
- Space Grotesk and Inter fonts for a modern, ethereal look.
