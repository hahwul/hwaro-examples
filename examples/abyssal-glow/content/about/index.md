## Secrets of the Deep

The abyssal zone is the abyssopelagic layer of the pelagic zone of the ocean. The name is derived from the Greek word *ἄβυσσος*, meaning bottomless.

Here, in the cold and the dark, bioluminescent creatures light up the void. This theme draws inspiration from those deep sea wonders, combining a deep void background with vibrant neon and teal accents.
