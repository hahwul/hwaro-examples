Groom was established in the autumn of 2025 as a dedicated quarterly print and digital journal. Our publication is committed to the quiet preservation and celebration of classical barbering, bespoke tailoring craftsmanship, and the rich history of men's grooming and style. In an era dominated by transient trends and mass-produced garments, Groom stands as a sanctuary for those who value the precision of the shears, the texture of washi paper, and the enduring grace of a perfectly structured suit.

Our editorial team explores the workshops of master tailors in London, Milan, and Seoul, documenting the basting stitches and canvassed frames that give a jacket its second life. We sit in the leather chairs of traditional barbers, recording the oral histories of straight-razor shaving, hot towel preparation, and the chemistry of botanical pomades. Every article is written with care, designed to be read slowly by lamplight in a cozy wood-paneled study. We believe that true grooming is not merely about appearance, but about the patience, discipline, and history behind the craft.
