## Focused Clarity

Incision is about the singular point of focus. We don't believe in broad strokes; we believe in the precision of the cut. By limiting the field of vision, we increase the intensity of the message. Every element is weighed and measured for its necessity.

### The Depth of the Cut

The further you go into the structure, the more truth you reveal. We are not afraid to go deep to find the essence of the work.

- **Intentionality**: Every line has a purpose.
- **Redaction**: Removing anything that doesn't add value.
- **Focus**: A single, undeniable point of interest.

> "The difference between a wound and a work of art is the intent behind the incision."
