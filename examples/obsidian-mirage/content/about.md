# The Philosophy of Obsidian

This is an about page designed to demonstrate the routing of multiple pages within Hwaro.

The **Obsidian Mirage** theme was forged in the void to offer an elegant, deep-dark canvas. It is characterized by stark contrast, smooth frosted glass panels, and the subtle, ghostly glow of neon accents that appear like a mirage in the desert night.

We hope you enjoy crafting your content within this aesthetic.
