Rather than focusing entirely on where the fabric sits on the skin, this study isolates the spaces where it is deliberately absent. By cutting vast architectural voids into double-layer bonded wool, we expose structural pockets of negative space.

> "The void is as active as the form."

### Structural Void Layout
- **Volume:** Asymmetrical body wraps
- **Cut-Outs:** Floating kidney panels and spinal splits
- **Material Support:** Interlined with heavy canvas to hold shape under gravity
