Dismantling the traditional bilateral symmetry of sleeve tailoring. This garment presents a singular, massive cocoon-like sleeve on the left arm, contrasted with a completely sleeveless right side.

### The Physics of Drape
Crafted using custom laser-melted polyester blends that possess a stiff, shell-like density. The resulting ballooned shape maintains its inflated posture while walking, mimicking an organic exoskeleton.
