A complete departure from standard pattern layouts. By utilizing a single 8-meter length of organic heavy linen, we fold a monolithic dress structure that secures itself entirely through tension.

### Zero-Waste Philosophy
Without a single cut or seam, this design creates zero scrap material. The organic drape contours around the waist and shoulders, folding over itself like paper origami to form deep, structured pockets and dynamic pleats.
