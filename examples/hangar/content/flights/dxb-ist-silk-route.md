## Flight Overview

A daytime service connecting two of the world's great crossroads cities. This route loosely traces ancient trade paths, now covered in under five hours at Mach 0.82.

<!-- more -->

## Route Details

Departed Dubai International from Runway 30L, climbing northwest over the Arabian Gulf. The route crossed Iraqi airspace at altitude before entering Turkish airspace near Lake Van.

## Conditions

Clear departure from Dubai with typical warm air and light winds. Smooth cruise over Iraq at FL380. Entered a frontal zone approaching Turkey with moderate chop at FL360, prompting a step climb. Istanbul approach was overcast at 2,500ft with light rain and crosswinds from 310 at 22 knots gusting 35.

## Notes

The A330-300 is well-suited to this sector length, offering widebody comfort without the operating costs of a larger twin-aisle. The new Istanbul Airport continues to impress with its capacity and efficient approach procedures. An excellent route connecting East and West.
