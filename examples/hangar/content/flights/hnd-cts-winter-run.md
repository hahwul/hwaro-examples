## Flight Overview

A brisk domestic flight from Haneda to New Chitose, Sapporo. Winter operations in Hokkaido demand precise planning, and this sector delivered challenging but manageable conditions on arrival.

## Route Details

Departed Haneda Runway 05 with a northbound track along the Tohoku coastline. Crossed the Tsugaru Strait at altitude before beginning descent over southern Hokkaido.

## Conditions

Haneda departure was clear with 15km visibility. Conditions deteriorated north of Sendai with layered cloud from 8,000ft. New Chitose was reporting light snow with 3km visibility and a ceiling at 1,200ft. ILS CAT I approach to Runway 01L with autobrake medium for the snow-covered surface.

## Notes

Winter operations at CTS always require extra attention. De-icing was applied at Haneda as a precaution. Runway condition reports indicated good braking action despite the snow. The B737-800 remains a reliable workhorse for these high-frequency domestic routes.
