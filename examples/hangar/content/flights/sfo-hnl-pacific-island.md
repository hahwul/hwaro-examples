## Flight Overview

Westbound over the Pacific from San Francisco to Honolulu. One of the most scenic approaches in commercial aviation, with Diamond Head and Waikiki visible on the descent into Runway 08L.

<!-- more -->

## Route Details

Departed SFO Runway 28R over the Pacific, tracking southwest on a great circle route. ETOPS-120 certified, though the B787 makes the crossing feel routine.

## Conditions

Coastal stratus at SFO cleared shortly after takeoff. The Pacific crossing was remarkably smooth at FL370 with a light tailwind component. Hawaiian approach featured scattered cumulus at 4,000ft and trade winds from 060 at 18 knots. Perfect VFR conditions for a visual approach.

## Notes

The B787-8 is the ideal aircraft for this route, offering widebody range and comfort on a sector that sits between narrowbody and true long-haul. Fuel efficiency across the Pacific was outstanding. The approach into Honolulu never gets old.
