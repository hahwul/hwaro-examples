## The Breadth of Innovation

Omnibus is about the power of the complete collection. We believe that innovation is often best understood when presented as a comprehensive whole, revealing the connections between different phases and facets of the work. Our focus is on the breadth and variety of the content within a unified structural framework.

### Collection-Focused Design

- **Variety**: Representing the diverse range of ideas within the project.
- **Unity**: The underlying logic that binds the collection together.
- **Reach**: Ensuring the work has a significant, comprehensive impact.

> "The omnibus reveals the story that only a complete collection can tell."
