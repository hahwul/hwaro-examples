Silent Geometry focuses on the essential structure of digital space. This space is constructed with intentional lines, solid boundaries, and unadorned surfaces.

We embrace the brutalist philosophy: content dictates form. There are no superfluous decorations, no false depth, and no unnecessary gradients.

### Core Principles

1.  **Structure over surface**: The grid is the foundation.
2.  **Clarity over cleverness**: Content must be readable and accessible.
3.  **Honesty in materials**: Web components should act and look like what they are.

This environment favors absolute contrast, definitive typography, and structural hierarchy.
