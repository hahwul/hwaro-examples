The grid is the fundamental constraint that liberates design. By imposing strict horizontal and vertical boundaries, we establish rhythm and order.

### Horizontal Rhythm

A reliable baseline grid ensures that typography aligns predictably, creating harmony across disparate elements.

### Vertical Harmony

Columns divide the space linearly, dictating the flow and hierarchy of information. In Silent Geometry, the grid is visible, unapologetic, and permanent.
