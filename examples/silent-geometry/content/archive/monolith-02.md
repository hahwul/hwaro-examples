Without contrast, form dissolves. Silent Geometry relies on the absolute differential between background and foreground to define its boundaries.

Gradients obfuscate; solid colors clarify.

We rely on near-black and off-white to provide the maximum structural definition without causing visual strain. This stark dichotomy reinforces the brutalist aesthetic.
