Three drive images, one shoebox of floppies, and about four hundred files
that still open cleanly. This is the part of Candela BBS that survived —
the art door, mostly, plus a few pieces that were never meant to leave the
message base at all. Everything below is presented exactly as it was
pulled off the disk: same line breaks, same handle, same upload stamp,
recolored to a single phosphor trace because that's closer to what most
callers actually saw on the other end of the line.
