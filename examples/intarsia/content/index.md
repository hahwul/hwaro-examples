Welcome to Intarsia, a bold, elegant geometric portfolio.

Intarsia brings sharp angles, thick outlines, and stark contrast to focus your readers' attention entirely on the content.

## Design Philosophy

The design avoids the use of smooth transitions, gradients, or soft shadows. By relying on solid blocks of color, it creates a striking aesthetic reminiscent of modernist woodworking and classic graphic design.

Check out our [About](/about/) page to learn more about the structure.
