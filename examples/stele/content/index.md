## Structural Significance

Stele is about the power of the vertical marker. We believe that true innovation should be established with a sense of presence and permanence. By focusing on the narrow, monolith aesthetic and the authoritative nature of the layout, we create a design that feels both significant and commemorative.

### Commemorative Innovation

- **Presence**: A design that occupies its space with absolute certainty.
- **Authority**: The use of classic, monolith-inspired typography and layout.
- **Endurance**: A commitment to the survival of the message through the weight of the structure.
