## The Divided Perspective

Schism is a study in the power of contrast. By separating the elements into two distinct fields, we gain a clearer understanding of the relationship between light and dark, presence and absence, left and right.

<div class="split-box">
    <div class="split-item">
        <h3>THESIS</h3>
        <p>The original idea, established in the light. It seeks to define the boundaries of the known world.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="split-item">
        <h3>ANTITHESIS</h3>
        <p>The opposing force, born in the shadow. It challenges the boundaries and seeks a new truth.</p>

### Core Duality

1. **Contrast**: The intentional use of difference to create impact.
2. **Symmetry**: Finding balance between opposing forces.
3. **Perspective**: Choosing the side from which to view the whole.

---

> "The truth is not found in one side or the other, but in the line that divides them."
