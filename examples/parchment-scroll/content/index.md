## Continuous Tactility

Parchment-Scroll is about the continuous and narrative nature of structural innovation within the historical environment. We believe that long-form content deserves a presentation that captures the flow of the early script era. By focusing on parchment-inspired textures, warm natural surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the script.

### Scroll Principles

- **Continuity**: A design that prioritizes the clear "flow" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the continuous space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of classical manuscript production.
