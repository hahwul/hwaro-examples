Sable files dispatches from the lower grid, where the velvet shadows fall thickest and the signal degradation is at its most theatrical. Most of the work involves walking, listening, and remembering.

## Beat

- Lower grid foot traffic
- Independent uplink installations
- Quiet rooms and the people who run them

## Field Notes

The current rotation covers the perimeter of the Old Loop, a stretch of disused trunk lines that nobody officially owns. The pirate operators there have been remarkably civil this season, though the weather has not. Reports filed when the relays cooperate.

Direct contact is discouraged. Leave a marker in the standard drop and someone will find you.
