## Recent Work

Reconstructed twelve hours of lost telemetry from a corrupted lattice node. The recovered fragments confirmed an earlier suspicion about packet drift during the autumn handover window. Findings will be published once the secondary review is complete.

Archive requests are handled in the order received. Patience helps.
