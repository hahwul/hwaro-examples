The nebula is one of the most striking phenomena in the universe. Composed of interstellar dust and gas, these massive clouds are often the birthplaces of new stars.

### The Colors of Space

When we look at images of nebulae, we see vibrant colors. These colors are not just for show; they tell us about the chemical composition of the cloud. For example, red indicates hydrogen, while blue often points to the presence of oxygen.

```python
def explore_nebula(name, color):
    print(f"Approaching the {name} nebula...")
    if color == "red":
        print("Detecting high levels of hydrogen.")
    elif color == "blue":
        print("Detecting oxygen signatures.")
    else:
        print("Unknown chemical composition detected.")

explore_nebula("Orion", "red")
```

As we continue our journey, we are reminded of the vastness and beauty of the cosmos. Every star, every cloud of gas, is a piece of the grand puzzle of the universe.
