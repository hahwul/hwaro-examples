Celestial Elegance is a demonstration of Hwaro's capabilities to build beautiful, fast static sites with modern CSS techniques.

Designed for developers, writers, and dreamers who want their content to float effortlessly in the digital void.

### Features

- **Dark Mode by Default**: Easier on the eyes, perfect for night owls.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: Beautiful translucent panels that blend with the background.
- **Fast & Responsive**: Powered by Hwaro, so it's blazingly fast.
