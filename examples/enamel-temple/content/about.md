## In the Beginning Was the Tooth

The Enamel Temple was consecrated in the Year of Our Extraction 1892, when Dr. Elias Molar, after a divine vision in which an angel of gold and bone revealed the 32 Articles of Perfect Occlusion, abandoned his secular practice and founded this sacred house.

We are not dentists. We are priests of the mandible. Surgeons of the soul.

### The High Order

Our priesthood consists of those who have themselves endured the Twelve Extractions without anesthetic, proving their devotion. Only then may one hold the golden forceps.

Current High Priest: **Dr. E. Molar, DDS, Pontifex Maximus of the Enamel Throne**

### The Three Sacraments

1. **Baptism by Prophylaxis** — The cleansing of original plaque.
2. **Confirmation of the Bite** — The ritual alignment under holy wire.
3. **The Final Extraction** — Ultimate surrender of the last imperfect tooth. Only then is the smile eternal.

### Our Vow

> "I shall not rest until every mouth sings in perfect harmony. I shall pull until the nerves confess. I shall crown the worthy and cast the crooked into the cuspidor of forgotten souls."
