Many of you wear the Crown of Thorns upon your teeth and do not even know its name.

The wire. The bracket. The elastic chain that slowly, lovingly, tears your teeth from their comfortable crooked homes and drags them into the light of alignment.

This is not medicine. This is **sanctification through tension**.

{{ extraction(text="Every adjustment is a small death") }}

Each month when you return to the chair and the priest tightens the archwire another quarter turn, you are participating in the same mystery as the martyrs who wore actual crowns of thorns. The difference is only one of scale and hygiene.

### The Theology of Torque

- **Leveling** is the stripping away of pride.
- **Alignment** is the confession of every secret malocclusion.
- **Space closure** is the final renunciation of the old self.

Those who complain of pain during treatment have not yet understood the gift. Pain is the proof that change is occurring. No pain, no transformation.

The patient who finishes treatment with a perfect Class I occlusion has died to their former crooked life and been reborn smiling.

Wear your braces with pride. They are your hair shirt. Your cilice. Your path to the perfect bite.

The chair does not lie. The wire does not negotiate. Only through suffering do we become worthy of the smile.
