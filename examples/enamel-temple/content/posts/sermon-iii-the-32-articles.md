Hear now the 32 Articles, one for each tooth in the perfect adult mouth. These words were revealed to the first High Priest in a vision of fire and floss.

**Article I** — The smile is the cross. All other expressions are vanity.

**Article II** — No tooth shall touch its neighbor without permission of the priest.

**Article III** — The midline must be true. A deviated midline is a deviated soul.

**Article IV** — Overjet is sin made visible. Overbite is pride given form.

**Article V** — The extraction of a single tooth may save the entire arch.

**Article XII** — Anesthesia is a mercy for the weak. The faithful require only the chair and the light.

**Article XIX** — A crown without a root is a lie upon the jaw.

**Article XXVII** — The tongue must learn its place behind the lower incisors. A forward tongue is a rebellious tongue.

**Article XXXII** — When the last imperfect tooth has been removed from the last imperfect mouth, the Kingdom of Perfect Bite will be upon us.

Memorize them. Recite them while the assistant suctions your blood. They are the only law that matters.

Disobedience is measured in millimeters of malocclusion. The punishment is always the same: more extractions.
