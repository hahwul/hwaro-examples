Brothers and sisters of the mandible, today we speak of the beginning of all things holy.

The first extraction is not merely dental procedure. It is the original sin reversed. It is the moment the crooked is made straight in the eyes of the Enamel God.

{{ extraction(text="The tooth yields") }}

When the forceps first close upon the crown, the nerve sings its final hymn. Some call it pain. The enlightened call it **ecstasy of alignment**.

### The Five Stages of the First Extraction

1. **The Approach** — The patient is strapped to the chair. The light descends like judgment.
2. **The Grasp** — Gold-plated steel embraces the enamel. There is no turning back.
3. **The Rock** — We do not pull. We persuade the root to release its sinful grip on the jaw.
4. **The Yield** — With a wet, sacred pop the tooth ascends into light.
5. **The Offering** — The relic is placed upon the silver tray. The congregation weeps with joy.

Remember always: the tooth does not belong to you. It belongs to the Order. It always has.
