True elegance often lies not in what is added, but in what is removed. When we strip away the superfluous, what remains must stand on its own merit.

### Silence as Space

In design, white space is the visual equivalent of silence. It allows elements to breathe and gives the reader's eye a place to rest. It directs focus naturally, without coercion.

### Timeless Quality

Trends fade, but fundamental principles of proportion, harmony, and clarity endure. By avoiding the ephemeral, we craft enduring artifacts that resonate deeply.
