## Elastic Tactility

Dough-UI is about the extreme nature of structural innovation through pliability. We believe that design should feel as substantial and elastic as dough. By focusing on very deep, soft shadows and highlights that mimic the look of molded material, we create a design that feels both organic and deeply three-dimensional. Our focus is on the elasticity and the integrity of the form.

### Pliable Innovation

- **Elasticity**: A design that feels like it can adapt and respond to the content.
- **Depth**: Using extreme shadows to define the boundaries of the pliable space.
- **Honesty**: A commitment to the essential, substantial nature of the material.

---

> "To mold dough is to find the perfect balance between structure and softess."
