## Clean Innovation

Soap is about the absolute purity of tactile innovation. We believe that design should be as clean and essential as a bar of soap. By focusing on very soft, high-depth shadows and a pale, tranquil palette, we create a design that feels both refreshing and deeply refined. Our focus is on the essential nature of the content.

### Pure Principles

- **Clarity**: A design that removes all unnecessary elements.
- **Purity**: Using soft highlights to define the field of information.
- **Renewal**: A palette that encourages a sense of calm and focus.
