## Temporal Tactility

Flip-Clock is about the relationship between time and the digital interface in a mechanical environment. We believe that state-focused content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the analog era. By focusing on dark panels, high-contrast shadows, and bold monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply nostalgic. Our focus is on the integrity of the state change.

### Temporal Principles

- **State**: The clear definition of the current mode of operation through mechanical flipping.
- **Tension**: Using high-contrast shadows to define the boundaries of the clock surface.
- **Nostalgia**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century mechanical technology.
