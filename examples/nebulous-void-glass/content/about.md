The **Nebulous Void Glass** architecture is designed for those who find beauty in the dark. It is an exploration of contrast—the deep, light-absorbing qualities of the void, punctuated by the vibrant, ethereal glow of neon nebulas.

This template leverages:
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent, frosted panels that create depth without obscuring the background.
- **Fluid Animation**: Drifting, pulsating ambient gradients that breathe life into the void.
- **Modern Typography**: A stark, highly legible contrast utilizing Space Grotesk and Inter.

We believe that the web should be an experience, not just a document.
