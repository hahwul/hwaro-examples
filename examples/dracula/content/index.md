Welcome to this digitized repository of Bram Stoker’s legendary gothic masterpiece, *Dracula*. Presented in its original epistolary format, this archive compiles the chronological journals, private letters, and urgent telegrams that document the horrifying events of 1893. Through these scattered records, the tragic narrative of Count Dracula’s voyage from the Carpathian Mountains to the shores of England is reconstructed piece by piece. 

Here, you will find the firsthand accounts of Jonathan Harker, whose legal mission to Transylvania quickly devolved into a fight for survival; Lucy Westenra, whose mysterious illness baffled the finest minds of London; and Mina Murray, whose devotion led her into the heart of darkness. Each text has been transcribed with care, preserving the frantic tone, the dates of recording, and the shifting mediums of the correspondence. Step into the shadows of the past, and follow the paper trail left by those who looked into the eyes of the undead.
