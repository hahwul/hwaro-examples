HOLMWOOD TO SEWARD, ASYLUM, PURFLEET. 
4 SEPTEMBER.—COULD YOU COME AT ONCE TO CARFAX? LUCY IS EXTREMELY ILL. SHE IS RAPIDLY WASTING AWAY WITHOUT ANY APPARENT CAUSE, AND HER MOTHER IS DEEPLY ALARMED. I FEAR FOR HER LIFE AND NEED YOUR MEDICAL EXPERIENCE. THE LOCAL DOCTOR SEEMS ENTIRELY AT A LOSS TO EXPLAIN THE SYMPTOMS. PLEASE DO NOT DELAY, AS EVERY HOUR SEEMS CRITICAL. MY CARRIAGE WILL MEET YOU AT THE STATION. SEND ANSWER IMMEDIATELY TO THE CLUB. ARTHUR HOLMWOOD.

SEWARD TO HOLMWOOD, BLAXTON CLUB, LONDON.
4 SEPTEMBER.—TELEGRAM RECEIVED. STARTING BY FIRST TRAIN. HAVE ALREADY TELEGRAPHED TO MY OLD FRIEND AND MENTOR, DR. VAN HELSING OF AMSTERDAM, WHO IS AN EXPERT IN EXOTIC BLOOD DISEASES AND MYSTERIOUS MALADIES. HE WILL JOIN US AS SOON AS HE CAN TRAVEL. REMAIN HOPEFUL, MY DEAR FRIEND; WE SHALL DO EVERYTHING IN OUR POWER. SEWARD.

VAN HELSING TO SEWARD, ASYLUM, PURFLEET.
4 SEPTEMBER.—WILL COME WITHOUT DELAY. SPECIMENS AND INSTRUCTIONS PREPARED. THE SYMPTOMS YOU DESCRIBE ARE OF THE UTMOST GRAVITY AND RARITY. BRING ALL INSTRUMENTS FOR TRANSFUSION. PREPARE THE PATIENT FOR QUIET. VAN HELSING.
