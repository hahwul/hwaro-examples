The Cyber Bloom Nexus is a showcase of what is possible with Hwaro and modern CSS features.

### The Vision

By combining deep background colors with soft, glowing animated orbs, we achieve a "bloom" effect that brings the interface to life. The glassmorphism panels provide structure and readability without obstructing the dynamic background.

### Technologies Used

- **Hwaro:** The static site generator powering this nexus.
- **CSS3:** For animations, gradients, and the `backdrop-filter` property.
- **Google Fonts:** Space Grotesk and Inter for refined typography.
