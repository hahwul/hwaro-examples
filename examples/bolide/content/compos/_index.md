Every entry runs on the big screen in front of the whole party, then everyone
votes. Below are the five compos running at Bolide 2026, smallest ceremony
first. Read the rules before you start cutting bytes, because the size checker
at the entry desk does not round down and does not care how close you got.

General rules apply to all compos: entries must be your own work, must run on
the compo machine or a documented configuration, and must be submitted through
the party system before the posted deadline. Stolen assets, AI-generated
graphics passed off as handmade, and entries that try to phone home are
disqualified on sight. When in doubt, ask an organiser at the desk.
