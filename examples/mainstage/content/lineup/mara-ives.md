## the set

Twenty-five minutes. One acoustic guitar, one vocal mic, one DI. Mara walks out to a half-lit stage on LX-01-A and plays seven songs without stopping to speak between them. She will say one sentence at the end, thank the room, and leave.

### production

- One SM7B downstage-center on a short boom
- One DI box for the guitar, routed to stage-left wedge and FOH
- LX-01 is a single downstage special plus a house wash at 20 pct
- No smoke, no haze, no rig drop

### setlist

1. THE SOONER PART
2. IF I HAVE TO ASK
3. BLUE HOUSE
4. THE LAST GOOD YEAR
5. EIGHT-MINUTE WALK
6. WHITE LIGHTS
7. THE NEXT ONE OUT

### changeover notes

Mara walks off at 19:40. Fifteen-minute changeover. Her mic stand and DI break down in under four minutes. The riser for Holdfast Orchestra comes in from upstage-right on dollies. Stage clear at 19:55 and house hold.
