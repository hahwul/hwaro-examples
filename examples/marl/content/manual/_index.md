Read this manual the way Marl stores data: top to bottom. The surface chapters
cover the shape of your data and how it enters the system; the middle chapters
describe how it ages through retention tiers and downsampling; the deepest
chapters cover querying across all of it, gaps and all. Every chapter is
written against Marl 0.9 and includes runnable examples.
