My co-founder and I ran a three-person brand consultancy before we ran a software company, and every CRM we tried was built for a department we didn't have. The market leader wanted an administrator. The marketing-suite upstart wanted a growth team. Even the tools marketed at freelancers crept toward enterprise pricing the moment we added a fourth client. So we spent a wet January building the tool we actually wanted: one pipeline, [one price](/pricing/), one plan. Traction has been profitable since month four, has never taken outside money, and is still run by the two of us — one of whom is probably answering [your support email](/contact/) personally, usually within the hour.
