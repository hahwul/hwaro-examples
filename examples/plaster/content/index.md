## Material Honesty

Plaster is about the beauty of the unfinished. We believe that innovation doesn't need to be polished to be effective. By embracing the raw texture of the work and revealing the underlying structure, we create a design that is both honest and enduring.

### Raw Innovation

- **Texture**: The tactile experience of the work, even in a digital medium.
- **Structure**: The clear revelation of the systems that support the design.
- **Endurance**: A design built to last beyond the current aesthetic trends.

---

> "The most beautiful structures are the ones that don't try to hide what they are made of."
