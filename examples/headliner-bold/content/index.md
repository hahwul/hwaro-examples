## How to read the billing

Every tier on the poster above is set at 60 percent of the previous tier's font size, starting from the 220px headliner name and descending through sub-headliners, afternoon slots, openers, and the small print for late additions. The brackets mark the boundaries of each tier; the stars indicate billing weight.

Contractually, font size on the poster is a real commercial asset: the headliner's name may not be set smaller than 60 percent of the festival's own name, and the second-billing acts may not share a line with anyone outside their tier. These rules are negotiated four months before the poster goes to press.
