# About Cinema

Cinema is a film review site dedicated to thoughtful critique and the celebration of storytelling through motion pictures.

We believe every film deserves a fair hearing. From studio blockbusters to independent productions, from animation to documentary, we approach each work on its own terms and judge it by how well it achieves what it sets out to do.

## Our Rating System

We rate films on a 5-point scale:

- **5** -- A masterwork. Essential viewing.
- **4** -- Excellent. Highly recommended.
- **3** -- Good. Worth your time with minor reservations.
- **2** -- Below average. Has some merit but falls short.
- **1** -- Poor. Not recommended.

## What We Cover

Our reviews focus on theatrical releases, festival selections, and notable streaming premieres. Each review includes director and cast credits, genre classification, runtime, and our honest assessment of the work.
