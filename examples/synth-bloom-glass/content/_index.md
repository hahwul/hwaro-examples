Welcome to **Synth Bloom Glass**.

This Hwaro theme brings together the deep, resonant aesthetic of a neon void with the elegant, frosted translucency of glassmorphism. Floating, animated radial gradients—the *blooms*—slowly pulse behind a pane of digital glass, creating an atmosphere that is both calm and fiercely modern.

## Features

- **Glassmorphism UI**: Panels that diffuse the background light with a `backdrop-filter` blur.
- **Synthwave Palette**: Glowing accents of neon pink, cyan, and deep purple set against an obsidian background.
- **Fluid Animation**: CSS-driven floating blooms that breathe life into the void without relying on JavaScript.
- **Modern Typography**: A sharp combination of Space Grotesk for headings and Inter for smooth readability.
