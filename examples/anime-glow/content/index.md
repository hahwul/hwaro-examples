## Animated Radiance

Anime-Glow is about the vibrant and atmospheric nature of innovation within the immersive environment. We believe that energetic content deserves a presentation that captures the glow of early digital anime. By focusing on dark backgrounds, glowing borders, and futuristic typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the aura.

### Glow Principles

- **Vibrancy**: A design that prioritizes the clear "aura" of the content.
- **Atmosphere**: Using light and shadow to define the boundaries of the energetic space.
- **Impact**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 21st-century digital production.
