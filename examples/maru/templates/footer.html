maru — an incremental build system with content-addressed caching and hermetic task graphs.

maru is named for the Korean word for the raised wooden floor of a hanok: a level, load-bearing surface that everything else rests on. The build graph is meant to feel the same way — quiet, structural, and there when you need it.
