The single line is the most primitive of architectural elements. It defines the horizon, dividing space and creating a sense of scale. Within our minimalist context, it is the boundary between existence and the void.

This study explores how a simple, continuous stroke can anchor an entire composition, providing an inescapable foundation for the ethereal elements floating above.
