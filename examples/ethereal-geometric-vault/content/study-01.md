The circle represents an infinite boundary, a form without beginning or end. Within the context of the ethereal vault, it acts as a central anchor, contrasting the linear structures that surround it.

By reducing the circle to a pure, unembellished stroke, we expose its fundamental geometric nature. It becomes not just a shape, but a focal point of meditation within the void space.
