A square, when rotated to rest on its point, introduces a sense of tension and dynamism while retaining its inherent stability. It challenges the viewer's perception of gravity and structure.

In this study, the angled square serves as a testament to deliberate disorientation, encouraging a deeper engagement with the architectural forms of the vault.
