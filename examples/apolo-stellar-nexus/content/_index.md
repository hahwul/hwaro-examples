Welcome to the **Apolo Stellar Nexus**.

Discover a realm where deep cosmic voids meet glowing interstellar energy and elegant frosted glass. This design leverages modern CSS properties like `backdrop-filter`, glowing animations, and futuristic typography to create a truly immersive experience.

## Features

- **Cosmic Void Background:** Deep, dark, and endless.
- **Stellar Orbs:** Animated glowing spheres that drift through the background.
- **Glassmorphism Panels:** Frosted, translucent interfaces providing clear visual hierarchy.
- **Elegant Typography:** Utilizes Space Grotesk and Inter for a clean, futuristic look.

Explore the nexus and dive into the unknown.
