I used to keep the windows closed at night.

I used to believe that if something wanted you badly enough, it would knock.

Now I leave the lamp burning on the desk and the casement cracked the width of two fingers. I tell myself it is an experiment. I tell myself I am only documenting.

The truth is simpler and much older: I have grown tired of being the only warm thing in the room.

If you are reading this, the cabinet is still open. The labels are still legible. The dust on the glass is only dust until it moves.

Do not touch the specimens.

Some of them are still learning how to answer.
