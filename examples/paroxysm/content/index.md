## Systemic Eruptions

Paroxysm is about the sudden, intense release of energy within a system. We don't believe in gradual change; we believe in the moment where the pressure becomes too great and the structure must erupt to survive.

### Characteristics

- **Suddenness**: The transition from stable to explosive is near-instant.
- **Intensity**: The peak level of energy output.
- **Transience**: A state that exists only in the moment of release.
