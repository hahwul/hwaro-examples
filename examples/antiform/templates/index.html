§ 01 — MANIFESTO

WE OPPOSE THE DEFAULT. WE BUILD THE ANSWER.

The design we inherit is exhausted. Soft corners, soft promises, soft work. Antiform was assembled to push back — to render the brief obsolete the moment it arrives. We argue with the page until it concedes.

Every project leaves with a position. Every position leaves with a reason. We do not decorate. We file documents into the record and trust the work to survive its first opinion.
