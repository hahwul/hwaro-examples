## The Power of the Margin

Marginalia is about the value of the supplementary thought. We believe that innovation is often found not in the primary text, but in the notes written in the edges. By using an asymmetrical layout and focusing on the relationship between the core and the commentary, we reveal the complexity of the creative process.

### Commentary Innovation

The space on the side is not empty; it is a field of potential. It is where we record our reactions, our challenges, and our refinements of the main idea.

> "The margin is where the conversation begins."
