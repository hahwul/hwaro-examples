Six creatures survive from the working set. Each was drawn as a full plate on the verso leaf and glossed on the facing recto, in the order the workshop finished them rather than any taxonomy the compilers might have preferred. A marsh dweller and a mountain dweller often share a spread by accident of scheduling, which the guild's supervising scribe considered a fault; readers four centuries on have generally disagreed.
