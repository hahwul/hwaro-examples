We curate thoughts on art, architecture, and minimal computer systems.

This space is dedicated to exploring the intersection of **luxurious digital media** and **raw static site compilation**. By leaving behind the heavy load of third-party tracking scripts, large utility styling files, and dynamic servers, we create a quiet oasis of performance and design.

---

## the purity of whitespace

In design, what you leave out is often more important than what you put in. By utilizing generous negative space, we allow our typography to breathe, ensuring that every sentence, word, and character carries maximum visual and intellectual weight.

* **High-contrast serif headings**: Using Playfair Display to command attention with classical grace.
* **Refined sans-serif body**: Using Inter to deliver seamless reading at any text size.
* **Invisible constraints**: Relying on structural margins instead of rigid grid lines.
