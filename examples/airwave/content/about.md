## About Airwave

Airwave is a biweekly podcast about technology and the people behind it. Each episode features in-depth conversations with engineers, designers, and founders who are building the tools and platforms that shape our industry.

## Format

Episodes range from 30 to 60 minutes. Each one centers on a single topic with a guest who brings hands-on experience. We skip the hype and focus on what actually works, what failed, and what comes next.
