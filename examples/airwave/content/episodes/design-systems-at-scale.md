Marcus Webb walks us through the real challenges of maintaining a design system used by dozens of product teams. Adoption, versioning, breaking changes, and the politics of shared infrastructure.

## Topics Covered

- Getting buy-in from teams that already have their own components
- Versioning strategies that minimize breaking changes
- Documentation that people actually read
- When to say no to one-off component requests
- Measuring adoption without turning it into a mandate
