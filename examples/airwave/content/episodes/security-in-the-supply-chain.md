After a year of high-profile supply chain incidents, Alex Rivera breaks down what went wrong, what the industry has learned, and practical steps teams can take today to reduce their exposure.

## Topics Covered

- Anatomy of recent supply chain attacks
- SBOM: useful tool or compliance checkbox?
- Lock files, pinning, and reproducible builds
- The human side: maintainer burnout and trust
- Policy approaches that work without slowing teams down
