In this episode, Sara Chen shares her experience building payment APIs that handle millions of requests per day. We discuss circuit breakers, retry strategies, idempotency keys, and why the simplest approach often wins.

## Topics Covered

- Designing for partial failure from day one
- Circuit breaker patterns and when they actually help
- The case for idempotency in every write endpoint
- Rate limiting as a feature, not just a safeguard
- Lessons from real outages and what changed afterward
