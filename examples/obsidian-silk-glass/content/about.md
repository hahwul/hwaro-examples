# The Philosophy

The **Obsidian Silk Glass** theme is designed for those who appreciate understated elegance. It strips away harsh contrasts in favor of deep, muted slate and obsidian tones.

## The Aesthetic

The glassmorphism effect is kept subtle—like light diffusing through dark silk.

- The layout is clean and uncluttered.
- The interactive elements provide a gentle, glowing feedback.
- The typography relies on `Space Grotesk` for distinctive headings and `Inter` for highly legible body copy.

Enjoy writing and reading in a serene, distraction-free environment powered by Hwaro.
