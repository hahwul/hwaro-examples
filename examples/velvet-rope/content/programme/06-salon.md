## Unhurried Conversation

The final room of the evening. Coffee and brandy are served in the library; the terrace opens for those who would like a cigar under the plane trees.

### Library

The library stays open until 01:30. A second pianist plays softly for the first hour; the music fades at 00:30 and conversation takes over.

### Terrace

The terrace opens at 23:30 and closes at 02:00. A small service of digestifs and the house smoking tray.

### Departures

Cars are called from the foyer by the cloakroom. Please allow ten minutes between request and arrival on a busy night.
