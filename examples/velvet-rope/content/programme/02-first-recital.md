## Bach, Suite №3 in C Major

Idris Lemaire performs the complete Bach Suite №3 in C Major on the Mercer House cello — an 1894 Bernardel, on loan to the gala each year.

### Movements

1. Prelude
2. Allemande
3. Courante
4. Sarabande
5. Bourrée I & II
6. Gigue

### Acoustic

The Grand Salon has a natural resonance warmest between 55 and 75 Hz — ideal for the low register of the suite. No amplification.
