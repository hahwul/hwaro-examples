## The Salon Tables

Seven tables of ten seat the remainder of the guest list. Tables are assigned by the seating secretary; guests are notified in writing no later than the Thursday before.

### Rotation

A short table rotation occurs between the dinner and the second recital. Four guests from each table move by prior arrangement. The rotation is not mandatory; guests who prefer to remain seated should inform the seating secretary on arrival.

### Numbering

Tables are numbered II through VIII, clockwise from the principal table. Place cards have the table number in the lower right; the seat number is on the reverse.
