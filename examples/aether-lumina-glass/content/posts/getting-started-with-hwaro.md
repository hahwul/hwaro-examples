Hwaro means furnace. It takes raw elements (Markdown, templates) and refines them into pure static assets.

## The Process

1.  **Initialize**: `hwaro init aether`
2.  **Forge**: Edit `templates/` to shape your light.
3.  **Render**: `hwaro build` to output the structure.

Enjoy the speed of Crystal.
