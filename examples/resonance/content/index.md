Resonance is a half-hour show about growing food where nobody expects it to grow — fire escapes, loading docks, church parking lots, and the six square feet behind a laundromat. Every episode pairs a working grower with the numbers behind their plot: square footage, sun hours, load limits, and the compost math that makes the whole thing hold together.

We record on-site whenever we can, so you will hear wind, pigeons, and the occasional forklift. We think that is the point.
