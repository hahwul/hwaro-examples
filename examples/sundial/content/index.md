## Material Chronology

Sundial is about the temporal and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that chronological content deserves a presentation that captures the light of the early horological era. By focusing on stone-inspired textures, warm natural surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the shadow.

### Light Principles

- **Temporal**: A design that prioritizes the clear flow of time-based information.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the temporal space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical time measurement.
