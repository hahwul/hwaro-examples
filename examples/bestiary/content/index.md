## Cataloging the Unknown

Bestiary is an exploration of the fantastical and the illustrative. We believe that innovation is often born from myth and imagination. By focusing on a medieval manuscript aesthetic and gothic-inspired typography, we create a design that feels both historical and deeply imaginative.

### Mythological Innovation

- **Illustration**: Using visual metaphors to explain complex concepts.
- **Narrative**: Framing the work within a larger, more fantastical story.
- **History**: Drawing on ancient forms to give weight to modern ideas.
