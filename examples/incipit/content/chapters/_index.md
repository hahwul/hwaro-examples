Each essay in this section opens on a faint fragment of its city's own street plan and follows a market, a ruin, a rebuilt grid, or a buried stream to its present arrangement. Read them in order — the arrows at the foot of each page carry you from one to the next — or return to the [foreword](/foreword/) for how the whole collection fits together.
