We are a collective of digital architects dedicated to stripping away the unnecessary fluff of the modern web. We believe that websites should be fast, highly structured, and confident.

## our manifesto

* **Grid is law**: Everything aligns to structural boundaries.
* **Colors must be pure**: We use raw pigment colors like flat Canary Yellow `#FFE600` and high-contrast Black `#0F0F0F` instead of soft gradient masks.
* **No icons without semantic meaning**: Emojis and generic symbols are excluded to avoid cluttering human cognition.
* **Speed is form**: Pages compile instantaneously and render using highly optimized, standard Vanilla CSS.

## technical construction

This theme is built as a premier demonstration of the **Hwaro** static site engine. It uses:
- Bespoke Vanilla CSS (zero utility frameworks).
- Google Fonts: Space Grotesk for geometric title power, and JetBrains Mono for system log readability.
- 100% responsive flex and grid layouts.
