Every patch below is a complete, reproducible signal path for the Karst-6 — not a starting point to tweak, but a destination you can dial in from a cold patch bay in a few minutes. Each entry documents its sources, its modulation routing, and its final settings, alongside a signal-flow diagram of the exact cabling.

Patches are organized by family in the sidebar. Ctrl+K (Cmd+K) opens the command palette if you already know the name you are looking for.
