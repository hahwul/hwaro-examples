## Graphic Storytelling

Manga-Panel is about the organized and foundational nature of structural innovation within the sequential environment. We believe that graphic content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic manga chapter. By focusing on white surfaces, bold black borders, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the panel.

### Panel Principles

- **Order**: A design that prioritizes the clear arrangement of visual units.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines to define the boundaries of the sequential space.
- **Clarity**: A layout that reflects the logical nature of graphic storytelling.
