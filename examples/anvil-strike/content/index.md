## The Strike

Every great work begins with a strike. The anvil is the silent witness to the transformation of raw material into refined excellence.

### Forging Process

1. **Heating**: Bringing the raw ideas to the necessary temperature for change.
2. **Shaping**: The repeated, intentional application of force.
3. **Quenching**: Cooling the work to lock in its new, stronger form.

> "Strength is not just what you build, but what you endure during the build."

### Characteristics

- **Durability**: Built to last beyond the current trend.
- **Weight**: A presence that cannot be ignored.
- **Precision**: Handcrafted details that define the work.
