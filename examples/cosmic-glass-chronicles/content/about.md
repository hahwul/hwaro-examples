The **Cosmic Glass Chronicles** is a demonstration of the power of minimal, aesthetic-driven design. It leverages the static site generation capabilities of Hwaro to deliver a lightning-fast, visually striking experience.

### Technical Details
- **Generator**: Hwaro
- **Theme Style**: Dark, Cyberpunk, Glassmorphism
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk for headings, Inter for body text.

*This project is part of the Hwaro Examples repository, showcasing unique and creative themes built with simple HTML and CSS.*
