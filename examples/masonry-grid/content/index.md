## Masonry Structure

Masonry-Grid is about the dynamic and foundational nature of structural innovation within the variable environment. We believe that high-density content deserves a presentation that captures the flow of a classic masonry layout. By focusing on white surfaces, variable heights, and clear hierarchies, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the flow.

### Grid Principles

- **Flow**: A design that prioritizes the clear arrangement of visual units across heights.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the variable space.
- **Clarity**: A layout that reflects the logical nature of structural storytelling.
