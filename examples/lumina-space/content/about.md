# The Lumina Protocol

Established in the year 2142, Lumina Space was originally a mining colony in the Omega sector. After the depletion of the local resouces, the station was retrofitted by a collective of independent engineers to serve as a neutral hub.

## Our Mission

We believe that knowledge should not be confined to planetary gravity wells. Our mission is to broadcast human culture, science, and history into the cosmos, ensuring our legacy endures long after the stars fade.

## Technical Specifications

The station operates on a decentralized energy grid, powered by a nearby quantum singularity. Our databanks utilize crystalline memory structures, capable of storing the sum of all known digital information with zero degradation.

*   **Orbit:** Geostationary around Gas Giant 'Aethelgard'
*   **Population:** 4,200 (Organic and Synthetic)
*   **Primary Export:** Information
