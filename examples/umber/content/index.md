Umber is a sound design practice for planetariums and museum halls. Every project starts in the room, not the studio — a speaker count, a dome's absorption curve, a display case that resonates at 340Hz whether anyone asked it to. What follows is the practice's install history: one piece per room, each with its own speaker topology and the decisions that came out of it. More on the method behind that on the [about](/about/) page.
