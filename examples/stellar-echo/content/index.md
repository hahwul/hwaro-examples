Welcome to **Stellar Echo**. This theme is designed for those who navigate the vast expanses of the digital universe. It features a deep, space-inspired aesthetic with minimal typography, smooth cosmic animations, and an elegant layout.

### Why Stellar Echo?

- **Immersive Environment:** Dive into a dark background speckled with animated starlight.
- **Minimalist Typography:** Utilizing the *Space Grotesk* font for a futuristic yet readable experience.
- **Fluid Layout:** Designed to look stunning on any screen, from mission control monitors to handheld communicators.
