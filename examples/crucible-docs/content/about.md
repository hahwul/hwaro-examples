# About Us

This is an about page to demonstrate multiple pages. The Crucible documentation set serves the materials testing group of a mid-sized aerospace contractor headquartered in Daejeon.

## The Team

Twelve technicians, four engineers, and two safety officers maintain the manual together. New revisions go through a weekly review on Wednesdays so that operators on the morning shift can read the changes before they touch the equipment.

## Scope

The pages cover everything between sample arrival at the loading dock and the moment a signed report leaves the document control office. The manual deliberately stops at the analytical chemistry interface, because a separate group owns those procedures.

## How To Contribute

Edits are welcome from anyone with a lab badge. Open a ticket in the internal tracker, attach the proposed change, and tag the section owner. Review usually closes within five business days, faster if the change blocks a scheduled run.
