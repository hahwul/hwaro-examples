## Thermal Testing

The foundational nature of structural integrity in extreme environments. Crucible is a documentation pack for engineering teams running materials through repeated heat-soak cycles.

## What This Manual Covers

Three areas form the bulk of the documentation. Each one assumes a baseline of metallurgy or polymer science but tries to keep the writing accessible to operators who joined the lab after January 2025.

### Furnace Procedures

Step-by-step routines for the four furnace types we currently support, including the rebuilt induction unit installed in March 2026. Each procedure lists the safety officer who signed off on the latest revision.

### Sample Preparation

Cutting, polishing, and mounting routines for ceramic, metal, and composite samples. The section ends with a short troubleshooting guide based on the failure modes most often reported through the lab ticket system.

### Result Reporting

Templates for recording dimensional change, mass loss, and visible surface effects. The reporting templates feed directly into the central archive so that downstream analysts do not need to reformat the raw measurements.
