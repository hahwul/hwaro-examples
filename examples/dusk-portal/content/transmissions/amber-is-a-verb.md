At Dusk Portal, amber is not a colour. Amber is a verb. It is the thing a map does when it wants to point at something. It never appears on a background. It never appears on a heading twice. It never *decorates*.

> One accent, used once, makes an argument. Used twice, it makes a pattern. Used three times, it makes a colour story. We stop at one.

Lavender does the ambient work. Amber does the pointing. That is the whole rig, and it has survived four revisions of the studio style sheet without needing amendments.
