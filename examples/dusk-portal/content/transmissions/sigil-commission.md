A single-page brand identity for Night Harbour Press, a small publisher of sci-fi novellas on folded paper. The sigil is a hexagon, an amber inset, and a line that implies a horizon. Nothing else. The entire brand fits on one printed page, delivered by hand.

> A sigil is an identity that refuses to become a system.

Night Harbour ships six novellas a year. The sigil ships on every cover.
