The studio&rsquo;s dusk observation log is a small, ongoing exercise: every dusk, for three years, we&rsquo;ve written one paragraph about the sky. The log is not a design project. It is a reminder. It keeps the palette honest.

Year three begins this week. A selection of recent entries:

- *Clear. Lavender bruised to navy by 19:12. One amber cloud, uninvited.*
- *Haze. No visible fade. The palette was replaced wholesale by grey.*
- *Rain. Impossible to log. Beautiful anyway.*

The log is not for sale. It is filed, weekly, in the studio&rsquo;s lavender binder.
