Signal Transport 04 is the studio&rsquo;s ongoing experiment in handheld field devices. It reads atmospheric data at a glance, reports weather shifts in three bands, and holds a charge for twenty-six hours. The dial is lavender. The needle is amber. There are no status lights, because a needle is a status light.

## Build

- Aluminium casing, brushed.
- Lavender dial face, hand-printed.
- Amber needle, counterweighted.
- No display. No buttons. A single dial.

## Philosophy

The best signal devices do one thing well and refuse to do anything else. Signal Transport 04 is not a watch. It is not a radio. It is a single glance, repeated as needed.
