Portable Atlas 03 was a commission from a signal-arts festival that did not yet have a venue. The brief asked for a map of a place that had not been chosen. The atlas was printed with blank coordinates, stamped by hand on arrival, and delivered to attendees in lavender envelopes.

## Plates

1. Overview (undrawn on delivery).
2. Local signal map (hand-stamped).
3. Field reference for portable antennas.
4. Invited frequencies.
5. Unassigned ribbons for personal notes.

The festival was held across three sites. The atlases are now collected in a single slim edition, if you ask.
