# The Mirage Project

The **Quantum Mirage** project focuses on identifying, observing, and documenting the rare cosmic phenomena that occur in deep space, primarily looking at intersections of quantum fields and gravity wells.

> "The universe is not only stranger than we imagine, it is stranger than we can imagine."
> — J.B.S. Haldane

We hope to catalog these findings and share them with the broader scientific community.
