## Material Vision

Telescope is about the distant and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that visionary content deserves a presentation that captures the observation of the early astronomical era. By focusing on brass-inspired textures, dark natural surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the lens.

### Vision Principles

- **Distant**: A design that prioritizes the clear focusing of complex information.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the visionary space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical observation.
