Uplift runs on two kinds of listener: people who show up for one author's book and leave, and people who stay for the whole shelf. Both are welcome, and we've priced accordingly rather than funneling everyone toward a subscription by default.

<!-- more -->

A single title is yours outright — no expiring license, no app lock-in, redownload it in ten years if the format still exists. The Shelf is for listeners who want the whole catalog plus the Friday release the moment it lands, automatically, without checking a calendar. The annual plan is the same deal at roughly two months off, billed once so you stop noticing the charge.

There is no free tier with ads, no algorithmic queue deciding what plays next, and no dynamic pricing based on how badly you want a particular title. The prices below are what everyone pays.
