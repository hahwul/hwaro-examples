The player is built around chapters, not minutes. Every scrubber in the app — on a lock screen, in a car dashboard, in the browser tab you left open at your desk — shows the same segmented bar you see on the catalog cards, one dash per chapter. Skip forward and you land on a chapter boundary the author chose, not wherever thirty seconds happens to land you mid-sentence.

<!-- more -->

Downloads are chapter-level too, so a spotty connection on a train doesn't cost you the whole book, just the chapter that was still copying. Progress syncs within a few seconds across every device signed into the same account, including the one you forgot you were using.

Pick your platform below for specific requirements. Everything ships from the same account, the same library, and the same weekly Friday drop.
