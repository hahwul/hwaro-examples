# The Vision

Aurora Cyber Forge was born from a desire to merge the raw, high-tech energy of cyberpunk with the sleek, modern elegance of glassmorphism. It serves as a testing ground for cutting-edge UI/UX design within the Hwaro ecosystem.

## Design Philosophy

We believe in interfaces that are not just functional, but atmospheric. By leveraging modern CSS features like `backdrop-filter` and animated radial gradients, we create a sense of depth and life on the screen.

> "In the neon glow, the glass reveals the code."
