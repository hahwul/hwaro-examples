# Welcome to Aurora Cyber Forge

This is a sanctuary of digital craftsmanship, blending deep void darkness with vibrant neon aurora elements. We forge elegant, responsive, and blazing fast static sites powered by Hwaro.

## The Aesthetic

- **Neon Auroras**: Fluid glowing gradients mimicking celestial phenomena.
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent, frosted glass panels that reveal the depths beneath.
- **Cyberpunk Edge**: Deep dark backgrounds (#030508) accented with cyan, magenta, and purple neon.
