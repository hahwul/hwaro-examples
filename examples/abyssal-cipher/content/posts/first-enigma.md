In the beginning, there was only void. Then, structure was born.

The first enigma lies not in the complexity of the design, but in its stark simplicity. By removing gradients and embracing solid contrasts, we reveal the true form of the digital space.

```css
:root {
    --bg-deep-navy: #02040A;
    --text-primary: #E2E8F0;
}
```

This contrast defines the boundary between presence and absence.
