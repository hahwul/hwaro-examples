Typography is more than the presentation of text; it is the scaffolding upon which meaning is hung.

> "A well-chosen typeface acts as the load-bearing wall of a digital construct."

By juxtaposing *Cormorant Garamond* with *Inter*, we create a tension between historical authority and modern utility. The serifs provide anchor points in the void, while the sans-serif ensures efficient traversal of information.
