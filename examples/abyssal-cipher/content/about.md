This project demonstrates a dark, structural, and elegant design language. The Abyssal Cipher emphasizes:

- **Solid Architecture**: Strong contrasting colors with deep navy, slate, and a touch of gold.
- **Absence of Gradients**: Pure color fields.
- **Typographic Depth**: Coupling the historical elegance of Cormorant Garamond with the stark modernism of Inter.

It is built as an example for the Hwaro static site generator.
