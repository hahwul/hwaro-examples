# About Terrace

Terrace is a Mediterranean lifestyle journal dedicated to the quiet pleasures of sun-warmed stone, coastal breezes, and unhurried afternoons. We believe that the best moments in life unfold slowly, on a terrace overlooking the sea with a glass of something cool in hand.

Our stories explore the landscapes, traditions, and daily rituals that define Mediterranean living. From the olive groves of Andalusia to the painted tiles of Lisbon, from the fishing villages of the Aegean to the hilltop towns of Provence, we follow the light wherever it leads.

Whether you are planning your next journey along the coast or simply longing for a slower pace, Terrace invites you to pull up a chair, feel the warmth of the afternoon sun, and linger a while. There is no rush here. The sea will still be there when you look up.
