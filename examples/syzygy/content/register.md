One ticket covers all three days: every keynote, every talk, both
workshop slots, lunch, and the Monday reception. There is no separate
track pass — Syzygy is small enough that everyone sees everything they
want to see.

## Tiers

| Tier | Price | Window | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Early bird | €420 | closed 31 Jan 2026 | Sold out four days early |
| Standard | €520 | now through 5 Mar 2026 | Current tier |
| Student | €180 | now through 5 Mar 2026 | Valid `.edu`-equivalent ID at pickup |
| On-site | €640 | 12–14 Mar 2026 | Cash or card, subject to capacity |

Workshop seats are limited to 40 per session and included in every tier —
reserve one during checkout, not at the door. The slab-allocator workshop
on day one has historically filled first.

## What is included

- All three days of talks and keynotes, Main Hall and Annex
- Both workshop sessions, materials provided
- Lunch and coffee breaks, all three days
- The Monday reception in the Turbine Hall foyer
- Video access to every talk recording within two weeks of the closing
  keynote

## Group and refund policy

Teams of four or more registering together take 15% off the standard
rate — email registration with your team roster before checkout and a
discount code follows within a day. Refunds are full up to 14 February
2026, fifty percent through 28 February, and unavailable after that;
transfers to a colleague are always free and can happen right up to
the morning of day one at the registration desk.

```text
$ syzygy register --tier=standard --workshop=slab-allocator
> reserving seat... confirmed
> badge: will print on arrival, 08:00–18:00 daily
> receipt sent to your registration email
```

Questions about accessibility, dietary requirements, or visa invitation
letters go to the same registration address — invitation letters are
turned around within three business days. For hall layout and transit
directions, see [the venue page](/venue/); for the full run of show,
see [the three-day programme](/talks/).
