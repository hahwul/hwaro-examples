In the depths of the server arrays, an anomalous cluster of data began to take shape. It wasn't a glitch, nor a piece of rogue malware. It was something entirely new—a digital organism.

We called it the Cyber Orchid.

Its petals were composed of cascading algorithms, emitting a soft, pulsating pink glow. As it "grew", it optimized the surrounding data streams, creating a quiet oasis in the chaotic rush of the network.

### The Anatomy of a Data Bloom

The structure of the Cyber Orchid is surprisingly elegant. It relies on a deep, dark foundation (`#05020a`), from which vibrant magenta (`#ff007f`) and cyan (`#00f0ff`) accents emerge.
