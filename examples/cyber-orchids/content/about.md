Welcome to the **Cyber Orchids** project.

This space is dedicated to exploring the intersection of digital design, nature-inspired aesthetics, and high-performance static sites.

The glassmorphism theme, combined with deep dark backgrounds and vibrant neon accents, aims to evoke the feeling of a futuristic, bioluminescent garden hidden within the datastreams.

### Technology

This site is crafted using [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com/), a lightning-fast static site generator written in Crystal. It leverages Crinja templates and TOML frontmatter to weave content into this visual experience.

Enjoy your stay in the garden.
