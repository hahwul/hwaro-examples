# Welcome to Solaris Forge

Experience the fusion of dark abyss aesthetics and radiant solar energy. This template leverages **Hwaro's** fast static site generation to deliver a unique, performant glassmorphism experience.

Here, celestial elements meet modern UI design. Explore the depths of the void illuminated by burning solar flares, casting light through frosted translucent panels.

### Features

- **Blazing Fast**: Powered by Hwaro.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: Frosted glass panels with `backdrop-filter`.
- **Solar Aesthetics**: Warm, glowing animated orbs representing celestial bodies.
- **Responsive Layout**: Adapts seamlessly to any device.
