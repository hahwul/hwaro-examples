## Atmospheric Translucency

Cloud-Glass is about the softest possible state of structural innovation. We believe that ideas can be powerful even when they are diffused and light. By focusing on very light, translucent elements and a serene, airy palette, we create a design that feels both expansive and deeply clear. Our focus is on the way light interacts with the atmosphere of the work.

### Floating Innovation

- **Diffusion**: The gentle spread of ideas throughout the system.
- **Ethereality**: A design that feels light and unburdened by excess.
- **Clarity**: Finding the core meaning within the cloud.
