## Material Printing

Lead-Type is about the foundational and unshakeable nature of structural innovation within the printing environment. We believe that core content deserves a presentation that captures the permanence of the early movable type era. By focusing on dark lead textures, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the type.

### Type Principles

- **Permanence**: A design that prioritizes the long-term stability of the printed word.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the leaden space.
- **Authority**: A layout that reflects the solid, uncompromised nature of the press.
