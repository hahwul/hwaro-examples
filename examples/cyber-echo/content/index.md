# Welcome to Cyber Echo

Enter the digital frontier. This is a neon-lit cyberpunk experience featuring deep dark backgrounds, fluid animations, and a sleek glassmorphism aesthetic.

The neon glow of the city reflects in the scattered shards of data.

> "In the neon-drenched streets, the echoes of data pulse to the rhythm of the city."

- **Neon Accents:** Cyan and Magenta glowing elements.
- **Glassmorphism:** Frosted, translucent interfaces.
- **Dark Void:** A deep #050510 backdrop.
