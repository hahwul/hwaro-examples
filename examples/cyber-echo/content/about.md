# About the Echo

The **Cyber Echo** project was forged in the depths of the digital void, designed to bring a vivid, immersive cyberpunk aesthetic to modern static sites.

It blends the nostalgic neon of retro-futurism with cutting-edge UI patterns like **glassmorphism**.

### Core Principles

1.  **Vivid Contrast:** Bright neon against deep dark backgrounds.
2.  **Translucency:** Layers of data shimmering through frosted glass.
3.  **Performance:** Powered by the lightning-fast Hwaro static site generator.
