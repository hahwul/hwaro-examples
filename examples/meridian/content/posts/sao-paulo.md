Sao Paulo runs on Brasilia Time, three hours behind UTC. As the financial capital of South America and the largest city in the Southern Hemisphere, its clock drives the economic pulse of an entire continent.

<!-- more -->

## B3 Exchange

The B3 (Brasil Bolsa Balcao) is the largest stock exchange in Latin America. Trading hours run from 10:00 to 17:00 BRT, overlapping with New York's session and creating a window where both Americas trade simultaneously.

## Time Zone Complexity

Brazil spans four time zones, from UTC-2 (Fernando de Noronha) to UTC-5 (Acre). Sao Paulo and Brasilia share UTC-3, but the country's vast east-west span means sunset in Acre comes nearly two hours after sunset in the eastern islands.

## Key Timezone Facts

- **Longitude:** 46.6333 W
- **Standard Time:** BRT (UTC-3)
- **Daylight Saving:** Discontinued in 2019
- **Previous DST:** BRST (UTC-2)
