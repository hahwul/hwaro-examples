As you descend, the pressure increases linearly. At the bottom of the Marianas Trench, the pressure is over 1,000 times standard atmospheric pressure at sea level.

<!-- more -->

How do creatures survive here? They have evolved with water-filled bodies that equalized pressure. In our digital design, "pressure" can be thought of as the constraint of the viewport. Our layout is flexible and responsive, designed to "equalize" and look stunning whether viewed on a massive desktop monitor or a compact mobile device.

The structure of our site, much like the resilient forms of deep-sea life, relies on fluid mechanics (CSS Grid and Flexbox) rather than rigid, brittle frameworks.
