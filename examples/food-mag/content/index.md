## Culinary Excellence

Food-Mag is about the warm and foundational nature of structural innovation within the culinary environment. We believe that food research deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic gourmet magazine. By focusing on white surfaces, subtle shadows, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply welcoming. Our focus is on the integrity of the culinary process.

### Mag Principles

- **Savoring**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of recipes and reviews.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the culinary space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical gourmet publishing.
