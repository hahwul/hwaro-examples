## The Cradle of Innovation

Incunabula is about the early, formative stages of a great movement. We believe that innovation is best understood when viewed in its most primitive, stable state. By focusing on the early-printing-press aesthetic and the integrity of the cradle, we create a design that feels both historically significant and deeply foundational.

### Primitive Printing

- **Origin**: The precise starting point of the current structure.
- **Stability**: A design built on the most reliable, tested methods.
- **Reach**: The gradual, outward movement of the idea from the cradle to the world.
