**Astral Zenith Glass** is an example site designed for the Hwaro SSG, illustrating how custom designs can easily be integrated.

By avoiding complex frameworks and writing CSS directly inside templates, this example prioritizes a streamlined workflow while achieving a breathtaking **dark cosmic** aesthetic. The design relies on the interaction between vivid neon orbs and the deep void-like background color.
