## The Editorial Vision

In a world of blur, we choose the sharp. Guillotine Cut is an exploration of high-contrast editorial design, where every element is intentional and every line is clean.

> "Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak."

### Seasonal Strata

- **Spring**: The softening of the edge.
- **Summer**: The intensity of the light.
- **Autumn**: The depth of the shadow.
- **Winter**: The purity of the line.

---

The guillotine represents the ultimate decision: the cut that defines the frame. We embrace that finality.
