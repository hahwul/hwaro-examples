## Imprint

**BLEED EDGE** is a full-bleed experimental quarterly. Our operating principle is simple: the rectangle of the finished page is a convenience, not a boundary. Ink stops where the guillotine stops, and the guillotine is never perfectly true.

Printed in 4-over-4 process CMYK with a single fluorescent spot. Press sheets are over-inked by 0.4 density units to compensate for the bleed. Cover stock is 170gsm recycled, uncoated. Interior stock is 90gsm.

## Production Principle

Traditional publishing leaves a 12mm inner margin, a 15mm outer margin, and a safe area in which all text must sit. We invert the hierarchy: the bleed area is where decisions happen. The safe area is a dwindling rectangle in the centre of the page, and it is not always safe.

> We have no respect for the trim. The trim is a suggestion made by the guillotine.
