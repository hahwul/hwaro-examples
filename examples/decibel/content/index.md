## Sonic Intensity

Decibel is an exploration of the power that lies within the sound. We don't just listen; we measure the impact of every frequency and every vibration. Our focus is on the sheer volume of innovation and the way it resonates within the environment.

### Measuring the Roar

The intensity of a message is often tied to its delivery. We use the decibel scale to quantify the reach and influence of our projects.

- **Gain**: The amplification of the original intent.
- **Pressure**: The force driving the sonic wave.
- **Resonance**: The duration of the echo after the initial strike.
