## Resident Ensemble

The North Meridian Quartet enter their second year as First Light's resident ensemble. Their opening-night set is a rearrangement of three chamber works by composers who have never been performed at the festival before.

**Programme**

- *Still Life*, Aliénor Prévost (2022)
- *For a Slow River*, Paul Wingate (2019)
- *Parallel Streets*, Pham Minh Khoi (2025)

**Line**

Nora Westlund (violin), Ilya Reznikov (violin), Zara Demir (viola), Arthur Holbein (cello).
