## Vocal and Electronics

After the interval, Mireille Okonkwo returns to the Horizon stage with a reworked version of her 2025 programme *Second Light*. The set mixes live vocals with a modular synth rig built around a single clocked sequence.

**Set**

1. *Small Rooms*
2. *Second Light (Reprise)*
3. *Stairwell Song*
4. *For the People Who Stayed*

**Note**

The stage will be lit only in amber for this set. Please remain seated during the opening three minutes.
