## Ceremonial Opener

Paloma Ríos returns to the First Light series for a third consecutive season. Her five-piece horn ensemble opens the night with a short arrangement of traditional brass fanfares reworked for the Horizon Hall acoustic.

**Set Notes**

- Three fanfares, run together without pause
- Rehearsed on the stage since Monday
- Lighting cue: cold white, slow rise

**Line**

Paloma Ríos (trumpet, direction), Tariq Okoye (trumpet), Helga Svensson (French horn), Marco Linati (trombone), Ezra Kim (tuba).

> The room has to wake up somehow. We try to be the first thing the audience hears, not the first thing they have to forgive.
