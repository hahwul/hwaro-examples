## New Commission

A twenty-six minute work commissioned for the opening night, written by the ensemble's resident composer Ayaka Miura and performed in two continuous movements.

**Programme**

1. *Dawn Interval* — 12:10
2. *Window, Facing East* — 13:48

**Line**

Ayaka Miura (violin, composition), Rafal Zielinski (violin), Ines Delorme (viola), Henrik Paavola (cello), Sora Tanaka (cello), Felix Moreau (double bass).

The piece was written between March and June and rehearsed for the first time in the Horizon Hall this week.
