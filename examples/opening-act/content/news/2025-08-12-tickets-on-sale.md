Tickets for Season 07 go on sale today at 10:00 local time. Season passes are capped at 180 this year, slightly below last season, after feedback on the Basement Black capacity.

Single-night tickets are available for every Friday except the opening. The opening night is ticketed separately with a short season-pass priority window.
