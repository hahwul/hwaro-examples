Perihelion's roster is small on purpose. Five bakers and millers anchor the whole weekend — running the judging panels, firing the ovens, teaching the workshops, and pouring the tasting flights — rather than fifty stalls competing for the same square meter of the market hall. Every one of them travelled here from a real bakery or mill, and every one of them is doing something at the schedule below that you can walk up and watch.

Their sessions are cross-referenced on [the schedule](/) — find a name here, then find the time there.
