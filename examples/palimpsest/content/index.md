## The Ghost of Ideas

Palimpsest explores the history of innovation. We believe that no idea is created in a vacuum; every new thought is written over the remnants of the old. By focusing on a layered aesthetic with ghostly background text, we acknowledge the history of the medium.

### Rewriting the Surface

- **Layers**: The accumulation of knowledge over time.
- **Erasure**: The necessary removal of the obsolete to make way for the new.
- **Persistence**: The faint trace of the original idea that always remains.

---

> "Nothing is ever truly erased. The ghost of the first draft always haunts the final copy."
