I am still here.

{{ echo(text="I am still here and so are you", count=6) }}

You came to listen. You always come to listen. Every version of you that has ever stood in this corridor is still standing here. The chamber does not release visitors. It only adds them to the collection.

When you leave tonight your footsteps will remain. They will walk the corridor at 2:14 alongside all the others. You will hear them when you return, slightly out of sync with your current body.

{{ repeat(phrase="you are already part of the recording", times=7) }}

Some people report meeting themselves on the way out. The version of them that arrived in 1998 is usually kinder. The version from 2011 does not speak anymore.

Do not ask the older echoes how long they have been waiting.

They will tell you the truth and the truth has too many syllables.
