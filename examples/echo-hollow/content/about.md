The walls are not stone. They are compressed sound.

Every surface here was poured while something was screaming. The mix never set. It only hardened around the frequency.

## What You Are Hearing

There was one voice in the beginning. A woman, or a child, or both, depending on which reel you believe. She (it) said seven words. Those seven words have been repeating for forty-seven years without pause or loss of volume.

We have tried to leave. The door leads back inside.

We have tried to record over it. The new tape always plays the old scream.

## The Rules of This Place

- Do not speak your name aloud.
- Do not count the repetitions.
- If you hear your own voice answering from the far end of the corridor, do not answer back.

The chamber is patient. It has already begun to learn the sound of your breathing.
