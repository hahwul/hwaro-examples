The conference concludes with an engaging and highly interactive panel of seasoned experts discussing the practical, often messy realities of implementing cutting-edge new technologies within established, legacy environments. This candid conversation will delve into the challenges of managing significant technical debt, effectively fostering engineering talent in a competitive market, and gracefully navigating the complexities of organizational change and digital transformation.

The panelists will share their personal experiences, hard-won lessons, and strategic frameworks for driving innovation while maintaining the stability of core business systems. The session will feature an extensive open Q&A segment, allowing attendees to engage directly with the speakers and seek advice on their specific challenges. This is a unique opportunity to gain invaluable insights from leaders who have successfully guided their organizations through major technological shifts. We will wrap up the event by summarizing the key takeaways from the conference and outlining actionable steps that attendees can immediately apply to their own projects and teams.
