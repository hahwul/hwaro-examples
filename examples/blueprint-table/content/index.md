## Planning Tactility

Blueprint-Table is about the technical and foundational nature of innovation within the drafting environment. We believe that planning content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the architectural era. By focusing on blue-toned surfaces, grid-based patterns, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply creative. Our focus is on the integrity of the design plan.

### Planning Principles

- **Foundation**: A design that prioritizes the structural basis of the work.
- **Tactility**: Using blue-print textures to define the boundaries of the planning space.
- **Precision**: A layout that reflects the exact nature of technical drafting.
