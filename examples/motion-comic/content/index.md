## Dynamic Narrative

Motion-Comic is about the continuous and narrative nature of innovation within the high-action environment. We believe that storytelling content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a digital motion comic. By focusing on dark panels, flat shadows, and impactful monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the sequence.

### Motion Principles

- **Dynamics**: A design that prioritizes the clear "movement" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using flat shadows to define the boundaries of the cinematic space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 21st-century digital narrative.
