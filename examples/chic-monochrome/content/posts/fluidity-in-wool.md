Wool is often associated with rigidity—with heavy coats and structured suits. However, when treated with modern weaving techniques, it reveals a liquid drape that rivals silk while retaining a tactile, matte density.

### The Contrast of Weights
This season's monochrome collections explore this exact duality. By pairing a structured, razor-sharp wool-crepe blazer with ultra-fine, fluid wool jersey trousers, designers create a compelling dialogue between structure and flow. 

The monochrome palette enhances this conversation, drawing all attention to the subtle play of light and shadow on different textile surfaces.
