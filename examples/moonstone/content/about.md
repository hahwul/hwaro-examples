# About Moonstone

Moonstone is a blog theme inspired by the enchanting beauty of moonstone gemstones — known for their adularescent glow, a soft, ethereal light that seems to float just beneath the surface.

## The Design

This theme captures the essence of moonstone through:

- **Adularescent gradients** — Soft blue-white sheens that shift and glow
- **Translucent layers** — Glassmorphic cards with dreamy depth
- **Subtle animations** — Gentle movements that evoke the stone's inner light
- **Rich typography** — Elegant serifs paired with modern sans-serif body text

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast static site generator written in Crystal. It features responsive design, syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, and tag-based navigation.
