We sell wholesale to landscapers, small commercial orchards, restoration plantings, and other nurseries reselling under their own name. The program runs on the same stock as the retail catalog &mdash; there is no separate, lesser wholesale grade.

## Minimums and sizing

Trade pricing starts at ten units of a single cultivar, mixed sizes within that cultivar allowed. Trees are available as one-year bare-root whips, two-year branched bare-root, or as potted stock in one- and two-gallon containers. Berry shrubs ship as bare-root liners or in one-gallon pots. Anything under ten units is billed at standard retail pricing, no exceptions.

## Availability and deadlines

Bare-root availability is confirmed each January once winter losses in the propagation beds are counted, and spring orders ship on a first-confirmed, first-served basis starting in March. Potted stock availability updates through the growing season as containers finish sizing up. For guaranteed spring quantities on a specific cultivar, place a standing order by December 1 &mdash; we hold back stock against confirmed standing orders before anything goes to the general list.

## Requesting the trade list

Write in with your business name, the zone you are planting into, and roughly what volume you are working at, and we will send back the current trade list with sizes and pricing. Repeat wholesale accounts get first notice when a new cultivar is ready to leave the trial rows and enter general production.

## Payment and pickup

Standing orders require a deposit at confirmation, balance due on pickup or before shipping. Will-call pickup is available at the sales yard during open hours; freight shipping is arranged per order for accounts outside driving distance, at cost. We do not drop-ship single units under a wholesale account &mdash; those go through the regular [catalog](/catalog/) instead. If you are new to Newton, visiting the [nursery](/visit/) first is the fastest way to see mature plant habit before you commit to a standing order.
