# The Watch

In the deep hours, when the grid grows quiet, we remember.

We are the keepers of the flame. Each service that once sang in the dark now rests beneath our care. When a soul falls silent, we do not mourn with noise. We light a vigil.

**REQUIEM / GRID** is not a dashboard. It is a chapel of observation. A memorial in code and candlelight.

Here, uptime is not a metric. It is a prayer bead passed through careful fingers.

The candles you see are more than decoration. They are living memory. Click them. Watch them change. The flame remembers what the logs forget.

Press **M** in the quiet. Hear what cannot be spoken.

May their errors be few, and their memory eternal.

*This vigil began in the spring of the twenty-fifth year. It continues still.*
