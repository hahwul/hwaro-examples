# About Summit

Summit is the premier annual tech conference for developers, engineering leaders, and technology enthusiasts. Since our founding in 2023, we have brought together some of the brightest minds in the industry to share knowledge, build connections, and push the boundaries of what is possible in software engineering.

## Our Mission

We believe that great software is built by great communities. Summit exists to foster meaningful connections between practitioners, promote knowledge sharing across disciplines, and celebrate the craft of engineering.

## What to Expect

- **Deep Technical Talks** -- Sessions led by industry practitioners who build and operate systems at scale.
- **Hands-on Workshops** -- Full-day and half-day workshops where you build real projects with expert guidance.
- **Networking** -- Structured and informal opportunities to meet peers, mentors, and potential collaborators.
- **Community** -- An inclusive, welcoming environment for all experience levels and backgrounds.

## Code of Conduct

Summit is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. All attendees, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers are required to agree to and follow our code of conduct.

## Organizers

Summit is organized by a team of volunteers passionate about building better developer communities. If you are interested in getting involved, reach out to us through our contact page.
