## About Vineyard

Vineyard is a personal wine tasting journal built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). It serves as a quiet record of bottles opened, aromas noticed, and impressions formed over time.

### How Notes Are Written

Each tasting note follows a consistent structure:

- **Wine Details** — Vintage, producer, region, and grape variety
- **Tasting Profile** — Aroma, palate, body, tannin, acidity, and finish
- **Score** — A 100-point scale reflecting overall quality and balance
- **Commentary** — Personal impressions and food pairing suggestions

### The 100-Point Scale

| Range | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 95-100 | Exceptional — a benchmark wine |
| 90-94 | Outstanding — highly recommended |
| 85-89 | Very good — notable character |
| 80-84 | Good — well-made and pleasant |
| Below 80 | Average or below |

### Disclaimer

These are personal notes and subjective impressions. Wine appreciation is deeply individual — trust your own palate above any score.
