Welcome to Ruby Fire. This is more than a blog theme — it's a statement.

## Why Ruby Fire?

Most themes play it safe. Muted grays, subtle blues, careful neutrals. Ruby Fire takes a different path. Inspired by precious gemstones and the raw energy of fire, this theme embraces boldness.

Deep crimson reds glow against obsidian backgrounds. Golden highlights catch the eye like facets of a cut gem. Every hover state, every transition is designed to feel alive.

## Built with Hwaro

This site runs on [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a lightning-fast static site generator written in Crystal. The combination of Hwaro's speed with Ruby Fire's rich visuals means you get a site that looks spectacular and loads instantly.

## What You'll Find Here

Expect posts about:

- **Design** — Bold color theory, CSS techniques, and visual storytelling
- **Technology** — Static sites, performance optimization, and modern tooling
- **Craft** — The intersection of art and engineering

## The Palette

The theme draws from four core colors:

1. **Ruby Red** `#e63946` — The primary accent, bold and passionate
2. **Ember Orange** `#e76f51` — Warm energy and movement
3. **Gold** `#f4a261` — Highlights and refinement
4. **Obsidian** `#0a0a0f` — Deep, rich darkness as the foundation
