## About Ruby Fire

Welcome to Ruby Fire — where passion meets precision. This site is a showcase of bold, glamorous design built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast static site generator written in Crystal.

## About This Theme

The **Ruby Fire** theme draws inspiration from precious gemstones and flame — deep crimson reds, fiery oranges, and brilliant golden highlights against a rich dark backdrop. Every element is designed to captivate.

### Design Philosophy

- **Bold & Unapologetic** — Vibrant ruby reds and fiery gradients that demand attention
- **Jewel-Like Refinement** — Polished surfaces, faceted glow effects, and gemstone-inspired accents
- **Dynamic Energy** — Subtle animations and transitions that bring the page to life
- **Readable Elegance** — High contrast text that remains effortless to read

### Features

- Deep obsidian background with ruby red and gold fire accents
- Gemstone-faceted glow effects on headings and interactive elements
- Animated gradient dividers with ember-like shimmer
- Card-style post listings with fiery hover states
- Responsive design for all devices
- Syntax highlighting with Monokai theme
