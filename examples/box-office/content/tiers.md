## What each tier includes

### VIP Premiere

Reserved seat in the first two rows, early entry at 17:30, a pre-show green room reception (non-alcoholic drinks and seasonal catering), and a tour-exclusive 180g vinyl release signed by the artist. Limited to 120 per night.

### General Floor

Standing pit access on a first-in, first-served basis. Doors open at 18:30. Wristbands are issued at entry and cannot be upgraded on the night. Coat check is complimentary for floor tickets.

### Mezzanine

Numbered seat on the upper level with clean sight lines to the entire stage. Same 18:30 door time as general floor. Mezzanine ticket holders have dedicated access to the upstairs bar and a shorter merchandise queue.

## Accessibility

Every tier has wheelchair-accessible positions. Accessible ticket holders receive a complimentary companion ticket at the same tier price. Booking contact: accessibility@meridiantheatre.example.
