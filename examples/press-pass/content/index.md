Correspondents are convened on Thursdays at 0700 UTC for the weekly Press Office briefing. Topics rotate across the beats: foreign policy, economic outlook, domestic legislation, climate response, and the occasional technical readout. Questions are accepted in writing through the accreditation desk the evening before.

## On The Record

Material marked **On Record** may be quoted with full attribution. The speaker's name, title, and organisational affiliation are publishable.

## On Background

Material marked **On Background** may be attributed only to unnamed &ldquo;Press Office sources&rdquo; or equivalent. Direct quotation is permitted; speaker identity is not.

## Embargoed

Material marked **Embargoed** may not be published, broadcast, tweeted, or privately circulated before the stated time. Violations result in the loss of accreditation for the correspondent and, in some cases, their outlet.

These distinctions are not suggestions. Credentials issued by this office carry consequences that persist beyond a single briefing.
