## Tactical Alignment

Crosshair is about the intersection of precision and timing. In a world of noise, we focus on the single point of impact. Every element is aligned, every variable is calculated, and the target is acquired with absolute certainty.

### Precision Metrics

- **Acquisition**: The speed at which we identify the core objective.
- **Stability**: The consistency of our execution under pressure.
- **Release**: The moment where the intent is converted into action.

### Design Philosophies

1. **Focus**: Eliminating the secondary to highlight the primary.
2. **Alignment**: Ensuring every component supports the main goal.
3. **Execution**: Performance that meets the highest standards of accuracy.
