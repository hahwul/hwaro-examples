## Material Precision

Compass-Needle is about the directional and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that core content deserves a presentation that captures the precision of the early navigational era. By focusing on brass-inspired textures, white paper surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the needle.

### Needle Principles

- **Precision**: A design that prioritizes the clear orientation of the user.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the precision space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of classical instrument production.
