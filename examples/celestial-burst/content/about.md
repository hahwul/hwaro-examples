# About the Cosmos

Celestial Burst is a design experiment emphasizing luxury, elegance, and astral drama. It relies on deep contrasting backgrounds and warm golden accents to simulate the awe-inspiring beauty of a cosmic explosion.

Our philosophy is simple: True elegance does not require overwhelming noise. We avoid excessive gradients and playful icons, focusing instead on structural integrity, classic typography, and the profound beauty of subtle luminescence.
