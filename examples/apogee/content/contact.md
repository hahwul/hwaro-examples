Write to us with a short description of what you are building, roughly when it needs to ship, and whether you already have brand or interface materials we should see. We read every inquiry ourselves — there is no intake form and no account team filtering what reaches the designers.

We take on four to six projects a year, which means we sometimes have to say no to good work because the calendar is full. If that happens, we will tell you honestly and, where we can, point you toward someone else who might be a fit.

## Before you write

A short brief goes a long way. If you can, tell us what stage your company is at, who on your team will be the main point of contact, and whether the work is mostly identity, interface, documentation, or some mix of the three — see the [services](/services/) page if you need the vocabulary. We will follow up within two working days with either a scoping call or, if we are at capacity, an honest no.
