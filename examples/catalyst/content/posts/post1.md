Every chemical reaction has an activation energy -- the minimum energy required to start the process. In product development, that barrier is often tooling, infrastructure, and coordination overhead.

Catalyst is designed to lower that barrier dramatically.

## What We Built

Over the past eighteen months, our team has been assembling a platform that treats product development as a series of well-understood reactions rather than unpredictable alchemy.

The core components include:

- **Reactor Core** -- A workspace where your team defines hypotheses, designs experiments, and tracks outcomes with the rigor of a laboratory notebook.
- **Bond Graph** -- A visual dependency mapper that shows how features, teams, and timelines connect, like molecular bonds holding a compound together.
- **Titration Dashboard** -- Analytics that help you add just the right amount of effort to each initiative, avoiding waste and overreaction.

## Why Now

The startup ecosystem has matured enough that we no longer need to treat product development as a black box. The reactions are knowable. The catalysts are identifiable. The outcomes are measurable.

We are excited to share this with you. Welcome to the lab.
