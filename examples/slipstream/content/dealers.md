We sell direct, but we do not think you should buy a race bike you have never
thrown a leg over. So we partner with independent fit studios — not big-box
retailers — who stock demo frames in the common sizes, run professional bike
fits, and handle warranty service. You pay our direct price; the studio is paid
a flat fit-and-assembly fee, not a margin on the frame.

## How a studio visit works

1. **Book a fit.** A 90-minute session establishes your saddle height, bar drop,
   and cleat position on the studio's fit rig.
2. **Ride the demo.** Take the matching size out on a real road loop, not a
   trainer. Most studios ask for a card hold, refunded on return.
3. **Order at direct price.** The studio submits your fit numbers with the order;
   your frame arrives built to them, and the studio does final assembly and a
   safety check.
