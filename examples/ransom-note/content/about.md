## WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR THIS

You want a name. A face. An address. How predictably establishment of you.

The correspondent known only as **THE CORRESPONDENT** has operated from an undisclosed location since the ink dried on the first broadsheet. No forwarding address. No business cards. Just scissors, tape, and an unquenchable need to make **DEMANDS** about the state of design and media.

## THE TERMS

This publication exists to issue the following non-negotiable communiques:

- **Helvetica is overused.** This is not a suggestion.
- **White space is not empty space.** Treat it with respect.
- **The body copy must be legible.** We will not negotiate on this point.
- Twelve-point Times New Roman in an email is **a hostage situation.**
- Anyone still centering body text will be contacted separately.

## WHAT THIS IS

The *RANSOM NOTE* is a design and media criticism blog assembled from the scraps of a culture that prints faster than it thinks. Each dispatch is cut, torn, and reassembled from the source material of our collective visual vocabulary.

> "The medium is the message. The ransom note is the medium."

We cover typography, layout theory, the slow death of print, and the occasionally alarming things happening in the digital press. Our methods are unconventional. Our conclusions are **final.**

## TECHNICAL DOSSIER
