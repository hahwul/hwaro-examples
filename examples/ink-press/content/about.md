# Colophon

Ink & Press is an editorial paper operated by three staff writers, a rotating guest columnist, and an editor-in-chief who spends three days a week on a hand-press. The publication has filed continuously since 2011.

## Typography

Display heads are set in **Playfair Display** at weights 800 and 900, with judicious use of italic for interjections and pull quotes. Body text is set in **Source Serif 4** at seventeen on twenty-eight, with the first paragraph rendered without indentation and subsequent paragraphs indented by one and a half em. Monospaced callouts and bylines are set in **IBM Plex Mono**.

## Palette

The paper's ink is black; its accent is a single red, drawn from the shade historically used for corrections, stop-press notices, and the masthead's ampersand. Gray is reserved for timestamps, bylines, and captions.

## Errata

Corrections are filed to the front page for one week, then moved to a permanent errata log. We have issued four corrections this year. None were libelous.

## How we file

Stories are filed on Monday, edited on Tuesday, laid out on Wednesday morning, proofed Wednesday afternoon. We do not file on the weekend. Every staff member has a standing desk, a shaded reading lamp, and access to the mill's bench press if a feature needs a hand-pulled pull quote for the feature well.
