Three notes from the week's composing room, lightly edited for the page.

## One

The proof reader caught a widow on page four that no one saw on Tuesday. We pulled the page at 11pm Wednesday and reset the last paragraph. Corrections cost the paper about forty minutes this week. Next week's goal: twenty.

## Two

A reader wrote in to say the masthead is one point too large. They may be right. We will not change it.

## Three

The weather on Thursday was good. Ink dries faster in low humidity. The Friday run was the cleanest we've shipped all year. There is no moral.
