03 / Process

Four phases,
no theatre.

Every project follows the same four phases. We try not to skip them, even when the timeline feels short. They are short, in proportion. They are not theatre.
01

Understand

Three weeks of reading, listening, and walking the operation. Most of the project is decided here, quietly.

02

Define

A short brief, jointly owned. A few principles. A set of constraints. No deliverables yet.

03

Make

The long middle. Sketches, drafts, builds, iterations. Daily check-ins, weekly written updates.

04

Land

Handover, documentation, training. A six-month follow-up to see how the work has aged in the wild.
