Welcome to my blog! I write about technology, programming, and other interesting topics. Crowd Surge is a personal site that has been running, with a few pauses, since the beginning of 2025.

## What I Cover

Posts cluster into three rough threads. Build logs from side projects make up the largest share, followed by reading notes from books and papers, and finally a small set of opinion pieces that I try to keep grounded in concrete examples.

## Posting Cadence

The aim is one post every two weeks. The schedule slips when client work is heavy, but the gap rarely exceeds a month. Older pieces are revisited and republished when the underlying tools have moved on enough to warrant a fresh take.

## Contact

Feel free to reach out through social media or email. The fastest way to get a reply is the contact form linked from the main navigation. Pitches for guest posts are welcome but please include a one-paragraph outline before sending the full draft.
