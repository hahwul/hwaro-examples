# The Moon & The Sea

> "The moon affects the tides of the ocean, and the tides of our souls."

This is **Lunar Tide**, a beautifully crafted theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), inspired by the quiet interplay of celestial gravity and the deep, silent seas.

## Rhythm of the Tides

The ebb and flow of the ocean is intimately connected to the phases of the moon. As it orbits the Earth, its gravitational pull creates the high and low tides, a daily reminder of our connection to the cosmos.

1. **New Moon**: The darkest nights, the highest tides.
2. **First Quarter**: The balance of light and shadow.
3. **Full Moon**: Radiant illumination, strong tidal forces.
4. **Last Quarter**: The waning pull, the receding waters.

## A Space for Thought

This theme offers a tranquil environment for your writing, wrapping your words in deep navies, soft lunar grays, and glowing cyan accents.
