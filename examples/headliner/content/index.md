## Occupying the Spotlight

Headliner is designed for the singular purpose of commanding attention. We don't believe in the periphery; we believe in the stage. By using massive typography and center-aligned layouts, we ensure that the message is the only thing the viewer sees.

### High-Impact Principles

1. **Hierarchy**: The most important thing is the biggest thing.
2. **Focus**: Eliminating the secondary to highlight the primary.
3. **Presence**: A design that doesn't just exist, but demands to be seen.

### Taking the Stage

- **Boldness**: The courage to use the entire screen.
- **Clarity**: High contrast for instant recognition.
- **Authority**: A layout that establishes itself as the final word.
