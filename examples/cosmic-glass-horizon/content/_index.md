Welcome to **Cosmic Glass Horizon**, a premium dark theme for Hwaro showcasing the elegant combination of deep space aesthetics and modern glassmorphism.

This theme features:
*   **Immersive Deep Dark Backgrounds**: A rich, subtly animated background that evokes the depth of space.
*   **Glowing Neon Accents**: Vibrant cyan, magenta, and purple gradients that highlight your content and typography.
*   **Frosted Glass Panels**: Beautiful, translucent containers that provide structure and depth using CSS `backdrop-filter`.
*   **Modern Typography**: Carefully selected fonts including `Space Grotesk` for headings and `Inter` for exceptional readability.

Feel free to explore the [Posts](/posts/) to see how various markdown elements render beautifully in this cosmic environment.
