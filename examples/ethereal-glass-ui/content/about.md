## Design Philosophy

The Ethereal Glass UI template was born out of a desire to merge the raw, high-performance generation speed of Hwaro with the bleeding-edge aesthetics of modern web design.

Glassmorphism relies on the concept of frosted glass. It leverages a combination of semi-transparent backgrounds, background-blur, and subtle borders to create a layered, 3D effect on a 2D surface.

### Key Elements

1. **Translucency (Frosted Glass Effect):** Uses background blur to create the illusion of glass panels floating over the background.
2. **Multi-layered approach:** Content floats above the background, creating a clear sense of depth and hierarchy.
3. **Vibrant backgrounds:** The animated orbs provide dynamic, vibrant colors that shine through the frosted glass, emphasizing the blur effect.
4. **Subtle borders:** Light, semi-transparent borders define the shape of the glass elements without breaking the illusion.
