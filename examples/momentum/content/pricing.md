Momentum's free tier is not a trial. It is the full editor, the full momentum feed, and unlimited notes on one device, indefinitely. You only pay to add devices or people.

## Personal

Everything a single person needs to stop losing thoughts to a folder tree. Install it on one laptop or one phone, and it stays free as long as you use Momentum at all — no feature countdown, no note limit, no watermark.

## Plus

Adds encrypted sync across every device you own, so the same feed and the same momentum ordering show up on your phone, your laptop, and a spare desktop, all merging automatically. Plus also unlocks full momentum-feed history, letting you scroll back through how your attention moved over the past year, not just the current pile.

## Teams

Built for small groups who want a shared pile without a shared folder structure. A team space has its own momentum feed, ordered by when *anyone* on the team last touched a note, so the group's collective attention — not an org chart — decides what surfaces. Admin controls cover seat management, space-level export, and an audit log of who touched what and when.

Annual billing on Plus and Teams saves two months versus paying monthly; both plans can be cancelled at any time, and your notes export to plain markdown files regardless of tier. Ready to try it? [Download Momentum](@/download.md) and stay on Personal for as long as you like.
