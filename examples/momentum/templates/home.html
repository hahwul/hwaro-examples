The loop

Three motions, no filing cabinet

{{ content | safe }}

  1. Capture

    Hit the shortcut from anywhere, type the thought, close the window. No project picker stands between you and the sentence.

  2. Resurface

    Notes you've touched recently rise to the top of your feed on their own. Old notes don't vanish — they just sink until you need them again.

  3. Sync

    Every device holds the same encrypted pile, peer to peer. No account is required to start, and no server ever reads your notes.
