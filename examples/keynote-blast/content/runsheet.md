## how the auto-cue works

Every slot has a hard end time. A minute before the end, a backstage clock on the speaker's podium shows a red countdown. Thirty seconds before the end, a producer in their ear. At the end, the mic cuts to a fade and the walk-off music comes up under it. If you are past the cue when it fires, you finish over the music or you leave the stage. There is no third option.
