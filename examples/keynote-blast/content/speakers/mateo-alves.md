## the talk

**"We Stopped Trusting the Dashboard."** Orbital's event pipeline processes 250 million events per hour. At that scale, dashboards lie in ways that take months to catch. Mateo talks through how the team realized their own observability was wrong and what they did about it.

### why he is on in slot 02

Slot 02 is the technical shot. The audience is warmed up by Ada, the energy is up, the attention is sharp. This is the talk where you can get into the hard material before anyone's fatigue sets in.

### what he will show

- The dashboard that said everything was fine during a seven-hour outage
- The observability rewrite that replaced it
- The one metric they stopped caring about
- Code snippets. Real ones. With file paths.
