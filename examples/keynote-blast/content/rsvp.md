# RSVP / ARM

Keynote Blast is invitation-only. If you are on the list you got a code in your inbox. Enter it below and your seat is armed. If you are not on the list, the waitlist is open — if a seat is released at T-00:15, the next name on the waitlist gets it and you have four minutes to claim it.

## waitlist

No code? Enter just name and email above. Your position on the waitlist is determined by the order your submission hits the server. We release seats at three checkpoints — T-7 days, T-24 hours, and T-00:15. You will receive exactly one email if a seat opens for you, and you have four minutes to confirm. After that, the seat drops to the next name.

## live stream

Live stream is free and unrestricted. The URL goes out at T-00:10 via the email list. The stream is not delayed.
