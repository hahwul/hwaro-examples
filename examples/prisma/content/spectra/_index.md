Five specimens from the logbook, newest first. Each plate records one boundary a beam of white light crossed on its way through cut glass — a flat pane, a cut wedge, a faceted drop, a pair of correcting lenses — and the spread of colour that boundary produced, redrawn to scale as a vector plate.
