## Surviving the Furnace

Firebrand is an exploration of the transformative power of heat and pressure. We don't fear the fire; we embrace it as the necessary agent of change. Innovation is not a cold process; it is a violent, energetic reorganization of the material.

### Internal Heat

The most resilient structures are those that have been forged in the most intense environments. We focus on the inner drive that keeps the fire burning long after the initial spark has faded.

1. **Combustion**: The conversion of raw ideas into usable energy.
2. **Radiation**: The reach of the influence generated by the work.
3. **Resilience**: The ability to remain structural despite the temperature.

---

> "To become a firebrand, you must first learn to love the heat."
