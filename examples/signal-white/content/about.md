Signal / White is a writing journal for people who believe the page has always done the heavy lifting. It is set in Inter. The accent colour is `#0057ff`. The grid is twelve columns, with twenty-four pixel gutters, and does not flex for decoration.

## What it refuses

- Gradients, in every form.
- Drop shadows that pretend to carry hierarchy.
- Icons where a label will read.
- More than one accent colour at a time.

## What it keeps

- White space, first.
- Typographic weight, second.
- A single, load-bearing blue, last.

The result is not fashionable. The result is readable at 2am, on a laptop that has seen better screens, which is the only design brief worth writing to.
