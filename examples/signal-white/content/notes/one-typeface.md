The modern web has infinite typefaces and almost no discipline. A site that picks one typeface and sticks to it, at two weights, will read better, load faster, and carry a stronger voice than a site that pulls four families and waves at variety.

## The argument, briefly

- One typeface reduces the cognitive load for readers.
- Two weights are sufficient to establish hierarchy.
- Three weights are defensible. Four is a panic.

We set Signal / White in Inter, at 400 and 700, with JetBrains Mono for metadata. Nothing else has applied and nothing else will.
