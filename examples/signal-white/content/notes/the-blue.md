A single accent colour, used with discipline, is a kind of punctuation. At Signal / White the accent is `#0057ff`. It appears on interactive elements, on a small number of headings, and on the swatch in the masthead. It never appears in body copy. It never appears on two adjacent elements. It never appears to decorate.

> The accent colour earns its presence by withholding it elsewhere.

Everything else is black on white, which is, and remains, the oldest and most reliable design system.
