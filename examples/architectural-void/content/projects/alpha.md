## Concept

Project Alpha examines the impact of an unyielding, monolithic presence within an open landscape. It utilizes a singular material—cast concrete—to form a structure that is both brutal and elegant.

## Execution

The structure features no windows, only narrow slits that allow controlled beams of light to pierce the interior darkness. The result is a space that feels simultaneously protective and oppressive, forcing the occupant to confront the sheer weight of the architecture.

The exterior surface is left raw, allowing time and elements to weather it naturally.
