# Studio *—*

Marble Haus is a four-partner design practice founded in 2014 with studios in Seoul and Berlin. We work by referral, take on a small number of engagements each season, and publish a folio every two years.

## Discipline

Our work is grounded in the Bauhaus principle that function and geometry are inseparable. We reject ornament for its own sake, but we do not confuse restraint with austerity. A gold line is restraint; an empty field is austerity. The difference matters.

## Palette

We work in three values and a thread: **off-white** drawn from unbleached paper, **charcoal** drawn from graphite, **bone** as a soft intermediary, and a single thread of **gold** drawn from unpolished brass. Every project ships in some arrangement of these four colors. No exceptions.

## Geometry

Three shapes carry the weight of everything we make: the circle, the rectangle, the triangle. They appear at the scale of a trademark, at the scale of a room, and at the scale of a book spread. Any new shape must earn its place. None have so far.
