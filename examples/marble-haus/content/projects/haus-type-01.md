# Haus Type 01 *—*

A self-initiated display typeface drawn from Bauhaus geometry, released in three weights under a small foundry license.

## Brief

We had spent several years borrowing a single display face from three different foundries and had grown tired of paying for what we already had in our heads. We drew our own.

## Response

A geometric sans in the spirit of Kabel and Futura, optimized for display at sixty points and up. Three weights: Light, Regular, and Bold. A single optical size. No italic, no small caps, no alternates. Numerals are tabular. The ampersand is drawn on a circle.

## Release

The face is available under a small foundry license for use by one practice on a single project. Larger licenses are negotiated case by case. The face is not available for resale.
