# Aqueduct

Like the Roman engineers who channeled water across vast distances through precision and ingenuity, we channel knowledge through structured writing on system design, infrastructure, and software architecture.

Every great system begins with a foundation. Every reliable pipeline starts with a well-drawn diagram. Here, we break down the architectures that power modern software -- one step at a time.

## What You Will Find

- **System Design** -- Load balancers, caches, queues, and the patterns that bind them together.
- **Infrastructure** -- From bare metal to orchestrated containers, the plumbing beneath the surface.
- **Architecture Patterns** -- Event sourcing, CQRS, hexagonal design, and the trade-offs each one demands.
- **Step-by-Step Tutorials** -- Diagrams first, implementation second. Always with a clear path forward.
