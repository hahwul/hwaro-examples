Fifty studios take part across the two halls; a handful are gathered here so you can plan a route before you arrive. Every maker signs the base of their pots with a chop — the small pressed circle beside each name below is that same mark, redrawn.

Studios are listed alphabetically. Follow a name through to read how they fire, what they bring, and where to find their stand on the weekend.
