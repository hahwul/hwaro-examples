## The Name

Verdigris is the blue-green patina that forms on copper, brass, and bronze over time. It is not a flaw -- it is evidence of a surface meeting the world.

## The Philosophy

This site believes in patience over haste, substance over spectacle, and the quiet authority of work that has endured weathering.

Every piece published here has been shaped by revision. Not all revision improves things. But the willingness to revisit, to question the first draft, is what separates writing from typing.

## The Design

Dark backgrounds. Sharp serifs. Measured whitespace. No decoration that does not earn its place. The palette draws from oxidized bronze: deep teal, warm metal, and the stark contrast of light text against dark surfaces.

## Contact

Reach out through any channel you find. If the writing resonates, it will find its way.
