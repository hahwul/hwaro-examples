## Challenging the Hierarchy

Insurgent is an exploration of non-linear structural design. We reject the comfort of the standard grid and embrace the beauty of deconstruction. Our goal is to challenge the way users navigate and perceive content within a system.

### Subversive Principles

- **Dissonance**: The intentional creation of structural conflict.
- **Asymmetry**: Finding balance through irregular placement.
- **Autonomy**: Content that defines its own space rather than following a template.
