## About This Site

Darkwave is a blog built on the aesthetics of synthwave and vaporwave -- the neon-lit highways, the chrome reflections, the pulse of an analog future that never arrived.

## Design Philosophy

This theme draws from the visual language of 80s retrofuturism:

- **Neon typography** -- glowing text that cuts through the darkness
- **Dark canvas** -- deep, rich backgrounds that let colors breathe
- **Grid lines** -- the infinite perspective grid stretching toward the horizon
- **Glitch texture** -- digital artifacts as a design element, not a flaw
- **High contrast** -- hot pink, cyan, and magenta against near-black

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast static site generator written in Crystal. The Darkwave theme is an original creation inspired by the outrun and Miami Vice aesthetic.
