## Data Management

All data are collected and stored in REDCap (Research Electronic Data Capture) hosted at Uppsala University. REDCap provides:

- Audit trails for all data entry and modifications
- Role-based access control (site coordinators see only their site)
- Automated range checks and validation rules
- Real-time data quality dashboards

Data are backed up daily to encrypted off-site storage. The dataset will be de-identified before export for analysis. The final analysis dataset will be deposited in the Uppsala University Data Repository within 12 months of publication.

## Quality Assurance

1. **Real-time monitoring:** REDCap data quality module flags missing data, out-of-range values, and protocol deviations within 24 hours
2. **Monthly site reports:** Enrollment, attendance, completion rates, and adverse events reviewed by the Data Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB)
3. **Source data verification:** 10 pct random audit of consent forms and eligibility documentation at each site
4. **Inter-rater reliability:** Audio-recorded fidelity checks scored independently by two raters; discrepancies resolved by a third rater

## Stopping Rules

The DSMB will conduct one planned interim analysis after 50 pct of participants have completed the primary outcome (approximately week 10 of enrollment). The interim analysis uses the O'Brien-Fleming alpha spending function, requiring p < 0.003 for early stopping for efficacy. The trial may also be stopped for:

- **Futility:** Conditional power < 10 pct at the interim analysis
- **Safety:** Any serious adverse event attributable to the intervention (unexpected given the low-risk educational nature)
- **Enrollment failure:** Fewer than 70 pct of target enrollment achieved by week 4

The DSMB operates under a charter filed with the trial registration and includes an independent biostatistician, an educational psychologist, and a clinical trialist with no involvement in the CLARITY trial.
