## SPIRIT Compliance

This protocol was prepared in accordance with the SPIRIT 2013 guidelines (Standard Protocol Items: Recommendations for Interventional Trials). All 33 SPIRIT checklist items are addressed either in this manuscript or in the supplementary materials on OSF. The completed SPIRIT checklist with page/section references is available as Supplementary Table S1 in the appendix.
