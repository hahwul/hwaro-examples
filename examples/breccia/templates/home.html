What the ledger enforces

Additive expand

New columns, tables, and indexes only. Nothing that already reads or writes today has to change for expand to ship.

Checkpointed backfill

Rate-limited batches with a resumable checkpoint, so a killed process picks back up instead of rescanning the table.

Gated contract

The old shape is dropped only once verify reports a clean parity window. --skip-verify is logged with a name attached.
