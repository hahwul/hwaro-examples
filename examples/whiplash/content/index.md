## Sudden Momentum

Whiplash is the result of a rapid change in direction. It is the physical manifestation of sudden, intense innovation. We don't just move; we accelerate until the world blurs.

### Intensity Levels

- **Level 1**: Noticeable shift.
- **Level 2**: Significant acceleration.
- **Level 3**: Maximum impact.

2. **South**: Away from the mundane.
3. **Internal**: The drive that fuels the movement.
