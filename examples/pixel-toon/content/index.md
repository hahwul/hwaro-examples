## Digital Tactility

Pixel-Toon is about the low-resolution and foundational nature of innovation within the gaming environment. We believe that retro-inspired content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of the early 8-bit era. By focusing on dark backgrounds, high-contrast white borders, and bold pixel typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply nostalgic. Our focus is on the integrity of the level.

### Toon Principles

- **Progression**: A design that prioritizes the clear "level" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using pixelated textures to define the boundaries of the digital space.
- **Nostalgia**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century video games.
