## Sonic Tactility

Neu-Music is about the relationship between sound and the digital interface in a high-tension environment. We believe that musical content should be as dynamic and substantial as the audio itself. By focusing on dark neumorphic-inspired shadows and bold, futuristic typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the wave and the way it is visualized.

### Amplifying Principles

- **Dynamics**: A design that reflects the energy and movement of sound.
- **Tension**: Using high-contrast shadows to define the boundaries of the sonic space.
- **Precision**: A logical, grid-based arrangement of tracks and waves.
