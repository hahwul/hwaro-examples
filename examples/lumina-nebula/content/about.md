# Mission Directive

The **Lumina Nebula** project was initiated to map the uncharted territories of the outer rim. Our goal is to catalog celestial phenomena and establish a network of relay stations using cutting-edge glassmorphism technology.

### Our Fleet

Our primary vessel, the *Aetheris*, is equipped with a state-of-the-art quantum slipstream drive and a reinforced hull capable of withstanding the intense radiation of a supernova.

### Contact

For inquiries regarding joint expeditions or to share navigational data, please transmit your coordinates on a secure subspace channel.
