## Material Duration

Hour-Glass is about the continuous and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that temporal content deserves a presentation that captures the flow of the early duration era. By focusing on glass-inspired textures, warm natural surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the sand.

### Flow Principles

- **Duration**: A design that prioritizes the clear flow of continuous information.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the temporal space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical duration measurement.
