# Regarding Directed Communication

Epistolary is an exploration of the personal nature of communication. We believe that innovation is often best shared through a direct, intimate exchange of ideas. By using a letter-writing aesthetic and classic, handwritten-inspired typography, we create a design that feels both personal and profound.

## The Power of the Word

A letter is more than just information; it is an artifact of the relationship between the sender and the receiver. We focus on the weight of the word and the permanence of the written thought.

### Personal Innovation

- **Intimacy**: A design that creates a one-to-one relationship with the user.
- **Intent**: Every sentence is crafted with a specific recipient in mind.
- **Permanence**: The feeling that the message is built to be kept and reread.
