Running comprehensive stress induction scenarios to verify failover mechanisms. The system correctly identified simulated node failures and rerouted traffic seamlessly without noticeable service interruption. We intentionally injected faults into various components of the infrastructure, simulating power outages, network partitions, and hardware failures. The automated recovery protocols functioned flawlessly, immediately isolating the compromised nodes and redistributing the workload to healthy servers. The impact on overall performance was negligible, demonstrating the high availability and fault tolerance of the architecture. The load balancers effectively managed the sudden shifts in traffic, preventing any single point of failure from causing a broader outage. The telemetry data collected during these tests provides valuable insights into the dynamic behavior of the system under extreme stress. We have identified a few minor bottlenecks in the inter-node communication protocols, which are currently being addressed by the development team. These rigorous tests ensure that the platform is fully prepared to handle the demands of a production environment.
