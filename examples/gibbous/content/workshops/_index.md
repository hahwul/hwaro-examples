In the four weeks before Gibbous we run one workshop a week, each on a different engine you could realistically ship a 48-hour build in. They are free, recorded, and aimed squarely at people who have never touched the tool before. Come to one, come to all, or watch the recordings at 2am the night before you jam. Sessions run for ninety minutes and end with a tiny working game you built along with the host.
