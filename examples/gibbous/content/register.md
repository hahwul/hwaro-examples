Entry is free and always will be. Registering does two things: it puts you on the list for the theme-reveal announcement, and, if you want one, it reserves a physical desk at the Glasgow hub. You can register right up to the moment the theme drops, but hub desks tend to run out a week beforehand.

## How to sign up

1. Join the community server (the invite link is emailed the moment you register).
2. Fill in the short form: your handle, whether you want a hub desk, and any access needs.
3. Read the [rules](/rules/) once so nothing surprises you on Friday.
4. Show up in `#lobby` before 18:00 BST on 5 June.

That is genuinely all of it. There is no application, no portfolio, no fee.

<!-- more -->

## Solo or team

You do not have to decide now. Register as an individual, then find a crew in `#team-up` any time before the theme drops, or stay solo. Teams are one to four people; you can merge two solo registrations into a team on the day with no paperwork.

## Bringing a class or a club

If you are organizing a group, a course cohort, or a workplace team, message the organizers first. We can hold a block of hub desks together and set up a private channel for your crew. We have hosted university modules and one very enthusiastic accountancy firm; both had a great time.

## What you get

- Access to all four [pre-jam workshops](/workshops/) and their recordings
- The theme reveal, live, at the same second as everyone else
- A submission page for your build with room for screenshots and notes
- A vote in the community ribbon, and a shot at the enamel pin
- A calm, well-run weekend with people who want you to finish something small and strange

## After you register

Watch your inbox for the server invite and a calendar file with every workshop and the reveal already pinned to the right timezone. If the invite has not arrived within a few minutes, check spam, then ping the organizers. See you under the waxing moon.
