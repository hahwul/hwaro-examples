## Rhythmic Force

Jackhammer is about the power of repetition. It's not a single hit that breaks the surface, but the consistent, high-frequency application of pressure. We focus on the industrial beauty of progress through persistence.

### Impact Parameters

- **Frequency**: The number of strikes per second.
- **Pressure**: The force behind every individual movement.
- **Depth**: The final result of the consistent effort.

### Core Mechanics

1. **Intake**: Gathering the necessary energy.
2. **Compression**: Building the internal pressure.
3. **Release**: The high-velocity strike against the target.
