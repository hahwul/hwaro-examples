## About The Wooden Spoon

The Wooden Spoon is a recipe collection theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), inspired by the warmth of a well-used kitchen notebook. Every recipe is written to be followed — clear ingredient lists, numbered steps, and honest timing estimates.

### Design Principles

- **Card grid layout** for browsing recipes at a glance
- **Warm, cream-toned palette** that feels like parchment
- **Clear section separation** between ingredients and cooking steps
- **Metadata at a glance** — cooking time, difficulty, and ingredient count
- **Tag-based filtering** for quick category navigation

### Setup

1. Clone or download the files into your Hwaro project
2. Run `hwaro serve` from the terminal
3. Open your browser to `http://localhost:3000/`

Happy cooking!
