The choice of typography is paramount in a theme striving for classical elegance. We turn to transitional and modern serif typefaces, whose high contrast and elegant serifs echo the chiselled Roman square capitals found on ancient monuments.

Serifs provide a visual baseline, guiding the eye across the line of text, much like the stylobate of a temple supports its columns. The rhythm of the letterforms creates a steady, measured pace, suitable for thoughtful reading.

### The Role of Contrast

High-contrast serif fonts, particularly in headings, command attention through their dramatic interplay of thick and thin strokes. They act as the architectural focal points of the page, drawing the eye and establishing a clear hierarchy.

By pairing these with a more robust, legible serif for body text, we ensure that the reading experience remains comfortable while maintaining the overarching aesthetic.
