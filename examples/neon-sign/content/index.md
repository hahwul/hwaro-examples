## Electric Tactility

Neon-Sign is about the vibrant and atmospheric nature of innovation within the immersive environment. We believe that electric content deserves a presentation that captures the glow of the nocturnal city. By focusing on dark backgrounds, glowing borders, and futuristic typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the light.

### Light Principles

- **Vibrancy**: A design that prioritizes the clear "glow" of the content.
- **Atmosphere**: Using light and shadow to define the boundaries of the nocturnal space.
- **Immersiveness**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 21st-century digital signage.
