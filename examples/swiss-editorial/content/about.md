Swiss Editorial is an architectural exploration of the International Typographic Style inside modern static build cycles.

We represent a rebellion against the over-animated, fluid, and heavily tracked modern web. We believe in visual clarity, objective layout design, and extreme efficiency.

## the visual system

* **Typography**: Clean, neutral, high-contrast sans-serif letters (Inter) and strict monospace code (DM Mono).
* **Color**: Flat Swiss Cream `#F4F3EF`, flat Swiss Red `#D32F2F`, and deep charcoal `#1A1A1A`.
* **Geometry**: Asymmetric vertical columns and bold horizontal lines.

## structural engineering

This theme compiles near-instantaneously using **Hwaro**, a crystal-compiled static compiler. Our stylesheet is written in raw Vanilla CSS, containing under 300 lines of highly optimized instructions.
