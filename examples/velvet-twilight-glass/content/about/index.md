The **Velvet Twilight Glass** design was born from the desire to merge ultra-modern UI trends with a deeply atmospheric, almost cinematic feel.

## The Aesthetic

We chose a palette that evokes the moments just after sunset—when the sky turns a deep, bruised purple and the first city lights begin to glow.

1.  **Depth:** We use multiple layers of `backdrop-filter` and carefully tuned opacity.
2.  **Light:** The floating background orbs act as diffuse light sources, illuminating the frosted glass from behind.
3.  **Restraint:** Despite the glowing colors, the overall layout remains clean, structured, and highly readable.

### Technology

Built natively for **Hwaro**, this theme requires zero external dependencies for its core visual effects. It leverages modern CSS properties like `backdrop-filter`, radial gradients, and keyframe animations to achieve its striking look.
