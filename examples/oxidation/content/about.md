# About Oxidation

Oxidation is a bold design theme built for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). It takes its cues from the chemistry and aesthetics of oxidized metals: the deep umber of rusted iron, the vivid teal of copper patina, and the stark contrast of aged surfaces against raw material.

## Design Principles

**No gradients.** Color transitions are abrupt and intentional, like the boundary between corroded and uncorroded metal.

**No decoration for its own sake.** Every visual element serves structure or emphasis. Borders mark boundaries. Shadows create depth.

**Typography leads.** Cinzel for headings evokes engraved metal. Lora for body text provides comfortable reading against the dark background.

## Built With

This site is powered by Hwaro, a fast static site generator written in Crystal. Templates use Jinja2 syntax. Content is authored in Markdown with TOML front matter.
