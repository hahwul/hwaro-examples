# The Philosophy of the Void

Galactic Resonance is not just a theme; it's an experience. We combine deep, dark backgrounds with translucent, frosted glass panels to create depth and mystery.

Glowing accents of cyan, magenta, and purple mimic the stunning colors of distant nebulae and auroras, bringing life to the static void.
