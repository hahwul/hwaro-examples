# About the Forge

The Ethereal Glass Forge was built in the darkest reaches of the void, harnessing the power of glowing nebulas and arcane energies.

Here, we craft interfaces and experiences that reflect the delicate balance between hard structure and ephemeral light. Our aesthetic is a tribute to the beauty of the unknown.
