## The Philosophy

Beauty should not be permitted to decay.  
Voice should not be permitted to fall silent.  
Art that costs everything is the only art worth preserving.

We do not resurrect the dead.  
We simply refuse to let them leave the stage.

Our methods are humane. Our results are eternal.

## House Rules

- No photographs. The camera performs its own taxidermy.
- No recordings. The voice belongs to the room.
- No late seating. The performance began in 1977 and has not concluded.
- Applause is optional. The performers cannot hear it.
