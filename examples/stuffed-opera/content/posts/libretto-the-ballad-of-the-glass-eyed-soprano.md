**THE SOPRANO** *(glass eyes reflecting the footlights)*

I have been beautiful for so long  
the dust has learned my name.  
They come to hear me every night  
and every night I remain the same.

The roses on the stage are silk.  
The applause is stuffed with cotton.  
I open my mouth on the downbeat  
and something that was once a voice  
escapes between the stitches.

**CHORUS OF THE PRESERVED** *(from the wings, unmoving)*

We are the final performance.  
We are the encore that never ends.  
Our lungs are cedar and our hearts are glass.  
We have been singing since before you were born.

**THE SOPRANO**

Do not photograph me.  
The flash remembers what the eye forgets.  
In the dark I can pretend  
that the audience is also preserved  
and that we are all simply waiting  
for the curtain to rise on something  
that looks like morning.
