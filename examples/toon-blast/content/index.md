## Graphic Explosion

Toon-Blast is about the vibrant and explosive nature of innovation within the high-energy environment. We believe that graphic content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a silver age action comic. By focusing on high-contrast black borders, solid shadows, and bold display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the flash.

### Blast Principles

- **Energy**: A design that prioritizes the clear "explosion" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and flat colors to define the boundaries of the action space.
- **Impact**: A layout that evokes the feeling of mid-20th-century pop culture.
