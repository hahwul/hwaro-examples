# Flare Meetup: Monthly Community Gathering

Every month, the community gathers to share work, exchange ideas, and connect with fellow builders.

## Format

Each meetup follows a consistent structure:

- **Lightning talks** -- Three speakers, ten minutes each. No slides required. Share what you built, what you learned, or what surprised you.
- **Show and tell** -- Open floor for demos. Bring your project, your prototype, or your failure. All are welcome.
- **Open discussion** -- A moderated conversation on a pre-announced topic. This month: "What would you build if you had six months and no constraints?"

## Who Should Attend

Anyone who builds things. Software developers, hardware hackers, designers, writers, researchers. The only requirement is curiosity and willingness to share.

## Logistics

- **When:** Second Thursday of each month, 18:30 to 21:00
- **Where:** The Solar Lounge, downtown
- **Cost:** Free. Food and drinks provided.
- **RSVP:** Not required but appreciated for capacity planning

## Speaking

Want to give a lightning talk? Submit your topic through our site. We prioritize first-time speakers and unconventional subjects.

## Code of Conduct

Flare meetups operate under a strict code of conduct. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone. Full details are available on our community guidelines page.
