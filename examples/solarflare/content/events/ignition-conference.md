# Ignition Conference 2026

Three days. Forty speakers. One mission: to light the fuse on the ideas that will define the next decade.

## Overview

Ignition brings together technologists, designers, researchers, and builders for an intensive exploration of what comes next. The conference is structured around three tracks, each representing a different wavelength of innovation.

## Tracks

### Track 1: Core Fusion

The technical track. Deep dives into systems architecture, programming languages, security, and infrastructure. Talks are dense, technical, and aimed at practitioners who build production systems.

### Track 2: Corona

The design and product track. Explorations of user experience, visual design, product strategy, and the intersection of human behavior and technology. Aimed at designers, product managers, and anyone who shapes what users see and feel.

### Track 3: Solar Wind

The culture and future track. Conversations about remote work, open source sustainability, AI ethics, and the societal implications of the technology we build. Aimed at leaders, community builders, and thoughtful practitioners.

## Venue

The conference takes place at the Solar Observatory Conference Center, a purpose-built venue designed for technical events. Every seat has power. Every room has reliable Wi-Fi. The coffee is strong.
