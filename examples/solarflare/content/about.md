# About Solarflare

Solarflare is an event landing page built for moments that demand energy. When the stakes are high and the countdown is running, the design should match the intensity of the occasion.

## Our Mission

We bring people together around ideas that matter. Every event is an ignition point -- a moment where knowledge, passion, and community collide to produce something brighter than any single contributor could achieve alone.

## Design Philosophy

The solar aesthetic is deliberate. Events are about energy transfer. A speaker transmits knowledge. An audience absorbs and amplifies it. The result is a chain reaction of ideas.

- **Plasma palette** -- Warm, energetic tones that convey urgency and excitement
- **Dark atmosphere** -- Focused attention, reduced distraction
- **Radiant effects** -- Visual energy that mirrors the intellectual energy of live events
- **Bold typography** -- Headlines that command attention across a crowded feed
