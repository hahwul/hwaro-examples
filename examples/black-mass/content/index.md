## Foundational Weight
Structural permanence within the void of design. The work begins from absence — a flat black field — and accretes mass only where it carries meaning. Nothing decorative survives the editing pass. What remains is load-bearing.

## Silent Procession
The grid moves at a single tempo. Each module advances one beat, then holds. Type sets in slow columns; figures resolve in the negative space between them. The rhythm is meant to be felt rather than counted. Read across rather than down, and the cadence reverses without ever changing speed.

## Rite of Composition
Layout as ceremony. The page is opened, the marks are placed in order, the page is closed. Symmetry where the eye expects it; deliberate breaks where the meaning demands them. Every element sits at a fixed station, and the reader walks the room rather than scrolling through it. Begun in the winter of 2025 and revised continuously since.
