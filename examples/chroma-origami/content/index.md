# Welcome to Chroma Origami

Discover a dynamic space where the vibrant energy of *chroma* meets the structured discipline of *origami*. This project reimagines classic paper folding as bright, neon-lit digital sculptures, emphasizing geometry, light, and shadow.

## The Art of the Fold

Every crease holds potential. In the physical world, a single sheet of paper transforms into three-dimensional art. In this digital theme, sharp layout angles and distinct corner cutouts emulate those meticulous folds, while explosive gradients provide a modern edge.

### Featured Concepts
- **Vibrant Gradients**: Deep purples, radiant pinks, and fiery oranges that bring layouts to life.
- **Geometric Folds**: Clipped paths and subtle drop shadows that mimic layered paper.
- **Minimalist Structure**: Clear typography that lets the visual design stand out.
