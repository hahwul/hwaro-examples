# About the Framework

Pentimento is a design framework rooted in the classic principles of Swiss design and Brutalism, reimagined for the modern web. We believe that a website should not be a canvas for endless decoration, but a structured vessel for content.

### Core Tenets

*   **Absolute Clarity:** Content must be readable, legible, and immediately comprehensible.
*   **Structural Integrity:** Grids and typography form the backbone of the experience.
*   **Restraint:** The absence of gradients, shadows, and unnecessary visual noise forces focus onto the message itself.

By stripping away the ephemeral trends, we create digital spaces that endure.
