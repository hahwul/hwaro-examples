# The Art of Subtraction

Pentimento reveals the layers beneath. It is a testament to the belief that true elegance lies in what is essential, shedding the superficial to expose raw, unfiltered form.

In this space, we celebrate bold contrasts, unyielding structure, and the quiet power of empty space. No gradients to soften the edges, no embellishments to distract the eye.

## Our Philosophy

1.  **Form over decoration.** Every element must serve a purpose.
2.  **Contrast brings clarity.** Light and shadow define depth.
3.  **Space is a material.** Use it deliberately.
