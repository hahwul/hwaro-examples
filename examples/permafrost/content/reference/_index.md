A technical reference of glacial core samples, cryo-indicators, and thermal soil mapping within the Permafrost Archive.

## Historical Datasets

- **Thermal Soil Profiles** - Periodic measurements from depths of 2m to 50m.
- **Isotope Markers** - Oxygen and hydrogen isotope data from glacial core samples.
- **Sediment Grain Size** - Quantitative analysis of frozen soil composition.
