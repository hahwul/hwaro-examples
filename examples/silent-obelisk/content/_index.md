## Structure Over Surface

The Silent Obelisk emphasizes raw structural forms and pure solid colors, stripping away non-essential decorations. It is a monument to brutalist minimalism.

### Core Principles

1. **Monolithic Form:** Unified, rigid container structures.
2. **Solid Color Palette:** Pure blacks, stark whites, and singular intense accents (like our crimson borders) without reliance on gradients or shadows.
3. **Typography as Architecture:** Utilizing `Inter` for robust readability and `Space Mono` for structural metadata and navigation.
