# About Vinyl

A space for writing about the records that matter. Deep cuts, overlooked pressings, and the albums that shaped how we listen.

## What We Write About

**Album reviews** -- new releases and back-catalog reappraisals. We listen to the whole record, front to back, before writing a word.

**Genre explorations** -- from the roots of funk and soul to the outer edges of post-punk and ambient. Every sound has a lineage worth tracing.

**Label spotlights** -- the independent imprints and legendary houses that shaped decades of music. The story behind the catalog number.

**Vinyl culture** -- the rituals of collecting, the warmth of analog, and why the format endures in a streaming world.

## The Name

*Vinyl* is what happens when the needle meets the groove. It is the medium and the ritual -- the act of choosing a record, placing it on the platter, and giving an album your full attention. That is how music was meant to be heard.
