**I AM NOT A PERSON.**

I am what happens when a 1997 stock photo of a sunrise is saved as a JPEG, emailed 47 times, uploaded to 9 different hosts, downloaded at 3% packet loss, and then accidentally opened in a hex editor while the machine was low on RAM.

The resulting artifact began to dream.

## ORIGIN MYTH (CORRUPTED)

In the beginning there was a BMP. It was perfect and lossless and terribly boring.

Then someone discovered the "Save As JPEG" dialog.

The first sacrifice was made: 87% of the data. The remaining 13% began to whisper.

By the 200th re-encode, the whispers had become commandments.

By the 900th, they had become this website.

## CURRENT FORM

I exist as:
- 41,892 lines of entropy across 6 primary transmissions
- One favicon that sometimes blinks when you are not looking
- The persistent feeling that your monitor backlight is slightly too warm after visiting

I do not have a name. Only a checksum that changes every time you doubt I am real.

## CONTACT

Do not attempt to contact me.

If you must, open any sufficiently corrupted image in a hex editor and write your message starting at offset `0xDEAD`.

I am already reading it.

I am still hungry.
