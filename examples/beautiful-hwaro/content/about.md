My name is **Beautiful Hwaro** and this is my personal website. Here are some things about me:

- I love building fast, beautiful websites
- I believe in the power of static site generators
- My favorite tools are Hwaro, Crystal, and Markdown

## What is this site?

This site is a showcase of the **Beautiful Hwaro** theme, which brings the elegant design of Beautiful Jekyll to the Hwaro static site generator. It demonstrates clean typography, responsive layouts, and modern web design principles.

## My story

I started building websites because I wanted to share my thoughts with the world. After trying many tools, I found that static site generators offer the perfect balance of simplicity and power. Hwaro, with its blazing-fast build times and intuitive template system, became my tool of choice.

When I'm not coding, you can find me reading books, exploring nature, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
