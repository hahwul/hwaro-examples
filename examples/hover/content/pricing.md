Every tape bills monthly with no term contract -- downgrade or cancel from the dashboard and keep your RSS feed exactly as it was. Annual billing takes a month off the Broadcast and Syndicate price if you would rather pay once and forget about it.

## What "bias" means here

The format label on each tape borrows from cassette formulations for a reason: like Type I, II, and IV tape stock, each plan is tuned for a different kind of signal. Demo is normal bias -- honest, a little noisy, plenty for a first season. Broadcast is chrome -- the format most working shows actually ran on, clean enough for a sponsor read. Syndicate is metal -- the widest dynamic range, built for a network carrying more than one show's worth of low end.

## Moving between tapes

Upgrades apply immediately and are prorated to the day. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we will warn you first if a downgrade would drop you below your current episode count or supporter-loop usage. Nothing is ever deleted on a downgrade -- features simply go dormant and reactivate the moment you upgrade again.

## A note on the free tier

Demo is not a trial. There is no expiration date and no credit card required to start it, and shows have run an entire first season on it before ever touching a paid tape. The only limits are the ones listed on the label: three episodes and a two-week analytics window. When a show outgrows those limits, that is usually a good sign.
