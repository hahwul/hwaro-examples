The full log, most recent departure first. Each passage is written the morning after making harbor, with the barometer and wind read at the helm rather than corrected in hindsight. The rising or falling arrow beside every date is the glass as it stood at the time — the number that shaped the decisions in the entry below it, not a tidy summary added afterward once the weather's outcome was already known.
