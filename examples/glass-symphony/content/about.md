Glass Symphony was created as an example to showcase the flexibility of the Hwaro static site generator.

## The Philosophy

The goal was to create a design that feels modern, lightweight, and deeply atmospheric. By using a dark palette combined with vibrant purple and pink accents, the theme evokes a sense of modern technology and creativity.

The glass panels ensure that the content remains legible while still allowing the dynamic background to shine through.
