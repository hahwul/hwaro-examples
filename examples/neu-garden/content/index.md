## Botanical Tactility

Neu-Garden is about the relationship between nature and the digital interface in a tactile environment. We believe that botanical content should be presented with a sense of growth and substantiality. By focusing on neumorphic-inspired soft shadows and clean, green-toned elements, we create an environment that feels both organic and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the garden and the way it is curated.

### Growing Principles

- **Organic Growth**: A design that reflects the natural evolution of ideas.
- **Translucency**: Using light to define the layers of the flourishing work.
- **Clarity**: Finding the core meaning within the botanical structure.
