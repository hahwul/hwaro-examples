The border is not merely a separator; it is a container. It gives the content shape and prevents the dispersion of ideas into the void.

By enforcing a twelve-pixel border around the viewport, we immediately signal that the space within is managed and deliberate. It acts as a frame, isolating the content from its surrounding context.
