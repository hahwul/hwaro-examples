title: "Study 02: Typography as Architecture"
date: "2023-10-26"
description: "Examining fonts as load-bearing elements."
tags: ["typography", "structure"]
---

Typography in this design is not decorative. The heavy serifs of Playfair Display anchor the page, serving as architectural pillars from which the rest of the text hangs.

The contrast between the robust headings and the utilitarian sans-serif body text creates a clear hierarchy, allowing the reader to navigate the document's structure effortlessly.
