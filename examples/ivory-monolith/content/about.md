The Ivory Monolith is an exercise in absolute restraint.

It was conceived as a reaction against the ethereal and the transient. Where others seek fluidity through gradients and soft shadows, this design demands solid lines and defined spaces. The off-white background serves as a canvas, while the stark borders act as the load-bearing walls of the layout.

### Specifications

The typography utilizes **Playfair Display** for its classic, robust serifs, and **Inter** for uncompromising clarity in secondary texts.

This design deliberately excludes:
1. Gradients
2. Soft shadows
3. Emojis
4. Translucent layers

The focus remains entirely on the structural composition of the page.
