The wall

Four bays, four angles

  • Bay One Slab · 15°

    Low-angle, footwork-first. Where new climbers start and strong climbers get humbled.

  • Bay Two Vertical · 90°

    The old tram entrance, dead straight up. The widest spread of grades in the building.

  • Bay Three Overhang · 20°+

    Power and core, short problems that ask a lot in six or seven moves.

  • Bay Four The Cave

    A horizontal roof traverse under the old truss line. Most regulars' favorite bay.
