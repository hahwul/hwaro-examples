Classes run out of [Bay One and Bay Two](@/the-wall.md) most weeknights, and every one of them is taught by someone who also sets routes here — the person teaching you to read a sequence on Wednesday is the same person who built it on Monday. Members climb free with any [plan](@/membership.md); drop-ins are $18 per class, gear included.

## Intro to Bouldering

An hour built for someone who has never touched a wall: how to fall on purpose, how to read a taped route without overthinking it, and enough footwork to leave tired instead of frustrated. No booking needed — just show up ten minutes early to grab shoes.

## Route-Setting Workshop

Once a month, ten members get a bay to themselves and a setting crew member walking them through the actual job: forerunning, grading arguments, and why a hold that looks impossible from the ground is usually a footjug in disguise. It fills up within a day of being posted, so members get first access before it opens publicly.

## Youth Squad

A weekly session for climbers 8 to 15, split by ability rather than age. Parents are welcome to watch from the stretching circle but coaching happens on the wall, not from the sidelines — we've found kids climb better when nobody's calling out the next hold for them. Read more [about the crew](@/about.md) who run it.
