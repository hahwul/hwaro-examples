There's one membership decision that actually matters: how often you think you'll come. Everything below gets you the same wall, the same classes at member pricing, and the same cubby key — the only difference is how the math works out. Shoe and chalk-bag rental is included with every visit, first-timer or lifer.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Day pass | $22 | Visiting, or trying us once |
| 10-visit pack | $180 | Once a week, no rush to use it |
| Monthly unlimited | $89 / month | Climbing twice a week or more |
| Annual | $850 / year | Monthly regulars — about two months free |

Ten-visit packs don't expire. Monthly unlimited has no lock-in; cancel any time before your renewal date at the front desk or by email. Students and climbers under 18 get 15% off any plan with valid ID — ask at the desk, we don't make you fill out a form for it.

## What's included

Every plan covers open climbing across all four bays during posted hours, one guest pass per month to bring a friend for free, and member pricing on every [class we run](@/classes.md), including the ones that usually sell out first. Members also get first access when we open registration for the monthly route-setting workshop, which otherwise fills within a day.

## First visit

New climbers need to sign a waiver once — bring ID, budget five minutes at the desk, and if it's genuinely your first time on a wall, ask for a quick orientation. It's free, it's not a class, and it covers falling technique, how [the grading tape works](@/the-wall.md), and where the beginner-friendly problems live on any given week. See [hours and directions](@/visit.md) before you come by.
