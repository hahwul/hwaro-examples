## The Eclipse Blog

Eclipse is a space for contemplation at the boundary of light and shadow. Named for the most dramatic event in our daytime sky, this blog explores astronomy, celestial mechanics, and the profound sense of wonder that comes from looking upward.

## What You Will Find Here

Writing on total solar eclipses, lunar phenomena, stellar observation, and the science that connects us to the cosmos. Each post is an invitation to step outside and consider the vast machinery turning above us.

## The Philosophy

A total solar eclipse lasts only minutes, yet those who witness one describe it as life-altering. That tension between brevity and depth -- the fleeting moment that reshapes understanding -- is what this blog aspires to capture in every piece of writing.
