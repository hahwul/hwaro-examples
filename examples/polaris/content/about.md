This project is part of the Hwaro example sites, showcasing a dark, astronomy-themed layout.

### Technical Concept

The Polaris theme uses:
- **Night Navy & Infinity Black**: To simulate the deep sky and the observatory interior.
- **Constellation Gold**: For navigation paths and highlighting celestial objects.
- **Star White**: For high-contrast text and bright stellar points.
- **Concentric Star Chart**: A background layout centered on the North Star.

Navigation is designed to mimic the connecting lines of a constellation, guiding the user through the observatory's data.
