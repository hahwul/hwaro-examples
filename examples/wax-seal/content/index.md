## Official Tactility

Wax-Seal is about the authentic and secure nature of structural innovation. We believe that verified content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of the early administrative era. By focusing on white paper surfaces, deep shadows, and elegant, modern typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the seal.

### Seal Principles

- **Authenticity**: A design that prioritizes the clear "official" nature of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using seal-like textures to define the boundaries of the verified space.
- **Security**: A layout that evokes the feeling of administrative protection.
