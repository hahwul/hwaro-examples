# The Philosophy of the Void

Quantum Veil is designed to feel like stepping onto the bridge of a next-generation starship or deep-diving into a virtual network.

By utilizing absolute blacks and deep space navy (`#030508`) as a foundation, the vivid accent colors—Cyan (`#00f2fe`), Magenta (`#fe00a1`), and Purple (`#8a2be2`)—can truly shine.

## The Stack

- **Generator**: Hwaro (Rust-powered, insanely fast)
- **Styling**: Pure CSS (Glassmorphism, CSS Variables, Animations)
- **Typography**: Google Fonts (Outfit & Inter)

It's entirely static, entirely customizable, and ready for deployment.
