## THE CLOSER

If Okonkwo owns the first forty meters, Matt Abrahams owns the last thirty. He is rarely the first across 60 meters. He is often the first across the finish.

### Top End Speed

Abrahams holds the fastest measured top-end speed in this field &mdash; 44.72 km/h through a laser-split in last year's Diamond League. That speed comes late. For the first 50 meters of every race he looks like he is running second. Then the field slows. He does not.

### The Back Half

Elite sprinters decelerate in the final 20 meters. Top speed is reached around the 60-to-80m mark. Everyone gets slower after that. Abrahams gets slower more gradually than anyone else. That is not a gift; that is a training program.

### The Tape

He has never beaten Santos in a major. Tonight is his fifth chance.

*Lane six. The start is not his weapon. The finish is.*
