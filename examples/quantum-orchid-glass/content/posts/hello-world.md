Welcome to the **Quantum Orchid Glass** theme!

This post demonstrates how content is displayed within the frosted glass panels over the animated cosmic background.

### Why Glassmorphism?
Glassmorphism creates a sense of depth and hierarchy without relying on solid, flat blocks of color. It looks stunning when combined with vibrant background elements like our glowing *quantum orchid* nodes!
