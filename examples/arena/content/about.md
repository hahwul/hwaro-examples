## The Arena

Arena is a sports blog dedicated to delivering sharp match reports, tactical breakdowns, and league coverage. We cover the Premier League, cup competitions, and transfer market analysis throughout the season.

## What We Cover

- **Match Reports** -- Detailed recaps of every matchday with key moments, tactical shifts, and player performances
- **Standings & Stats** -- Up-to-date league tables, top scorers, and statistical deep-dives
- **Transfer Analysis** -- Breaking down the deals that shape the season
- **Cup Competitions** -- Full coverage of domestic and continental tournaments

## About This Theme

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and lightweight static site generator. The Arena theme features a dark, scoreboard-inspired design with team color coding, diagonal decorative accents, and bold typography suited for sports content.
