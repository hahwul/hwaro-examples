Royal City are through to the cup semi-finals after a nerve-shredding penalty shootout victory that extended their remarkable run in the competition. This is the furthest the club has progressed since 2019.

## The Match

The tie was goalless through 90 minutes, with both sides cancelling each other out in a tight, tactical affair. Extra time produced two goals -- one for each team -- before the shootout decided the outcome.

Goalkeeper Emil Larsen was the hero, saving two penalties and sending the travelling supporters into a frenzy. His dive to deny the fourth opposition penalty was world-class -- full stretch, fingertips, pushing the ball onto the post and away.

## The Road to the Final

Royal City's cup path has not been straightforward:

- **Round 3**: 2-1 vs Iron Athletic (away)
- **Round 4**: 3-0 vs lower-league opposition (home)
- **Quarter-final**: Penalties after 1-1 draw (away)

The semi-final draw pairs them against either Phoenix Rising or Thunderbolts FC. Regardless of the opponent, reaching the final from this position would be a genuine achievement for a club sitting fifth in the league.
