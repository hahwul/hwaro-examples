Welcome to the **Stellar Nexus**.

This site is a demonstration of Hwaro's capabilities, featuring a futuristic, elegant design with deep space aesthetics.

### Features
* Glassmorphism elements powered by CSS `backdrop-filter`.
* Glowing cyan, magenta, and purple accents.
* Fluid, animated background gradients.
* Clean and highly readable typography using **Space Grotesk** and **Outfit**.

Explore the [Transmissions](/posts/) or access the [Data Core](/archives/).
