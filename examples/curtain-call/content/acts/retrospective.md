## ACT V, Scene ii

### Retrospective

*The stage fills with warm amber light. Projections flicker across the cyclorama -- fragments of scenes past.*

Now we look back. Not with sorrow, but with the clear-eyed appreciation of a company that knows the value of what it has built.

### Milestones

The season opened with ambition and uncertainty in equal measure. What followed was a series of achievements that none of us could have predicted:

- **The First Night** -- when the doors opened and the vision became reality
- **The Turning Point** -- the moment the production found its voice
- **The Breakthrough** -- when craft became art and effort became effortless
- **The Full House** -- every seat taken, every heart engaged

### Moments That Defined Us

Between the milestones were the quiet moments. The late rehearsals. The costume adjustments made minutes before curtain. The understudy who stepped into the role and made it their own.

These are the moments that do not appear in programmes or reviews, yet they are the mortar that held this production together.

### The Highlights Reel

If we were to distill this entire journey into a single image, it would not be the standing ovation or the five-star review. It would be the company gathered in a circle backstage, breathing together before the house opened.

That is who we were. That is what we accomplished.

---

*Scene ii concludes. The projections fade.*
