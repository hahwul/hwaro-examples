    <p class="credit">Four Maisons &middot; Ten Commissions</p>

    <p class="credit">Estate &middot; Contemporary &middot; Custom</p>
    <p>The jewellery register for the evening lists forty-two individual pieces. Twenty-three are estate, insured at a combined carpet value in the mid-eight figures and guarded by two discreet houses working independently. The remainder are contemporary commissions, three of which were made specifically for this premiere.</p>

## Most-photographed moments

- Odessa Lumi&egrave;re's turn at the colonnade's fourth arch, a deliberate mark set by the choreography team six hours before the carpet opened.
- Nomvula Dlamini's quiet exchange with the lead actress just inside the rope line, captured by the wire crews at 19:40.
- Ada Okonjo's twirl to show the full brocade cape &mdash; three full rotations, applause from the press pit on the third.

## Credits
