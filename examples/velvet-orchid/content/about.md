# About

Velvet Orchid is a theme designed for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), aiming to provide a luxurious, dark, and sophisticated reading experience.

It uses a refined color palette consisting of deep purples, rich golds, and pure whites for maximum contrast and readability.
