PHILOSOPHY

Lattice is read documentation, not marketed documentation. Every page assumes you have already decided to use the engine and now need to understand it. No comparison tables. No badges. No content engineered for skim.

The hairline grid behind every page is the same grid the planner uses to align its plan trees. It is intentional, not decoration.
