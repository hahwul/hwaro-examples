Beyond the switch, the shape of the keycap significantly impacts the typing feel. Profiles like Cherry, OEM, and SA offer different sculpting that guides the fingers across the board.

A flat profile might suit a minimalist aesthetic, while a deeply sculpted spherical profile can cradle the fingertips, providing a vintage, tactile experience. The geometry of the keys is as important as the mechanics beneath them.
