The choice of switch defines the soul of a mechanical keyboard. Whether you prefer the crisp click of a blue switch or the smooth, silent travel of a linear red, the switch dictates the physical feedback of every keystroke.

Understanding the actuation point and bottom-out force is essential for finding the right hardware. A well-chosen switch not only improves typing speed but also reduces fatigue during long programming sessions.
