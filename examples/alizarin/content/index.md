Alizarin is the solo lighting design practice of Noor Adeyemi, working across dance houses and small opera companies in the Low Countries and Belgium. Every [production](/productions/) here is documented the way it was hung: fixture by fixture, gel by gel, cue by cue. For the practice behind the paperwork, see [About](/about/).
