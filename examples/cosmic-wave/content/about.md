# About

This is a cosmic journey in digital design. Cosmic Wave is a small studio of three working between Seoul and Lisbon, taking on a handful of long projects each year.

## How We Work

We start every engagement with a two-week study phase. No mockups, no moodboards, just reading, listening, and walking the brief. Most of the time, the design problem we end up solving is not the one written into the contract.

## What We Make

Our output falls into three rough buckets. First, editorial sites for publishers who need their archives to last a decade. Second, generative installations for galleries and science centers. Third, identity systems for organizations that have outgrown their first logo and are not ready for their last one.

## Who We Work With

Past clients include two public broadcasters, a national maritime museum, a university press, and a handful of independent magazines. We take on roughly six projects a year and turn down many more. References are available after a first call.
