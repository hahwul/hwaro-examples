# Enter the Cosmic Wave

Explore a fluid universe of design and light. The Cosmic Wave portfolio collects work that lives at the intersection of motion, depth, and quiet structure.

## Recent Work

The studio has been focused on three threads in the last twelve months. Each one started as a small experiment and grew into something that earned a place on the homepage.

### Atlas of Tides

A long-form essay on coastal design rhythms, paired with a custom typeface and an interactive map. The piece launched in February 2026 and was featured in two design weeklies during its first week. Most of the typographic decisions were driven by readability at small sizes on saltwater-flecked screens.

### Lumen Field

A real-time generative installation built for a science museum in March 2026. Visitors steer light through a mesh of mirrors using a single dial. The installation logged 18,000 interactions in its first month without a single reset.

### Quiet Channel

A podcast site redesign delivered in late 2025. The brief asked for fewer surfaces, slower transitions, and a typographic system that could carry guest contributions for the next five years. Listening time per session climbed by 22 percent after launch.
