Every episode of Timbre opens the same way: one object on the table, one hour to take it apart. A magazine spread, a subway sign, a manifesto cover, a mimeographed poster. We date it, we place it, and we ask what it was arguing for.

Hosted by design historian Adaline Marchetti and working designer Desmond Okafor, and produced out of a converted print shop in Providence, Rhode Island.
