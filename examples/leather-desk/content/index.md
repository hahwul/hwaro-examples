## Material Authority

Leather-Desk is about the tactile and authoritative nature of innovation within the professional workspace. We believe that serious work deserves a serious environment. By focusing on a dark, leather-inspired palette and classical, high-contrast typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the workspace.

### Craft Principles

- **Authority**: A design that commands respect and focus.
- **Tactility**: Using subtle textures and shadows to define the workspace surface.
- **Permanence**: A layout that reflects the long-term value of the work.
