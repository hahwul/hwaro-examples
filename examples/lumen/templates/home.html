Aldermere Light · illumination archive 1904–2004

A century of lamplight, redrawn as data-art.

Every night for a hundred years, the keepers of Aldermere logged the beam: its bearing, its burn, the hours it failed. Lumen turns those ledgers into glowing plots — each tile framed like an instrument on the lamp-room bench.
