The first cup is for waking. The second is for writing. The third is for forgiving the first two. We keep to the second cup whenever the hour permits, because the reporting it produces is slower, and the slower reporting is the better reporting.

> A dispatch filed at the first cup is a guess. A dispatch filed at the second is, at minimum, a small admission.

We say this with the usual disclaimers. Your mileage, your kitchen, your grounds.
