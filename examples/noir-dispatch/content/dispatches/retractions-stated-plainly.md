The Dispatch, like every paper, corrects itself. We believe corrections belong on the page they were earned on, not buried under a later edition.

## Corrections

1. In **Vol. IX, No. 44**, we referred to the lamp at Seventh and Laurel. The lamp is at *Ninth* and Laurel. We apologise to Laurel.
2. In **Vol. IX, No. 45**, a dispatch attributed a letter to *a woman in a green coat*. The coat was olive. We apologise to the coat.
3. In **Vol. IX, No. 46**, the time of the streetcar was listed as 02:04. It was 02:14. We apologise to the streetcar, which has enough to contend with.

Further retractions will run in this column as the week requires.
