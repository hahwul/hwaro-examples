# BASIS-25 Full Instrument

The full BASIS-25 instrument is presented below. All 25 items use a common five-point Likert response scale with the same anchor labels. Recall window is the past 7 days.

## Response scale

All items are scored **1 (Never) to 5 (Always)** with the following anchors:

1. **Never**
2. **Rarely**
3. **Some of the time**
4. **Most of the time**
5. **Always**

## Scoring

- **Subscale score:** Mean of non-missing items in the subscale, scaled from 1 to 5. Do not compute when more than two items are missing in a subscale.
- **Composite score:** Mean of subscale scores (not item-level mean), scaled from 1 to 5.
- **Reverse scoring:** No items are reverse-scored; all items are framed positively.
