## About This Photo Blog

Hi there! I'm a photographer, traveler, and storyteller. This blog is my digital scrapbook where I collect moments that made me pause, smile, or think.

## Why Polaroid?

There's something magical about Polaroid photos. They're imperfect, slightly off-center, and that's what makes them beautiful. Every shake, every faded corner tells a story. This blog tries to capture that same feeling in digital form.

## My Gear

I shoot with whatever I have on hand. Sometimes it's a DSLR, sometimes it's just my phone. The best camera is the one you have with you.

## Get in Touch

Found a photo that resonated with you? Want to share your own stories? Drop me a line - I'd love to hear from you!
