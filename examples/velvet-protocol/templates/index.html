The Lot —
four bottles for the late hour.

Each lot contains four bottles, distilled in the months before release and labelled by the person who finished them. We do not repress a lot; when it is gone, the next lot begins.

§ IIThe Protocol — Abridged
— Marin VelascoHouse Distiller

The house opens at twenty-two, the still is lit at midnight, the bottles are labelled at three. We answer the door, the letter, and the telephone — in that order, and only at the appointed hour.

Everything else is filed for the morning, which the house leaves to others.
