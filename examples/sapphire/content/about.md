## About Sapphire

Sapphire is a glamorous, eye-catching blog theme inspired by the deep brilliance of blue sapphire gemstones — where royal jewel tones meet faceted light refractions that dance across every surface.

Every element of this theme draws from the world of precious stones: the rich midnight blue of a Ceylon sapphire, the golden shimmer of star sapphire inclusions, and the brilliant light that plays across expertly cut facets.

### Design Philosophy

- **Bold & Luxurious** — Rich jewel-tone palettes with regal gold accents
- **Faceted Geometry** — Angular shapes inspired by gemstone cuts
- **Light & Brilliance** — Shimmering effects and luminous highlights
- **Glamorous Polish** — Every detail radiates elegance and intention
