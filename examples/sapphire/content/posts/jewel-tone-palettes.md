Jewel tones — the rich, saturated colours named after precious gems — have been a staple of fashion and interior design for centuries. On the web, they offer a powerful alternative to the muted pastels and flat greys that dominate modern interfaces.

<!-- more -->

## The Core Jewel Palette

Each tone draws its name and character from a specific stone:

- **Sapphire** (#1a3a6c) — Deep royal blue, commanding and wise
- **Emerald** (#0d6b4e) — Vivid green, vibrant and alive
- **Ruby** (#9b1b30) — Intense red, passionate and bold
- **Amethyst** (#6b3fa0) — Regal purple, mysterious and creative
- **Topaz** (#c78d2e) — Warm gold, luxurious and inviting
- **Garnet** (#722f37) — Dark crimson, sophisticated and timeless

## Using Jewel Tones Effectively

The key to jewel tone palettes is restraint within boldness:

1. **Choose one dominant stone** — Let a single jewel tone lead your palette
2. **Pair with metallics** — Gold, silver, or rose gold accents add luxury
3. **Deep backgrounds** — Jewel tones shine brightest against near-black bases
4. **White space as light** — Use generous spacing to let rich colours breathe

## Accessibility Considerations

Rich, dark colours can create accessibility challenges:

- Always verify contrast ratios against WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for body text)
- Use lighter tints of jewel tones for text on dark backgrounds
- Provide sufficient size and weight for text over gradient backgrounds
- Test with colour blindness simulators — jewel tones can be problematic for deuteranopia

## The Psychology of Gemstone Colours

Jewel tones carry inherent associations that strengthen brand messaging:

- Deep blue evokes **trust, stability, and expertise**
- Rich purple suggests **creativity, luxury, and wisdom**
- Saturated green communicates **growth, prosperity, and harmony**
- Warm gold implies **premium quality, achievement, and warmth**

> "Jewel tones don't whisper — they declare. Use them when your design needs to command a room."

When applied thoughtfully, jewel tone palettes create interfaces that feel premium, intentional, and unforgettable.
