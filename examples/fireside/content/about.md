## About Fireside Reads

Fireside Reads is a personal book review blog born from the simple pleasure of reading by the fireplace on cold evenings. Every review here comes from genuine reading -- no sponsored content, no algorithmic recommendations, just a reader sharing thoughts on books that matter.

### What You Will Find

**Honest Reviews** -- Each book gets a fair reading and a candid assessment. Star ratings give a quick snapshot, but the real substance is in the words.

**Reading Progress** -- Some reviews come from ongoing reads. The progress bar shows where I am in the journey, because sometimes the best conversations happen before the last page.

**Diverse Shelves** -- From literary fiction to science writing, from classic novels to contemporary essays. Good writing knows no genre boundary.

### The Rating Scale

- Five stars -- A book that changes how you see the world
- Four stars -- Excellent. Will recommend to anyone who asks
- Three stars -- Solid and worthwhile, with some reservations
- Two stars -- Has merit, but did not quite land
- One star -- Not for me, though I respect the attempt
