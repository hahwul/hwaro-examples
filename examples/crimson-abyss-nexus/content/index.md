## Forged in the Void

The aesthetic relies on deep contrast, glowing ethereal elements, and frosted glass containers that seemingly float above an infinite background.

* **Deep Palette:** Void blacks and abyssal dark purples set the stage.
* **Neon Accents:** Crimson red (`#ff003c`) and neon cyan (`#00f0ff`) provide stark, luminous contrast.
* **Translucency:** `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)` powers the glassmorphism.

## Chiaroscuro in Cyberspace

By blending heavy shadows with vibrant light, the interface achieves a depth that traditional flat design lacks. The animated orbs drifting in the background represent raw data flows through the nexus.
