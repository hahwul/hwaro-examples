Welcome to **Neon Koi Pond**, a mesmerizing dive into the dark depths of the web.

This Hwaro theme is designed for those who appreciate the aesthetic of glassmorphism combined with the vivid contrasts of cyberpunk neon. Watch as the glowing koi fish swim endlessly through the dark, rippling waters of your content.

## Features

- **Deep Space Aesthetic**: A deep dark teal and black background, making neon elements pop.
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent panels that create a sense of depth and elegance.
- **CSS Animations**: Pure CSS animated glowing koi fish and background water ripples.
- **Typography**: Clean and modern typography powered by `Space Grotesk` and `Inter`.

Feel free to explore the depths!
