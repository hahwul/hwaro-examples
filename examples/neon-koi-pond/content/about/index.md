The **Neon Koi Pond** theme is an exploration of serene yet futuristic design.

In traditional Japanese culture, koi fish symbolize luck, prosperity, and perseverance. In this theme, these timeless symbols are reimagined as glowing entities of pure data, navigating the vast, dark ocean of the internet.

### The Design Language

The design relies heavily on the `backdrop-filter` CSS property to create frosted glass effects, a trend known as **glassmorphism**. This is combined with subtle radial gradients to simulate the underwater lighting and refraction.

The typography pairs the geometric, tech-focused `Space Grotesk` for headings with the highly legible `Inter` for body copy.
