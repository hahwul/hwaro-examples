## The First Records

Papyrus is about the earliest forms of structural documentation. We believe that innovation begins with the first mark made on a resilient surface. By focusing on the ancient-scroll aesthetic and the organic nature of the medium, we create a design that feels both primal and deeply foundational.

### Reed-Based Innovation

- **Origin**: The starting point of the written record.
- **Weave**: The complex structural relationship between individual fibers.
- **Endurance**: A design that survives through the integrity of the material.
