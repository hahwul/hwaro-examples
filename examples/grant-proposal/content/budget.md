## Senior Personnel

**Dr. Anika Patel (PI, MIT)** -- 2 months summer salary per year for 3 years. Dr. Patel will provide overall project leadership, coordinate the multi-institutional team, oversee the development of the integrated modelling framework, and serve as primary liaison with NSF programme officers. Salary: $62,500/year.

**Dr. Marcus Chen (Co-PI, Stanford)** -- 1.5 months summer salary per year. Dr. Chen will lead the hydraulic network modelling component (WP2) and contribute to tool development (WP6). Salary: $65,000/year.

**Dr. Fatima Al-Rashidi (Co-PI, UT Austin)** -- 1.5 months summer salary per year. Dr. Al-Rashidi will lead the climate downscaling component (WP3) and provide climate projection expertise to the optimization team. Salary: $55,000/year.

**Dr. James Okafor (Co-PI, Georgia Tech)** -- 1 month summer salary per year. Dr. Okafor will lead the multi-objective optimization framework (WP4). Salary: $45,000/year.

**Dr. Yuki Tanaka (Co-PI, U. Michigan)** -- 1 month summer salary per year plus 10% academic year for stakeholder engagement coordination. Dr. Tanaka will lead WP5 and contribute social science methodology. Salary: $28,333/year.

## Travel

Travel funds support:
- **Conference presentations**: $72,000 (6 major conferences over 3 years, 2-3 team members each)
- **Inter-institutional meetings**: $48,000 (semi-annual in-person team meetings at rotating locations)
- **Partner municipality visits**: $36,000 (quarterly site visits to Boston, Houston, and Phoenix)
- **Advisory board meetings**: $24,000 (annual in-person advisory board meetings)
