{{ card(call_number="Z682.4.M87 O33 2026", heading="Summary", subject="Librarians, rare book and manuscript — biography", drawer="Reference — Vertical File") }}

Fifteen years in music special collections, the last seven leading acquisitions and provenance research at Thornfield Conservatory Library. I specialize in manuscripts that arrive without clean paperwork — partbooks split across estate sales, autograph scores misattributed for a century, choirbooks too fragile to unbind for cataloging. My work is to make each one legible: assign it a place in the collection, a citation a scholar can trust, and a finding aid that still holds up after I've left the job.

{{ card(call_number="ML93 .O33 2024", heading="Special Collections Projects", subject="Manuscripts, musical — conservation and digitization", drawer="Project Log") }}

- **The Colwyn-Hale Partbook Recovery (2022–2024).** Reassembled a dispersed set of six seventeenth-century vocal partbooks split across three institutions and one estate sale, using matched rastrology and a shared watermark family to confirm the set belonged together before a single volume changed hands.
- **RISM Online Migration (2020–2021).** Led migration of 4,800 manuscript records from FileMaker into RISM-compliant hybrid MARC/EAD records, including a full crosswalk document now used as a training reference by two partner institutions.
- **Hallworth Choirbook Digitization (2023).** IIIF-compliant imaging of a fragile fifteenth-century choirbook, completed without unbinding via a custom foam cradle built with the conservation lab.
- **Vance Bequest Processing (2019–2020).** Arrangement, description, and finding-aid authorship for a 1,200-item estate donation of scores, correspondence, and concert programs.
