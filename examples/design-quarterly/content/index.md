## Design Excellence

Design-Quarterly is about the refined and foundational nature of structural innovation within the design environment. We believe that design research deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic academic journal. By focusing on white surfaces, clear hierarchies, and elegant sans-serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the research.

### Quarterly Principles

- **Precision**: A design that prioritizes the clear presentation of research and analysis.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the academic space.
- **Authority**: A layout that reflects the high-value nature of the primary review.
