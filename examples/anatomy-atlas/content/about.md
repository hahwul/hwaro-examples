# About the Atlas

Welcome to the **Ex Libris Medicus**, an anatomy atlas designed to explore the intricate systems of the human body. This collection serves as a reference for the muscular, nervous, and skeletal systems, providing detailed insights into our biological architecture.

Our mission is to bring classic anatomical studies into a digital age, combining the rigor of historical medical journals with modern accessibility.
