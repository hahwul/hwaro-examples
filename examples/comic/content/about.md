## About This Site

Welcome to Comic, a webtoon and comic strip inspired blog built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).

This theme brings the visual language of comics to the web:

- **Panel grid layouts** that frame content like comic strips
- **Speech bubble blockquotes** for commentary and highlights
- **Bold outlines and pop colors** for a lively reading experience
- **Episode-based navigation** to move between entries
- **Light theme** with a bright, energetic color palette

## The Style

Inspired by webtoons and classic comic strips, every element on this site is designed to feel like a page from your favorite comic. Thick borders define content panels, speech bubbles call out key ideas, and the color palette stays vibrant without overwhelming.
