Welcome to the onboard operations wiki and engineering reference manual for the Class-IV Deep-Space Cruiser (SSC-2026-A). This terminal interface is designed to provide the ship's crew and engineering team with direct access to subsystems diagnostic logs, telemetry databases, and propulsion system documentation. This wiki is maintained by the chief operations officer and updated after every planetary transit or warp cycle.

The Class-IV Starship class is a high-endurance orbital vessel designed for deep-space cartography and interstellar research missions. Equipped with advanced antimatter propulsion cores and dual-layered magnetic deflector shields, the vessel can sustain autonomous space exploration runs for up to five years without requiring major orbital dock refurbishments.

Inside this console, you will find comprehensive data regarding the ship's active propulsion cores, life support scrubbers, tactical sensors, and communication grid configurations. Each page is generated from the live markdown logs compiled by the ship's automated logging units. For emergency bypass or hard restarts of the console, refer to the printed checklists located in the bridge cabinet.
