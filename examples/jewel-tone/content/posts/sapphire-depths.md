The richness of sapphire blue provides a grounding foundation for elegant web design. Unlike harsh primary colors, deep jewel tones offer a sophisticated contrast that feels both luxurious and readable.

By utilizing profound, solid blues as a background, we create a sense of infinite depth. Text and subtle gold accents can rest upon this surface, catching the user's attention without overwhelming the visual senses.
