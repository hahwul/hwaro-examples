## About Voltage

Voltage is an energy technology blog charged with the intensity of high-voltage systems. The design draws from the visual language of electrical engineering -- sharp contrasts, warning indicators, and the raw energy of arc discharge.

## Design Philosophy

- **Electric palette** -- volt yellow accents against deep black backgrounds
- **Danger zone atmosphere** -- inspired by high-voltage substation aesthetics
- **Controlled energy** -- clean typography with electric edge details
- **Dark theme** -- like a control panel in a power distribution center

## About the Theme

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), Voltage is a dark blog theme for energy technology, electrical engineering, and power systems content.
