Welcome to **Acoustic Soundwaves**, a design experiment that turns frequencies into visual elements.

Here, sound is not just heard, it is seen. The interface pulsates with the rhythm of the data, creating a dynamic and immersive experience.

> "If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency and vibration." — Nikola Tesla

### Features of this theme

*   **Animated SVG Backgrounds:** Continuous wave animations that mimic sound frequencies.
*   **CSS Equalizers:** A pulsing footer that reacts like a graphic equalizer.
*   **Track-style Listing:** Posts and sections are styled to look like audio tracks in a media player.
*   **Cyber-Acoustic Palette:** Deep blacks, neon cyans, and teals create a modern, high-tech aesthetic.
