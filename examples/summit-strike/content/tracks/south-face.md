## the route

South Face is the flagship track and the one most climbers book. Sessions mix 90-minute architecture deep-dives with three-hour hands-on workshops. The elevation gain across the three days is steepest on this route &mdash; pace yourself.

### what to expect

- 90-minute deep-dive sessions with live system demos
- 3-hour labs with working code, not slides
- Open mic during the final 15 minutes of every session
- Whiteboard corners reserved for side conversations after every talk

### the headliners on this route

- **Rebuilding the platform without downtime** &mdash; Day 02 / 09:00 / 1,800 M
- **The forty-person team, one codebase** &mdash; Day 02 / 10:45 / 2,100 M
- **Incident review: the outage that took a weekend** &mdash; Day 02 / 14:00 / 2,500 M
- **The summit keynote** &mdash; Day 03 / 15:00 / 4,210 M (all tracks converge)

### who this is for

You are mid-to-senior with architecture responsibility. You are a staff engineer who wants to stress-test your positions against other practitioners. You are a founder, CTO, or first engineer thinking about building for ten times your current scale.
