## the route

North Ridge is the conference's graded approach. It is the widest trail, the longest, and the most forgiving. Sessions are 45 minutes of presentation and 15 minutes of discussion. No surprise whiteboard work. You will sit, listen, and leave with notes.

### what to expect

- Long-form talks (60 minutes) with a single speaker
- Q&A moderated by a session chair &mdash; no open mic
- Breaks between sessions run 30 minutes, not 15
- Morning coffee stop at the second switchback

### the headliners on this route

- **Retrospectives: the five years we got wrong** &mdash; Day 02 / 09:00 / 1,800 M
- **Naming things: a practical discipline** &mdash; Day 02 / 10:45 / 2,100 M
- **Technical writing for people who are climbing with you** &mdash; Day 02 / 14:00 / 2,500 M
- **The quiet parts of software leadership** &mdash; Day 03 / 09:30 / 3,400 M

### who this is for

You are early-to-mid career and you want to build a broader mental map. You are a senior IC who wants a restful route to clear your head. You are coming to the summit with a team and you want a track they can all follow without losing each other.
