Three tracks, all climbing to the same summit. North Ridge is a graded approach for people who want long-form sessions at a moderate pace. South Face is the flagship route, with deep-dive labs and architecture work. East Couloir is the technical route — expect pre-reading, whiteboard time, and no slides.
