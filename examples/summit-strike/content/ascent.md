# ASCENT PLAN

Three days. Base camp to summit. Each day climbs farther and higher. The schedule below is the master plan &mdash; each track publishes its own sub-schedule, but the named checkpoints on this page are fixed for everyone.

## DAY 01 &middot; Base Camp (1,600 M)

A light day. Arrive, register, settle in. The opening keynote runs 90 minutes and is the only mandatory session on day one.

| TIME | ELEV | WHAT |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 13:00 | 1,600 M | Registration opens &middot; badges, packets, track assignments |
| 15:00 | 1,600 M | Mixer &middot; low-stakes networking in the lobby |
| 17:30 | 1,700 M | Opening keynote &middot; Ridge Hall main auditorium |
| 19:30 | 1,700 M | Welcome dinner &middot; Peak Pavilion &middot; seated |
| 21:30 | 1,700 M | Stargazing (weather permitting) &middot; summit deck |

## DAY 02 &middot; Checkpoint (2,940 M)

The long day. Three parallel tracks run from 09:00 to 17:30 with a long lunch in the middle. This is where you do the most elevation gain, so plan breaks.

| 09:00 | 1,800 M | Morning block &middot; three tracks, parallel |
| 10:30 | 2,000 M | Hydration stop &middot; cafe open |
| 10:45 | 2,100 M | Late-morning block &middot; three tracks, parallel |
| 12:30 | 2,300 M | Lunch &middot; high-meadow catering |
| 14:00 | 2,500 M | Afternoon block &middot; three tracks, parallel |
| 16:15 | 2,700 M | Fireside panel &middot; all tracks combine |
| 17:30 | 2,940 M | Checkpoint pour &middot; day-02 wrap |
| 19:30 | 2,940 M | Track dinners &middot; three small rooms |

## DAY 03 &middot; Summit (4,210 M)

The shortest day and the tallest. Morning sessions run in the lab rooms. The summit keynote at 15:00 is mandatory for everyone. The ascent walk follows.

| TIME | ELEV | WHAT |

| 12:00 | 3,700 M | Lunch &middot; summit-day meal |
| 13:30 | 4,000 M | Summit panel &middot; three speakers, one hour |
| 15:00 | 4,210 M | Summit keynote &middot; Ridge Hall |

## stamina notes

- Drink twice as much water as you think you need
- The altitude will affect how much energy you have in sessions; that is normal
- No session is mandatory except the two keynotes and the summit panel
- Skip a session. Take a nap. The mountain is not a stress test
