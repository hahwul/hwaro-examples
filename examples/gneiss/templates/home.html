Graphs, not tables

Nodes and edges arrive as events. MATCH walks a pattern against whatever shape the graph holds at the current watermark, not a snapshot from disk.

Windows keep results live

A WINDOW clause slices unbounded time into tumbling, hopping, or session buckets, so aggregates stay meaningful over a stream that never ends.

Emission is explicit

Nothing streams out until you say so. EMIT chooses between every incremental change, one final row per window, or a grace-period flush.
