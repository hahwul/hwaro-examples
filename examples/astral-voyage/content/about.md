The *Stardust Voyager* is manned by a team of elite explorers, scientists, and engineers dedicated to expanding the frontiers of human knowledge.

### Commander Elara Vance

Commander Vance leads the expedition. With over 20 years of experience in deep-space missions, she brings unparalleled expertise and steady leadership to the crew.

### Dr. Julian Thorne (Chief Science Officer)

Dr. Thorne is responsible for all scientific research and analysis. His work in quantum astrophysics has paved the way for our current understanding of slipstream travel.

### Engineer Jax (Chief Engineer)

Jax ensures that the *Voyager* remains operational under the most extreme conditions. Their innovative modifications to the warp core have significantly increased our travel efficiency.
