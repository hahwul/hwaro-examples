## Who we are

Greenroom is a loose collective of curators who run seasonal residencies in small venues &mdash; usually black-box theatres, studio spaces, and the occasional upstairs room of a jazz club. We program four seasons a year, and we invite between six and nine artists to each season.

We started in 2019 with a pair of back-to-back monologues in a 40-seat room above a bookshop. We've expanded, cautiously, from there. The rooms have gotten slightly bigger. The stakes haven't.

## What we curate

We're drawn to performances that lose something when they scale up &mdash; solo instrumentation, staged readings, devised theatre, one-person stand-up hours, long-form improvisational sets. We're not purists, but we tend to say no to anything that needs a line array.

## How to be invited

Residencies are assembled by the programming committee from open submissions and direct invitations. We read every submission, but we respond slowly &mdash; usually two or three months after the window closes. Submissions for the next cycle open quietly; the only way to find the form is to subscribe to the seasonal letter.

## Press &amp; inquiries

Write to `hello@greenroom.example`. We try to reply within ten days. If you're an artist, lead with a few sentences about the work and a link to a recording or reel. If you're press, tell us the angle and the outlet, and include your deadline.
