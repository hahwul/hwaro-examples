## The Brief

We asked each of the three artists to prepare a set that would lose some quality if it were performed in a larger room. We weren't sure what we'd get back. What we got back was three very different readings of *stillness* &mdash; Leona's nocturnes written for the hour after a dinner party, Asa's monologues composed in a single sitting, and Noor's reworking of four traditional ballads with the ornament stripped out.

> The room is another instrument. When you take it seriously, you play fewer notes.
>
> &mdash; Leona Marchetti, in her introductory note

## Greenroom Notes

The dressing room at Studio Four is tiny &mdash; a long rectangle with a mirror on the short wall and two rails along the length. For this residency, we kept the whiteboard out; each artist wrote their pre-show note in the same corner, in the same marker, and left it for the next night.
