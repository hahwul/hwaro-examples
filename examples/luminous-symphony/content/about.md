<div style="font-size: 1.2rem; margin-bottom: 3rem;">
    <strong>Luminous Symphony</strong> is a dark-themed aesthetic exploration designed for the Hwaro static site generator. It blends the profound depths of the cosmos with the sleek, modern feel of glassmorphism UI.
</div>

<div class="glass-panel" style="margin-bottom: 3rem;">
    <h2 style="color: var(--accent-magenta); margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">The Vision</h2>
    <p>
        The goal was to create an atmosphere that feels both expansive and grounded. By utilizing a deep void background (`#020108`) paired with slow-moving, animated glow orbs, the design creates a sense of infinite space.
    </p>
    <p>
        The content is then grounded through frosted glass panels (`backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`). These panels capture the ambient light of the background orbs, refracting the cyan, magenta, and purple hues to create a dynamic, ever-changing visual symphony.
    </p>
</div>

<div class="glass-panel">
    <h2 style="color: var(--accent-cyan); margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">Typography & Color</h2>
    <p>
        The typography pairs <strong>Inter</strong> for clean, highly legible body text with <strong>Space Grotesk</strong> for bold, structural headings that carry a slightly futuristic tone.
    </p>
    <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">
        The color palette relies on neon accents—Cyan (`#00f0ff`), Magenta (`#ff0055`), and Purple (`#8a2be2`)—to cut through the darkness and guide the user's attention.
    </p>
</div>
