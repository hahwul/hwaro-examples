Welcome to Cyanotype, a clean and elegant theme designed to emphasize typography and contrast.

Inspired by the historic photographic printing process, this design deliberately eschews modern embellishments like gradients and excessive decorations, focusing instead on stark clarity, crisp boundaries, and the beautiful interplay between deep blue hues and stark white spaces.

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).
