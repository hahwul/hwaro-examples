Cyanotype is an exploration of restraint. It embraces the idea that content should speak for itself without the crutch of over-designed interface elements. By focusing on typography, margin, and a singular accent color, we create an environment where reading is effortless and distractions are minimized.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — Leonardo da Vinci

### The Color Palette

The dominant color is a deep, resonant cyan (`#008eb0`), balanced against soft, almost-white backgrounds (`#f5f9fa`) and rich, dark slate text (`#1a2c38`). The absence of gradients ensures that edges remain sharp and forms remain distinct.

### Typography

We utilize timeless serif typefaces for the body text to provide a comfortable reading experience, accented by clean sans-serif fonts for navigation and metadata. This classic pairing ensures the design feels both established and contemporary.
