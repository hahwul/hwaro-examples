Chinook is the working log of a small kitchen at 1,048 metres, where thin air
rushes the proof, water boils cool, and yesterday's pillowy roll is today's dry
cake. I write down every gram, every proof window, and every altitude tweak —
chasing one cinnamon roll with a crackle-soft top and a molten spiral that
stays tender to the last.
