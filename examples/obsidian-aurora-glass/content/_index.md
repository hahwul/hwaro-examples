Welcome to **Obsidian Aurora Glass**, an elegant, dark-themed glassmorphism aesthetic for your Hwaro site.

This design embraces a deep, dark void background interlaced with glowing, slow-moving aurora orbs that create a mesmerizing backdrop. The content is elevated on frosted glass panels that capture and refract the ambient neon light.

### Features
- **Ambient Glowing Orbs**: A soft, animated background using CSS radial gradients.
- **Glassmorphism Panels**: Translucent content areas with `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`.
- **Modern Typography**: Utilizing *Space Grotesk* for striking headings and *Inter* for clean body text.
- **Responsive Layout**: Designed to look stunning on any device.
