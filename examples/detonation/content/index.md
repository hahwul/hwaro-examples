## The Expansion Radius

Detonation is the moment where the internal pressure of an idea becomes too great to contain. It is an expansive, outward movement that changes the landscape of everything it touches. We focus on the epicenter of innovation and the resulting shockwaves.

### Explosive Parameters

- **Yield**: The total energy output of the initial idea.
- **Wave**: The rapid, outward movement of the innovation.
- **Debris**: The remaining fragments that form the basis for future growth.
