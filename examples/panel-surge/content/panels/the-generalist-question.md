## The question

Is the generalist a real category, or is it a retrospective label we use for specialists who changed specialism once or twice?

## Panelists

- **01. Dr. Nadia al-Mansour** &mdash; Generalist physician, Cairo Teaching Hospital
- **02. Finnigan Rohr** &mdash; CTO, Atlas Software
- **03. Prof. Shu Lan** &mdash; Department chair, Comparative Literature, Peking
- **04. Marcus Abasi** &mdash; Film director (and occasional essayist)

## Moderator

Priya Venkat, in her second Panel Surge after the move from policy work at the European Commission to the conference&rsquo;s moderator faculty.

## Format

Sixty-minute discussion, ten-minute audience questions. No vote.

## Moderator&rsquo;s brief

> &ldquo;The generalist question is a self-flattering question: everyone on this panel thinks the answer is &lsquo;yes&rsquo; because the audience thinks the answer is &lsquo;yes&rsquo;. Do not let that happen. I want each panelist to argue the position they would dislike to be right.&rdquo;

&mdash; Priya Venkat

## Note from the programme committee

The programme committee considered pairing this panel with the keynote debate on the office. The committee decided against, reasoning that two sixty-minute discussions on identity-of-the-firm in a single afternoon would exhaust the audience&rsquo;s appetite. The generalist question is placed in Stage B for that reason.
