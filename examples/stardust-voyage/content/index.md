# Into the Stars

Welcome to the **Stardust Voyage**, a cosmic journey through endless galaxies. This vessel is designed for deep space exploration, uncovering the mysteries of the universe.

## Mission Objectives

1. Navigate through asteroid belts.
2. Discover new nebulae.
3. Document extraterrestrial anomalies.
