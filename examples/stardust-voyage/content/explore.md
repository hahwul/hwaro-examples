# Beyond the Unknown

Our ongoing mission is to seek out new worlds and new civilizations. The stellar maps below indicate our primary areas of interest.

### The Orion Spur
A minor spiral arm of the Milky Way galaxy, roughly 3,500 light-years across and approximately 10,000 light-years in length.

### The Carina Nebula
A large, complex area of bright and dark nebulosity in the constellation Carina, located in the Carina–Sagittarius Arm.
