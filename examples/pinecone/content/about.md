## About Pinecone

Pinecone is a place for long-form writing about the natural world. The essays here explore forests, seasons, ecology, and the quiet patterns that emerge when you pay attention to the land.

This site draws its name and spirit from the conifer forests of the northern hemisphere -- places where spruce, pine, and fir trees form vast, silent canopies. The pinecone itself is an emblem of patience: a seed carrier that opens only when conditions are right.

### What You Will Find Here

Essays on forest ecology, seasonal observation, botany, and the relationship between human culture and wild landscapes. The writing favors depth over frequency, observation over opinion.

### Design Philosophy

The visual language of this site reflects its subject matter: dark earth tones, generous white space for unhurried reading, and the silhouettes of conifers along the horizon. The typography is serif-based, chosen for sustained reading comfort.

### Built With
