## Sonic Nostalgia

Vintage-Radio is about the relationship between broadcast and the digital interface in a nostalgic environment. We believe that sonic content deserves a presentation that captures the warmth of the early radio era. By focusing on dark wood textures, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply immersive. Our focus is on the integrity of the signal.

### Broadcast Principles

- **Warmth**: A design that reflects the rich, organic nature of analog audio.
- **Tactility**: Using dark textures to define the boundaries of the radio surface.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century communication technology.
