## Collaborative Tactility

Cork-Board is about the informal and collaborative nature of innovation. We believe that ideas should be allowed to be pinned, moved, and discussed in a tactile environment. By focusing on cork-inspired textures and a warm, natural palette, we create a design that feels both accessible and deeply functional. Our focus is on the integrity of the board.

### Pinning Principles

- **Accessibility**: A design that encourages participation and exploration.
- **Tactility**: Using textures to define the collaborative workspace.
- **Informality**: A layout that reflects the dynamic nature of brainstorming.
