> "In the depths of the digital abyss, lumina glows shine brightest."

The **Lumina Abyss Forge** is designed to showcase the capabilities of Hwaro while providing a visually striking and modern template for developers and creators.

### Design Philosophy
The design philosophy centers around combining the deep, infinite feeling of a dark void with the vibrant energy of neon colors and the structured, elegant look of frosted glass.

1.  **The Void**: Represents the infinite canvas of the web.
2.  **The Lumina**: Represents ideas and content shining through.
3.  **The Glass**: Provides structure and focus without completely obscuring the background.

```css
/* Example of the glassmorphism effect used in this theme */
```
