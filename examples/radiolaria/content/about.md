This project exists as an exploration of constraints. By completely removing gradients and emojis from the toolkit, the design is forced to rely on raw structural elements:

1.  **Line and Shape:** The primary tools for division and grouping.
2.  **Color Blocking:** Solid fields of color create definitive contrast.
3.  **Typography:** The weight and style of the text bear the burden of expression.

The result is a brutalist yet elegant aesthetic, reminiscent of early blueprints or scientific diagrams of microscopic life.
