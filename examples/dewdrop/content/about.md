## About Dewdrop

Dewdrop is a personal wellness journal dedicated to the quiet hours of early morning. Here you will find reflections on mindful routines, gentle movement, nourishing meals, and the small rituals that shape a balanced life.

This space was born from a simple observation: the way we begin our mornings often sets the tone for everything that follows. By paying attention to those first waking moments -- the light through the curtains, the warmth of a cup of tea, the stillness before the day begins -- we can cultivate a deeper sense of presence.

### What You'll Find Here

- **Morning Routines** -- Practical guides for building sustainable morning habits
- **Wellness Notes** -- Thoughts on sleep, movement, nutrition, and mental clarity
- **Seasonal Living** -- Adapting your self-care practice to the rhythm of the seasons
- **Mindfulness** -- Simple techniques for staying grounded throughout the day

Whether you are an early riser or someone learning to love the morning, you are welcome here. Take what resonates, leave what doesn't, and move gently through your day.
