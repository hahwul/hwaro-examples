## About Hibiscus

Welcome to Hibiscus — a glamorous tropical theme where vivid reds, pinks, and lush botanicals create a bold visual experience. This site celebrates the vibrant energy of island flowers in full bloom.

## Design Philosophy

Hibiscus draws inspiration from the bold beauty of tropical flowers — vivid crimson petals, deep coral gradients, and the lush greens of island foliage. Every element is designed to captivate and delight, balancing maximum visual impact with comfortable readability.

### Key Design Elements

- **Vivid red-to-pink gradients** inspired by hibiscus petals in bloom
- **Warm dark backgrounds** that let tropical colors radiate
- **Animated floating petals** that drift across the page
- **Bold serif typography** for an elegant, magazine-like feel
- **Glowing hover effects** with warm coral and pink accents
- **Rich visual layers** with botanical depth and texture

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and lightweight static site generator written in Crystal. The entire visual design is achieved with pure CSS animations and gradients — no JavaScript frameworks required.
