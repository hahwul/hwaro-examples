Welcome to Hibiscus — where tropical glamour meets modern web design.

## The Inspiration

The hibiscus flower is a symbol of bold beauty. Its vivid crimson and pink petals unfurl with unapologetic confidence, demanding attention against lush green foliage. This theme channels that same energy into every pixel.

## Design Highlights

This theme is built on three principles:

1. **Bold Color** — Vivid reds, hot pinks, and warm corals create an atmosphere of tropical luxury
2. **Dynamic Motion** — Floating petals and smooth transitions bring the page to life
3. **Elegant Typography** — Serif headings paired with clean body text for a magazine-quality reading experience

## What Powers It

Everything you see is pure CSS — gradients, animations, and layered visual effects achieved without any JavaScript framework. The site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), keeping things fast and lightweight.
