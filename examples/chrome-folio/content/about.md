# Studio

Chrome Folio is a two-person design practice working between Seoul and Rotterdam. We take on a small number of projects each year, chosen on fit rather than fee.

## Practice

Our work sits at the intersection of **identity systems**, **industrial product**, and **software interface**. We believe the same principles should hold across all three: a committed palette, a disciplined type system, and the courage to leave a surface flat.

Most of what we ship looks cold at first. That is intentional. A cold surface holds its line under pressure — at a trade show, on a factory floor, inside a daylit office. Warmth is the job of content, not chrome.

## Collaborators

We work with a rotating bench of specialists: a typographer based in Basel, a motion designer in Mexico City, a fabricator in Osaka, and a sound designer who will remain anonymous until the project ships.

## Process

1. **Brief.** Two weeks of interviews, a written document with five non-negotiables and three stretch goals.
2. **System.** Grid, palette, type stack, motion rules. Shipped as a PDF and a live style doc.
3. **Apply.** We design three comprehensive applications before declaring the system complete.
4. **Hand off.** A documented library, a written rationale, and a three-month retainer.

We do not pitch. We interview.
