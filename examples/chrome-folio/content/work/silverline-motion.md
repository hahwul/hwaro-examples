# Silverline Motion

A motion system and title sequence for a four-part documentary on high-speed rail engineering across Europe and East Asia.

## System

We treated each episode as a run down a single rail. Titles slide along a hairline, elements snap to a six-column grid, and nothing eases — everything either moves or stops. The palette is a single silver on a single black.

## Sequence

The opening runs fifty-two seconds and contains a single animated gesture: the wordmark flattens from a perspective view to an orthographic one as the camera passes through it. No music. Only rolling stock.

## Outcome

The documentary premiered at a film festival in Rotterdam. Our title sequence was picked up by a motion design annual later that year.
