# Foundry Mark

An identity, wordmark, and signage system for an independent type foundry moving into a refurbished industrial space. The brief asked for a mark that was legible at the scale of a building facade and at the scale of a specimen sheet.

## System

The wordmark is cut from the foundry's own in-progress display face, preserved at a single weight and locked to a four-value palette of greys. The only accent is a single diagonal slash used to mark the foundry's signature italic.

## Applications

Exterior signage, interior wayfinding, specimen books, web presence, business cards, and a short run of enamel pins in the studio's silver. Everything is produced in-house with the exception of the enamel, which is fabricated in Kanagawa.

## Outcome

The system has held for two years without revision. The foundry's first release under the new identity sold out within a month of launch.
