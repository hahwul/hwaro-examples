# Transit Signal

A realtime departure board for a metropolitan transit authority, installed across seventeen stations and one hundred and forty-two platform displays.

## Constraints

Boards must be legible at distances from one to thirty meters, through glass, at any time of day. Data refreshes every twelve seconds. The system must gracefully degrade when a feed is delayed.

## Response

A single typographic scale, strict hierarchy, and a high-contrast palette. We removed every ornament and every decorative line, then added two — a single rule below the header and a single slash marking a delayed service.

## Rollout

Pilot in three stations, expansion over eighteen months. The monochrome system reduced manufacturing cost per unit by seventeen percent versus the previous three-color specification.
