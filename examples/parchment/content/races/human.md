Humans are the most diverse and widespread of the common races. They are known for their short lives compared to elves or dwarves, which fuels an intense ambition and a drive to leave a lasting legacy.

## Characteristics

*   **Adaptability:** Humans thrive in almost any environment, from frozen tundras to scorching deserts.
*   **Ambition:** Their short lifespans drive them to achieve great things in a relatively small amount of time.
*   **Cultural Diversity:** Each human kingdom has its own unique traditions, languages, and laws.

### The Great Migration

Historians believe that the first human tribes originated from the **Sun-Drenched Plains** before migrating across the Great Sea during the Second Age.

> "To a human, a century is a lifetime; to an elf, it is but a season. Thus, the human builds for tomorrow, while the elf preserves today."
