We believe that design should serve the content, not the other way around.

Aurora Glimmer was crafted as an exercise in reduction—stripping away excessive visual noise until only the essential elements remained. The result is a quiet space for your words.

### Typography

The typographic scale is designed for legibility and rhythm, using system fonts to ensure fast loading times and a native feel across all devices.

### Contact

If you have questions about the design or the Hwaro framework, feel free to reach out via our [repository](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro-examples).
