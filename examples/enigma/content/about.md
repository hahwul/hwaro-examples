# About the Enigma Project

Welcome to the Enigma Machine cryptography training platform. This site is designed to challenge, educate, and inspire those interested in the art and science of code-breaking.

## Our Mission

We recreate the atmosphere of Bletchley Park's Code and Cypher School, where brilliant minds worked tirelessly to crack the seemingly unbreakable Enigma cipher during World War II.

## What You'll Find Here

* **Puzzles:** A growing collection of cryptographic challenges
* **History:** Learn about the real Enigma machine and its impact
* **Tools:** Rotor simulators and cipher analysis utilities
* **Community:** Join fellow code-breakers in solving challenges

## The Technology

Built with Hwaro static site generator, this platform delivers fast, secure puzzle challenges without compromising on the immersive experience of a 1940s decryption room.

## Get Started

Ready to test your skills? Head to the Puzzles section and begin your journey into cryptanalysis.
