We sell one thing — manual source-code review by engineers who have built the kind of systems we are reading — packaged into four engagement shapes. All four include exploit verification and a review call; none of them include a scanner report with our logo on it.

## Four engagement shapes

<div class="service-list">

<div class="service">
<h3>Pre-launch source audit</h3>
<p class="service-scope">A single, bounded engagement before you take on real customer funds: one codebase, one threat model, one report. The standard entry point for a company approaching its first money-movement launch or its first institutional customer.</p>
<dl class="service-facts">
<div><dt>Duration</dt><dd>2–4 weeks</dd></div>
<div><dt>Team</dt><dd>Two auditors</dd></div>
<div><dt>Deliverable</dt><dd>Full written report, review call, one remediation-verification pass</dd></div>
<div><dt>Typical client</dt><dd>Seed to Series B, pre- or at-launch</dd></div>
</dl>
</div>

<div class="service">
<h3>Continuous assurance retainer</h3>
<p class="service-scope">A standing relationship for teams that ship weekly and cannot afford a point-in-time audit to go stale. We review significant diffs to money-movement and authorization code on a rolling basis and hold a quarterly deep read of the full surface.</p>
<dl class="service-facts">
<div><dt>Duration</dt><dd>Ongoing, quarterly renewal</dd></div>
<div><dt>Team</dt><dd>One dedicated auditor, escalation pair on call</dd></div>
<div><dt>Deliverable</dt><dd>Rolling findings log, quarterly report, Slack channel with our engineers</dd></div>
<div><dt>Typical client</dt><dd>Series B and later, post-launch</dd></div>
</dl>
</div>

<div class="service">
<h3>Ledger and settlement deep dive</h3>
<p class="service-scope">A narrow, intensive read of one subsystem — double-entry ledger, settlement batching, reconciliation, or reversal logic — for teams that already have general application security in hand and want an adversarial read of the part that actually holds the money.</p>
<dl class="service-facts">
<div><dt>Duration</dt><dd>3–5 weeks</dd></div>
<div><dt>Team</dt><dd>Two auditors, one with prior ledger-systems background</dd></div>
<div><dt>Deliverable</dt><dd>Findings report plus a written model of every state transition we reviewed</dd></div>
<div><dt>Typical client</dt><dd>Any stage, building or extending a ledger</dd></div>
</dl>
</div>

<div class="service">
<h3>Regulator-ready report package</h3>
<p class="service-scope">For teams heading into a charter application, an acquiring-bank review, or an SOC 2 gap assessment, we produce a report written to be read by a non-engineering reviewer: plain findings, clear severity rationale, and a remediation timeline your compliance team can attach directly to a filing.</p>
<dl class="service-facts">
<div><dt>Duration</dt><dd>4–6 weeks</dd></div>
<div><dt>Team</dt><dd>Two auditors plus a technical writer pass</dd></div>
<div><dt>Deliverable</dt><dd>Engineering report, executive summary, remediation timeline</dd></div>
<div><dt>Typical client</dt><dd>Companies in an active regulatory or partner review</dd></div>
</dl>
</div>

</div>

Every engagement, regardless of shape, follows the same [six-step methodology](/methodology/) and produces findings in the same format you can preview in the [report library](/reports/). If you are not sure which shape fits, tell us what you are building and we will recommend one on the scoping call, at no charge.
