Stride has no dedicated sales staff and no bench of junior consultants running checklists. Every person who touches a client's code has shipped production payments systems, and every engagement is staffed by people who have personally built the kind of thing they are now trying to break.

We stay small on purpose. A six-person firm cannot double-book its best auditor onto three engagements the way a larger practice can, and every client gets the same two senior people from scoping call to remediation verification, not a rotating cast. If you want to know who specifically would work on your engagement before you sign anything, ask on the [scoping call](/contact/) — we will tell you by name.
