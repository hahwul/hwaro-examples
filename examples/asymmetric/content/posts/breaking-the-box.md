+++
title = "Breaking Out of the Box"
date = "2024-02-28"
summary = "Moving beyond the standard container widths and symmetrical column grids."
categories = ["Layout"]
+++

We are conditioned to design within 12-column grids and fixed max-widths. While these are invaluable tools for maintaining order, strict adherence can lead to monotonous websites. 

By strategically breaking elements out of their containers—allowing a border to extend further than the content it wraps, or an image to bleed off the edge of the screen—we introduce movement. It suggests that the canvas extends beyond what is immediately visible, encouraging the user to keep scrolling and exploring.
