## Editorial Authority

Broadsheet-Mag is about the massive and foundational nature of structural innovation within the publishing environment. We believe that high-volume content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic print broadsheet. By focusing on white paper surfaces, multi-column layouts, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the daily edition.

### Broadsheet Principles

- **Authority**: A design that prioritizes the clear "voice" of the publication.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the high-volume space.
- **Narrative**: A layout that reflects the depth and complexity of broadsheet journalism.
