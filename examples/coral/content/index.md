  <h2>The Ocean's Hidden Cities</h2>
  <p>Coral reefs cover less than one percent of the ocean floor, yet they support roughly twenty-five percent of all marine species. These intricate structures, built over millennia by tiny polyps no larger than a pencil eraser, form some of the most complex and beautiful ecosystems on Earth.</p>
</div>

<div class="blob-card">
  <h2>Why Coral Matters</h2>
  <p>Beyond their breathtaking beauty, coral reefs protect coastlines from storm damage, provide livelihoods for millions of people, and serve as nurseries for countless fish species. Understanding and preserving these ecosystems is one of the defining challenges of our time.</p>
</div>
