<span class="section-label">PIT RULES</span>
<h1 class="section-title">DO / DON'T</h1>

<p style="font-family:'Special Elite',monospace;line-height:1.8;margin-top:1.5rem;">This is not a list of rules the venue made up. This is a list the crowd made up over fifteen years of doing this. Respect it.</p>

<ul class="rules-list">
  <li class="good">pick people up when they fall.</li>
  <li class="good">leave your drink off the dance floor. hand it to a friend. hand it to a stranger.</li>
  <li class="good">hydrate. water is free.</li>
  <li class="good">the bouncer is not security. she is a band member filling in. be respectful.</li>
  <li>no headwalking. this is not that kind of show.</li>
  <li>no phones in the pit. you can film from the back if you need to.</li>
  <li>no fighting. the pit is a workout, not a brawl. if you are mad at someone, go outside.</li>
  <li>no creeps. you will be walked out. that is the only rule enforced from the top.</li>
</ul>

<p style="font-family:'Permanent Marker',sans-serif;font-size:1.5rem;color:var(--blood);transform:rotate(-1.5deg);display:inline-block;margin-top:1rem;">THE DOOR HAS FINAL SAY.</p>
