## replacement opener

Black Route's van threw a rod somewhere between Pittsburgh and here. They are stuck. Dry Heat is driving up from the next town over to cover the slot. They will play six songs and leave.

### what they sound like

Screamo with a drum kit that sounds like it is being assaulted instead of played. The vocals are not mixed. They are shouted off-mic into a room. The guitars are tuned low and the bass is unplugged for two of the six songs on purpose.

### the setlist (as of 14:30 today)

1. title withheld
2. the obvious one
3. a cover of a song they refuse to name
4. the new one
5. the other new one
6. whatever they want

### what to do

Get there early. Get close to the front. Do not wait until the third band to warm up. The openers are opening for a reason.
