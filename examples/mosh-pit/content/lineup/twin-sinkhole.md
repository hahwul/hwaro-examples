## the band this show is for

Twin Sinkhole put the show together. They picked the bands. They mailed the flyers. They are the reason the basement is open tonight.

### what they sound like

Hardcore, full stop. Twelve-song sets, 28 minutes, no stage banter. Two guitars tuned to D. Two vocalists who trade off every other verse. A rhythm section that has been playing together since high school and sounds like it.

### the set

The set changes but the first song is always "Boiler" and the last song is always "Wake". In between is anyone's guess. Do not request things. They don't take requests. They have a list.

### the pit during their set

The pit is the thickest during Twin Sinkhole. It is also the most welcoming. The front of the pit is run by people who have been doing this for ten years and know how to catch a stage diver without anyone getting kicked in the teeth. Trust them.

> "no apologies. no sponsors. no breakdowns." &mdash; from their tape liner
