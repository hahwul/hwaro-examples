## the replacement we actually wanted

Rusted Teeth canceled at 11 AM this morning. They said they did not feel like driving. Breakwater said yes at 11:07 AM.

### what they sound like

Powerviolence with a sense of humor. 28 songs in 22 minutes. The set ends when the drummer stands up and walks off, usually mid-song, because he has decided the set is over.

### the setlist approach

There is no setlist. There is a list of every song they have ever written on a piece of butcher paper taped to the kick drum. The guitarist points to the next one. If the room is not into it, the drummer overrules and picks a different one.

### what to do

Move back from the front one person. Protect the shorter people in the crowd. Water is free at the bar tonight. Drink some.

> "a show is not a gig and a gig is not a show" &mdash; breakwater, liner notes to their only 7"
