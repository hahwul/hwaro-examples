## Spooky Action at a Distance

Einstein famously referred to quantum entanglement as "spooky action at a distance." When two particles become entangled, their states are linked. Measuring one instantaneously determines the state of the other, regardless of the distance separating them.

In our symphony, this is the basis of harmony.

### The Visual Chord

We translate this instantaneous connection into our design system through the use of translucent, frosted glass panels.

When a neon accent—like our quantum cyan or resonant magenta—glows behind the glass, the color diffuses. The glass and the light become entangled in a visual chord, creating a softer, more complex hue.

- **Cyan:** The sharp, high frequency of immediate observation.
- **Magenta:** The deep, resonant undertone of hidden variables.
- **Purple:** The harmonic synthesis of the two.

> The beauty of entanglement is that the whole is fundamentally greater than the sum of its parts. You cannot describe the system by describing its individual components.
