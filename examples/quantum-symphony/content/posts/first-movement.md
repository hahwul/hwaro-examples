## The Silence Before the Sound

Before there was light, there was the void. But the void was never truly empty. In the *Quantum Symphony*, the vacuum is a boiling sea of potential, where virtual particles continuously pop into existence and annihilate each other in an eternal, silent dance.

### The Fluctuations

This "zero-point energy" creates a baseline hum, a cosmic static that permeates all of reality. It's the hum of creation waiting for a spark.

```python
def create_particle_pair():
    particle = QuantumEntity(charge=1, spin=0.5)
    antiparticle = QuantumEntity(charge=-1, spin=-0.5)
    return particle, antiparticle

# They exist for a fleeting moment
p, ap = create_particle_pair()
annihilate(p, ap)
```

In our design, this is represented by the deep space void background (`#020108`)—not entirely black, but a profound, resonant dark that hints at the depths within.
