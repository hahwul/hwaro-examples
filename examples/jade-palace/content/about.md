# About Jade Palace

Jade Palace is a theme inspired by the revered jade stone — symbol of purity, nobility, and eternal beauty in East Asian culture. For millennia, jade has been prized above gold, carved into imperial seals and palace ornaments, and regarded as the bridge between heaven and earth.

## Our Philosophy

**Imperial Elegance** — Drawing from palace architecture and court aesthetics, every design element evokes the grandeur of ancient Eastern capitals.

**Translucent Depth** — Like jade itself, the design layers translucent greens over rich dark surfaces, creating a sense of luminous depth that shifts and glows.

**Harmony of Contrasts** — Bold gold accents meet serene jade greens. Dynamic animations coexist with meditative whitespace. The result is a design that commands attention while maintaining inner balance.

## The Stone of Heaven

Jade (*玉*, yù) has been called the "Stone of Heaven" in Chinese culture. Emperors wore jade pendants, scholars kept jade desk ornaments, and the finest jade was valued more than any precious metal.

The stone's characteristic translucency — the way light passes through it, revealing hidden depths — is the central design metaphor of this theme. Layers of semi-transparent color create richness that flat design cannot achieve.
