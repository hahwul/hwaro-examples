# Neo Twilight Glass

Step into the twilight zone. This template features a deep, immersive background with abstract floating orbs and a perspective grid, all overlaid with sleek, frosted glassmorphism containers.

The aesthetic blends cyberpunk neon accents with elegant, minimalist typography to create a unique visual experience.

## Features

- **Immersive Background**: A deep canvas with animated glowing orbs and a moving grid.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: Beautiful, translucent panels with blurred backdrops and subtle borders.
- **Neon Accents**: Vibrant magenta, cyan, purple, and gold highlights.
- **Responsive Design**: Looks great on desktop and mobile.

> "The twilight is a place of transition, where the light of day meets the dark of night."
