## About Magenta Sunset

Welcome to Magenta Sunset — a bold, glamorous theme inspired by the most dramatic moments of twilight. When the sun dips below the horizon, the sky erupts in layers of deep magenta, molten orange, and violet fire. This theme captures that fleeting spectacle.

## Design Philosophy

Magenta Sunset embraces the concept of 화려함 — glamour with maximum visual impact. Every design decision prioritizes boldness, richness, and drama while maintaining readability.

### Key Design Elements

- **Fiery horizon gradients** that sweep from deep magenta through burnt orange to twilight purple
- **Glowing text effects** that radiate warmth like the last rays of sun
- **Dark canvas backgrounds** that evoke the deepening night sky
- **Animated gradient transitions** that shift like a living sunset
- **Bold serif typography** that commands attention with elegance
- **Rich layered visuals** with depth and atmospheric texture

## Built With

This site is powered by [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and lightweight static site generator written in Crystal. The entire visual design is achieved with pure CSS — no JavaScript frameworks required for the effects.
