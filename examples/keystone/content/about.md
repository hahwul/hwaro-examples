# About Keystone ADR

Keystone ADR is the architecture decision record repository for the Keystone platform engineering team. This site is maintained by the Architecture Board and serves as the single source of truth for all significant technical decisions.

## Purpose

Architecture Decision Records help us:

- **Capture context** -- Record why a decision was made, not just what was decided
- **Onboard new engineers** -- Provide historical context for the system's current shape
- **Avoid revisiting settled decisions** -- Unless the original context has materially changed
- **Track superseded decisions** -- Understand the evolution of our architecture over time

## Architecture Board

The Architecture Board meets bi-weekly to review proposed ADRs and assess whether existing decisions remain valid. All engineers are welcome to propose new ADRs or request reviews of existing ones.
