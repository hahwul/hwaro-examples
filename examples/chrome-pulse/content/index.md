# Welcome to Chrome Pulse

A glamorous, trendy design showcasing dynamic metallic surfaces and rhythmic reflections.

This example explores the elegant application of subtle luminescent box-shadows to simulate a high-end chrome aesthetic, completely avoiding gradients or emojis to adhere to a strict design ethos.

## Aesthetic Principles

1. **Metallic Elegance:** Clean lines and subtle shadows.
2. **Rhythmic Reflections:** Glowing hovers and focused elements.
3. **Restrained Typography:** Clear, legible, and unadorned text.
