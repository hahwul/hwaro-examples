The **Neon Prism Forge** is an experimental design space focused on the interplay between deep void backgrounds, translucent glass panels, and vibrant, floating neon geometries.

By applying heavy `backdrop-filter: blur()` properties on dark layers and underlaying them with CSS radial gradients, we create an illusion of depth and light refraction.

## Core Elements

* **Deep Space Background:** A void that lets neon colors pop.
* **Animated Prisms:** Moving light sources that give life to the static glass layers above.
* **Frosted Glass:** UI components that blur and inherit the colors of the elements passing underneath them.
* **Crisp Typography:** Utilizing **Space Grotesk** for high-impact geometric headers and **Inter** for supreme readability.
