The **Luminescent Ripple Forge** is a design experiment aimed at merging futuristic aesthetics with usable, modern web interfaces. By combining dark backgrounds with bright, blurred, moving shapes, we create a sense of depth and energy without overwhelming the content.

### The Stack

Built on [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com), this template demonstrates the flexibility of Crinja (Jinja2) templates and lightweight static generation.

Feel free to break it apart, study the CSS animations, and use the structural glassmorphism patterns in your own Hwaro-powered sites.
