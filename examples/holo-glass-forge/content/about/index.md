# About the Forge

The **Holo Glass Forge** project was born from a desire to combine the slick aesthetic of cyberpunk with the clean, elegant lines of modern UI design.

We leverage the full capabilities of CSS3 with `backdrop-filter`, `radial-gradient`, and fluid animations to create a look that is both striking and readable.

## Design Philosophy

* **Deep Space Contrast:** Starting with a near-black base to make colors pop.
* **Refraction & Diffusion:** Mimicking real-world glass properties with soft blurs and semi-transparent layers.
* **Vivid Accents:** Using precise strokes of cyan (`#00f0ff`) and magenta (`#ff0055`) to draw the eye.
