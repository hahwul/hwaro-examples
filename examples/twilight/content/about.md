## About Twilight

Twilight is a photo essay journal dedicated to the transient beauty found in the spaces between light and dark. Each essay pairs contemplative prose with the atmosphere of a specific time of day, exploring how shifting light transforms landscapes, cities, and quiet moments into something worth remembering.

There is a particular quality to the hours when the world is in transition. Dawn breaks slowly, uncertain. Daylight reveals everything with unforgiving clarity. Dusk softens every edge. Night reduces the world to shapes and silence. These essays live in those transitions.

## Time of Day

Essays are organized by the light they inhabit:

**Dawn** -- The first uncertain glow before sunrise, when streets are empty and the air is still cool.

**Day** -- Full sun, sharp shadows, the honest and sometimes harsh clarity of midday.

**Twilight** -- The golden hour and its aftermath, when the sky holds colors that exist for only minutes.

**Night** -- Darkness, stars, city lights reflected on water, and the particular stillness that comes after the world has gone quiet.

## Colophon

Twilight is built with Hwaro, a lightweight static site generator. The typefaces are Cormorant Garamond for body text and Outfit for interface elements. The color palette is drawn from the sky at dusk: deep purple for the gathering dark, soft peach for the last warmth of the sun, and muted lavender for the space between.
