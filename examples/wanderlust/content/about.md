## About This Journal

Wanderlust is a travel journal for the unhurried traveler. It is a place to slow down, to look closely, and to write about what we find when we stop moving long enough to notice.

This site is not about bucket lists or itineraries. It is about the texture of a morning in a city you have never visited, the weight of an afternoon in a village where nobody speaks your language, and the quiet satisfaction of getting completely, wonderfully lost.

### The Name

*Wanderlust* --- from the German *wandern* (to hike) and *Lust* (desire). A strong longing for or impulse toward wandering. Not tourism, not sightseeing, but the act of moving through the world with open eyes and an open notebook.

### Colophon

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://hwaro.hahwul.com), a fast static site generator written in Crystal. The design uses DM Serif Display and Source Serif 4 for its warm, readable typography, paired with Inter for navigation and metadata.

The layout draws inspiration from print travel magazines --- generous whitespace, strong typographic hierarchy, and an emphasis on letting the writing breathe.
