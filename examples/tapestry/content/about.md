Tapestry is a curated visual timeline that traces the arc of human history through its most defining moments. Each entry is a thread in the larger fabric of civilization, from ancient foundations to modern breakthroughs.

This project draws inspiration from the tradition of illuminated manuscripts and historical chronicles, presenting events in a format that invites exploration and contemplation. The timeline is organized by era, each distinguished by its own color to help readers orient themselves within the sweep of history.

## Eras

- **Ancient** -- The foundations of civilization, from early writing systems to the great empires of antiquity.
- **Medieval** -- The middle ages, a time of feudal order, religious expansion, and cultural exchange.
- **Renaissance** -- The rebirth of classical learning, artistic revolution, and the dawn of modern science.
- **Modern** -- The age of industry, revolution, and the rapid transformation of human society.

## Sources

Events are drawn from widely accepted historical scholarship. Dates follow the conventional Gregorian calendar where applicable. This is not an exhaustive record but a selective tapestry of moments chosen for their lasting significance.
