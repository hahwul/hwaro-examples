The Sun Stone is perhaps the most famous work of Aztec sculpture. The stone is 358 centimetres in diameter and 98 centimetres thick, and it weighs about 24,590 kilograms.

Its geometric precision is astounding, with concentric rings representing cosmic cycles and the face of the sun god at the center.

### Structure

- Central disc: Tonatiuh, the sun god.
- First ring: Four previous eras (suns).
- Second ring: 20 day signs of the calendar.
