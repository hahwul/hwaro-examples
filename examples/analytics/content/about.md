    <h2>Data Collection Policy</h2>
    <p>This analytics dashboard represents aggregated, anonymized user data. We prioritize user privacy and ensure that no personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored within this system.</p>

    <h3>Metrics Tracked</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Page views and unique visitors</li>
      <li>Traffic sources and referrers</li>
      <li>Device types and browser versions</li>
      <li>Session duration and bounce rates</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Data Retention</h3>
    <p>Raw event data is retained for 30 days before being aggregated into hourly and daily summaries. Historical aggregated data is retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis.</p>
