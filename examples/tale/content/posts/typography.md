This post demonstrates the typographic styles available in the Tale theme.

## Headings

The heading styles use a clean sans-serif font for contrast against the serif body text, creating a clear visual hierarchy.

### Third Level

Headings at every level are designed to stand out while maintaining harmony with the surrounding text.

#### Fourth Level

Even smaller headings remain distinct and readable.

## Body Text

The body text uses Libre Baskerville, a web-optimized serif typeface that provides excellent readability on screens. The generous line height of 1.7 ensures comfortable reading even for longer articles.
