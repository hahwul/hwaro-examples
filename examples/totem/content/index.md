<section class="totem-base">
  <h2>The Craft</h2>
  <p>Every totem tells a story. These projects draw from the rich artistic traditions of Pacific Northwest Coast Indigenous peoples -- the Haida, Tlingit, Kwakwaka'wakw, and Coast Salish nations. Each piece honors the formline design system, the reverence for natural materials, and the deep connection between art and the living world.</p>
  <p>The pole stands as a vertical narrative. From base to crown, each figure carries meaning -- clan identity, historical events, spiritual guardians, and the eternal interplay between the human world and the world of spirits.</p>
