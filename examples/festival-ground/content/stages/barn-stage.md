## Footprint

The converted Meadowlark dairy barn, built 1934, retains its original post-and-beam frame. Capacity is hard-capped at 900 by the town fire marshal; bale seating arranged in three concentric rings around a 20 ft thrust stage.

## Backline & Sound

- QSC K12.2 mains flown from the hayloft
- Smaller wedge monitors - no in-ear feeds
- Four pendant incandescent rigs, no smoke machines
- Acoustic piano in stage left alcove

## Show Notes

Two exits (main doors and hayloft ladder) are on the south wall. The east wall is an original fieldstone foundation; please do not lean instruments against it. Doors close when the bales are full; ushers will redirect you to a second-chance overflow in the hayloft via separate staircase.
