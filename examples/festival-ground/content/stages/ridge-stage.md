## Footprint

The Ridge Stage sits with its back to the north treeline, a 42 ft truss span opening south across the slope. The natural 2% grade improves sightlines from 120 ft back and cuts low-frequency bleed toward the campgrounds.

## Backline & Sound

- FOH at 150 ft - raised 3 ft on scaffold
- L-Acoustics K3 mains, 4 hangs per side
- Subs flown in cardioid array, 8 KS28 per side
- SPL cap 103 dB(A) at FOH per county ordinance

## Show Notes

Nightly fireworks are launched from the plateau behind the stage between 22:45 and 23:00. A 150 ft clearance is enforced around the mortar rack. Do not enter the service road north of the barrier fence during that window.
