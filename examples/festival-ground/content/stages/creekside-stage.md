## Footprint

A rectangular open-sided pavilion built on a floating platform 6 ft above the Fawn Creek flood plain. The dance floor is exposed pine boards laid on rubber shock mounts; the DJ booth faces upstream.

## Backline & Sound

- Funktion-One F-218 mains with F-1201 tops
- Dedicated subwoofer ring, 4 corner stacks
- Strobes permitted; green lasers under 15 mW only
- All power routed through GFCI with creek-side monitoring

## Access

| Route | Surface | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| T3 - From Grove | Mowed field | 14 min |
| Service loop from Gate B | Paved | 6 min |
| Camp C direct trail | Boardwalk | 4 min |

## Show Notes

Sets run from 23:00 to 02:00 Friday and Saturday. The stage is cleared and winched to its storm position if Fawn Creek crests above the amber gauge. Swimming in the creek is prohibited during festival hours; water safety crew patrols until 03:00.
