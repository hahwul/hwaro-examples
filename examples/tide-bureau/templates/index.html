Warnings & Bulletins

Tabula III · Monitiones
Bar Crossing AdvisoryNo. 041

Poole · 04 May

The Training Bank has shoaled to 0.18 m below datum at low water. Vessels drawing more than 1.4 m are to delay outbound passage to within ninety minutes of high water.

Issued 04:00 GMT
Gale NoticeNo. 042

Lerwick Sound · 05 May

South-westerly winds Force 7, veering Force 8 by the second watch. Small craft to remain in moorings. The keeper of Bressay reports breaking seas across the outer reef.

Issued 18:00 GMT
Datum CorrectionNo. 043

Cobh Quay · 06 May

Following resurvey of the Spit Bank, the chart datum at Cobh is to be lowered by 0.04 m, effective the second of June. All printed tables of subsequent date carry the amendment.

Issued 12:00 GMT
