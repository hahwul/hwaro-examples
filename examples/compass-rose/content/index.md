## Navigational Tactility

Compass-Rose is about the relationship between direction and the digital interface in a navigational environment. We believe that path-finding content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the early exploration era. By focusing on parchment-inspired textures, high-contrast shadows, and elegant, classical typography, we create an environment that feels both adventurous and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the path.

### Navigational Principles

- **Direction**: A design that prioritizes the clear orientation of the user.
- **Tactility**: Using textures to define the boundaries of the navigation space.
- **Adventurousness**: A layout that evokes the feeling of discovery and exploration.
