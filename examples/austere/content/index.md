## Structural Necessity

Austere is about the removal of everything that is not required. We believe that true innovation is found in the bare minimum that a system needs to function. Our focus is on the honesty of the material and the integrity of the structure.

### Functional Innovation

1. **Purpose**: Every element is defined by its function.
2. **Honesty**: No attempt is made to hide the underlying mechanics.
3. **Durability**: A design built to last through the removal of the fleeting.
