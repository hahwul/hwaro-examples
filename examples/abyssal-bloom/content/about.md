## Concept & Design

**Abyssal Bloom** is an experimental design language that merges organic and digital aesthetics. It asks the question: *What if the deep ocean had a cyberpunk district?*

### Key Components

1. **The Void:** The fundamental layer is a deep, almost black expanse. This provides infinite contrast.
2. **The Flora:** Glowing, animated orbs represent bioluminescent blooms scattered through the void.
3. **The Glass:** The structural elements of the UI. Frosted, translucent, and reflective, they catch the light from the blooms below.

> "The deep sea is not empty; it is filled with light of its own making."
