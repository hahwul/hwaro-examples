Welcome to **Abyssal Bloom**, a dark-themed UI showcasing the power of glassmorphism combined with ethereal, glowing gradients.

{{ alert(type="Notice", message="This is a demonstration of Hwaro's rendering capabilities with complex CSS and custom templates.") }}

### The Aesthetic
Inspired by the bioluminescence of deep-sea creatures and the neon glow of cyberpunk cities, this template features:
- **Dark Void Backgrounds:** Deep colors (`#05020a`) to make glowing elements pop.
- **Animated Orbs:** Smoothly drifting background elements that provide dynamic lighting to the frosted glass panels.
- **Glassmorphism:** Translucent panels using `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)` to create a sense of depth and hierarchy.
- **Neon Accents:** Vibrant cyan (`#00f0ff`) and pink (`#ff007f`) highlights to draw attention and guide the user.

Explore the [About](/about/) page to see more details and standard markdown rendering within these glass panels.
