# Elegance in the Night Sky

Welcome to a visual exploration of dynamic bursts and sparkling trails. This theme embraces the dramatic interplay between deep darkness and brilliant luminescence. Let the elegant trails of gold, silver, and bronze guide your narrative.

## Features

- **Particle Explosions:** Dynamic bursts that light up the page without overwhelming the content.
- **Sparkling Trails:** Soft glowing effects achieved through sophisticated styling, completely devoid of gradients.
- **Night Sky Drama:** A deep, atmospheric backdrop that enhances readability and visual impact.
