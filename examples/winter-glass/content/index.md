## Frozen Translucency

Winter-Glass is about the power of stillness and the clarity of the cold. We believe that innovation thrives in the quiet, frozen moments where the world slows down and the underlying structure becomes visible through the frost. By using light, blue-grey translucent elements and delicate serif typography, we create a design that feels both tranquil and deeply permanent.

### The Beauty of the Cold

- **Stillness**: A design that encourages deep contemplation.
- **Frost**: Using light and translucency to create a sense of delicate protection.
- **Clarity**: Finding the core meaning through the crisp, clear environment.
