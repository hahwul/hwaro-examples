What the mesh handles for you

Automatic mTLS

Every sidecar requests a short-lived certificate from the mesh's identity authority on boot. Connections between services are encrypted and mutually authenticated before the first request lands — no operator ever handles a key.

Weighted traffic splits

Route a percentage of live traffic to a new revision, watch the golden metrics, and shift the weight in one line. Splits are enforced at the sidecar, so rollback is a config change, not a redeploy.

Zero-config sidecars

Label a namespace and every pod scheduled into it gets a sidecar proxy injected at admission, wired to the mesh with no annotations required. Opting a workload out is one label, not a migration.
