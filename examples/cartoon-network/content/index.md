## Animated Tactility

Cartoon-Network is about the vibrant and narrative nature of innovation within the broadcast environment. We believe that animated content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a classic television network. By focusing on high-contrast black borders, solid shadows, and bold display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the episode.

### Network Principles

- **Broadcast**: A design that prioritizes the clear "show" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and flat colors to define the boundaries of the animated space.
- **Energy**: A layout that evokes the feeling of late-20th-century cable television.
