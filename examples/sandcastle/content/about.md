## About Me

Hello! I'm a writer and developer who believes in the power of simple, readable design. This blog is a space for sharing thoughts on technology, design, and the craft of writing.

## About This Site

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) using the **Paper** theme, inspired by [Hugo Paper](https://github.com/nanxiaobei/hugo-paper).

Paper emphasizes warmth and readability:

- **Warm color palette** — soft cream backgrounds and earthy tones
- **Serif typography** — Lora for body text, Inter for UI elements
- **Print-like feel** — generous whitespace, focused reading experience
- **Minimal distractions** — no dark mode toggle, no flashy animations
- **RSS feed** — subscribe and read your way
- **Search** — find articles with client-side search
- **SEO optimized** — sitemap, OpenGraph, and Twitter cards
