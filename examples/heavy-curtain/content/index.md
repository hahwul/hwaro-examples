<div>
  <h2>Two Masks, Three Acts, One House</h2>
  <p>Priska Adelmann's fourth stage work returns her to the chamber style of her early career: a small cast, a single proscenium set, and a three-act structure built around a single doorway that opens only once in the final scene.</p>
  <blockquote>"The lava is already in the garden. The only door we have not opened is the one we must open before morning."</blockquote>
  <p>A principal stage production features full orchestral underscoring by the Grand Monarch Chamber Ensemble, conducted from the pit by Ansel Hobermann. Period costume design by the Vaquero atelier. Set, sound, and lighting engineered in house.</p>
</div>
</section>
