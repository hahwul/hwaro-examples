## About Me

I am a game developer and interactive experience designer focused on real-time rendering, shader programming, and immersive cockpit interfaces. This portfolio showcases projects built at the intersection of gaming technology and visual performance.

## About This Site

This site is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) using the **Warpzone** theme, a dark portfolio design inspired by hyperspace tunnel effects.

Warpzone emphasizes speed and immersion:

- **Warp Blue and Hyperspace Purple palette** -- deep space color language
- **Star streak background** -- converging to a central vanishing point
- **Perspective tunnel effects** -- translateZ depth for spatial immersion
- **Orbitron display font** -- angular, futuristic heading typography
- **Dark cockpit aesthetic** -- minimal chrome, maximum focus
- **Search** -- scan the warp field with client-side search
- **SEO optimized** -- sitemap, OpenGraph, and Twitter cards
