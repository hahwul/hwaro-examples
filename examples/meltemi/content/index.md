The plan comes from a photo of a whiteboard. Six columns for six running
days, a rest day left out on purpose, and a running total in the corner
that Coach Runa circles twice before she sends it. Every Monday that photo
lands in my inbox, and by Sunday night it has become a week of this log —
splits, heat, the wind starting up off the strait, and whatever my legs
had to say about the whole arrangement.

I am not fast. I am consistent, which over thirty-some weeks of building
toward Myrtali turns out to matter a great deal more.
