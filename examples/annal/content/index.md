Commonplace keeps every note as a plain file on your disk, links every idea
by title, and draws the backlinks nobody remembers to write down. Annal is
the ledger of what's shipped, folio by folio, since the first release.

Nothing here is rewritten after the fact. A folio, once posted, stands —
corrections arrive as a new entry, never a silent edit to an old one. That's
the whole editorial policy, and it's also roughly the point of the app this
ledger is about.
