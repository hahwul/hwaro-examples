## The Raw Truth

Visceral is an exploration of the deeper, often overlooked elements of the creative process. We look past the polished surface to find the heartbeat within the structure.

> "True innovation is felt long before it is understood."

2. **Bone**: The structural necessity that remains after the excess is stripped away.

- **Unfiltered Expression**: The courage to show the work in its most honest form.
- **Primal Drive**: The underlying force that demands to be heard.
