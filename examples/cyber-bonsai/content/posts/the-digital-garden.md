The concept of a digital garden isn't new, but in the era of transient streams and ephemeral content, it is more necessary than ever.

We must build spaces that are:
1. **Enduring:** Resistant to the decay of link rot.
2. **Interconnected:** Nodes linking to nodes, forming a resilient mycelial network.
3. **Personal:** Unbound by the algorithms of the feed.

This `Cyber Bonsai` is a small attempt at such a space. A quiet corner on a loud server.
