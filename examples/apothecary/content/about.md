## About The Apothecary

The Apothecary is a wellness journal built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). It draws from the tradition of herbalists and apothecaries who catalogued nature's remedies with care and precision.

## What You'll Find

- **Herbal Remedies** -- preparations, tinctures, and formulas organized as recipe cards with ingredients, benefits, and dosage
- **Botanical Monographs** -- profiles of medicinal plants, their properties, and how to grow them
- **Wellness Journal** -- seasonal health practices, dietary notes, and holistic living

## Design Philosophy

The theme takes its cues from old apothecary labels and herbalist manuscripts: understated typography, natural earth tones of sage green and lavender, and a clean card-based layout that echoes the filing drawers of a traditional dispensary. Cormorant Garamond provides the editorial weight, EB Garamond brings warmth to body text, and Lato keeps labels sharp and legible.
