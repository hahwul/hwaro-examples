Welcome to the **Neon Abyss Prism**, an immersive exploration of glassmorphism within the deep cosmic void.

This environment utilizes a stark dark background illuminated by floating prismatic gradients in cyan, magenta, and deep violet. As you traverse through the pages, observe the subtle blur and translucency of the frosted panels reacting to the dynamic lights beneath.

### Core Principles

*   **Deep Space Darkness**: Grounding the composition in #03010A.
*   **Vibrant Prisms**: Concentrated bursts of neon colors creating depth.
*   **Tactile Glass**: Interfaces that feel tangible, frosted, and slightly reflective.

Enjoy your descent into the luminous depths.
