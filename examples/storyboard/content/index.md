## Iterative Storytelling

Storyboard is about the process of development. We don't believe in perfect final states; we believe in the sequence of decisions that leads to the outcome. By breaking the content into panels, we reveal the structural logic of the project and the way it evolves over time.

### Structural Development

1. **Flow**: The way the viewer moves through the sequence.
2. **Transition**: The relationship between individual panels.
3. **Clarity**: Ensuring each frame contributes to the whole.

---

> "The process is the story. The outcome is just the last page."
