# Structural Foundation

Welcome to the Zenith Monolith. This framework is designed with strict adherence to architectural principles, utilizing absolute solid colors and pure typographic layout.

We explicitly reject the use of gradients and emojis to maintain clinical structural purity.

## Core Directives

1. **Absolute Contrast**: Black and white, augmented by a single solid accent color.
2. **Typographic Grid**: Layouts driven by grid systems and monospaced proportions.
3. **Purity of Form**: No extraneous visual decorations.
