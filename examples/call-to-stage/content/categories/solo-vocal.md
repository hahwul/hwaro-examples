## Brief

A single vocalist with optional backing track. The judging panel is listening for tone, intonation, stagecraft, and the emotional pivot a ninety-second refrain can or cannot sustain.

## Stage Setup

- Shure SM58 on a round-base stand, stage left of center.
- Wedge monitor down-front; you will not hear yourself from the back of the room.
- No strobes, no smoke, no fog.

## Backing Track Rules

| Rule | Note |
|------|------|
| Format | WAV or MP3 |
| Length | 120 seconds maximum |
| Filename | FIRSTNAME-LASTNAME.wav |
| Stems | One or two; no multitrack sessions |
| Submission | USB handed to audio tech at check-in |

## What the Panel Rewards

- Choosing a song you can sing every take, not the song you sang best once.
- Using the full stage width, not the microphone as an anchor.
- Ending on time; the panel does not grant extensions.

## What the Panel Cuts

- Tracks louder than the voice.
- Key changes attempted that were never rehearsed.
- Anything over 95 seconds on a cold mic.
