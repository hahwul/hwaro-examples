## Brief

Poetry, monologue, or short narrative. Memorised or read from a printed page. The panel scores on voice, pacing, stagecraft, and whether the piece is shaped for the ear and not only for the page.

## Stage Setup

- Stand mic at the front of the thrust.
- Lectern available on request at check-in.
- House lights up 30%; you will see your audience.

## Performance Style

You may use:

- A printed script, on a clipboard or music stand.
- A memorised piece performed cold.
- A piece with one short silence (under 5 seconds) built into it.

You may not:

- Work from a phone or tablet.
- Use an earpiece or prompter.
- Ask the panel to repeat a line if you lose your place.

## Content Guidance

Heavy subject matter is welcome. Flag anything that involves graphic self-harm in your registration so we can line up a counsellor if needed; the line is for you, not against you.
