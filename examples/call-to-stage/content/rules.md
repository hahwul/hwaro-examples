<section class="categories-block">
<h2><small>COMPETITION RULES</small>What the Panel Enforces</h2>
<div class="category-cards">

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 01</p>
<h3>Time is Time</h3>
<p>Ninety seconds. The panel will end your slot at 91 seconds. No warning bell; watch the stage clock.</p>
</article>

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 02</p>
<h3>One Category</h3>
<p>You may enter one category. If you submit to two we will default to the first received and discard the second.</p>
</article>

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 03</p>
<h3>No Backup Act</h3>
<p>If your prop fails or your track does not load, you have the remaining seconds without it. No second chance slot.</p>
</article>

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 04</p>
<h3>Panel is Final</h3>
<p>The five panellists score independently. The result is the median of five. There is no appeal.</p>
</article>

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 05</p>
<h3>One Prop, Declared</h3>
<p>Bring one prop under 30 cm, declared at registration. Unlisted props stay in the dressing room.</p>
</article>

<article class="category-card">
<p class="category-code">RULE 06</p>
<h3>Safe Subject Matter</h3>
<p>No targeting of protected classes as the punchline or subject. No incitement. The panel will cut and we will tell you why.</p>
</article>

</div>
</section>
