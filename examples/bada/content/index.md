Bada is the Korean word for sea. This is a log of getting into one — the same grey northern sound, all twelve months, with a thermometer on a wrist lanyard and no ambition beyond coming out slightly better company than I went in. The round badge beside each date is the water temperature at entry: the darker the blue, the colder the sea. Start with [the full log](swims/), or read [how this record is kept](about/).
