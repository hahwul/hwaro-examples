The fastest way to get a real answer is email — we read every message ourselves and route it to whoever owns that part of the product.

## Sales

Considering Growth or Scale, or want a walkthrough with your own billing data connected before you commit? Write to **sales@flywheelanalytics.com** with your current MRR and billing provider, and we'll set up a 20-minute call within two business days.

## Support

Already a customer with a question about a workflow, a webhook that didn't fire, or a number that doesn't look right? Write to **support@flywheelanalytics.com**. Growth and Scale customers hear back within one business day; Starter customers can also reach us through the community forum linked in the app.

## Press and partnerships

For press inquiries or integration partnerships, write to **hello@flywheelanalytics.com** and mention what you're working on — we're a small team, so a specific ask gets a faster reply than a general pitch.
