## How trials work

Every plan, including Scale, starts with a fourteen-day trial connected to your real billing provider — we don't do sample data, because the whole point of Flywheel is showing you your actual leak. [Connect a billing provider](/integrations/) and cancel before day fourteen and nothing is charged; recovered revenue during the trial is yours to keep regardless.

## Frequently asked

**What counts as tracked MRR?** The sum of active subscription revenue Flywheel ingests from your billing provider each month, measured at the start of the billing cycle.

**Can I change plans later?** Yes, at any time, from the billing settings inside the app. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next cycle; upgrades take effect immediately so a workflow never stops mid-recovery.

**Do you take a cut of recovered revenue?** No. Flywheel is a flat monthly fee regardless of how much you recover — we don't want an incentive to keep a leak open longer than it needs to be.
