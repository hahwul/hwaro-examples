# The Vision

Polyglass Aurora represents a fusion of modern web design trends: the atmospheric depth of dark mode, the energetic vibrancy of glowing neon colors, and the sophisticated layered feel of glassmorphism.

## Design Philosophy

The intent was to create an environment that feels both deep and illuminating. By anchoring the site with a very dark background (`#070914`), the cyan, magenta, and purple aurora elements are able to pop out vividly, while the translucent glass panels (`rgba(20, 22, 35, 0.5)`) sit elegantly on top, allowing the background animations to shift beautifully underneath.

### Key Elements

1. **Lightweight**: The animations rely purely on optimized CSS keyframes.
2. **Accessible**: High contrast text (`#f0f0f5`) on dark panels ensures readability.
3. **Responsive**: The layout and aurora scale seamlessly across different viewport sizes.
