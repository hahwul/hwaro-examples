## Intentional Conflict

Dissonance is not a mistake; it is a choice. We find innovation in the notes that don't quite fit, the lines that don't quite align, and the structures that challenge our sense of balance. By introducing intentional tension, we create a design that demands engagement.

### The "Off" Note

In music, dissonance creates the need for resolution. In design, it creates the need for exploration. We focus on the gaps between the expected and the reality of the work.

- **Asymmetry**: A balance found through irregular placement.
- **Tension**: The visual energy generated by conflicting elements.
- **Resolution**: The moment where the whole structure reveals its logic.

---

> "Perfect harmony is static. Dissonance is where the movement happens."
