We have finalized the schedule for the day and wanted to share the timing so guests can plan travel and arrival.

## Arrival

Doors at the chapel open at 3:30 PM. We ask that guests be seated by 4:00 PM. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:15 PM and run approximately thirty minutes. A short receiving line follows on the chapel terrace.

## Cocktail Hour

From 5:00 to 6:00 PM, please join us in the garden pavilion for sparkling wine, light hors d'oeuvres, and string quartet music. This is a good window for portraits with the wedding party, so we appreciate your patience while photography is in progress.

## Reception

The reception hall opens at 6:00 PM with assigned seating. Dinner begins at 6:30 PM and includes three courses with vegetarian and gluten-free options available. Toasts and the first dance follow at 8:00 PM, with dancing through 11:00 PM. Shuttle service to the hotel block runs every thirty minutes from 9:30 PM onward.

## Attire and Weather

The dress code is formal. The ceremony is indoors, but cocktail hour takes place outside under cover. Average September temperatures in the area run from 18 to 24 degrees Celsius, with a chance of light rain. A wrap or light jacket is recommended for the evening.

If you have any conflicts with this timing or need accessibility accommodations, please contact us by August 15th so we can make adjustments where possible.
