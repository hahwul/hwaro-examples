Several guests have asked about transportation between the hotel block, the chapel, and the reception hall. Here is the plan we have arranged.

## Shuttle Service

A complimentary shuttle will operate between the Loveville Hotel and both venues. The first pickup at the hotel is at 3:00 PM, with subsequent runs every twenty minutes until 4:00 PM. After the ceremony, the shuttle continues to the reception hall directly. Return trips to the hotel begin at 9:30 PM and run every thirty minutes through 11:30 PM.

The shuttles are wheelchair accessible. If you require this accommodation, please let us know in advance so the operator can stage the appropriate vehicle on your route.

## Self-Parking

The chapel has a small lot with approximately forty spaces. We recommend the shuttle for guests staying at the hotel block, since chapel parking will be reserved primarily for the wedding party, immediate family, and guests traveling from outside the area.

The reception hall has ample parking on site, including a marked accessible row near the main entrance. Valet service runs from 5:30 PM to midnight at no cost to guests.

## Rideshare

Both venues are within standard rideshare zones. The reception hall has a designated pickup point at the south entrance to keep the main drive clear for the shuttle.

## Out-of-Town Guests

If you are flying in, the regional airport is forty minutes from the hotel. We can coordinate group rides for guests arriving on similar schedules; please share your flight information with us so we can connect travelers heading the same direction.

For any questions about logistics or to request an alternate arrangement, contact our day-of coordinator at the number we shared in the welcome packet.
