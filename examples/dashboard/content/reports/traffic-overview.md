## Traffic Overview -- March 2025

Total sessions increased by **12.5%** compared to February, driven primarily by organic search growth following the v2.0 launch blog post.

### Key Findings

- Organic search traffic grew 24% month-over-month
- Mobile sessions now account for 58% of total traffic
- Average page depth increased from 2.1 to 2.8 pages per session
- Bounce rate decreased across all top landing pages
