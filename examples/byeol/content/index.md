The fisher families of the Darae Archipelago drew their own sky: a heron made of salt, an ox that walks the tides, a gate with nine thresholds. Of the forty-one asterisms the astronomer Mun Se-jin collected between 1873 and 1902, seven plates survive — reproduced here exactly as drawn, hairline strokes joining graded points of light. Begin with [the Ninth Gate](@/charts/ninth-gate.md), or read [about the folio](@/about.md).
