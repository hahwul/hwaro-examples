The wait is over. Hellfire Championship 2026 officially opens registration today, and the prize pool has reached an all-time high of $250,000 USD.

## Tournament Details

**Format:** Double elimination bracket  
**Teams:** 128 clans maximum  
**Entry Fee:** Free for Inferno members, $25 for external teams  
**Prize Pool:** $250,000 total  

### Distribution

- 1st Place: $120,000
- 2nd Place: $60,000
- 3rd–4th Place: $25,000 each
- 5th–8th Place: $5,000 each

## Schedule

Registration closes **April 15, 2026**. The group stage begins **May 1, 2026** and runs through June. The Grand Final takes place **June 28, 2026** at the Ashgate Arena — live streamed globally.

## What to Expect

Last year's Championship drew over 800,000 concurrent viewers at peak. This year, with a new format and expanded prize structure, analysts are projecting over 1.2 million. Sponsors include three major gaming peripheral brands, two energy drink companies, and a GPU manufacturer.

## How to Register

Log in to your Inferno account, navigate to the Tournaments section, and select Hellfire Championship 2026. You will need a minimum clan size of five players with at least 200 ranked matches played across the team.

Do not wait. Clan slots filled within 48 hours last year. The fire favors the prepared.
