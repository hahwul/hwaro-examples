This is a unique Hwaro example theme showcasing a deep dark glassmorphism design.

It utilizes a dark background `#050814` with glowing bioluminescent animated orb gradients and translucent frosted glass panels using `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`.

The typography uses the sleek **Space Grotesk** and **Inter** fonts.
