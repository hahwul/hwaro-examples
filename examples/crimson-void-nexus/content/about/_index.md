The **Crimson Void Nexus** is an experiment in dark, focused interfaces. The site has been refined across three releases since the first prototype shipped in late 2025.

## Origins

The aesthetic started as a one-night sketch for a hardware launch. The launch never happened, but the panel system survived and was rebuilt as a general-purpose theme during the first quarter of 2026.

## What Sits Inside

The theme ships with a single layout, a small set of typography presets, and a tuned dark palette. There are no carousel widgets, no pop-ups, and no third-party fonts loaded at runtime. The crimson accent is the only color decision the visitor will register.

## Where It Goes

Crimson Void Nexus is intended for personal sites, small portfolios, and announcement pages. Anything that benefits from a single screen of focused attention, rather than a long scrolling marquee, is a good fit. Future updates will keep adding small, optional pieces without expanding the core layout.
