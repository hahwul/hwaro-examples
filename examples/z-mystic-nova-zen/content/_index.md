# Embrace the Calm

Welcome to a sanctuary of digital peace. This design focuses on clarity, typography, and empty space to let your content breathe.

## Features

- **Serene Typography:** Carefully selected font stacks for maximum readability.
- **Subtle Accents:** Soft, earthy tones that guide the eye without overwhelming it.
- **Minimalist Structure:** Everything you need, nothing you don't.
