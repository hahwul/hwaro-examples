## Communication Tactility

Rotary-Phone is about the relationship between connection and the digital interface in an analog environment. We believe that communication content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the early telephony era. By focusing on dark panels, high-contrast shadows, and bold monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply nostalgic. Our focus is on the integrity of the connection.

### Communication Principles

- **Connection**: A design that prioritizes the clear flow of information between parties.
- **Tactility**: Using dark textures to define the boundaries of the communication space.
- **Nostalgia**: A layout that evokes the feeling of 20th-century analog technology.
