The Courant runs one desk, six critics, and a house rule: if a record does not earn the ink, it does not run. We file every Thursday, after the promo dust has settled and before the weekend's first bad opinion gets typed. Scores are out of ten, printed loud, and never negotiated after the fact. New to the sheet? Read [how the desk works](about/) and how a record actually earns a number.
