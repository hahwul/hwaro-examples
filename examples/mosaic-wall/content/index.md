## Mosaic Structure

Mosaic-Wall is about the organized and foundational nature of structural innovation within the dense environment. We believe that high-density content deserves a presentation that captures the authority of a classic tile mosaic. By focusing on white surfaces, bold black borders, and clear hierarchies, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply clear. Our focus is on the integrity of the tile layout.

### Wall Principles

- **Order**: A design that prioritizes the clear arrangement of visual units.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and high contrast to define the boundaries of the dense space.
- **Clarity**: A layout that reflects the logical nature of structural storytelling.
