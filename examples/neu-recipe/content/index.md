## Culinary Tactility

Neu-Recipe is about the physical and sensory nature of innovation in the kitchen. We believe that recipe presentation should be as inviting as the food itself. By focusing on neumorphic-inspired soft shadows and elegant, classical typography, we create an environment where the content feels both substantial and deeply refined. Our focus is on the warmth and integrity of the presentation.

### Savoring Principles

- **Warmth**: A palette that encourages a sense of comfort and home.
- **Depth**: Using subtle shadows to define the boundaries of the ingredients.
- **Elegance**: A design that respects the quality of the artisanal work.
