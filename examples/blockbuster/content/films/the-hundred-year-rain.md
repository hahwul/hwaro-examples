## Premiere

Egyptian Theatre, Hollywood, Saturday 09 May, 19:00. Director in attendance. Following the Cannes Un Certain Regard screening on 17 May.

## Synopsis

A flooded Kerala town in 1924, a notebook no one can read, and a young bride who will not leave until she has finished translating the last page of her grandmother&rsquo;s marriage diary. The picture spans the three days of the flood and the ninety-four years that have passed since &mdash; and does so in a single, unhurried chronology.
