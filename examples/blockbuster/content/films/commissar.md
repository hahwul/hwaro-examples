## Premiere

Academy Museum, Los Angeles, Saturday 23 May, 20:00. Closing-night picture of the museum&rsquo;s spring programme. Invitation only.

## Synopsis

A commissar&rsquo;s wife, a field hospital in the Carpathians in the autumn of 1944, and an interrogation that reverses halfway through. Nominated for the directors&rsquo; prize at the Athens Festival on the strength of a four-minute assembly cut that was leaked, to the director&rsquo;s reported annoyance, in February.

## The reversal

At the approximate midpoint of the picture &mdash; at the end of the fifth of nine reels, if you are counting &mdash; the interrogation reverses. The interrogator becomes the subject; the line of questioning is unchanged. The picture does not flag the reversal, and early press screenings have reported that roughly one viewer in five fails to notice it on first viewing.
