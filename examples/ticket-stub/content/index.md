## Event Tactility

Ticket-Stub is about the ephemeral and commemorative nature of innovation through the lens of an event ticket. We believe that temporary or significant moments deserve a presentation that captures the tactility of the analog era. By focusing on yellowed paper textures, dashed boundaries, and bold monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply nostalgic. Our focus is on the integrity of the event.

### Event Principles

- **Exclusivity**: A design that prioritizes the clear "admit one" nature of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using paper textures to define the boundaries of the event space.
- **Nostalgia**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical ticket collection.
