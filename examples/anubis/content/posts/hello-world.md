Welcome. This is the first post on a site that values simplicity above all else.

## Why Minimal?

The web is noisy. Most sites load megabytes of JavaScript, track your every click, and bury content under layers of decoration. This site does none of that.

- No JavaScript
- No analytics
- No cookie banners
- No web fonts

Just HTML and CSS. The way it should be.

## Why Hwaro?

[Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) is a fast static site generator written in Crystal. It builds pages in milliseconds and stays out of your way. Pair it with a theme like Anubis and you get a site that loads instantly, works everywhere, and requires zero maintenance.

That's the goal: write, publish, done.
