Welcome to **Astral Symphony Glass**, a digital portfolio exploring the convergence of deep space aesthetics, glowing neon orbs, and refined glassmorphism interfaces.

This template is meticulously crafted to showcase content within translucent panels, where subtle lighting interacts with frosted glass surfaces. We utilize a combination of CSS `backdrop-filter`, radial gradients, and fluid animations to evoke a sense of deep cosmic presence.

### The Elements

1. **Frosted Glass:** Translucent content panels (`.glass-panel`) provide structure without completely obscuring the void behind them.
2. **Neon Orbs:** Ethereal, floating colored light sources (`--cyan`, `--magenta`, `--purple`) drift continuously in the background, shifting the mood and illuminating the glass edges.
3. **Typography:** We pair **Space Grotesk** for prominent, architectural headings and **Inter** for clean, readable body text.

> "A symphony of light, shadow, and refraction across the digital cosmos."

### Example Code Block

Here is a quick look at the CSS powering our glass panels:

```css
.glass-panel {
  background: rgba(15, 10, 30, 0.4);
  backdrop-filter: blur(16px);
  -webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(16px);
  border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08);
  border-radius: 16px;
  box-shadow: 0 8px 32px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3);
}
```
