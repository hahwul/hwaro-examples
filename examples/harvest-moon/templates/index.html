§ 02The Practice

We are a small house. We cut what we can sew by name, and we sell what we can post by hand. The collection is small because the year is short, and because every garment leaves with a letter from the person who finished it.

The moon turns. The harvest comes in. We make for that hour — for the slow walk home, the late supper, the long winter that follows. Everything else can wait until spring.

— Jin-ho & Marin, Founders
