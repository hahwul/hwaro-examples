## The Initiative

The Chrono Odyssey was established to map the complex web of interconnected timelines and prevent catastrophic paradox events. We employ a blend of retro-mechanical engineering and advanced quantum theory to navigate the chronosphere safely.

### Technology

Our primary vessels utilize **Aether-Bronze** plating and **Cyan-Plasma** propulsion, allowing for smooth transition through dimensional barriers.
