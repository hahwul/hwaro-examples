## The Origins

The **Chromatic Singularity** was born from the collision of two opposing forces: absolute darkness and infinite light.

It represents the ultimate aesthetic state, where glass-like structures float in a cosmic void, illuminated by radiation from an accretion disk of pure, vibrant colors.

- **Neon Magenta** `#ff007f`
- **Cyan Resonance** `#00f0ff`
- **Void Purple** `#5a00ff`

Join us as we explore the edge of the visual universe.
