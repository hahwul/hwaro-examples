## Angular Translucency

Prism-Glass is about the way innovation divides and reveals the different spectrums of light. We believe that clarity is achieved through the intentional use of angles and translucency. By focusing on the bold glassmorphic aesthetic and the integrity of the angular structure, we create a design that feels both energetic and deeply ordered.

### Prism Innovation

- **Angles**: The intentional use of geometry to define the flow of information.
- **Spectrum**: Revealing the different facets of a single, unified idea.
- **Division**: Breaking down complex systems into their essential components.
