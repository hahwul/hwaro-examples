# The Concept

Stellar Aura Glass was forged in the cosmic void to bring an elegant, modern, and immersive experience to Hwaro sites.

## Typography & Colors

It uses the `Inter` font family (or system sans-serif fallbacks) for clean legibility against the dark, translucent elements. Primary colors are neon cyan (`#00e5ff`) and magenta (`#d500f9`).
