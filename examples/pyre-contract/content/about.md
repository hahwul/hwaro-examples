# The Archives of the Pyre

In the deepest vaults beneath the Ninth Circle, the original contracts are kept under constant flame. These are the pacts that forged empires of suffering, the signatures written in the blood of the first damned.

Every soul that has ever bartered with the abyss is recorded here in burning ink on parchment that refuses to turn to ash. The ledger does not forget. The ledger cannot forgive.

## Notable Entries

- **The First Pact** — Signed by the Morning Star himself. Debt: Infinite.
- **The Ashen Choir Indenture** — 40,000 voices bound for a single chorus of eternal lament.
- **The Throne Rot Covenant** — The contract that toppled the Seventh Kingdom in a single immolation.

The Auditor walks these halls nightly. Every contract is inspected. Every flame is tended.

*There is no escape clause. There never was.*
