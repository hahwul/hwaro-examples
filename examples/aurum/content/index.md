Welcome to **Aurum**. Designed for writers, thinkers, and creatives who appreciate the elegance of a dark aesthetic paired with striking golden accents.

## A New Perspective

Aurum is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro). It embraces a minimal, structured approach to web design. The focus remains strictly on your content, framed by deep charcoal and soft gold.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." — Leonardo da Vinci

### Features

- **Dark Mode by Default**: Deep, rich backgrounds that are easy on the eyes.
- **Typography First**: A carefully selected combination of *Playfair Display* for headings and *Inter* for body text.
- **Minimalist Aesthetics**: Content breathes easily with generous whitespace and clear hierarchy.

### Example Code Block

```crystal
# A simple hello world in Crystal
def greet(name : String)
  puts "Hello, #{name}! Welcome to Aurum."
end

greet("Traveler")
```

Whether you are writing a journal, sharing a portfolio, or documenting a project, Aurum provides a beautiful canvas for your thoughts.
