Capital expenditures recorded against fixed asset accounts during the first quarter of the 2026 fiscal year. Each entry represents a long-term acquisition rather than a recurring operational charge. Items are depreciated according to the schedule maintained in the asset register and reconciled against the general ledger at month end.

The accounting policy follows the straight-line method for office equipment and furniture, with useful lives ranging from three to seven years. Hardware purchases above the capitalization threshold of five hundred currency units are recorded here; smaller acquisitions are charged directly to operations.
