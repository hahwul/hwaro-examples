## Material Detail

Microscope is about the minute and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that detailed content deserves a presentation that captures the observation of the early laboratory era. By focusing on metal-inspired textures, white natural surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the slide.

### Detail Principles

- **Minute**: A design that prioritizes the clear focusing of complex information.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the laboratory space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical detail observation.
