# Solder Fumes

Three notes from a weekend on the bench.

## 01

Lead-free solder makes a different sound when it sets. It sighs. I do not have scientific support for this but I am prepared to die on this hill.

## 02

The iron has been on for four years. The tip is original. It is not supposed to last four years. The trick is oxidation-resistant tin, a damp sponge, and the discipline to tin the tip before you shelve it. Every time.

## 03

A debugging session at 2am ends in one of two ways: a working device, or a working understanding of why the device will not work. Only one of them is a good ending and it is not the one you think.
