# whoami

Handle: `volt`. Day job in embedded firmware for industrial control systems; night job in whatever the last broken piece of hardware on the bench happens to be.

## what this is

A log, not a blog. Entries are filed with a Unix timestamp, lightly edited for grammar, and left otherwise raw. If a log is wrong, it stays wrong and gets a follow-up. No stealth edits.

## stack

- Static generator: Hwaro
- Editor: neovim
- Shell: zsh with a carefully curated list of grievances
- Hardware: a thinkpad on a wooden block, a soldering iron, and an oscilloscope that has outlived three apartments

## colophon

- Display: JetBrains Mono, 14.5px, 1.65 line height
- Palette: black, terminal-green, voltage-yellow. Nothing else allowed.
- Cursor: blinking. Non-negotiable.
