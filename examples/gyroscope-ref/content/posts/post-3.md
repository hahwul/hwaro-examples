Capturing data from a rapidly rotating mass requires specialized telemetry systems. Standard transmission protocols often suffer from latency and signal degradation due to the intense electromagnetic fields generated.

We utilize a proprietary optical slip-ring design to ensure uninterrupted, high-bandwidth data transfer from the core sensors to the stationary processing units. This allows for microsecond-level analysis of rotational anomalies.
