## Radii Are Tone of Voice

A 4px radius is a typewriter: precise, efficient, a little stiff. A 24px radius is a conversation: relaxed, warm, less guarded. The choice is not aesthetic — it is rhetorical.

### Practical Mapping

- **2–4px**: forms, code editors, dense data tables. Anywhere the user is working hard.
- **8–12px**: standard cards, modals, default buttons. The neutral middle ground.
- **20–32px**: hero panels, marketing surfaces, anything that wants to feel approachable.
- **999px**: pills, avatars, status chips. Treat as its own category.

Mixing two radius scales on the same surface confuses the message. Pick a base radius and let secondary elements step down by no more than half — never more than that, or the components stop reading as a system.
