Tuff SDK · v0.9 “walnut” · serial + wifi

Hey! Let’s get your robot moving.

Tuff is a friendly Python SDK for hobby robot arms and rovers. Plug in over USB serial or pair over wifi, and something on your desk is waving within ten minutes — promise.

Start tutorial one Browse the trail

Up and running in three moves

  1. 1

    Install

    pip install tuff

    One package: the library, the drivers, and the tuff command line.

  2. 2

    Connect

    tuff devices

    Arms over USB serial, rovers over wifi — Tuff finds whatever is switched on.

  3. 3

    Move

    arm.wave()

    Every session starts with a wave. Tradition. Also an excellent smoke test.
