# The Art of Silence

This is an elegant, minimal theme inspired by the quiet strength of slate. It focuses on typography and solid colors, entirely avoiding gradients and emojis.

## Core Principles

- **Typography:** Serif fonts for a timeless aesthetic.
- **Colors:** Slate grey, off-white background, deep charcoal text.
- **Simplicity:** No unnecessary embellishments.
