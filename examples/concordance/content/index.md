## The Power of the Connection

Concordance is about the relationship between different parts of a whole. We believe that innovation is often found in the way that ideas are connected and cross-referenced. By using an alphabetical-index layout and focusing on the thematic unity of the work, we reveal the complex network of meaning that supports the project.
