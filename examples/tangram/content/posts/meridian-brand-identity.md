# Meridian: A Brand Identity Built on Precision

Meridian is a navigation technology company that builds positioning systems for autonomous vehicles. Their technology demands accuracy measured in centimeters. Their brand identity needed to reflect that same rigor.

## The Brief

Meridian approached us with a clear challenge: they needed a visual identity that communicated technical precision without feeling cold or inaccessible. Their audience includes both engineers who evaluate the technology and executives who approve purchasing decisions. The brand needed to speak to both.

## Discovery

We began with a two-week discovery phase, interviewing team members across engineering, sales, and leadership. A consistent theme emerged: the company's culture centered on the idea of "finding the exact right point." Not approximation. Not estimation. Precision.

This concept became our creative anchor.

## The Mark

The Meridian logotype uses a custom geometric sans-serif with optically precise letter spacing. The letterforms are constructed on a strict grid, with every curve defined by mathematical relationships rather than freehand drawing.

The icon -- a crosshair formed by the intersection of two arcs -- references both navigational instruments and the concept of convergence. It works at sizes from favicon to billboard without losing clarity.

## Color and Type

The primary palette pairs a deep navy with a precise teal accent -- colors that suggest both the depth of technical expertise and the clarity of reliable guidance. A warm gray serves as the neutral foundation for body text and secondary elements.

For typography, we selected a humanist sans-serif for body copy, balancing the geometric precision of the logo with warmth and readability in longer text.

## System

The full identity system includes business cards, presentation templates, technical documentation standards, vehicle livery guidelines, and a comprehensive digital component library. Every element follows the same underlying grid that governs the logotype.

## Outcome

Six months after launch, Meridian reported that the new identity had measurably improved their positioning in competitive evaluations. More importantly, the team described feeling a stronger sense of cohesion -- the visual system gave them a shared language for how they present themselves to the world.
