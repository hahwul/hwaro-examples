## Material Observation

Astrolabe is about the celestial and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that cosmic content deserves a presentation that captures the observation of the early astronomical era. By focusing on brass-inspired textures, dark paper surfaces, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply rooted in tradition. Our focus is on the integrity of the sphere.

### Observation Principles

- **Celestial**: A design that prioritizes the clear mapping of complex information.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the astronomical space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of classical celestial mapping.
