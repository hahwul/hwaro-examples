  <h2>The Void Speaks</h2>
  <p>Immerse yourself in a landscape of absolute darkness, punctuated by the slow, resonant pulse of neon orbs. The <strong>Neo Symphony Glass</strong> aesthetic is an exploration of depth, utilizing translucent frosted layers that capture and diffuse ambient light from the depths below.</p>
  <p>Built with <em>Hwaro</em>, this design emphasizes stark contrasts, minimalist typography (Space Grotesk and Inter), and the organic movement of background elements.</p>
