<div class="glass-card">
  <h2>The Architecture of Light</h2>
  <p>The <strong>Neo Symphony Glass</strong> theme relies on a delicate balance of CSS variables and fixed, animated elements. The background is not merely dark, but an active participant in the visual hierarchy.</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>The Void:</strong> A deep, almost-black background (<code>#030008</code>) that provides infinite depth.</li>
    <li><strong>The Orbs:</strong> Fixed divs utilizing heavy <code>filter: blur(120px)</code> and slow translation animations to create shifting ambient lighting.</li>
    <li><strong>The Glass:</strong> Semi-transparent containers utilizing <code>backdrop-filter: blur(24px)</code> to catch the light of passing orbs, creating a dynamic, frosted effect.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="glass-card">
  <h2>Typography</h2>
  <p>To complement the futuristic and ethereal vibe, we utilize two distinct typefaces:</p>
  <p><strong>Space Grotesk:</strong> Used for all headings and navigational elements. Its slightly rigid, geometric forms provide a stark contrast to the organic shapes in the background.</p>
  <p><strong>Inter:</strong> Used for body text, ensuring maximum legibility and a clean, modern aesthetic.</p>
</div>
