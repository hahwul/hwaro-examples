The success of pointillism relies heavily on color theory. Placing contrasting or complementary colors side-by-side relies on the human mind to perceive a third, vibrant color. This technique requires meticulous planning and execution to achieve the desired visual impact.

Understanding how colors interact when placed in close proximity is key to mastering the pointillist style, whether on canvas or on a screen.
