Translating pointillism to the web involves utilizing CSS and SVG filters to mimic the traditional dot structure. This creates unique textures and borders that stand out against the conventional flat designs, offering a fresh, artistic aesthetic.

Using modern web technologies, we can generate dynamic pointillistic effects that would take hours to paint by hand, bringing this classic style into the digital era.
