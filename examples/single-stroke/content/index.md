## The Purity of the Stroke

Single-Stroke is about the reduction of design to a single, essential gesture. We believe that innovation doesn't need to be complex to be effective. By focusing on the purity of the line and the weight of the single stroke, we create a design that feels both minimal and profoundly intentional.

### Line Innovation

- **Purity**: Removing all elements that don't contribute to the primary path.
- **Direction**: A single, clear movement through the information.
- **Impact**: The result of a focused, uncompromised execution.
