## About Reef

Reef is a personal dive log and marine observation journal. Every descent beneath the surface tells a story -- the creatures encountered, the conditions faced, and the quiet wonder of moving through a world where humans are only visitors.

### The Log

Each dive entry records key parameters: maximum depth, water temperature, visibility range, and total bottom time. Beyond the numbers, each log captures what was seen, heard, and felt during the dive.

### Field Guide

The field guide section catalogs species encountered across dive sites. Identification notes, habitat preferences, and behavioral observations are recorded over time to build a personal reference.

### Gear and Methods

All dives are logged with a focus on open-water recreational diving. Depth and temperature readings are taken from dive computers. Visibility is estimated in meters. Species identifications are cross-referenced with regional marine life databases.
