When you begin with a perfect void, every mark you make carries immense weight.

The decision to use a stark, dark background is not merely aesthetic; it is structural. It demands that every line of text, every border, and every deliberate contrast serves a purpose. The absence of light forces clarity upon the content.

We embrace this silence to amplify the signal.
