## where the noise came from

The surge was not one fluke. Three channels carried most of the weight, and a fourth came out of nowhere on Friday night.

- **Neon Disco Hour** held the top spot all week, never dropping below 25 thousand daily plays.
- **Confetti Funk** climbed from rising to nearly hot in four days.
- **Zigzag Late Night** owned the after-midnight slot, as usual.

## the streak holds

The network listening streak is now at 126 days, the longest we have ever tracked. We celebrated by leaving a giant pink circle on the dashboard. It felt right.
