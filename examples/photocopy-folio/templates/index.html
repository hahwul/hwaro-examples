About the studio

What the studio does

Brand identities and identity systems. Editorial direction and book design. Motion idents and title sequences that don't pretend to be live action.

The studio works on a flat day-rate and refuses to bill by the hour. Most projects last 6–14 weeks.

What the studio refuses

  • Unpaid pitches, ever.
  • Crypto, gambling, military.
  • Decks longer than 16 slides.
  • Stock photography.
  • Mascots, "fun" emojis, exclamation marks.
  • Anyone who says "make it pop."
