# About Gazebo

Gazebo is a journal dedicated to the art of garden entertaining. We chronicle seasonal gatherings, tea ceremonies, harvest dinners, and every manner of outdoor celebration held beneath open skies and trailing vines.

Our belief is simple: the finest moments happen outdoors, surrounded by good company, thoughtful details, and the quiet beauty of a well-tended garden.
