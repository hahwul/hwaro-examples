## About This Site

Sandstorm is a blog built for those who thrive in harsh conditions. Inspired by the raw power of desert storms, this theme embraces the beauty found in hostile environments.

## Design Philosophy

- **Dust and Earth palette** -- warm oranges, sandy yellows, and deep browns
- **Particle effects** -- subtle CSS animations that evoke drifting sand
- **Dark atmosphere** -- like standing in the eye of a Sahara storm
- **Readability first** -- clear typography against the dark canvas

## About the Theme

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), Sandstorm is a dark blog theme inspired by desert survival and adventure storytelling.
