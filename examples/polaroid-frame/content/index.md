## Instant Tactility

Polaroid-Frame is about the commemorative and immediate nature of innovation through the lens of an instant photograph. We believe that significant moments deserve a presentation that captures the tactility of the physical print. By focusing on white paper surfaces, deep shadows, and elegant, modern typography, we create an environment that feels both adventurous and deeply personal. Our focus is on the integrity of the snapshot.

### Snap Principles

- **Immediacy**: A design that prioritizes the clear "instant" nature of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using frame-like borders to define the boundaries of the memory space.
- **Nostalgia**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical photo collection.
