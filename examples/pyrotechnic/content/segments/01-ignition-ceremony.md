## Overture

The show opens with a twelve-cue sequence fired from the four westernmost mortars on the pier. The first shell is a red comet peony; the following eleven are paired rises at two-second intervals.

### Flare Walk

Between cues 010 and 011 the pier crew walks a line of low ground flares along the pier edge. Audience note: the flares are deliberately dim to preserve the audience's dark adaptation for the blue hour that follows.
