## Green Willow

Willow shells have the longest tail of any effect in the show. This segment uses only willow effects, in emerald green, fired in three waves from mortars 14 through 22.

### Pattern

Wave one is five shells, ten seconds apart. Wave two, the same five mortars, five seconds apart. Wave three, all nine mortars at once.

### Timing

The total running time is eight minutes. Wave one occupies the first five minutes; waves two and three fill the last three.
