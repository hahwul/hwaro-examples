## Blue Hour

Eight minutes of deep-blue chrysanthemums. Silver strobe cores held in the centre for a slow fade. Four cue pairs, each 10 seconds apart, fifty-metre target altitude.

### Notes

- All shells in this segment are 3-inch blue chrysanthemums with silver strobe inserts.
- The target altitude is kept deliberately low so the silver strobe reads clearly to the tier.
- No multicolour shells in this segment; the colour discipline is central to the effect.

### Crew

The blue hour is the quietest segment. Deliberately. It follows the opening overture's crackle and gives the audience space before the green willow segment that follows.
