## The Cascade

Eight minutes. Gold brocade shells with crackle cores fire west to east in the show's longest continuous cue line. The pier's full width is used for the first time since Display 14 in 2023.

### Line

Twenty-two mortars along the pier fire at 1.5-second intervals. The crackle core gives the cascade a quieter tail than the opening red peonies.

### Pre-show

The cascade is the segment the crew has rehearsed longest — eleven dry runs during the week, three live tests on Thursday, two final tests on Friday night.
