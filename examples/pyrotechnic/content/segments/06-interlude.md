## Interlude

The midpoint of the show. Six minutes of low-altitude comet tails, one pause of forty-five seconds in the middle, then a closing shell that sets up the second half.

### Pause

The pause is deliberate. Crew use the time to reset the mortar tubes for the grand finale. The audience is invited to breathe out.

### Closing Shell

A single 4-inch gold brocade shell closes the segment and marks the halfway point of the show.
