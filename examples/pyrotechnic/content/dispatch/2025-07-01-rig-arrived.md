The mortar rig arrived on the pier at 07:00 Monday. Twenty-eight mortars unloaded in forty minutes, in three rows. The rig has been on site for five displays in the past three years and shows no rust — a reassuring sign.

The firing panel is set up in the command hut at the pier entrance. Cable runs are complete by 14:00.
