## Audio

Marco runs the audio system. The opening countdown, the segment breaks, the post-show audio — all from his position at the east end of the pier.

**Countdown**

The countdown at T-10 is Marco's. The audio cue is rehearsed with the firing panel to ensure the first shell fires at T-00, not T+0.2.
