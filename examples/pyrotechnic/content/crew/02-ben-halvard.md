## West Bank Loader

Ben loads mortars 1 through 14 — the west bank. He is responsible for verifying every shell against the cue sheet, in order, and signalling load complete to the firing panel.

**Reload Window**

Between segments 5 and 6 the west bank is reloaded for the grand finale. Ben has eight minutes; the sheet calls for 58 shells.
