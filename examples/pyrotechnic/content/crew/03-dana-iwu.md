## East Bank Loader

Dana loads mortars 15 through 28 — the east bank. Same responsibilities as Ben, mirrored.

**Flare Walk**

The ground-flare walk during the opening overture is Dana's. She crosses the pier once, ignites the line of low flares, and returns to the east bank for the blue hour.
