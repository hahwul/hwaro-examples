## Lead Technician

Orla runs the firing panel during the show. This is her fourth year leading the crew; before that she worked display rigs in Porto and Valletta.

Her responsibility is the firing panel, the wind hold, and the final decision on whether to proceed at T-10. She holds the emergency stop switch throughout.

**Rehearsal Weeks**

Orla keeps a paper cue sheet in addition to the digital firing panel. Both are reviewed each afternoon during rehearsal week.
