## Four Viewing Zones

| Zone         | Price       | Capacity | Range  |
|--------------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Pier West    | Free        | 2,000    | 450 m  |
| Tier Mid     | 32 EUR      | 400      | 220 m  |
| Front Tier   | 68 EUR      | 120      | 140 m  |
| Crew Bench   | 145 EUR     | 40       | 90 m   |

### Pier West

Free admission, standing only, open viewing. The west end of the pier is unticketed and first come, first served. Gates open at 20:00.

### Tier Mid

Numbered seat on the main wooden tier. The programme booklet is included with the ticket. Seated from 21:30.

### Front Tier

The front two rows of the tier. Ear protection is recommended and available at the box office. Complimentary pre-show tea from 21:00.

### Crew Bench

Forty seats only. Includes a walk-through of the mortar line with the crew at 20:30 and a visit to the command hut before the show (not during). Post-show Q&A from 23:30 to 00:30.

## Access

The pier is step-free up to the tier. The Crew Bench has three wheelchair-accessible positions; contact the box office to reserve.

## Weather

If wind exceeds 25 km/h at T-30, the show holds. If wind is still above the limit at T+30, the show is rescheduled. Tickets carry to the reschedule date.
