## Animated Softness

Cartoon-Pastel is about the gentle and foundational nature of structural innovation within the whimsical environment. We believe that animated content deserves a presentation that captures the warmth of early children's programming. By focusing on soft, pink-tinted textures, white paper surfaces, and elegant sans-serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply welcoming. Our focus is on the integrity of the tone.

### Pastel Principles

- **Softness**: A design that prioritizes the clear "tone" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the whimsical space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical cel animation production.
