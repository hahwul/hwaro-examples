## Material Permanence

Granite-Block is about the unshakeable and foundational nature of structural innovation. We believe that core content deserves a presentation that captures the permanence of the natural stone. By focusing on dark granite textures, high-contrast shadows, and monospaced typography, we create an environment that feels both utilitarian and deeply authoritative. Our focus is on the integrity of the core.

### Block Principles

- **Permanence**: A design that prioritizes the long-term stability of the work.
- **Tactility**: Using natural textures to define the boundaries of the monolithic space.
- **Authority**: A layout that reflects the solid, uncompromised nature of core ideas.
