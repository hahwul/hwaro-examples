## Expressive Tactility

Quill-Pen is about the expressive and foundational nature of innovation within the writing environment. We believe that creative content deserves a presentation that captures the tactility of the early manuscript era. By focusing on white surfaces, subtle shadows, and elegant serif typography, we create an environment that feels both sophisticated and deeply creative. Our focus is on the integrity of the stroke.

### Pen Principles

- **Expression**: A design that prioritizes the clear "writing" of the content.
- **Tactility**: Using subtle textures to define the boundaries of the creative space.
- **Heritage**: A layout that evokes the feeling of physical manuscript production.

> "In the quill pen, every stroke is a facet of the whole expression."
