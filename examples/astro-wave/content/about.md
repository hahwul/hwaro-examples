The **Astro Wave** project was born from a desire to combine early 80s synthwave aesthetics with modern, clean, static site architecture.

Our mission is to explore the outer reaches of web design without relying on heavy client-side frameworks. We believe that HTML, CSS, and a powerful static generator like Hwaro are all you need to build engaging, blazing-fast experiences.
