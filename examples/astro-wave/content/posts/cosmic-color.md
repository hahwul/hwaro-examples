The default "space" color palette has hardened into a cliché: deep navy, electric purple, and cyan accent. Used carefully, it still works. Used by default, it announces *I picked the first hues that came up when I searched for "cosmic gradient."*

## Less Worn Alternatives

- **Auroral palette**: deep teal, sage green, with a single rose-gold accent. Reads as cold but organic.
- **Nebula warm**: warm taupe, dust-orange, and a single aqua accent. Borrowed from infrared astrophotography rather than visible-light marketing.
- **Pulsar mono**: near-black with a single saturated accent (electric yellow, hot pink, or burnt orange) used at less than 5% surface coverage.

## The Test

Print your accent at 8px on a busy background. If you cannot pick it out at a glance, it is not pulling its weight. Cosmic accents need to *cut through* — they should not blend into the dark field.
