## Sampling Strategy

Participants were selected by stratified random sampling from municipal population registers in each city. Strata were defined by 5-year age band (65-69, 70-74, 75-79, 80-84, 85-89, 90+) and sex (male, female), yielding 12 strata per city and 72 strata total.

Within each stratum, individuals were randomly sampled with replacement until the target of approximately 700 respondents per city was achieved.

## Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

**Inclusion:** aged 65 or older on the sampling date, registered at a residential address in the participating city, community-dwelling (not in institutional care).

**Exclusion:** moderate-to-severe cognitive impairment (6-item Cognitive Impairment Test score > 10), unable to communicate in the local survey language, hospitalized during the data collection window.
