## Stratification Details

The 12 strata per city were designed to ensure adequate representation across the full age range of the 65+ population, including the oldest-old (85+) who are often underrepresented in surveys. Within each stratum, the sampling fraction was adjusted to achieve approximately equal cell sizes, oversampling the 85+ age bands where population counts were smaller.
