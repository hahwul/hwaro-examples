## Data Availability

The anonymized dataset and analysis code are available on Zenodo (doi: 10.5281/zenodo.cross-sectional-2026). The DCS-OA instrument is freely available for research use at dcs-oa.eu.

## CRediT Author Contributions

- **Claire Moreau:** Conceptualization, Methodology, Writing -- Original Draft, Supervision, Funding Acquisition
- **Oluwaseun Adeyemi:** Investigation (Barcelona, Lisbon sites), Data Curation, Writing -- Review
- **Piotr Kowalski:** Investigation (Warsaw site), Formal Analysis, Visualization
- **Haruto Nakagawa:** Methodology (DCS-OA validation), Writing -- Review and Editing

## Funding

Swiss National Science Foundation (grant 200021_204832). EU Horizon Europe Digital Inclusion Programme (grant 101058294). The funders had no role in study design, data collection, analysis, or manuscript preparation.
