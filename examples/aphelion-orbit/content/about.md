## The Concept

*Aphelion* represents the point in an orbit farthest from the core star. This theme is built around that concept: providing a calm, quiet, and beautiful reading experience that feels unhurried and infinite.

### Design Principles

1. **Space:** Let the content breathe.
2. **Light:** Use glow and shadow to create depth.
3. **Motion:** Subtle, continuous orbital animation keeps the interface feeling alive.

> "In the vastness of space, the silence is a canvas for your thoughts."
