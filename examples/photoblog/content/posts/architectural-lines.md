A study in brutalist architecture. The concrete structures feel imposing and aggressive from the ground level, but when you look up, the leading lines converge in a satisfying, mathematical way against the sky.

The concrete textures are what make this style of architecture so raw and unapologetic. No ornamentation, just structure.
