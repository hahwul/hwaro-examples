date = "1987-03-10"
description = "A concept for a bookshelf that doubles as an art piece, featuring asymmetric shelves."
+++

Moving beyond 2D design, this concept brings the Memphis aesthetic into the physical realm. The bookshelf uses bright yellow, red, and blue laminates.

It intentionally avoids standard horizontal and vertical lines where possible, opting for slanted shelves and circular accents that challenge the viewer's expectations of functional furniture.
