## The Observation of Innovation

Missal is about the importance of formal procedures and significant events. We believe that true innovation should be observed with a level of ceremony and respect that reflects its impact on the structure of the world. By focusing on the formal aesthetic and the integrity of the structured rites, we create a design that feels both significant and deeply authoritative.

### Formal Innovation

- **Procedure**: The strictly defined steps by which the work is executed.
- **Ceremony**: The visual importance given to every major breakthrough.
- **Authority**: A design that establishes the work as a significant, lasting record.
