## Measuring Innovation

Thermometer is about the quantification of progress. We don't believe in vague improvements; we believe in the measurement of every rising level. Our focus is on the precision of the scale and the way it reveals the underlying trends of the work.

### Rising Levels

- **Precision**: The accuracy of every measurement.
- **Trend**: The direction in which the level is moving.
- **Impact**: The result of reaching the peak of the scale.

---

> "You cannot improve what you do not measure."
