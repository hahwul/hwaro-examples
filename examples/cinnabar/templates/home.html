Gather to anneal

  1. 01GatherA molten mass of glass wound onto the end of a preheated blowpipe, straight from the 1100°C furnace.
  2. 02MarverRolled on a flat steel plate to center the gather and set its skin before the first breath goes in.
  3. 03BlockShaped true and round in a wet cherrywood block, the first form the piece actually holds.
  4. 04JackLines, necks, and feet are defined with the jacks — two spring-loaded blades working the hot glass.
  5. 05PuntyThe piece transfers to a solid rod at its base so the blowpipe end can be opened and finished.
  6. 06AnnealHours in a slow-cooling lehr, relieving the internal stress that would otherwise crack the glass.
