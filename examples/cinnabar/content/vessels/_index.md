Every entry below follows one vessel from the moment it leaves the furnace on the end of a blowpipe to the morning it comes out of the annealer cool enough to touch. Weights, temperatures, and tool changes are recorded as they happened on the bench, including the reheats and near-misses that do not usually make it into a photograph of the finished piece.
