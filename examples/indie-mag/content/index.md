## Independent Expression

Indie-Mag is about the creative and dynamic nature of structural innovation within the subculture environment. We believe that independent content deserves a presentation that captures the energy of a classic indie publication. By focusing on white surfaces, bold black borders, and impactful display typography, we create an environment that feels both expansive and deeply impactful. Our focus is on the integrity of the scene.

### Indie Principles

- **Expression**: A design that prioritizes the clear "voice" of the publication.
- **Tactility**: Using bold lines and flat colors to define the boundaries of the creative space.
- **Vibrancy**: A layout that evokes the feeling of late-20th-century independent publishing.
