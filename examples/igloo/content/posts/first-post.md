Scandinavian design is not just an aesthetic; it is a way of life. It is about finding the balance between what is necessary and what is beautiful.

### Less is More

When we remove the unnecessary, we make room for what truly matters. In web design, this means:

- Generous margins
- Minimalist color palettes
- Focused content

> "Design is a way of life, a point of view. It involves the whole complex of visual, communications, interior and office tools and objects." — Paul Rand

We follow these principles to create experiences that are both calming and effective.
