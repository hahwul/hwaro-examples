Welcome to the **Anaglyph** example site. This design explores the visual tension and depth created by offsetting cyan and red color channels.

## The Aesthetic

The anaglyph effect, traditionally used for 3D imagery, is repurposed here as a bold typographic and structural element. By shifting color layers, we create a sense of vibration and three-dimensionality on a flat digital canvas.

- **Daring:** High-contrast color offsets.
- **Clean:** No gradients, no emojis, just pure color and geometry.
- **Elegant:** Utilizes structured layouts and sophisticated typography.
