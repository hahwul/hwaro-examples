Welcome to the **Celestial Plasma Forge**, a deep void glassmorphism aesthetic theme for [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).

This design merges a dark `#01020a` abyss with animated, glowing celestial plasma orbs, all viewed through sleek frosted glass UI containers. It features crisp typography pairing *Space Grotesk* for headings and *Inter* for body copy.
