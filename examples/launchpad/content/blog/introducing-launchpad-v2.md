We are thrilled to announce Launchpad v2.0 -- the biggest update since our initial launch. This release represents months of feedback from our community and a fundamental rethinking of how teams manage their product workflows.

## What's New

### Workflow Automation

Build sophisticated automations without writing a single line of code. Our new visual workflow builder lets you create custom pipelines for any process -- from bug triage to feature releases.

### Real-time Collaboration

Every part of Launchpad is now collaborative by default. See who is editing, leave inline comments, and get instant notifications when things change.

### Analytics Engine

Our new analytics engine processes data in real time, giving you up-to-the-minute dashboards. Create custom metrics, set alerts, and export reports with a few clicks.

## Migration Guide

Upgrading from v1? We have made the transition seamless. Your existing projects, settings, and integrations will carry over automatically. Check our documentation for the full migration guide.

## What's Next

This is just the beginning. We have an ambitious roadmap for the rest of 2026, including AI-powered task suggestions, advanced dependency tracking, and native mobile apps.

Thank you to everyone who provided feedback during the beta. Your input shaped every decision in this release.
