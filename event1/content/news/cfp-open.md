We're excited to announce that the **Call for Proposals** for DevConf 2026 is now open!

We're looking for talks, workshops, and lightning talks across four tracks:

- **Frontend** — React, CSS, WebAssembly, browser APIs, and more
- **Backend** — APIs, databases, distributed systems, and architecture
- **DevOps** — CI/CD, Kubernetes, observability, and infrastructure
- **AI/ML** — LLMs, MLOps, data pipelines, and applied machine learning

## What We're Looking For

- Technical talks (30 min) with practical takeaways
- Hands-on workshops (2 hours) with guided exercises
- Lightning talks (10 min) on any developer topic

All experience levels welcome — from first-time speakers to seasoned veterans.

## Important Dates

- **CFP closes**: January 31, 2026
- **Notifications sent**: March 1, 2026
- **Speaker confirmation deadline**: March 15, 2026

Submit your proposal at [cfp.devconf.dev](https://cfp.devconf.dev).
