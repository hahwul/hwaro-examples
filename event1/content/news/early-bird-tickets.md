**Early bird tickets** for DevConf 2026 are now on sale!

## Pricing

| Ticket Type | Early Bird | Regular |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| General Admission | $399 | $599 |
| Workshop Pass | $549 | $799 |
| Student | $99 | $149 |

Early bird pricing is available until **April 1, 2026**.

## What's Included

All tickets include:

- Access to all talks across 4 tracks
- Breakfast and lunch for all 3 days
- Conference swag bag
- Access to networking events
- Video recordings of all talks (post-conference)

**Workshop Pass** additionally includes access to all hands-on workshops on Day 3.

## Group Discounts

Bringing your whole team? Groups of 5+ get an additional 15% off. Contact [tickets@devconf.dev](mailto:tickets@devconf.dev) for group pricing.
