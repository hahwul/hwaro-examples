James Taylor is a Developer Advocate at WasmEdge, where he helps developers adopt WebAssembly for both browser and server-side applications.

A polyglot programmer fluent in Rust, Go, and TypeScript, James focuses on performance optimization and novel runtime architectures. He has contributed to the WebAssembly specification and maintains several popular Wasm toolchain projects.

His closing keynote will synthesize the key themes and insights from all three days of DevConf 2026.
