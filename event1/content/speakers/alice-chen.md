Alice Chen leads the engineering organization at StreamScale, where her team builds real-time data infrastructure processing billions of events per day.

Previously, she was a principal engineer at Amazon Web Services, where she worked on DynamoDB and contributed to the Raft consensus protocol implementation.

Alice is a frequent speaker at systems conferences and has authored several papers on distributed consensus and fault-tolerant systems.
