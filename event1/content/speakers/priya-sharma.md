Priya Sharma is a Lead ML Architect at NeuralPath, where she designs and builds the ML infrastructure that powers recommendation systems for 100M+ users.

She specializes in making ML systems reliable, observable, and maintainable in production. Her open-source contributions include popular tools for model versioning and experiment tracking.

Priya holds a PhD in Machine Learning from Stanford and was previously a research scientist at Google DeepMind.
