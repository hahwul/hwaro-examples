David Okonkwo is a Principal Site Reliability Engineer at CloudNative Inc., where he manages Kubernetes clusters serving 50M daily active users.

His expertise spans container orchestration, observability, and incident response. David is a CNCF ambassador and a maintainer of several popular open-source monitoring tools.

Before CloudNative, David led the platform team at Shopify, scaling their Kubernetes infrastructure through multiple Black Friday events.
