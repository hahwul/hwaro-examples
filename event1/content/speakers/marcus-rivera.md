Marcus Rivera is the CTO and co-founder of DataForge, a startup building the next generation of edge computing infrastructure.

With 15 years in distributed systems, Marcus has led engineering teams at Cloudflare and Fastly. He's passionate about pushing compute closer to users and reducing latency to near-zero.

His talk covers real-world lessons from deploying applications to 300+ edge locations worldwide.
