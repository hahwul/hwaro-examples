The entertainment for this year's Grand Gala promises to be our most ambitious yet. We are honored to present a lineup of extraordinary talent that spans classical virtuosity and contemporary artistry.

<!-- more -->

## The Performances

**The Vienna Chamber Ensemble** will open the evening during the champagne reception, performing selections from Strauss, Debussy, and contemporary composers in the Grand Foyer.

**Soprano Elena Vasquez**, recently acclaimed for her performance at La Scala, will deliver a breathtaking set of arias that will echo through the Crystal Ballroom during dinner.

**The Laurent Dance Company** will present a specially commissioned contemporary ballet piece, blending classical technique with modern choreography in a visual spectacle designed exclusively for our guests.

## The After-Party

As the formal evening transitions to our rooftop celebration, internationally renowned DJ collective **Midnight Luxe** will take over the decks, blending deep house, jazz-infused electronica, and classic disco to keep the dance floor alive until the early hours.

We look forward to sharing this extraordinary evening of artistry and celebration with you.
