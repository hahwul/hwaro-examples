# About SubZero Laboratory

SubZero is a state-of-the-art cryogenic research facility equipped with advanced cooling systems capable of reaching temperatures mere fractions of a degree above absolute zero.

## Our Mission

To advance our understanding of matter and energy at ultra-low temperatures, developing new technologies and applications that benefit science and society.

## Facilities

- **Liquid Helium Cryostats** - Reaching 4.2K and below
- **Dilution Refrigerators** - Sub-millikelvin temperatures
- **Liquid Nitrogen Dewars** - Standard 77K operations
- **Cryogenic Electron Microscopy** - Biological imaging
- **Quantum Computing Testbed** - Superconducting qubit research

## Research Team

Our multidisciplinary team includes physicists, materials scientists, biologists, and engineers working collaboratively to unlock the secrets of the cryogenic realm.

## Safety Protocols

Working with cryogenic fluids requires strict adherence to safety protocols. All personnel undergo comprehensive training in handling liquid nitrogen, liquid helium, and working in ultra-cold environments.
