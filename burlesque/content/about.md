## The Stage Awaits

Burlesque is a bold, glamorous theme inspired by the golden age of variety theater. It brings the drama, spectacle, and elegance of the cabaret stage to the web.

Rich crimson velvet, shimmering gold accents, and the warm glow of stage lights set the tone for a reading experience that is as captivating as a showstopper performance.

## Design Philosophy

Every element is designed to evoke the theatricality of a grand burlesque revue:

- **Bold Color Palette** — Deep burgundy, black, and gold create an atmosphere of opulent nightlife
- **Dramatic Typography** — Serif display fonts and elegant body text recall vintage playbills
- **Visual Spectacle** — Gradients, shimmer effects, and decorative flourishes add layers of glamour
- **Stage Presence** — Strong visual hierarchy guides the eye like a spotlight on a performer

## Built with Hwaro
