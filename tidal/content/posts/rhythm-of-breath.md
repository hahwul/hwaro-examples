Breath is our internal tide. In this session, we explore how to synchronize our inhales and exhales with the rising and falling of the sea.

### The Inflow (High Tide)

As you inhale, imagine the cool, vibrant energy of the high tide rushing into your lungs. This is a moment of expansion, of filling up with the richness of life.

### The Outflow (Low Tide)

As you exhale, let go like the receding tide. Feel any tension, worry, or stress drifting away with the water, leaving a clean, empty expanse of sand behind.

<!-- more -->

### Practice

1. **Find a quiet space.** If you are not near the ocean, find a sound recording of waves.
2. **Close your eyes.** Focus on the sound.
3. **Match the rhythm.** Let your breath naturally slow to match the cycle of the waves (approx. 6 seconds per cycle).
4. **Be the beach.** Imagine yourself as the shoreline, passively receiving and releasing the water.

May your day be as steady and certain as the tides.
