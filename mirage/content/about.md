# About Mirage

Mirage is a visual exploration of desert landscapes and the optical phenomena that make them feel otherworldly. Every image in this collection was chosen for the way it captures that fleeting moment when heat distorts the air and the familiar becomes strange.

## The Concept

A mirage is not an illusion -- it is real light, refracted by layers of heated air. We approach photography the same way: not fabricating what isn't there, but revealing what the eye almost misses. The shimmer at the edge of a dune. The way a distant mountain seems to float above the sand. The gold that saturates everything in the hour before sunset.

## The Aesthetic

Our visual language draws from the desert itself: warm sand tones, haze gold, the pale blue of a sky bleached by midday sun, and the deep burnt orange of exposed rock at golden hour. Every element on this site echoes those colors and that feeling of vastness, stillness, and gentle distortion.

## Built With

This gallery is built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro), a fast and flexible static site generator. The heat-haze effects are achieved through SVG filters and CSS animations -- no images harmed, only light bent.
