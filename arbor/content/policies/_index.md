Team policies define the rules that govern how code moves through your branching model. A branching strategy without clear policies leads to inconsistency and confusion.

This section covers three essential policy areas:

- **Merge vs Rebase** -- when to use each integration strategy and the trade-offs involved.
- **Naming Conventions** -- standardized branch naming prefixes and formats.
- **Code Review** -- required approvals, CI checks, and ownership rules.
