## The Vision

Autumn Noir is a meditation on restraint. Each piece in this collection strips away the unnecessary, leaving only clean lines and rich textures that speak for themselves.

The palette is deliberately narrow: charcoal, obsidian, midnight blue. Against these deep tones, the quality of each fabric becomes unmistakable. A single cashmere coat tells you everything you need to know.

## Key Pieces

**The Obsidian Overcoat** -- Double-faced cashmere, unlined, with a single concealed button closure. The fabric is so fine it drapes like water.

**The Shadow Suit** -- Cut from Super 180s merino wool with a half-canvas construction. The silhouette is slim but never restrictive, designed to move with the body.

**The Dusk Scarf** -- Hand-woven silk twill in a 200cm length. Each scarf takes three days to complete on a traditional Jacquard loom.

## Materials

Every fabric in this collection has been sourced from mills we have partnered with for over a decade. The cashmere comes from Loro Piana, the silk from Johnstons of Elgin, and the wool from Vitale Barberis Canonico.
