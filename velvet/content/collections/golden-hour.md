## The Vision

Golden Hour exists in the space between day and evening. This collection captures that transient moment when light turns warm and everything it touches becomes extraordinary.

The colour story centres on champagne, antique gold, and ivory, accented with deep bronze. These are not bold colours that demand attention. They are quiet colours that reward it.

## Key Pieces

**The Aureate Gown** -- Silk organza layered over a structured bodice, with hand-embroidered gold thread detailing along the neckline. The embroidery alone requires forty hours of work.

**The Solstice Blazer** -- Unstructured linen in a warm sand tone, lined with silk habotai. The relaxed cut belies the precision of its construction.

**The Lumiere Clutch** -- Hand-stitched calfskin with a brushed gold clasp. The interior is lined with suede and features a single pocket, because nothing more is needed.

## Craftsmanship

The gold thread embroidery in this collection is executed using the Luneville technique, a method developed in 18th-century France and still practised by fewer than two hundred artisans worldwide.
