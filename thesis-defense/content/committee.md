## Acknowledgments

I am deeply grateful to my advisor, Prof. Kenji Watanabe, for his unwavering support, scientific rigor, and patience over the past five years. His insistence on connecting theory to experiment at every stage shaped the core methodology of this thesis.

I thank the members of my doctoral committee -- Profs. Liang Fu, Nuh Gedik, and Pablo Jarillo-Herrero -- for their guidance during the annual committee meetings and their careful reading of this manuscript.

The STM measurements in Chapter IV were performed with essential assistance from Dr. Hiroki Isobe and Dr. Jie Shan, who maintained the dilution refrigerator and provided training on the instrument.

This work was supported by the Department of Energy (DE-SC0020149), the National Science Foundation (DMR-2104964), and a MIT Presidential Fellowship. Computational resources were provided by the MIT SuperCloud facility.

Finally, I thank my family -- my parents, my brother Andres, and my partner Maya -- for their love and encouragement throughout this journey.
