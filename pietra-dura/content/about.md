Pietra Dura translates to "hard stone" - a technique of inlaying cut and fitted, highly polished colored stones to create images.

## The Concept

This theme adapts that philosophy to the web. Instead of physical stones, we use stark typography, rigid borders, and high-contrast colors to construct a digital monolith.

> "Form follows function, but form also dictates presence."

### Technical Foundation

Built on **Hwaro**, this site leverages a simple scaffold transformed through aggressive CSS choices. The result is a lightweight, incredibly fast site that commands attention through sheer visual weight rather than complex animations or heavy assets.
