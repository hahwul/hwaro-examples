Welcome to Quantum, a science blog dedicated to exploring the profound mysteries at the heart of reality. From the double-slit experiment to quantum entanglement, we venture into the realm where particles exist in multiple states simultaneously and observation itself changes what is observed.

Here, we examine the cutting edge of quantum physics research, the philosophical implications of quantum theory, and what this strange science means for the future of computing, cryptography, and our understanding of the universe.
