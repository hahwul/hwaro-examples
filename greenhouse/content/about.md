## Our Mission

The Greenhouse is a digital space dedicated to the art and science of indoor gardening. We believe that everyone can have a green thumb with the right information and a little bit of patience.

This site serves as both an encyclopedia of common houseplants and a personal log of their growth and development.

### Why Gardening?

- **Wellness:** Plants improve air quality and reduce stress.
- **Learning:** Every plant has its own story and requirements.
- **Connection:** Bringing a piece of nature into our modern lives.
