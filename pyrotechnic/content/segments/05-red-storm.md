## Red Storm

Eight minutes total, but the density is in the centre: sixty red peonies in eighty seconds. Double rises from the whole mortar line, cue pattern 0-0-2, 0-0-2, 0-0-2 through the entire segment.

### Colour

A deep, low-oxide red rather than the warmer red of the opening overture. The two reds are deliberately different — the opening is welcoming; the storm is not.

### Sound

The shells used here have an audible report. Ear protection is available at the box office for the front tier.
