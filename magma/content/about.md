Magma is a dashboard theme inspired by volcanic lava flows and the deep heat of the earth. Metric cards glow with ember warmth, progress bars carry the texture of molten rock, and every interaction triggers a subtle temperature rise.

The dark charcoal interface evokes a control room built into the side of a volcano -- monitoring forces that demand constant attention.

Built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro).
