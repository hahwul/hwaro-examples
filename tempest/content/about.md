# About Tempest

Tempest is a news magazine built for moments that demand attention. When the story is urgent, the design should match the intensity of the reporting.

## Our Mission

We cover the stories that shape the world, with a focus on depth over speed. Every headline is earned through rigorous reporting, every analysis grounded in evidence.

## Design Philosophy

The storm aesthetic is deliberate. News is turbulent. The world is unpredictable. Our design reflects that reality while maintaining the clarity and readability that serious journalism demands.

- **Bold typography** -- Headlines that command the page
- **Dark atmosphere** -- Reduces eye strain during extended reading
- **Visual urgency** -- Animations and contrast that mirror breaking-news energy
- **Clean layout** -- Information-dense without being cluttered

## The Team

Tempest is powered by a dedicated team of journalists, editors, and designers who believe that how a story is presented matters as much as the story itself.

## Contact

For tips, corrections, or inquiries, reach out through our established channels. All communications are treated with the appropriate level of confidentiality.
