Flux Keyboard learns your typing patterns and adjusts actuation force per key. Frequently used keys become lighter. Rarely hit keys stay firm to prevent accidental presses. The adaptation happens gradually over the first week of use.

The keycaps are injection-molded polycarbonate with an internal light diffusion layer. Each key produces a soft, even glow without visible hotspots.

Connectivity supports USB-C, Bluetooth 5.3, and a proprietary low-latency 2.4GHz wireless mode.
