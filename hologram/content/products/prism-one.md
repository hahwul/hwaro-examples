Prism One delivers spatial audio that adapts to your environment in real time. Twelve precision drivers map the room acoustics and adjust output to create a sound field that feels three-dimensional.

The housing is milled from a single block of recycled aluminum, finished with a ceramic coating that shifts between cool silver and warm pearl depending on the light.

Battery life extends to 18 hours. Charging takes 40 minutes to full capacity.
