## System Specifications

The **Silicon Forest** is built upon the robust Hwaro framework, rendering high-speed neural links and aesthetic cyber-constructs.

- **Theme:** Dark Cyberpunk Nature
- **Architecture:** Glassmorphism UI
- **Processing Unit:** Hwaro

This layout serves as an exhibition of how disparate concepts—the chaotic growth of a forest and the structured logic of a digital grid—can merge into a singular, unified design paradigm.
