## Purpose

Tundra is a dark, expedition-journal style blog theme for Hwaro. It draws inspiration from polar field research logs -- structured, precise, and stripped of unnecessary ornamentation.

Each dispatch includes standardized metadata: geographic coordinates, ambient temperature, wind speed, and visibility conditions at the time of writing. This structured approach brings the feel of a real expedition journal to your blog posts.

## Design Philosophy

The visual language is built on restraint. A dark slate background provides the foundation. Ice white and pale blue text create contrast without harshness. Monospace type for metadata evokes the precision of scientific instrumentation. The overall effect is quiet, focused, and functional.

No gradients. No decorative flourishes. Just clean typography, structured data, and enough negative space to let the content breathe.

## Technical Details

Tundra is built with Hwaro, a fast and lightweight static site generator written in Crystal. The theme uses IBM Plex Mono and IBM Plex Sans from Google Fonts. It is fully responsive and includes support for syntax highlighting, search, RSS feeds, and taxonomies.
