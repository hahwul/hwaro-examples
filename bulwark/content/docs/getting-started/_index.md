# Getting Started

This section covers the initial setup of your disaster recovery environment, including tooling installation, business impact analysis, and risk assessment. Follow the guides below to establish a baseline DR posture for your organization.

## What You Will Learn

- How to install and configure DR monitoring and failover tooling
- How to conduct a business impact analysis (BIA)
- How to build a risk assessment framework for your infrastructure
- How to define RPO/RTO targets based on business requirements

Start with [Assessment](installation/) to set up the required tooling and prerequisites.
