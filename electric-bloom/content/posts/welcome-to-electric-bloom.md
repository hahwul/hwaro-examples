Welcome to Electric Bloom — a theme where digital flora meets neon luminescence.

## The Vision

Electric Bloom is more than a color palette. It's an experience. Inspired by bioluminescent deep-sea organisms and electric flower gardens, this theme transforms your content into something extraordinary.

Every heading glows. Every link pulses with energy. The dark canvas lets each element bloom with vibrant intensity.

## Design Principles

1. **Maximum Visual Impact** — Bold colors, rich gradients, and dynamic effects
2. **Dark Canvas** — Deep backgrounds that make neon elements pop
3. **Organic Motion** — Smooth transitions inspired by natural bloom cycles
4. **Readable Glamour** — Flashy but never at the expense of content clarity

## What Powers the Glow

All visual effects are achieved through pure CSS:

- `text-shadow` creates the neon glow on headings
- `background: linear-gradient()` drives the color transitions
- `box-shadow` adds depth and bloom to cards
- CSS custom properties make the palette easy to customize
- `@keyframes` bring subtle animation to key elements

Welcome to the garden. Let the light guide you.
