# The Architecture

The **Cyber Sakura** theme is a demonstration of how Hwaro can be styled to fit a specific niche aesthetic—in this case, a dark, neon-infused cyberpunk vibe.

## Design Philosophy

This theme strips away the stark whites and subdued grays of typical minimalist design, replacing them with:

*   A deep space background (`#0d0221`)
*   Vibrant neon pink accents (`#ff2a6d`) representing the *sakura*
*   Electric cyan highlights (`#05d9e8`) for contrast
*   Monospace fonts for that authentic terminal feel

### Typography

We rely on **Space Grotesk** for clean, futuristic body text, and **Share Tech Mono** to give headings and interactive elements a technical, hard-coded appearance.

This theme is perfect for developers, hackers, and anyone looking to add a bit of edge to their digital presence.
