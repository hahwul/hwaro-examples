This dashboard provides a real-time visualization of device orientation and motion data.

### 3-Axis Gimbal Stabilization
The central visualizer uses CSS 3D transforms to simulate a physical gimbal system, responding to your device's movement.

### Telemetry Data
- **Alpha (Yaw)**: Rotation around the Z-axis.
- **Beta (Pitch)**: Rotation around the X-axis.
- **Gamma (Roll)**: Rotation around the Y-axis.
- **Acceleration**: Linear movement tracking across 3 axes.
