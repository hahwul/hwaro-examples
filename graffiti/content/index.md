# Welcome to Graffiti

The city speaks through its walls. Every tunnel, overpass, and alleyway holds a story -- written not in words, but in color, form, and raw expression.

This is a space dedicated to the art that lives outside the gallery. From the pioneers who tagged New York subway cars in the 1970s to the muralists transforming entire neighborhoods today, street art has always been the voice of those who refuse to be ignored.

## What You Will Find Here

**History** -- The roots of graffiti culture, from Philadelphia to the Bronx, from Berlin to Sao Paulo.

**Technique** -- How artists work with spray cans, stencils, wheat paste, and everything in between.

**Culture** -- The unwritten rules, the crews, the rivalries, and the community that holds it all together.

**Muralists** -- Profiles of artists whose work has shaped the urban landscape.
