## Concept

Structure explores the tension between softness and rigidity. Dense wool meltons meet fluid cashmere knits. Tailored shoulders dissolve into relaxed hemlines. The palette is muted: black, slate, cream, and a single accent of deep burgundy.

## Key Pieces

### Double-Breasted Wool Coat

Constructed from Italian wool melton with a full canvas build. Peak lapels, horn buttons, and a belt-back detail. This is a coat meant to last decades.

- Fabric: 100% Italian wool melton, 550gsm
- Colors: Black, Slate
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Cashmere Turtleneck

A mid-gauge cashmere turtleneck knitted in a single piece to minimize seams. The ribbed cuffs and hem are slightly tighter for a clean finish. The neck folds once, naturally.

- Fabric: 100% Mongolian cashmere, 12-gauge
- Colors: Cream, Slate, Burgundy
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Tapered Wool Trousers

Slim-tapered trousers with a medium rise. Single pleat, side tabs instead of belt loops, and a clean back with jetted pockets. Pressed crease runs the full length.

- Fabric: Wool twill, 300gsm
- Colors: Black, Slate
- Sizes: XS -- XL

### Quilted Liner Jacket

A lightweight quilted jacket designed as a layering piece. Diamond quilting, snap closure, and a stand collar. Worn under the wool coat or alone in transitional weather.

- Fabric: Recycled nylon shell, wool fill
- Colors: Black, Cream
- Sizes: XS -- XL
