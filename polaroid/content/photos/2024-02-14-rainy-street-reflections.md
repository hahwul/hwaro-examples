Valentine's Day and the city is crying. But in the most beautiful way. Every puddle becomes a portal to an upside-down world where neon signs dance and traffic lights blur into watercolor dots.

I walked for two hours in the rain just to capture this. My shoes are still drying.

## What I Love About Rain Photography

Rain transforms ordinary streets into something cinematic. The reflections double every light source, the wet pavement becomes a mirror, and people move differently - faster, more purposeful, huddled under umbrellas.

> "Anyone who says sunshine brings happiness has never danced in the rain."

Worth every soggy sock.
