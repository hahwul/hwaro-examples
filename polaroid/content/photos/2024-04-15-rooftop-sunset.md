My friend knows a guy who knows a guy who has roof access to the old textile building downtown. We climbed six flights of concrete stairs and pushed through a rusty door, and then...

This.

The whole sky looked like someone spilled orange paint across blue canvas. The city below was starting to light up - office windows turning into little rectangles of warm yellow. Two worlds meeting at the horizon line.

## Camera Settings

- **ISO:** 200
- **Aperture:** f/8
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/250s
- **Focal Length:** 24mm

I took about 50 shots in 10 minutes. The light changes so fast during golden hour. This one was shot right at the moment the sun touched the buildings on the horizon.
