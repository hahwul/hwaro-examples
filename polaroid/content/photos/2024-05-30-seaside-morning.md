Woke up at 5 AM for this. The beach was completely empty except for a guy walking his dog way down the shoreline. The sand was still smooth from the overnight tide, and my footprints were the first ones.

There's something about the ocean at dawn that resets your brain. The sound, the salt air, the endless horizon. All the noise in your head just... quiets down.

## The Trip

We rented a small cottage right on the coast. No wifi, no TV, just waves. I've never slept better.

- **Location:** South coast, about 3 hours from the city
- **Best season:** Late spring, before the summer crowds arrive
- **Must bring:** A good book and nowhere to be

I already want to go back.
