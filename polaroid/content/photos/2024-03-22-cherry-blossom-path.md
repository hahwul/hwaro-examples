I walked this same path yesterday and it was bare branches and gray sky. Today? A tunnel of pink. Spring doesn't knock on the door around here - it kicks it down.

The petals were falling like slow-motion confetti. Kids were trying to catch them. Dogs were confused. Everyone was smiling.

## The Ephemeral Beauty

Cherry blossoms last about two weeks. That's it. And somehow that makes them more beautiful. You can't take them for granted. You have to stop and look *right now* because tomorrow the wind might take them all.

- **Peak bloom:** Maybe 3 more days
- **Best time to visit:** Early morning before the crowds
- **Pro tip:** Bring a blanket and just sit under them
