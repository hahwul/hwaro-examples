## Holds

No. We do not hold books. The remainder bin is not a reservation service and is not a waiting list. If you want a book, take it to the counter and pay for it. If you put it back upon the shelf or leave it at the counter without paying, it is no longer yours.

This policy exists because the bin relies upon turnover. A held book ties up shelf space and sticker and prevents another customer from making the purchase. We would rather sell the book to the customer who is here now than promise it to the customer who may or may not return tomorrow.

## Refunds

No refunds upon bin items. The sticker price is the price; if you buy it, you own it. Please check the book at the counter before paying - we are happy to hold the book aside for five minutes while you examine a flyleaf or a plate, but once the transaction is complete the book belongs to you and comes with no return privilege.

This policy does not apply to our new stock (the front room), where ordinary bookshop terms of refund apply. It applies only to books from the bin, which are sold as-is.

## Trade-ins

No trade-ins on bin stock. If you wish to trade books against credit, we welcome the offer for our new stock but cannot apply credit against remaindered titles, which already carry a margin too thin to support further discount.

## Mail Order

No mail order on bin items. The bin is a physical browsing experience; come by the shop and make your selection in person. We do not list bin inventory on any online marketplace, and we do not accept phone orders for bin books.

This is not because we do not wish to sell to you; it is because the cost of packing, posting, and processing a single ten-pound book is greater than the margin on that book would allow. We would rather the book went to someone who wants it enough to come by.

## Pricing

The sticker price is binding. We do not negotiate upon sticker, even for stock that has been in the bin for some weeks. If a book has lingered long enough that we feel further reduction is warranted, we peel the sticker and apply a new one beneath. The new sticker is what you pay; asking for a further discount will not produce one.

Exception: on the last Friday of every month, all stock upon the bin floor takes a further thirty per cent off the sticker at the counter. This reduction is automatic and does not need to be requested.

## Condition Grades

Every book in the bin carries a condition grade upon its inside front cover, marked in pencil by whichever member of staff opened the lot. The grades are:

**As New** - Indistinguishable from a new book save for the remainder spray upon the top edge.

**Very Good** - Minor shelf-wear at most; all pages and plates clean; binding tight.

**Good** - Ordinary shelf-wear; possibly minor ex-owner marks (bookplate, gift inscription, or light pencil marginalia); binding sound.

**Fair** - Noticeable wear; possibly ex-library with stamps; possibly some foxing or mild damp staining; still legible and complete.

**Working Copy** - Only buy if you do not care how it looks.

Grades are applied strictly; if a book is a borderline Good/Very Good, it is graded Good. We would rather underpromise.

## Paying

Cash, chip-and-pin card, and contactless are accepted. We do not take American Express (our terminal provider's fees are prohibitive on small transactions) and we do not take digital currency in any form.

## Opening

The shop opens Tuesday to Saturday from ten to six, and Sunday from noon to five. Closed Mondays and all bank holidays. The bin is restocked every Wednesday morning and the fresh stock is generally on the shelves by opening.
