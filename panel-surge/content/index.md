<div class="debate">
  <div class="debate-side for">
    <div class="debate-side-label">For the motion</div>
    <h3>The office is a legacy cost accountants will eventually end.</h3>
    <p>&ldquo;We are defending a thousand-square-metre lease because it looked right on a brochure in 1994. The balance sheet does not share our nostalgia.&rdquo;</p>
    <p>&ldquo;The productivity question is settled. What remains is a cultural one, and cultures change one lease termination at a time.&rdquo;</p>
    <div class="speaker">Dr. Saskia Nordstrom</div>
    <div class="affiliation">LSE &#183; Chair, Organisational Behaviour</div>
  </div>
  <div class="debate-vs">vs.</div>
  <div class="debate-side against">
    <div class="debate-side-label">Against the motion</div>
    <h3>The office is where the firm learns to disagree in the same room.</h3>
    <p>&ldquo;On video, everyone is politely correct. In a room, people correct each other. The latter produces better work; the former produces better meetings.&rdquo;</p>
    <p>&ldquo;You can measure output in either mode. The question is where you measure the firm&rsquo;s judgement. For that, you need a room.&rdquo;</p>
    <div class="speaker">Aleksandr Moussa</div>
    <div class="affiliation">Atrium Architecture &#183; Founder</div>
  </div>
