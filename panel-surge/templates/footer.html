The Conference

Panel Surge is a three-day conference of moderated expert collisions. Each panel is a discussion, a debate, or a dispute — never a presentation. Held annually in March.

House Rules

Each panelist speaks in turn. The moderator may cut off; the panelist may not. The microphone is passed, never seized. Questions come after, not during.
