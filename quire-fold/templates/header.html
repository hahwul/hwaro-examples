Of Folded Sheets & Gathering Marks {{ site.title }}

Colophon Printed in octavo, gathered in quires of four, sewn on raised bands with linen thread.
Plate I On the construction of a gathered signature.

A quire is eight pages folded from a single sheet, nested within its companions and sewn at three stations along the inner fold. Our publication follows the old method — gather, press, sew, round — so that each article below arrives as a signature in its own right.
