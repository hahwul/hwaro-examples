# About Tectonic

Tectonic is a content hub dedicated to the study of Earth's lithospheric plate dynamics. Our mission is to make the science of plate tectonics accessible to researchers, students, and anyone fascinated by the forces that shape our planet.

## What We Cover

**Plate Boundaries** -- From convergent zones where mountains rise to divergent rifts where new crust forms, we examine every type of plate interaction in detail.

**Seismic Activity** -- Earthquakes are the direct consequence of tectonic stress. We analyze seismic data, fault mechanics, and the physics of wave propagation through Earth's interior.

**Volcanic Systems** -- Volcanism is intimately linked to plate tectonics. We explore subduction-driven arc volcanoes, hotspot plumes, and mid-ocean ridge eruptions.

**Earth's Interior** -- The mantle convection currents that drive plate motion remain one of geology's most active research frontiers. We track the latest findings in deep Earth geophysics.

## Our Approach

We believe in rigorous, evidence-based content. Every article draws from peer-reviewed research and established geological surveys. Complex topics are presented with clarity, but never at the expense of scientific accuracy.

The name "Tectonic" comes from the Greek word *tektonikos*, meaning "pertaining to building." The tectonic plates are, in a very real sense, the builders of Earth's surface -- constructing mountains, opening ocean basins, and reshaping continents over hundreds of millions of years.
