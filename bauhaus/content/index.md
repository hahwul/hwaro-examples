Form follows function. The aesthetics of the object are dictated by its purpose.

## Elements of Design

* Bold typography
* Primary colors: Red, Blue, Yellow
* Stark contrast: Black and White
* Asymmetric balance
* Geometric purity

We seek to eliminate unnecessary decoration. Every line and shape must have a reason for existing.
