## Solo Premiere

Felix Oborin premieres a twenty-four-minute suite for piano and a single modular synth rig. Commissioned for the festival's twelfth season and performed only once before the studio record is released in November.

**Set**

1. *Final Act (Prelude)* — 4:12
2. *Confetti Waltz* — 7:30
3. *Room After* — 12:18

Piano will be tuned the morning of the performance. The synth rig remains on stage between Felix's set and the Wishbone Republic changeover.
