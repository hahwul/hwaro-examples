## Touring the Third Record

Wishbone Republic bring their third record, *Long Days in the Market*, to the main stage for a forty-five-minute set. The new record has been touring since May and this is the twentieth date on the run.

**Set**

A short selection from the record's first half, then four older cuts, then the encore — a version of *Window Song* arranged for the festival's string section.

**Band**

Pilar Vaca (vocals, guitar), Otto Shoemaker (bass), Cedric Nash (drums), Lena Prine (fiddle), Walter Demir (steel guitar).
