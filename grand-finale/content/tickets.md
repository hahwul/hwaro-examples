## Festival Passes

The festival pass covers all three nights including the hall after-parties.

| Pass              | Price      | Notes                                 |
|-------------------|------------|---------------------------------------|
| Festival Pass     | 145 EUR    | All three nights, standing            |
| Festival Seated   | 225 EUR    | All three nights, reserved seating    |
| Festival Patron   | 620 EUR    | Seated + cloakroom + backstage bar    |

## Single Nights

Single nights are released two weeks before each show and capped at half the festival capacity.

- Friday — 65 EUR standing / 95 EUR seated
- Saturday — 75 EUR standing / 110 EUR seated
- Sunday — 70 EUR standing / 100 EUR seated

## Access

All three stages are step-free. Cloakroom is free for Patron tier, 3 EUR otherwise.
Quiet room available in the hall between sets on Saturday and Sunday.

## Children

Under 12s are free on Sunday only. Children's ear protection available at the box office on request.
