## Mission Overview

Proxima Cb is the closest confirmed rocky exoplanet to the Sol system at 4.24 light-years, orbiting within the habitable zone of Proxima Centauri. The PIONEER-CENTAURI mission reached the system after a 42-year transit using the third-generation laser sail array.

This briefing covers Phase 1 survey findings and the initial viability assessment for long-term terraforming candidacy.

## Survey Findings

### Positive Indicators

- Planet mass confirmed at 1.17 Earth masses. Surface gravity 1.1g — within acceptable human tolerance
- Presence of a thin nitrogen-dominated atmosphere detected. Baseline pressure: 0.04 atm
- Subsurface ice deposits confirmed at both polar regions via radar survey
- No evidence of active volcanism that could produce unmanageable atmospheric toxins
- Tidal locking confirmed but terminator zone shows promising temperature gradients (approximately -20 C to +10 C)

### Challenges

- Tidally locked rotation creates extreme day/night temperature differential (+180 C dayside, -120 C nightside)
- Host star Proxima Centauri is an M-dwarf flare star — UV and X-ray flux presents ongoing biosphere threat
- Magnetic field is weak and irregular — estimated 0.03 times Earth baseline
- No indigenous plate tectonics detected — carbon cycle requires full artificial implementation

## Proposed Terraforming Pathway

**Phase 1 (Current):** Complete surface mapping, atmospheric sampling, subsurface ice quantification.

**Phase 2 (Year 5-20):** Deploy magnetosphere generator satellites at L1 and L4 points. Begin atmospheric pressure enhancement using nitrogen extraction from subsurface reserves.

**Phase 3 (Year 20-60):** Temperature regulation via orbital mirror arrays targeting terminator habitation zones. Greenhouse gas introduction for polar and nightside warming.

**Phase 4 (Year 60-120):** Biosphere seeding in terminator band. Pioneer ecosystems using radiation-hardened organism variants.

**Phase 5 (Year 120+):** Permanent settlement infrastructure. Estimated habitable zone: 18% of surface area (terminator band).

## Resource Requirements

The Proxima Cb project is projected to require significantly greater resource commitment than inner-system operations due to the transit distance and flare star hazard environment. A dedicated shielded orbital station will be required before Phase 2 operations can begin.

## Recommendation

**PROCEED TO PHASE 2.** Survey data confirms Proxima Cb as a viable long-term terraforming target. The terminator-zone habitability model is scientifically sound. Resource allocation request submitted to Terraform Command Council.

Estimated full mission duration to minimal habitability: **140-180 years**.
