## About This Site

Welcome to Origami, a blog built with [Hwaro](https://github.com/hahwul/hwaro) using a Material Design-inspired theme.

The design draws from two traditions: Google's Material Design system with its emphasis on surfaces, depth, and deliberate motion, and the Japanese art of origami with its precise folds and clean geometry.

## Design Principles

- **Elevation and depth** -- Cards cast realistic shadows to establish visual hierarchy
- **Paper fold details** -- Folded corners hint at the origami metaphor without overwhelming the content
- **Material color palette** -- Bold primaries with considered accent colors
- **Typography** -- Roboto for body text, Roboto Slab for headings
- **Clean surfaces** -- White card backgrounds against a light gray canvas
