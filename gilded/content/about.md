# About Gilded

Gilded is a celebration of opulence, grandeur, and the timeless allure of gold. This theme draws inspiration from the gilded ages of art and architecture — from Byzantine mosaics to Baroque palaces, from illuminated manuscripts to Art Deco ballrooms.

## Our Philosophy

**Maximum Visual Impact** — We believe design should captivate and command attention. Every element is crafted to create a sense of awe and wonder.

**Regal Typography** — Words deserve to be presented like royal proclamations. We use serif typefaces that echo the elegance of engraved invitations and gilded book spines.

**Rich Textures & Layers** — Flat design has its place, but here we embrace depth. Gold gradients, ornate borders, and layered frames create a tactile, three-dimensional experience.

## The Art of Gilding

*Gilding* is the ancient craft of applying thin sheets of gold leaf to surfaces — from picture frames to cathedral domes. The result transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary, catching light and commanding reverence.

In the digital realm, we translate this craft through golden gradients, warm color palettes, decorative borders, and typography that speaks of heritage and distinction.
