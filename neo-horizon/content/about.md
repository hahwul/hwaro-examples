# The Neo Horizon Project

Neo Horizon is a conceptual design study exploring futuristic web aesthetics. It combines deep dark backgrounds with luminous neon accents and translucent, glass-like UI elements to create an immersive digital experience.

## The Aesthetic

The design language of Neo Horizon is inspired by cyberpunk culture, synthwave art, and modern digital interfaces. It emphasizes:

- **Deep Shadows:** Dark backgrounds (`#0d0d12`) that reduce eye strain and make colors pop.
- **Neon Lights:** Bright, highly saturated accents (`#00f0ff` cyan and `#ff003c` red) used sparingly to guide attention.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use of `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)` and semi-transparent backgrounds (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`) to create depth and hierarchy without heavy borders or shadows.
