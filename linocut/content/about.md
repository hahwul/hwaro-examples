The **Linocut** theme is a study in high contrast and physical texture. It is designed for those who want their website to feel like it was pulled fresh from a printing press, ink still tacky to the touch.

### Key Features

1.  **High Contrast**: We use a stark palette of Paper, Ink, and Accent Red.
2.  **Rough Edges**: Every major container uses SVG displacement maps to simulate the irregular edges of hand-carved linoleum.
3.  **No Gradients**: In the world of block printing, colors are solid. We respect that limitation.
4.  **Tactile Typography**: Using fonts like 'Bebas Neue' and 'Special Elite' to evoke vintage posters and typewriter labels.

> "The block of linoleum is a stubborn medium. It resists the knife, and in that resistance, character is born."

### How to use this theme
Simply apply the `rough-edges` and `ink-texture` classes to your elements to bring them into the linocut world.
