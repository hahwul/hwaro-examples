The Linocut theme brings the tactile, imperfect beauty of traditional printmaking to the web. No gradients. No soft edges. Just bold ink and raw paper.

The Philosophy

Linocut is about the removal of material. We carve away the unnecessary to reveal the core message. It's a high-contrast approach to design that demands attention.

Design Principles

  • High contrast, limited palette
  • Substantial, physical borders
  • Simulated ink bleed and paper texture
  • Dynamic, hand-cut silhouettes
